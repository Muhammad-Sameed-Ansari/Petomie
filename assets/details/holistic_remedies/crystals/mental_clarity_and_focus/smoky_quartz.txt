#Smoky Quartz#

###Location###
Placed near the lower body, paws, or resting space.
Aligns with grounding and clearing mental fog.

###Shape###
Brown-grey translucent crystal with slow, grounding waves.

###Function###
Clears mental fog caused by fear, stress, or overwhelm.
Helps animals refocus and stay centered in their environment.

###Energetic Meaning###
Mental clearing
Grounding
Emotional stabilization
Focused calm
