#Fluorite#

###Location###
Placed near the head, training area, or upper energy centers.
Supports calm mental focus and reduces overstimulation in animals.

###Shape###
Green, purple, or multicolored stone with structured, steadying waves.

###Function###
Enhances concentration, learning ability, and calm awareness.
Helps animals stay attentive during training without becoming anxious.

###Energetic Meaning###
Calm focus
Mental clarity
Learning support
Energetic organization
