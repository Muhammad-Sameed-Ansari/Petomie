#Smoky Quartz#

###Location###
Placed near the lower body or sleeping space to support grounding.
Aligns with stabilizing and protective pathways.

###Shape###
Brown to grey translucent crystal radiating grounding, slow-moving waves.

###Function###
Anchors fear, overstimulation, and nervous tension.
Helps animals relax into safety and sleep deeply.

###Energetic Meaning###
Grounding
Safety
Release of tension
Deep rest
