#Malachite#

###Location###
Placed near the heart or lower body for deep emotional extraction.
Aligns with transformation and release of old pain.

###Shape###
Green banded stone with strong, detoxifying waves.

###Function###
Draws out emotional blockages, old wounds, and held trauma.
Supports transformation and helps animals release layers of past hurt.

###Energetic Meaning###
Emotional detox
Transformation
Release of old pain
Courage to heal
