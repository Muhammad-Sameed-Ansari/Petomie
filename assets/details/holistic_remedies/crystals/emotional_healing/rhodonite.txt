#Rhodonite#

###Location###
Placed within the heart field, held by the practitioner, or positioned near areas associated with emotional memory.
Aligns with deep emotional repair and trauma release pathways.

###Shape###
Pink stone often veined with black, radiating grounding yet heart-centered waves.

###Function###
Heals emotional wounds stored in the body and energy field.
Supports forgiveness, reduces reactivity, and helps animals release fear or past hurt.
Ideal for animals recovering from trauma, abandonment, or inconsistent care.

###Energetic Meaning###
Emotional healing
Forgiveness
Stability during release
Heart-centered resilience
