#Hematite#

###Location###
Placed near the lower body or grounding centers.
Aligns with grounding, stability, and physical vitality.

###Shape###
Dark metallic stone emitting stabilizing, strengthening waves.

###Function###
Boosts physical vitality and muscle strength.
Grounds and energizes animals who feel weak, shaky, or depleted.

###Energetic Meaning###
Grounded strength
Vitality
Physical stability
Energy reinforcement
