#Agrimony#

Bach Flower Remedies support emotional harmony and self-awareness through natural plant essences.

###Description###
Agrimony (Agrimonia eupatoria) is the remedy for those who hide inner pain behind a cheerful or carefree exterior.  
It is used when someone suppresses discomfort, avoids conflict, or distracts themselves to escape emotional truth.  
Agrimony restores authenticity, allowing inner peace and honest expression.

###Emotional Pattern##
Hidden distress  
Smiling through pain  
Avoidance of discomfort  
Restlessness  
Fear of conflict

###Positive State###
Inner peace  
Authenticity  
Honest expression  
Emotional acceptance  
Ability to face challenges calmly

##Testing Number 47##

###Energetic Meaning###
Agrimony dissolves the mask of forced cheerfulness and brings gentle inner truth to the surface.  
Energetically, it supports emotional integration, honesty, and peaceful self-acceptance.
