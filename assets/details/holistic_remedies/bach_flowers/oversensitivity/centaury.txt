#Centaury#

Bach Flower Remedies help restore boundaries, self-confidence, and balanced giving.

###Description###
Centaury (Centaurium erythraea) is the remedy for people who struggle to say no, are overly helpful, or easily dominated by others.  
It supports those who give away their power, feel obligated to please, or become exhausted through over-service.  
Centaury strengthens self-worth and healthy boundaries.

###Emotional Pattern##
People-pleasing  
Weak boundaries  
Difficulty saying no  
Over-service  
Allowing others to dominate

###Positive State###
Healthy boundaries  
Empowerment  
Balanced giving  
Inner strength  
Self-respect

##Testing Number 48##

###Energetic Meaning###
Centaury reinforces the energetic boundary field and restores personal power.  
Energetically, it supports sovereignty, self-respect, and balanced relationships.
