#Holly#

Bach Flower Remedies support emotional purity and harmony.

###Description###
Holly (Ilex aquifolium) is the remedy for intense feelings of anger, jealousy, hatred, suspicion, or emotional aggression.  
It is used when the heart feels closed or threatened, leading to defensive emotional reactions.  
Holly restores love, openness, and emotional warmth.

###Emotional Pattern###
Anger  
Jealousy  
Hatred  
Suspicion  
Emotional defensiveness

###Positive State###
Love  
Openness  
Compassion  
Emotional harmony  
Warm-hearted connection

##Testing Number 50##

###Energetic Meaning###
Holly clears emotional toxicity and opens the heart center.  
Energetically, it supports compassion, softness, and the free flow of unconditional love.
