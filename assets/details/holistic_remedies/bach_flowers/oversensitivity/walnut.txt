#Walnut#

Bach Flower Remedies support transitions, protection, and inner stability.

###Description###
Walnut (Juglans regia) is the remedy for change, transition, and sensitivity to outside influences.  
It helps when someone feels thrown off by new phases of life, external pressure, or energetic intrusion.  
Walnut protects the emotional body and supports clear forward movement.

###Emotional Pattern##
Difficulty with change  
Oversensitivity to influences  
Feeling derailed by others  
Lack of adaptation  
Energetic vulnerability

###Positive State###
Adaptability  
Emotional protection  
Consistency  
Clear direction  
Healthy boundaries during change

##Testing Number 49##

###Energetic Meaning###
Walnut strengthens the energetic shield and supports aligned transition.  
Energetically, it brings stability, clarity, and resilience to external influences.
