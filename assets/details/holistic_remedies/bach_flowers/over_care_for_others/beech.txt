#Beech#

Bach Flower Remedies support emotional acceptance and compassionate perception.

###Description###
Beech (Fagus sylvatica) is the remedy for criticism, intolerance, or a tendency to judge others harshly.  
It helps develop understanding, patience, and the ability to see goodness in people despite imperfections.

###Emotional Pattern##
Criticism  
Intolerance  
Judgment  
Irritation with others  
Lack of empathy

###Positive State###
Tolerance  
Understanding  
Acceptance  
Compassion  
Objective clarity

##Testing Number 62##

###Energetic Meaning###
Beech opens perception and softens judgment.  
Energetically, it supports compassion, emotional flexibility, and harmonious relationships.
