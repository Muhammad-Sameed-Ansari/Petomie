#Rock Water#

Bach Flower Remedies restore emotional flexibility, gentleness, and inner harmony.

###Description###
Rock Water is the only remedy not derived from a plant, but from natural spring water.  
It is the remedy for self-denial, rigid self-discipline, and perfectionism.  
It supports those who place high expectations on themselves and may suppress joy or spontaneity.
