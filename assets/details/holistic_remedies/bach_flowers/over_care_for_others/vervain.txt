#Vervain#

Bach Flower Remedies support emotional ease, balance, and gentle enthusiasm.

###Description###
Vervain (Verbena officinalis) is the remedy for over-enthusiasm, tension, or excessive effort.  
It helps those who push themselves too hard, feel driven by strong ideals, or struggle to relax or unwind.

###Emotional Pattern###
Tension  
Over-enthusiasm  
Rigidity of beliefs  
Overexertion  
Inner pressure

###Positive State###
Calm inspiration  
Balanced enthusiasm  
Inner relaxation  
Flexibility  
Peaceful motivation

##Testing Number 60##

###Energetic Meaning###
Vervain softens intense energy and restores calm vitality.  
Energetically, it encourages flow, relaxation, and balanced passion.
