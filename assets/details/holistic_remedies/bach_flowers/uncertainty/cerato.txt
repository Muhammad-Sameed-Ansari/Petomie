#Cerato#

Bach Flower Remedies are a system of 38 plant and flower essences developed by Dr. Edward Bach to support emotional and mental balance.

###Description###
Cerato (Ceratostigma willmottianum) is the remedy for doubt and lack of trust in one’s own judgment.  
It is used when someone constantly seeks advice, reassurance, or validation from others, feeling unsure of their own decisions or inner knowing.  
Cerato strengthens intuition and confidence in one’s inner guidance.

###Emotional Pattern##
Self-doubt  
Seeking reassurance  
Lack of confidence in decisions  
Over-reliance on others’ opinions  
Difficulty trusting intuition

###Positive State###
Trust in inner wisdom  
Clear intuition  
Confident decision-making  
Self-reliance  
Inner certainty

##Testing Number 31##

###Energetic Meaning###
Cerato reconnects the being with intuitive truth and strengthens inner knowing.  
Energetically, it enhances clarity, discernment, and self-trust.
