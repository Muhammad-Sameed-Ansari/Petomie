#Scleranthus#

Bach Flower Remedies are a set of 38 natural essences developed to support emotional balance and clarity.

###Description###
Scleranthus (Scleranthus annuus) is the remedy for indecision, especially when torn between two choices.  
It supports those who waver, feel internally unbalanced, or struggle to remain steady emotionally or mentally.

###Emotional Pattern##
Indecision  
Inner imbalance  
Mood swings  
Difficulty choosing  
Uncertainty between two options

###Positive State###
Inner balance  
Clear choices  
Stability  
Emotional steadiness  
Decisiveness

##Testing Number 32##

###Energetic Meaning###
Scleranthus brings balance to fluctuating emotions and scattered energy.  
It stabilizes the inner landscape, allowing grounded clarity and confident choices.
