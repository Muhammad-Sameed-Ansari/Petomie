#Gentian#

Bach Flower Remedies support emotional and mental harmony through natural plant essences.

###Description###
Gentian (Gentiana amarella) is the remedy for discouragement after setbacks.  
It is used when progress stalls due to disappointment, doubt, or a tendency to give up easily.

###Emotional Pattern###
Discouragement  
Setbacks  
Loss of confidence  
Easily disheartened  
Difficulty maintaining perseverance

###Positive State###
Optimism  
Persistence  
Confidence  
Resilience  
Ability to keep going

##Testing Number 33##

###Energetic Meaning###
Gentian lifts the spirit after challenges and restores faith in progress.  
Energetically, it strengthens perseverance, motivation, and steady forward movement.
