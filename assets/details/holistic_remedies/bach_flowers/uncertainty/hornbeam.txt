#Hornbeam#

Bach Flower Remedies provide gentle emotional support through natural flower essences.

###Description###
Hornbeam (Carpinus betulus) is the remedy for mental fatigue, procrastination, or feeling unprepared for daily tasks.  
It is linked to the “Monday morning feeling”—tiredness before starting, not after.

###Emotional Pattern##
Mental fatigue  
Procrastination  
Lack of motivation  
Feeling unprepared  
Weariness at the thought of tasks

###Positive State###
Fresh energy  
Motivation  
Mental strength  
Readiness  
Balanced vitality

##Testing Number 35##

###Energetic Meaning###
Hornbeam revitalizes mental energy and restores readiness.  
Energetically, it brings renewal, strength, and the spark to begin.
