#Rescue Remedy (Five Flower Remedy)#

Bach Flower Remedies are a system of 38 natural plant essences created by Dr. Edward Bach to support emotional and mental balance.  
Rescue Remedy is a combination remedy, designed for use during moments of acute stress, shock, emotional upset, or crisis.  
It contains five individual Bach Flowers working synergistically to stabilize the mind, body, and emotional field.

###Description###
Rescue Remedy is blended from:
- Rock Rose – panic, terror  
- Impatiens – tension, impatience  
- Clematis – dissociation, drifting away  
- Star of Bethlehem – trauma, shock  
- Cherry Plum – fear of losing control  

It is used during emergencies, emotional overwhelm, sudden distress, or high-intensity situations requiring immediate energetic support.  
The remedy helps restore presence, calmness, clarity, and emotional grounding.
