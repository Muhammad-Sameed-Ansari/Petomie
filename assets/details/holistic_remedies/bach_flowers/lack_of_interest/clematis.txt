#Clematis#

Bach Flower Remedies assist emotional balance through gentle vibrational support.

###Description###
Clematis (Clematis vitalba) is the remedy for daydreaming, spaciness, or drifting away from the present moment.  
It supports individuals who are imaginative but disconnected, absent-minded, or living more in fantasy than reality.

###Emotional Pattern##
Daydreaming  
Disconnection from present  
Lack of focus  
Impracticality  
Mental drifting

###Positive State###
Grounded presence  
Focused attention  
Creative expression with practicality  
Awareness  
Engagement in the now

##Testing Number 38##

###Energetic Meaning###
Clematis grounds scattered energy and brings presence into the physical world.  
Energetically, it strengthens embodiment, concentration, and practical creativity.
