#Olive#

Bach Flower Remedies help restore energetic and emotional balance during times of exhaustion.

###Description###
Olive (Olea europaea) is the remedy for deep physical and mental fatigue, often following prolonged stress, illness, or overexertion.  
It supports recovery, renewal, and restoration of vitality.
