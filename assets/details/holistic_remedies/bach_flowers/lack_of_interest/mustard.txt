#Mustard#

Bach Flower Remedies support emotional steadiness and inner light.

###Description###
Mustard (Sinapis arvensis) is the remedy for sudden, unexplained sadness that comes and goes like a cloud.  
It assists when melancholy appears without reason and lifts again unpredictably.

###Emotional Pattern###
Sudden sadness  
Melancholy without cause  
Emotional heaviness  
Unpredictable mood drops  
Dark, cloud-like feelings

###Positive State###
Inner light  
Emotional stability  
Joy  
Calm resilience  
Balanced mood

##Testing Number 42##

###Energetic Meaning###
Mustard lifts emotional darkness and restores inner brightness.  
Energetically, it helps reconnect with joy, clarity, and stable emotional flow.
