#Wild Rose#

Bach Flower Remedies are a natural system created to support emotional harmony and restore balance.

###Description###
Wild Rose (Rosa canina) is the remedy for apathy, resignation, or giving up without resistance.  
It is used when someone has emotionally “checked out,” accepting difficulties without attempting to improve their circumstances.  
Wild Rose restores spark, engagement, and renewed interest in life.

###Emotional Pattern###
Apathy  
Resignation  
Lack of motivation  
Disengagement  
Passive acceptance of struggle

###Positive State###
Enthusiasm  
Renewed interest  
Motivation  
Emotional awakening  
Active participation in life

##Testing Number 37##

###Energetic Meaning###
Wild Rose reignites inner vitality and restores the will to engage with life.  
Energetically, it brightens the aura and reawakens curiosity, purpose, and joy.
