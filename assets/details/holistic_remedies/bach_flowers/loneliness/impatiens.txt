#Impatiens#

Bach Flower Remedies help restore emotional ease and inner flow.

###Description###
Impatiens (Impatiens glandulifera) is the remedy for impatience, irritability, or frustration with delays.  
It supports those who move quickly, think quickly, and become stressed when others cannot keep up.  
Impatiens brings tolerance, calm, and rhythmic ease.

###Emotional Pattern##
Impatience  
Irritation  
Frustration  
Feeling rushed  
Low tolerance for delays

###Positive State###
Patience  
Calm  
Ease with timing  
Balanced pace  
Inner softness

##Testing Number 45##

###Energetic Meaning###
Impatiens slows the energetic tempo and restores natural rhythm.  
Energetically, it supports softness, calm timing, and tolerance for differing speeds and styles.
