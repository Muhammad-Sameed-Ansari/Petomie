#Water Violet#

Bach Flower Remedies support emotional harmony and gentle inner balance through natural plant essences.

###Description###
Water Violet (Hottonia palustris) is the remedy for individuals who appear self-contained, reserved, or emotionally distant.  
It is used when someone withdraws from others, preferring solitude, or when pride or independence creates emotional isolation.  
Water Violet restores openness, connection, and quiet warmth.

###Emotional Pattern##
Emotional distancing  
Reserved behavior  
Pride or self-containment  
Difficulty connecting  
Feeling separate from others

###Positive State###
Connection  
Warmth  
Emotional openness  
Humility  
Healthy independence with closeness

##Testing Number 44##

###Energetic Meaning###
Water Violet softens emotional walls and encourages gentle connection with others.  
Energetically, it supports humility, relational ease, and the ability to share presence without losing independence.
