#Elm#

Bach Flower Remedies provide support for emotional overwhelm and self-expectation.

###Description###
Elm (Ulmus procera) is the remedy for sudden overwhelm, especially in capable individuals who temporarily feel unable to cope.  
It supports those who take on much responsibility and then collapse under pressure.

###Emotional Pattern##
Overwhelm  
Temporary loss of confidence  
Feeling inadequate  
Stress from responsibility  
Emotional overload

###Positive State###
Capability  
Confidence  
Resilience  
Balanced responsibility  
Renewed strength

##Testing Number 53##

###Energetic Meaning###
Elm restores grounded strength and stable capacity.  
Energetically, it supports resilience, centering, and the ability to handle responsibilities with ease.
