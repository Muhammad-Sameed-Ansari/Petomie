#Oak#

Bach Flower Remedies support strength, perseverance, and balanced effort.

###Description###
Oak (Quercus robur) is the remedy for those who push themselves beyond their limits, never giving up even when exhausted.  
It helps restore balance, allowing strength without strain.

###Emotional Pattern##
Overwork  
Pushing through exhaustion  
Duty without rest  
Rigidity  
Ignoring personal limits

###Positive State###
Endurance  
Balanced strength  
Rest with purpose  
Resilience  
Healthy determination

##Testing Number 57##

###Energetic Meaning###
Oak restores sustainable energy and balanced perseverance.  
Energetically, it supports grounded stamina and the wisdom to rest.
