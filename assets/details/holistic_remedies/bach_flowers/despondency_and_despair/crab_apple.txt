#Crab Apple#

Bach Flower Remedies help cleanse emotional and energetic clutter.

###Description###
Crab Apple (Malus pumila) is the remedy for feelings of impurity, contamination, or strong self-dislike.  
It is known as the “cleansing remedy,” used when someone fixates on imperfections or feels uncomfortable in their own skin.

###Emotional Pattern##
Feeling unclean  
Dislike of self or body  
Obsessive focus on flaws  
Discomfort with imperfection  
Emotional contamination

###Positive State###
Self-acceptance  
Clarity  
Emotional cleanliness  
Balanced perspective  
Sense of purity

##Testing Number 58##

###Energetic Meaning###
Crab Apple clears emotional and energetic toxicity.  
Energetically, it supports purification, acceptance, and harmonious self-perception.
