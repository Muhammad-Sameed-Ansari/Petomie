#Star of Bethlehem#

Bach Flower Remedies help soothe shock, trauma, and emotional wounds.

###Description###
Star of Bethlehem (Ornithogalum umbellatum) is the remedy for shock—whether recent, long past, physical, or emotional.  
It is known as the “comforter and soother of pain,” assisting healing after difficult experiences.

###Emotional Pattern##
Shock  
Trauma  
Emotional numbness  
Aftereffects of grief or distress  
Delayed emotional responses

###Positive State###
Comfort  
Healing  
Emotional integration  
Inner peace  
Restoration

##Testing Number 55##

###Energetic Meaning###
Star of Bethlehem repairs energetic fractures from trauma.  
Energetically, it brings deep soothing, comfort, and reintegration of the emotional field.
