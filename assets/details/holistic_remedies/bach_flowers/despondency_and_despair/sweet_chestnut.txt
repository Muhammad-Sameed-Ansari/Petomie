#Sweet Chestnut#

Bach Flower Remedies support deep emotional transformation and renewal.

###Description###
Sweet Chestnut (Castanea sativa) is the remedy for moments of extreme anguish, “dark night of the soul” states, or feeling at the limit of endurance.  
It brings spiritual light, release, and hope during profound emotional suffering.

Despair

Hopelessness

###Positive State###

Faith  
Spiritual renewal  
Light in darkness  
Emotional breakthrough

###Energetic Meaning###
Sweet Chestnut clears deeply embedded emotional suffering and opens the channel for spiritual light.
