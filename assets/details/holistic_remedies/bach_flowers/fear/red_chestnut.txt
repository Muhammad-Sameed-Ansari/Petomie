#Red Chestnut#

Bach Flower Remedies are a system of 38 natural plant essences created by Dr. Edward Bach to support emotional harmony and balance.

###Description###
Red Chestnut (Aesculus carnea) is the remedy for fear for others — worry, overprotectiveness, or imagining worst-case scenarios about loved ones.  
It helps restore emotional boundaries and reduces the tendency to absorb or project fear onto others.

###Emotional Pattern##
Excessive worry for others  
Overprotectiveness  
Fear something bad will happen  
Emotional entanglement  
Hypervigilance around loved ones

###Positive State###
Healthy emotional boundaries  
Trust in others’ resilience  
Calm reassurance  
Clear thinking  
Supportive presence without anxiety

##Testing Number 30##

###Energetic Meaning###
Red Chestnut clears energetic entanglement and rebalances emotional boundaries.  
It brings trust, composure, and the ability to support others without becoming overwhelmed.  
Energetically, it reduces fear projection and strengthens emotional independence.
