#Rock Rose#

Bach Flower Remedies are a system of 38 plant and flower essences developed by Dr. Edward Bach in the 1930s to support emotional and mental balance. The remedies are gentle, non-toxic, and work by addressing specific emotional states to help restore emotional harmony.

###Description###
Rock Rose (Helianthemum nummularium) is one of the Bach Flower Remedies used for states of extreme fear, panic, or terror.  
It supports beings who become overwhelmed to the point of paralysis or shock, where the mind and body freeze or lose the ability to act.  
Rock Rose is also one of the core ingredients in Rescue Remedy, reflecting its role in stabilizing intense emotional crises.

###Energetic Meaning###
Rock Rose stabilizes shock, dissolves terror, and restores inner strength.  
It reconnects the being with courage and grounded presence, strengthening the energetic field and reducing fragmentation during crisis.  
Energetically, it supports resilience, bravery, and the ability to move forward even under extreme pressure.
