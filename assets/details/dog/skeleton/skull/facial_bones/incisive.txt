#Incisive Bone#

##Location##
Located at the front of the upper jaw, forming the anterior part of the face and the front section of the hard palate.  
It lies in front of the maxilla and supports the upper incisor teeth.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Houses the sockets for the upper incisor teeth.  
   Shapes the front of the muzzle and forms the upper lip support.

2. **Palatine Process** – Extends caudally to form part of the hard palate.  
   Joins with the maxilla and palatine bones, separating the oral and nasal cavities.

3. **Nasal Process** – Projects upward to meet the nasal bone.  
   Helps define the upper part of the nasal aperture and supports the nasal cartilage.

##Function##
 Anchors the upper incisors.  
 Forms the front part of the hard palate and nasal opening.  
 Provides structure to the muzzle and nasal area.  
 Contributes to prehension and facial movement.
