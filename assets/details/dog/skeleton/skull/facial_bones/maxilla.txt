#Maxilla#

##Location##
Forms the upper jaw and part of the face.  
Extends from the incisive bone at the front to the zygomatic bone at the back.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Contains alveoli for the upper teeth.  
   Houses the maxillary sinus and supports facial structure.

2. **Facial Surface** – Forms the side of the face and provides muscle attachment.  
   Contributes to the formation of the nasal and oral cavities.

3. **Palatine Process** – Extends medially to form part of the hard palate.  
   Separates the nasal and oral cavities and connects to the opposite maxilla.

4. **Infraorbital Foramen** – Opening below the orbit for the passage of nerves and blood vessels.  
   Provides sensation to the upper lip and face.

##Function##
 Houses upper teeth and supports chewing.  
 Forms part of the hard palate, nasal cavity, and orbit.  
 Provides attachment for facial and masticatory muscles.  
 Contributes to overall facial structure and function.
