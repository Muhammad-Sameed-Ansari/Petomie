#Maxilla#

##Location##  
Forms the **upper jaw**, shaping the **face** and holding upper teeth, as well as contributing to the nasal and orbital cavities.  

###Shape###  
A **large paired bone** forming the primary framework of the mid-face.  

###Parts###  

##Alveolar Process##  
Houses upper canine, premolar, and molar teeth.  
Energetically, represents **support and nourishment**.  

##Palatine Process##  
Creates part of the hard palate.

##Infraorbital Foramen##  
Allows passage of sensory nerves.  
Energetically, reflects **connection and awareness**.
