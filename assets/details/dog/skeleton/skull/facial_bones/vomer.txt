#Vomer#

##Location##
A thin midline bone located within the nasal cavity.  
Forms the lower portion of the nasal septum, dividing the nasal cavity into two halves.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Long, narrow plate forming the ventral part of the nasal septum.  
   Extends from the sphenoid to the incisive region.  
   Lies between the two nasal cavities.

2. **Wings (Alae)** – Small lateral expansions articulating with the sphenoid and palatine bones.  
   Provide stability and support to the septum.

##Function##
 Divides the nasal cavity into right and left passages.  
 Supports the septal cartilage and nasal structure.  
 Contributes to airflow direction and nasal cavity shape.
