#Nasal Bone#

##Location##
Located along the midline of the face, forming the bridge of the nose.  
Extends from the frontal bone to the nasal cartilages.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Elongated plate forming the dorsal surface of the nasal cavity.  
   Meets its pair at the midline to cover the nasal passages.

2. **Caudal End** – Articulates with the frontal bone, shaping the top of the nasal cavity.  
   Supports the nasal cartilages and the soft tissues of the muzzle.

##Function##
 Forms the upper boundary of the nasal cavity.  
 Protects the nasal passage and supports facial structure.  
 Contributes to airflow and nasal integrity.
