#Palatine Bone#

##Location##
Located at the caudal part of the nasal cavity, forming the back section of the hard palate and part of the nasal passage.  
Lies between the maxilla and the pterygoid bones.

###Parts###
1. **Horizontal Plate** – Forms the caudal part of the hard palate.  
   Joins with the opposite palatine bone along the midline.  
   Separates the nasal cavity from the oral cavity.

2. **Perpendicular Plate** – Extends upward into the nasal cavity.  
   Forms part of the lateral nasal wall and supports mucosal lining.

##Function##
 Forms part of the hard palate separating nasal and oral cavities.  
 Provides attachment for muscles of the soft palate.  
 Contributes to airflow structure and skull stability.
