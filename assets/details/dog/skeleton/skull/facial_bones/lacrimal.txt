#Lacrimal Bone#

##Location##
A small, thin bone located on the medial wall of the orbit near the nasal cavity.  
Forms part of the eye socket and connects with the maxilla and frontal bones.

###Parts###
1. **Orbital Surface** – Smooth plate forming part of the orbit wall.  
   Provides support for ocular structures.

2. **Lacrimal Fossa** – Depression that contains the lacrimal sac.  
   Leads into the nasolacrimal canal for tear drainage into the nasal cavity.

##Function##
 Forms part of the orbit and supports the eye.  
 Provides structure for the tear drainage system.  
 Contributes to facial symmetry and ocular stability.
