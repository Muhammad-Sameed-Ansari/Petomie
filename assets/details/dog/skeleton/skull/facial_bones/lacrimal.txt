#Lacrimal Bone#

##Location##  
Found at the **inner corner of each eye socket**, supporting the lacrimal sac and tear ducts.  

###Shape###  
A **small, thin bone** forming the medial wall of the orbit.  

###Parts###  

##Lacrimal Fossa##  
Houses the tear duct system.  
Energetically, represents **release and cleansing**.  

##Orbital Surface##  
Forms part of the eye socket.  
Energetically, symbolizes **clarity and perception**.  

###Function###  
Channels tears from the eyes to the nasal cavity, maintaining lubrication and emotional flow.  
Energetically, fosters **compassion and emotional purification**.

**Emotional Meaning:** Acceptance and release.  
**Balanced Energy:** Emotional clarity and gentle awareness.
