#Zygomatic Bone#

##Location##
Also known as the cheekbone, it is located on the lateral side of the face.  
Forms the prominence of the cheek and part of the orbit.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Main portion forming the lateral wall of the orbit.  
   Provides surface for muscle attachment.

2. **Temporal Process** – Extends backward to meet the zygomatic process of the temporal bone.  
   Together, they form the zygomatic arch.

3. **Orbital Margin** – Contributes to the lateral border of the eye socket.  
   Provides strength and protection to the orbit.

##Function##
 Forms the zygomatic arch and supports the orbit.  
 Provides attachment for chewing muscles.  
 Contributes to facial strength and contour.
