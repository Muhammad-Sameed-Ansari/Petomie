#Canine#

##Location##
Located behind the incisors on the upper right jaw, firmly anchored in the maxilla.  
Used for gripping, tearing, and holding.

###Shape###
Long, pointed tooth with a deep root and curved tip for strength and precision.

###Function###
Crucial for catching and holding prey, expressing dominance, and maintaining alignment.  
Energetically represents courage, focus, and direction.
