#Canine#

##Location##
Located behind the incisors on the lower left jaw, anchored deeply in the mandible.  
A strong tooth for gripping, tearing, and defending.

###Shape###
Long, curved tooth with a deep root for strength and precision.

###Function###
Used for grasping, tearing, and expressing dominance or confidence.  
Energetically represents power, assertiveness, and will.
