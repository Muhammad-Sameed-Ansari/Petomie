#Patella#

##Location##  
Located in front of the **stifle joint**, embedded within the tendon of the quadriceps muscle.  
It acts as a protective and functional component of the joint mechanism.  

###Shape###  
A **small, triangular sesamoid bone** that glides in the groove of the femur.  

###Parts###  

##Base##  
Connected to the quadriceps tendon.  
Energetically, symbolizes **leadership and movement**.  

##Apex##  
Links to the tibial crest through the patellar ligament.  
Energetically, represents **anchoring and steadiness**.  

##Articular Surface##  
Glides along the femoral trochlea.  
Energetically, reflects **ease and alignment**.  

###Function###  
Improves leverage for knee extension and shields the joint.  
Energetically, harmonizes **direction, confidence, and smooth progress**.
