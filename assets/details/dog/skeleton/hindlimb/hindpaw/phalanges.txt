#Phalanges#

##Location##  
Located at the **ends of the limbs**, forming the **toes (digits)** of the paw.  
Each toe (except the dewclaw) contains three phalanges — proximal, middle, and distal.  

###Shape###  
Small, **elongated bones** that provide flexibility and dexterity for motion and balance.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal Phalanx##  
Connects to the metatarsals, forming strong yet flexible joints.  
Energetically, symbolizes **foundation and purpose**.  

##Middle Phalanx##  
Acts as a bridge for coordinated movement.  
Energetically, represents **balance and rhythm**.  

##Distal Phalanx##  
Carries the claw and aids in traction.  
Energetically, reflects **expression and assertiveness**.  

###Function###  
Enable grasping, digging, and stability in movement.  
Energetically, express **willpower, precision, and natural instinct**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Root and Throat Chakras.  
**Meridian:** Liver Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Confidence in expression and control.  
**Balanced Energy:** Graceful strength and self-assurance.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Claw injury, joint stiffness, or deformity.  
Energetically, **frustration or hesitation in taking action**.
