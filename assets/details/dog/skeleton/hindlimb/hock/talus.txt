#Talus#

##Location##  
Situated in the **upper hock**, between the **tibia/fibula** and the **calcaneus/central tarsal bones**.  
It forms part of the main hinge joint of the hock.  

###Shape###  
A **rounded, block-like bone** with smooth surfaces that articulate for motion and stability.  

###Parts###  

##Head##  
Connects with the central tarsal bone.  
Energetically, symbolizes **connection and transition**.  

##Body##  
Supports the tibia and transfers weight through the limb.  
Energetically, reflects **stability and flow**.  

##Trochlea##  
The curved surface that articulates with the tibia.  
Energetically, represents **guidance and direction**.  

###Function###  
Acts as a pivot and weight-bearing bone, supporting smooth flexion and extension of the hock.  
Energetically, ensures **balance, alignment, and grounded movement**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Root Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Bladder Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Trust in stability and flow.  
**Balanced Energy:** Secure and grounded mobility.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Joint pain, stiffness, or dislocation.  
Energetically, **fear of instability or misalignment in trust**.  

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###  
Encourage balanced movement and stability.  
**Colors:** Brown and red.  
**Crystals:** Hematite and smoky quartz.  
**Elements:** Earth.  
**Affirmation:** “I am balanced, steady, and supported.”
