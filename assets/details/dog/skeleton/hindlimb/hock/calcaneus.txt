#Calcaneus#

##Location##  
Located at the **back of the hock (tarsus)**, forming the **heel bone**.  
It connects with the talus and acts as a lever for the extensor muscles of the hind limb.  

###Shape###  
A **long, prominent bone** with a raised projection (calcaneal tuber) forming the point of the hock.  

###Parts###  

##Calcaneal Tuber##  
The upper projection where the Achilles tendon attaches.  
Energetically, symbolizes **strength, propulsion, and endurance**.  

##Body##  
Forms the shaft of the bone, transmitting forces during movement.  
Energetically, reflects **support and determination**.  

##Articular Surfaces##  
Join with the talus and tarsal bones for joint flexibility.  
Energetically, represent **balance and cooperation**.  

###Function###  
Acts as a lever for hind limb extension and provides strength for jumping and running.  
Energetically, embodies **momentum, grounded energy, and motivation**.
