#Fibula#

##Location##  
Situated parallel to the **tibia**, on the outer side of the hind limb.  
It extends from the **stifle** to the **hock**, offering balance and muscular attachment.  

###Shape###  
A **thin, elongated bone** that tapers distally, complementing the tibia for stability and coordination.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal End##  
Articulates with the tibia and supports muscle attachment.  
Energetically, symbolizes **support and connection**.  

##Shaft##  
Serves as an anchor for several muscles of the lower leg.  
Energetically, reflects **resilience and harmony**.  

##Distal End##  
Articulates with the tarsals and completes the hock joint.  
Energetically, represents **balance and adaptability**.  

###Function###  
Provides lateral stability, attachment for muscles, and aids fine balance of movement.  
Energetically, enhances **fluidity, alignment, and adaptive motion**.
