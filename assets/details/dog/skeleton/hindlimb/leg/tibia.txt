#Tibia#

##Location##  
Found in the **hind limb**, connecting the **stifle joint (knee)** to the **hock joint**.  
It lies medial to the fibula and bears most of the weight in the lower leg.  

###Shape###  
A **robust, triangular bone** that provides major support and articulation at both ends.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal End##  
Articulates with the femur to form the stifle joint.  
Energetically, symbolizes **foundation and initiation**.  

##Shaft##  
Strong and slightly curved to withstand tension.  
Energetically, represents **stability and progression**.  

##Distal End##  
Forms part of the hock joint, articulating with the tarsals.  
Energetically, reflects **transition and grounded motion**.  

###Function###  
Supports body weight, assists in flexion and extension, and provides leverage for movement.  
Energetically, sustains **balance, endurance, and grounded confidence**.
