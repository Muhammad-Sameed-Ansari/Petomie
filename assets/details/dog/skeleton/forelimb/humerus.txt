#Humerus#

##Location##  
The humerus is the **main bone of the upper forelimb**, connecting the **scapula** to the **radius and ulna**.  
It forms the **shoulder joint** proximally and the **elbow joint** distally.  
It lies between the body and the forearm, providing leverage for powerful limb movement.

###Shape###  
A **long bone** with a rounded head at the top, a narrow shaft, and a broad distal end for articulation with the radius and ulna.

###Parts###  

##Head##  
Forms the shoulder joint with the glenoid cavity of the scapula.  
Energetically, represents **union, trust, and flow between body and limb**.

##Greater and Lesser Tubercles##  
Provide attachment for strong shoulder muscles.  
Energetically, symbolize **strength and self-assurance**.

##Deltoid Tuberosity##  
A raised area on the lateral surface for deltoid muscle attachment.  
Energetically, reflects **expression through action**.

##Shaft##  
The long central portion that supports weight and motion.  
Energetically, embodies **direction and endurance**.

##Distal End##  
Includes the condyles and epicondyles forming the elbow joint.  
Energetically, symbolizes **transition and adaptability**.

###Function###  
The humerus provides leverage for movement of the forelimb, enabling walking, running, and lifting.  
It supports strong muscular attachment and efficient limb motion.  
Energetically, it represents **courage, forward drive, and the manifestation of will into motion**.
