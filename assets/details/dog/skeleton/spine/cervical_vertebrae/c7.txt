#C7#

##Location##  
Located in the neck region of the dog, forming the upper portion of the spinal column between the skull and thoracic vertebrae.  
C7 supports the head and enables flexibility, posture, and smooth neck motion.  

###Shape###  
A small, irregular bone with a central body, transverse processes, and an opening for the spinal cord.  
C1 and C2 are specialized for head movement; the others allow flexible neck motion.

###Function###  
Supports the skull, protects the spinal cord, and enables motion such as turning, lifting, and nodding the head.
