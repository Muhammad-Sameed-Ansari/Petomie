#Premolar 4#

##Location##
Located behind the third premolar on the lower right jaw, forming the main slicing surface with the upper carnassial tooth.

###Shape###
Large, multi-pointed tooth with strong ridges.

###Function###
Slices, crushes, and prepares food for grinding.  
Energetically represents resilience, strength, and transformation.
