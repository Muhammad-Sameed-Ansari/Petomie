#Premolar 1#

##Location##
Located just behind the canine on the lower right jaw.  
Begins the sequence of premolars for slicing and cutting.

###Shape###
Small with sharp edges and a single root for slicing soft food.

###Function###
Starts the cutting process and transitions food toward the main chewing area.  
Energetically represents readiness and focus.
