#Premolar 1#

##Location##
Situated just behind the canine on the lower left jaw.  
The first premolar begins the chewing sequence.

###Shape###
Small with sharp edges for cutting and separating food.

###Function###
Cuts smaller pieces of food before deeper chewing.  
Energetically represents readiness, clarity, and transformation.
