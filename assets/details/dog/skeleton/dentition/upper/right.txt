#Upper Right Dentition#

##Location##
Located in the upper right quadrant of the dog's mouth, within the maxilla (upper jaw).  
Includes the teeth from the midline (between the upper central incisors) to the last molar on the right side.

###Parts###
, Incisors (3): Used for grasping and nibbling food and objects.  
, Canine (1): Long, pointed tooth for holding and tearing.  
, Premolars (4): Sharp-edged teeth for cutting and shearing food.  
, Molars (2): Broad, flat teeth for grinding.

##Function##
, Grasping, tearing, and crushing food.  
, Maintaining jaw alignment and chewing efficiency.  
, Supporting facial structure and muscle attachment.  
, Essential for grooming, carrying, and communication behaviors.
