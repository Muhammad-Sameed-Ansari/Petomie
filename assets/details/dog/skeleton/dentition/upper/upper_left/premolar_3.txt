#Premolar 3#

##Location##
Located behind the second premolar on the upper left jaw.  
Acts as a major slicing and shearing tooth.

###Shape###
Sharp-edged with double roots for strength and precision.

###Function###
Cuts and slices dense food material.  
Energetically symbolizes transformation and collaboration.
