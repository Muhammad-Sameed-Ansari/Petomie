#Premolar 4#

##Location##
Located behind the third premolar on the upper left jaw, forming the upper carnassial tooth.  
The most powerful cutting tooth in the upper jaw.

###Shape###
Large, multi-pointed tooth with broad cutting edges.

###Function###
Slices and crushes tough food effectively.  
Energetically represents strength, resilience, and transformation.

###Energetic Connection###
Linked Organ/System: Stomach and Spleen.  
Chakra: Solar Plexus Chakra.  
Meridian: Spleen Meridian.  
Emotional Meaning: Endurance and grounded power.  
Balanced Energy: Balanced vitality and stability.

###Common Imbalances###
Wear or inflammation.  
Energetically: Fatigue or overexertion.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Golden yellow restores strength.  
Crystals: Tiger’s eye and amber energize.  
Element: Earth stabilizes energy.  
Affirmation: “I am strong, balanced, and in harmony.”
