#Premolar 1#

##Location##
Located just behind the canine on the upper left jaw.  
The smallest premolar, initiating the cutting motion.

###Shape###
Sharp and single-rooted, adapted for slicing small pieces of food.

###Function###

Energetically represents awareness and transformation.
