#Premolar 2#

##Location##
Located behind the first premolar on the upper right jaw.  
Assists in slicing and preparing food for chewing.

###Shape###
Medium-sized with two cusps for precise cutting.

###Function###
Cuts and divides food before major grinding occurs.  
Energetically represents processing, structure, and stability.
