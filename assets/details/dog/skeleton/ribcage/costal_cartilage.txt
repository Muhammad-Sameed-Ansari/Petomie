#Costal Cartilage#

##Location##
The costal cartilage connects the ribs to the sternum, forming the flexible part of the dog’s ribcage.
It allows the chest to expand and contract during breathing.

###Shape###
Elastic cartilage extending from the ventral ends of the ribs, curving inward toward the sternum and blending gradually with bone.

###Function###
Provides elasticity for breathing and movement while maintaining protection for internal organs.
Energetically, it represents adaptability, emotional openness, and breath of life.
