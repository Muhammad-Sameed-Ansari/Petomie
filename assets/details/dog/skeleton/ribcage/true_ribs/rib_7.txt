#Rib 7#

##Location##
Rib 7 belongs to the group known as true ribs, which include ribs 1 to 8.
These ribs attach directly to the sternum through costal cartilage, forming the sturdy and protective front section of the ribcage.
They create a stable framework for the chest and play a vital role in breathing.

###Shape###
Curved, elongated bones that arch from the thoracic vertebrae toward the sternum, with rounded dorsal ends and flatter ventral ends.

###Function###
Protect the heart and lungs, support breathing, and provide strength and stability to the chest wall.
Energetically, they represent security, strength, and balance between protection and openness.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Heart and Solar Plexus Chakras.
Meridian: Lung and Stomach Meridians.
Emotional Meaning: Calm strength and balanced openness.
Balanced Energy: Deep, rhythmic breathing and centered stability.

###Common Imbalances###
Rib stiffness or muscle strain around the chest.
Energetically, guarded emotions or limited self-expression.
