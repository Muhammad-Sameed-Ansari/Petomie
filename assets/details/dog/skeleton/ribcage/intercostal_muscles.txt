#Intercostal Muscles#

##Location##
The intercostal muscles occupy the spaces between the ribs, forming the muscular wall of the dog’s chest.
They are divided into external and internal layers that work together during breathing.

###Shape###
Thin sheets of muscle fibers running diagonally between the ribs, providing flexibility and strength to the ribcage.

###Function###
Assist in breathing by raising and lowering the ribs during inhalation and exhalation.
Energetically, they symbolize rhythm, harmony, and the balance of expansion and release.

Balanced Energy: Calm breath and emotional balance.

###Common Imbalances###
Muscle tension, shallow breathing, or restricted movement.
Energetically, suppressed emotion or disrupted rhythm.
