#Rib 13#

##Location##
Rib 13 belongs to the group known as false ribs, which include ribs 9 to 13.
These ribs do not attach directly to the sternum but connect to one another through cartilage, forming the flexible rear section of the ribcage.
They allow greater motion for breathing and bending of the torso.

###Shape###
Curved and slightly flattened bones that become narrower toward the lower end, providing both protection and flexibility.

###Function###
Protect the lungs and abdominal organs while maintaining elasticity in the ribcage for smooth respiration.
Energetically, they represent adaptability, balance, and emotional flexibility.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Heart and Solar Plexus Chakras.
Meridian: Lung and Liver Meridians.
Emotional Meaning: Openness, flow, and adaptability.
Balanced Energy: Calm breathing and steady rhythm in life.

###Common Imbalances###
Rib tension, muscle strain, or limited chest movement.
Energetically, resistance to change or holding emotional tension.
