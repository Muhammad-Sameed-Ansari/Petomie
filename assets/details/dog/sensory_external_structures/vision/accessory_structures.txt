#Accessory Structures#

##Parts##
Extraocular Muscles – Control precise eye movements in all directions.
Lacrimal Apparatus – Includes lacrimal glands and ducts, producing and draining tears.
Eyelids – Protect eyes from injury, debris, and excessive light.
Conjunctiva – Thin mucous membrane lining eyelids and sclera, aiding lubrication.
Tear Film – Multi-layered fluid coating the eye surface for protection and clarity.

##Function##
Support and protect the eyeball, maintain a moist and clear ocular surface, and allow coordinated movement for binocular vision.
