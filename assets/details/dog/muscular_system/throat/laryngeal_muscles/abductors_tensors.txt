##Location##
Located on dorsal and lateral surfaces of the larynx. These muscles open the glottis and tense the vocal cords.

##Shape##
Small paired muscles connecting cricoid, thyroid, and arytenoid cartilages.

##Parts##
###1. Cricoarytenoideus Dorsalis###
Only muscle that opens the glottis
Abducts arytenoid cartilages
Allows air passage during inspiration
###2. Cricothyroideus###
Connects cricoid and thyroid cartilages
Tenses vocal cords
Raises pitch of sound

##Function##
Open glottis for breathing
Tense vocal cords for high-pitched vocalization
