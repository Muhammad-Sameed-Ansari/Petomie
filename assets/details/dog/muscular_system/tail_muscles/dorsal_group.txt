##Location##
Located on the dorsolateral and dorsal midline of the tail, just caudal to the sacrum. These muscles are responsible for tail elevation and extension.

##Shape##
Long, narrow muscle columns lying above the caudal vertebrae.

##Parts##
###1. Sacrocaudalis Dorsalis Lateralis###
Dorsolateral tail muscle
Elevates and bends tail laterally
###2. Sacrocaudalis Dorsalis Medialis###
Medial dorsal tail muscle
Extends tail and elevates it straight

##Function##
Raise and extend the tail
Assist with lateral wagging and elevation for expression and balance
