##Location##
Located on the ventral and ventrolateral surfaces of the caudal vertebrae. These muscles are responsible for tail flexion and tucking movements.

##Shape##
Paired muscle columns running beneath the tail vertebrae.

##Parts##
###1. Sacrocaudalis Ventralis Lateralis###
Ventral tail muscle
Flexes tail ventrally and laterally
###2. Sacrocaudalis Ventralis Medialis###
Ventral midline muscle
Flexes tail ventrally

##Function##
Lower or tuck the tail
Assist in defecation posture and emotional signaling (fear, submission)
