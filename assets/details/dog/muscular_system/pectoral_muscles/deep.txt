##Location##
Lies beneath the superficial pectorals, covering the ventral chest and extending caudally.

##Shape##
Broad, strong muscle forming the deep layer of the pectoral region.

##Parts##
###1. Deep Pectoral (Pectoralis Profundus)###
Origin: Entire sternum and ventral midline
Insertion: Lesser tubercle of humerus and greater tubercle via aponeurosis
Draws limb caudally (retraction)
Supports trunk between forelimbs
Assists in adduction and stabilization

##Function##
Retract the forelimb when weight-bearing
Pull trunk cranially when limb is fixed (support during pulling actions)
Provide strong adduction and stabilize the thorax during locomotion
