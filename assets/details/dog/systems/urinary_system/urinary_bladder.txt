#Urinary Bladder#
The urinary bladder in the dog stores urine before elimination.  
It expands and contracts as needed, maintaining flexibility and control.  
Energetically, it represents emotional containment, timing, and conscious release.

###Parts###

##Bladder Wall##  
Smooth muscle layers allowing expansion.  
Energetically, symbolize adaptability and patience.

##Trigone Area##  
Junction between ureters and urethra.  
Energetically, reflects balance and readiness for release.

###Function###  
Stores and releases urine through voluntary and reflex actions.  
Energetically, balances holding and letting go.

###Common Imbalances###  
Cystitis: irritation or emotional tension.  
Incontinence: lack of control or anxiety.  
Energetic imbalance: fear of release or emotional overflow.
