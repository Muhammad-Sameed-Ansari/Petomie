#Urinary Bladder#

##Location##
Situated in the lower abdomen (pelvic cavity), behind the pubic bone.
When full, it expands into the abdominal cavity.

###Shape###
A hollow, elastic, muscular sac that changes shape as it fills or empties.

###Parts###
Apex: Rounded cranial end.
Body: Central portion.
Neck: Narrows into the urethra.
Trigone: Triangular area where ureters and urethra meet.

###Function###
Stores urine until voluntary urination.
Expands and contracts to manage urine flow.
Controls release through internal and external sphincters.
