#Urethra#

##Location##
Extends from the bladder neck to the external opening.
In males, it runs through the penis; in females, itâ€™s shorter and opens behind the vaginal opening.

###Shape###
A tubular passage lined with mucous membrane and surrounded by smooth and skeletal muscles.

###Parts###
Internal Sphincter: Located at the bladder neck; involuntary control.
External Sphincter: Near the exit; voluntary control.
Pelvic Section (both sexes): Carries urine out.
Penile Section (males): Also carries semen during reproduction.

###Function###
Transports urine from bladder to outside of the body.
Controls urination through sphincter action.
In males, serves as a dual passage for semen and urine.
