#Urethra#
The urethra carries urine from the bladder to the exterior of the body.  
In the dog, it completes the urinary process and aids elimination.  
Energetically, it represents release, trust, and the freedom to express and let go.

###Parts###

##Internal and External Openings##  
Control flow from bladder to outside.  
Energetically, symbolize transition and clarity.

###Function###  
Conducts urine out of the body.  
Energetically, supports final release and emotional cleansing.

###Common Imbalances###  
Obstruction: blocked energy or fear of exposure.  
Irritation: over-sensitivity or tension.  
Energetic stagnation: withheld emotion or fear of vulnerability.
