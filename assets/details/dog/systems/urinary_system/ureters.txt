#Ureters#

##Location##
Extend from the kidneys to the urinary bladder, running along the dorsal abdominal wall.

###Shape###
Narrow, flexible muscular tubes about 2â€“3 mm in diameter.

###Parts###
Upper Section: Begins at the renal pelvis.
Middle Section: Travels through the abdomen.
Lower Section: Enters the bladder at an angle to prevent backflow.

###Function###
Carry urine from the kidneys to the bladder using rhythmic contractions (peristalsis).
Prevent reflux of urine with valve-like openings into the bladder.
