#Dermis#

The dermis is the middle layer of the skin, composed of connective tissue, nerves, and blood vessels.  
It provides elasticity, nourishment, and sensory perception.  
Energetically, it represents support, grounding, and emotional awareness.

###Parts###

##Papillary Layer##  
Upper region containing capillaries and sensory receptors.  
Energetically, reflects responsiveness and openness.

##Reticular Layer##  
Dense connective tissue giving structure and flexibility.  
Energetically, symbolizes strength and emotional grounding.

###Function###  
Provides structural support, sensation, and nutrient delivery to the epidermis.  
Regulates temperature and anchors follicles and glands.  
Energetically, maintains balance between sensitivity and stability.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: emotional stress or irritation.  
Weak elasticity: fatigue or loss of flexibility.  
Pain sensitivity: hyper-awareness or nervous tension.
