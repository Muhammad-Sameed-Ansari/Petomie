##Location##
Covers nearly the entire body except the paw pads, nose, and mucous membranes.

##Shape##
Made up of a **double-layered coat** with coarse outer guard hairs and a soft insulating undercoat.

##Parts##

###1. Guard Hairs###
- Long, coarse hairs forming the outer coat.  
- **Function:** Protect against water, dirt, and sunlight.  

###2. Undercoat###
- Dense, fine hairs beneath the guard hairs.  
- **Function:** Provides insulation and temperature control.  

###3. Hair Follicle###
- Anchored in the dermis with an attached sebaceous gland.  
- **Function:** Supports hair growth and oil secretion.  

##Function##
- **Thermoregulation:** Maintains body temperature through insulation.  
- **Protection:** Shields the skin from UV rays and mechanical injury.  
- **Sensory:** Assists with tactile awareness and communication.
