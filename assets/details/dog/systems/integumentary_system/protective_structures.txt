##Location##
Located on **paws, claws, and nose**, and other thickened skin regions exposed to wear or impact.

##Shape##
Composed of **thick, keratinized tissue** adapted for durability and friction resistance.

##Parts##

###1. Paw Pads###
- Cushioned pads of thick skin under the feet.  
- **Function:** Provide traction and absorb shock.  

###2. Claws###
- Curved keratinized extensions on each toe.  
- **Function:** Used for defense, digging, and gripping surfaces.  

###3. Nose Leather (Planum Nasale)###
- Textured, hairless skin on the nose.  
- **Function:** Enhances scent detection and moisture retention.  

##Function##
- **Protection:** Guards against mechanical injury and friction.  
- **Support:** Provides grip and stability during movement.  
- **Sensation:** Detects ground texture and pressure.
