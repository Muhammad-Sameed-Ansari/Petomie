##Location##
Present in the **epidermis and hair follicles** throughout the body.

##Shape##
Varies with breed, genetics, and sunlight exposure.  
Pigment cells distribute melanin into hair and skin cells.

##Parts##

###1. Melanocytes###
- Cells that produce melanin pigment.  
- **Function:** Provide color and UV protection.  

###2. Pigmented Hair Shafts###
- Hairs infused with melanin granules.  
- **Function:** Determine coat and marking patterns.  

###3. Skin Markings###
- Areas with concentrated pigment.  
- **Function:** Create distinctive patterns or shading.  

##Function##
- **Protection:** Shields skin from UV rays.  
- **Identification:** Defines coat and breed appearance.  
- **Communication:** Expresses health and hormonal balance.
