#Hypodermis#

The hypodermis, or subcutaneous layer, lies beneath the dermis and connects skin to muscle.  
It contains fat, connective tissue, and blood vessels that cushion and insulate the body.  
Energetically, it represents safety, comfort, and inner stability.

###Parts###

##Fat Cells##  
Store energy and regulate warmth.  
Energetically, symbolize nurturing and protection.

##Connective Tissue##  
Binds skin to underlying muscles.  
Energetically, reflects connection and security.

###Function###  
Cushions the body, conserves heat, and stores energy.  
Energetically, grounds vitality and emotional comfort.

###Common Imbalances###  
Thin or depleted layer: insecurity or lack of support.  
Inflammation: tension or overprotection.  
Poor insulation: vulnerability or emotional exhaustion.
