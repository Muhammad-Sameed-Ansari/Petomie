##Location##
Located at the **base of each hair follicle** within the dermis.

##Shape##
Fine network of sensory nerve endings wrapped around the hair bulb.

##Parts##

###1. Merkel Cells###
- Specialized tactile cells at follicle bases.  
- **Function:** Detect light touch and vibration.  

###2. Sensory Nerve Endings###
- Nerve fibers surrounding the follicle.  
- **Function:** Respond to hair movement and pressure changes.  

##Function##
- **Sensation:** Provides detailed tactile information.  
- **Awareness:** Detects nearby movement and environmental stimuli.
