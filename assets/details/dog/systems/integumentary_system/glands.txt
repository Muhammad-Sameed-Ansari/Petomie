##Location##
Scattered throughout the dermis and connected to hair follicles or skin pores.

##Shape##
Microscopic tubular or sac-like structures adapted for secretion.

##Parts##

###1. Sebaceous Glands###
- Produce an oily substance called sebum.  
- **Function:** Lubricates hair and skin, preventing dryness.  

###2. Sweat Glands###
- Found mainly on paw pads and nose.  
- **Function:** Aid in cooling and scent distribution.  

###3. Scent Glands###
- Located in anal, tail, and facial regions.  
- **Function:** Release pheromones for marking and communication.  

###4. Mammary Glands###
- Modified sweat glands located along the abdomen.  
- **Function:** Produce milk in females for nursing.  

##Function##
- **Lubrication:** Maintains skin and coat moisture.  
- **Communication:** Releases scent markers for interaction.  
- **Temperature regulation:** Supports cooling mechanisms.
