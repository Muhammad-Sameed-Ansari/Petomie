#Mane and Tail#

While dogs lack a true mane, their tail and ruff hair express emotion, protection, and balance.  
These areas enhance communication and maintain body temperature.  
Energetically, they represent expression, flow, and emotional release.

###Parts###

##Tail Hair##  
Acts as an expressive flag for emotion and balance.  
Energetically, symbolizes flow and freedom.

##Neck Ruff##  
Longer guard hairs for protection and display.  
Energetically, reflects pride and confidence.

###Function###  
Assists communication, balance, and insulation.  
Energetically, channels emotional energy and vitality through motion.

###Common Imbalances###  
Hair loss: emotional suppression or stress.  
Matting: tension or blocked flow.  
Weak tail carriage: fatigue or emotional imbalance.
