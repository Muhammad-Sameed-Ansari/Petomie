##Location##
Found on the **muzzle, above eyes, lower jaw, and forelimbs**.

##Shape##
Long, thick hairs anchored deep into the dermis, surrounded by blood-filled follicles rich in nerves.

##Parts##

###1. Follicle-Sinus Complex###
- Capsule of connective tissue, blood sinuses, and nerves.  
- **Function:** Detects vibration and movement around the whisker.  

###2. Whisker Shaft###
- Long keratinized hair projecting from the follicle.  
- **Function:** Acts as an antenna for spatial awareness.  

##Function##
- **Navigation:** Helps dogs gauge distance and object proximity.  
- **Sensation:** Detects air movement, vibration, and obstacles.  
- **Communication:** Conveys emotional and sensory signals.
