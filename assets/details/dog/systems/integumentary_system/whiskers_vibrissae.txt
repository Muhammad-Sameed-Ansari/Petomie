#Whiskers (Vibrissae)#

Whiskers, or vibrissae, are specialized sensory hairs on the face and body.  
They sense air movement and physical proximity, guiding navigation and expression.  
Energetically, they represent perception, intuition, and personal space awareness.

###Parts###

##Mystacial Whiskers##  
Located on the muzzle for detecting distance and vibration.  
Energetically, symbolize sensitivity and instinct.

##Superciliary and Mandibular Whiskers##  
Above the eyes and below the chin for protection and spatial sensing.  
Energetically, represent awareness and foresight.

###Function###  
Provide spatial orientation and protect the face from injury.  
Energetically, strengthen intuition and sensitivity to surroundings.

###Common Imbalances###  
Whisker stress: confusion or disconnection.  
Damage: disorientation or insecurity.  
Overgrowth: sensory overload or tension.
