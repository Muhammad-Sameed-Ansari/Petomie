#Pancreas#

The pancreas lies near the stomach and small intestine.  
It produces digestive enzymes and hormones that regulate blood sugar.  
Energetically, it represents balance, nourishment, and the sweetness of life.

###Parts###

##Exocrine Portion##  
Releases digestive enzymes into the small intestine.  
Energetically, symbolizes flow and transformation.

##Endocrine Portion##  
Produces insulin and glucagon to regulate blood sugar.  
Energetically, reflects harmony and giving/receiving balance.

###Function###  
Secretes enzymes that aid digestion and hormones that maintain metabolic stability.  
Energetically, it supports self-worth, nourishment, and joyful giving.

###Common Imbalances###  
Pancreatitis: stress or over-responsibility.  
Low enzyme output: emotional depletion.  
Blood sugar imbalance: difficulty balancing giving and receiving.
