#Rectum#

The rectum is the final straight portion of the large intestine.  
It stores feces until defecation.  
Energetically, it represents readiness for closure, patience, and controlled release.

###Parts###

##Upper Section##  
Receives feces from the colon.  
Energetically, symbolizes preparation and completion.

##Lower Section##  
Leads to the anal canal.  
Energetically, reflects grounding and surrender.

###Function###  
Stores waste before elimination and triggers defecation reflex.  
Energetically, supports closure, patience, and completion of cycles.

###Common Imbalances###  
Constipation: fear of change or attachment.  
Inflammation: emotional irritation.  
Pain: resistance to letting go.
