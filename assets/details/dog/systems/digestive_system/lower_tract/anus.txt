#Anus#

The anus is the final opening of the digestive tract through which waste leaves the body.  
It is surrounded by sphincter muscles that control defecation.  
Energetically, it represents release, completion, and letting go of what no longer serves.

###Parts###

##Internal Sphincter##  
Involuntary muscle maintaining closure.  
Energetically, symbolizes unconscious control and protection.

##External Sphincter##  
Voluntary muscle that allows conscious release.  
Energetically, represents trust and readiness to let go.

###Function###  
Controls the passage of feces from the rectum to the outside.  
Maintains continence and regulates final elimination.  
Energetically, it supports healthy boundaries and emotional cleansing.

###Common Imbalances###  
Constipation: fear of release or control issues.  
Anal gland impaction: buildup of stagnant emotion or tension.  
Irritation: stress or resistance to change.
