#Large Intestine#

The large intestine absorbs water and electrolytes from undigested material and forms solid waste.  
Energetically, it represents release, clarity, and the final stage of assimilation — keeping what is useful and letting go of the rest.

###Parts###

##Cecum##  
Small pouch connecting small and large intestine.  
Energetically, symbolizes transition and adjustment.

##Colon##  
Main section divided into ascending, transverse, and descending parts.  
Energetically, represents structure, purification, and order.

##Rectum##  
Terminal section storing feces before elimination.  
Energetically, reflects readiness to complete cycles.

###Function###  
Absorbs remaining nutrients and water, compacts waste, and moves it toward elimination.  
Energetically, it helps clear the past and bring emotional balance.

###Common Imbalances###  
Diarrhea: inability to hold experiences.  
Constipation: holding on to old energy.  
Inflammation: irritation with life’s process of change.
