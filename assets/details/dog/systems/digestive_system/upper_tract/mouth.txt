###Parts###

##Lips##  
Flexible edges forming the mouth opening, aiding in grasping food and expression.  
Energetically, they symbolize openness and communication.

##Cheeks##  
Muscular walls that help contain food during chewing.  
Energetically, they represent emotional containment and balance between giving and receiving.

##Oral Vestibule##  
Space between lips/cheeks and teeth.  
Energetically, it reflects preparation and readiness.

##Oral Cavity Proper##  
Inner region containing tongue, teeth, and salivary openings.  
Energetically, it signifies emotional processing and the integration of experience.

###Function###  
Receives food, begins chewing, and mixes it with saliva.  
Supports tasting, expression, and bonding through licking and play.  
Energetically, it reflects openness, affection, and readiness to engage with life.

###Common Imbalances###  
Oral injury: unspoken emotions or suppressed affection.  
Dryness: lack of flow or emotional withdrawal.  
Pain: stress or tension around communication and nourishment.
