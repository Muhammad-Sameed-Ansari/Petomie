#Mouth#

##Location##
At the front of the face, forming the entrance to the digestive system. It extends from the lips to the pharynx.

##Shape##
A cavity lined with mucous membranes and equipped with teeth, tongue, and salivary openings.

##Parts##
Lips: Hold food and guide it into the mouth.
Cheeks: Help keep food between the teeth during chewing.
Palate: Separates mouth from nasal cavity.
Teeth and Tongue: Aid chewing and swallowing.
Salivary Ducts: Openings for saliva secretion.

##Function##
Receives food and begins digestion.
Mixes food with saliva to form a bolus.
Starts carbohydrate digestion.
Prepares food for swallowing.
