#Salivary Glands#

The salivary glands produce saliva to moisten food, begin digestion, and maintain oral health.  
Energetically, they represent flow, adaptability, and joyful anticipation.

###Parts###

##Parotid Glands##  
Located below the ears, secreting watery saliva.  
Energetically, represent refreshment and alertness.

##Mandibular Glands##  
At the angle of the jaw, producing mixed saliva.  
Energetically, symbolize stability and support.

##Sublingual Glands##  
Under the tongue, secreting mucous-rich saliva.  
Energetically, reflect gentleness and balance.

##Zygomatic Glands##  
Situated near the eye, adding subtle lubrication.  
Energetically, represent intuition and subtle awareness.

###Function###  
Moisten and lubricate food, begin digestion, and support oral comfort.  
Energetically, they embody emotional flow, readiness, and connection.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling: held emotions or overstimulation.  
Dry mouth: stress or imbalance in flow.  
Overproduction: excitement or anxious anticipation.
