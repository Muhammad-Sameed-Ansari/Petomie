#Tongue#

The tongue is a flexible, muscular organ that aids in eating, drinking, and grooming.  
It allows taste, swallowing, and temperature sensing.  
Energetically, it represents joy, communication, and sensitivity to the environment.

###Parts###

##Apex##  
Tip used for tasting and licking.  
Energetically, symbolizes affection and curiosity.

##Body##  
Central muscular portion.  
Energetically, reflects adaptability and emotional intelligence.

##Root##  
Anchored base attached to the hyoid bone.  
Energetically, represents grounding and instinctive truth.

##Papillae##  
Tiny projections containing taste buds and rough surfaces.  
Energetically, reflect awareness and sensory engagement.

###Function###  
Moves food for chewing and swallowing, helps with grooming and licking.  
Energetically, governs communication, affection, and expression of emotion.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: frustration or overstimulation.  
Injury: blocked expression or excessive excitement.  
Color changes: imbalance in vitality or emotion.
