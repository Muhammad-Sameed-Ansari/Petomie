#Esophagus#

The esophagus is a muscular tube connecting the throat to the stomach.  
It transports swallowed food through rhythmic contractions.  
Energetically, it represents trust, instinctive flow, and the ability to follow one’s path with confidence.

###Parts###

##Upper Esophageal Sphincter##  
Controls entry from the pharynx.  
Energetically, symbolizes allowing support and receiving care.

##Esophageal Body##  
Main muscular passage transporting food.  
Energetically, represents perseverance, instinctive trust, and direction.

##Lower Esophageal Sphincter##  
Prevents reflux into the esophagus.  
Energetically, reflects boundaries and protection.

###Function###  
Carries food smoothly to the stomach through coordinated muscular movement.  
Energetically, it symbolizes the natural flow of instinct and trust in life’s rhythm.

###Common Imbalances###  
Choking: blocked energy or overexcitement.  
Reflux: weakened energetic boundaries.  
Spasm: anxiety, fear, or tension around receiving.
