#Esophagus#

##Location##
A long, muscular tube running from the pharynx to the stomach through the chest.

##Shape##
Elastic, collapsible tube lined with mucous membranes and circular muscle layers.

##Parts##
Cervical Portion: In the neck.
Thoracic Portion: Passes through the chest.
Abdominal Portion: Ends at the stomachâ€™s entrance.

##Function##
Moves food via peristalsis.
Prevents reflux with the cardiac sphincter.
Connects upper and lower digestive tracts.
