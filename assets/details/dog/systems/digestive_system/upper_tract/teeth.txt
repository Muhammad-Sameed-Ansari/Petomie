#Teeth#

The teeth are strong, calcified structures used for grasping, tearing, and chewing food.  
They mark the first step of digestion and serve in defense and communication.  
Energetically, they symbolize grounding, confidence, and the strength to assert oneself.

###Parts###

##Incisors##  
Front teeth for nibbling and grooming.  
Energetically, represent precision and care.

##Canines##  
Long, pointed teeth for grasping and protection.  
Energetically, embody courage, instinct, and loyalty.

##Premolars##  
Flattened teeth for crushing.  
Energetically, symbolize balance and teamwork.

##Molars##  
Back teeth for grinding food.  
Energetically, reflect patience and endurance.

###Function###  
Tear, cut, and grind food into small pieces for digestion.  
Also aid in expression and defense.  
Energetically, they show how strength and gentleness coexist.

###Common Imbalances###  
Broken teeth: stress or suppressed energy.  
Wear: overexertion or emotional strain.  
Pain: imbalance in grounding or security.
