#Teeth#

##Location##
Embedded in the upper (maxilla) and lower (mandible) jaws.

##Shape##
Hard, calcified structures specialized for cutting, tearing, and grinding.

##Parts##
Incisors: Cut and nibble.
Canines: Tear meat.
Premolars and Molars: Grind food.
Roots & Enamel: Anchor and protect.

##Function##
Mechanically break down food.
Assist in grooming and defense.
Support jaw alignment and digestion efficiency.
