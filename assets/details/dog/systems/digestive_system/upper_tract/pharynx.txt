#Pharynx#

The pharynx is the muscular chamber behind the mouth and nasal cavity.  
It directs food into the esophagus and air into the larynx.  
Energetically, it represents coordination, communication, and trust in instinctive transitions.

###Parts###

##Nasopharynx##  
Upper portion connecting to nasal passages.  
Energetically, reflects awareness and sensory perception.

##Oropharynx##  
Middle part behind the mouth.  
Energetically, symbolizes choice and cooperation.

##Laryngopharynx##  
Lower part leading to the esophagus.  
Energetically, represents grounding and movement toward nourishment.

###Function###  
Coordinates swallowing and breathing to protect the airway.  
Energetically, it balances expression and action, helping the dog trust its instincts.

###Common Imbalances###  
Irritation: conflict or emotional stress.  
Difficulty swallowing: hesitation or insecurity.  
Tension: emotional guarding or fear of losing control.
