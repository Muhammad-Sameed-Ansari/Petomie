#Stomach#

The stomach is a muscular organ that receives food from the esophagus and begins digestion.  
It mixes food with acid and enzymes to form chyme.

###Parts###

##Cardia##  
Entry region from the esophagus.  
Energetically, symbolizes acceptance and readiness.

##Fundus##  
Upper portion that stores food and gases.  
Energetically, represents expansion and trust.

##Body##  
Main section that mixes and breaks down food.  
Energetically, reflects transformation and integration.

##Pylorus##  
Exit area controlling passage to the small intestine.  
Energetically, symbolizes timing and patience.

###Function###  
Stores and digests food chemically and mechanically.  
Prepares nutrients for absorption.  
Energetically, it mirrors emotional processing and adaptability.

###Common Imbalances###  
Vomiting: rejection or overstimulation.  
Indigestion: stress or emotional overload.  
Bloating: tension, anxiety, or holding energy.
