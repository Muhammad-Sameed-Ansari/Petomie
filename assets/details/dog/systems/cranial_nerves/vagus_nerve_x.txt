#Vagus Nerve (X)#

The dog’s vagus nerve connects the brain with the heart, lungs, and digestive organs.  
It regulates rest, digestion, and emotional calm, influencing overall wellbeing.  
Energetically, it symbolizes peace, balance, and alignment of heart and mind.

###Location###
Arises from the medulla and travels through the neck, chest, and abdomen.

###Parts###
Cardiac branches – influence heart rhythm.  
Pulmonary branches – control breathing.  
Abdominal branches – support digestion and gut balance.

###Function###
Manages involuntary functions like heart rate, breathing, and digestion.  
Energetically, it governs relaxation, flow, and trust in natural rhythm.
