#Vestibulocochlear Nerve (VIII)#

The dog’s vestibulocochlear nerve governs hearing and balance.  
Dogs detect higher frequencies and subtle vibrations far beyond human range.  
Energetically, it represents harmony, sensitivity, and awareness of vibration.

###Location###
Arises from the inner ear and extends to the medulla.

###Parts###
Vestibular branch – senses position and motion for balance.  
Cochlear branch – transmits sound vibrations.

###Function###
Maintains equilibrium and interprets sound for communication.  
Energetically, it aligns hearing, intuition, and balance between motion and stillness.
