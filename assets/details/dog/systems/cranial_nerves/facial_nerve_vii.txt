#Facial Nerve (VII)#

The dog’s facial nerve governs expression, ear movement, and emotional response through subtle facial changes.  
It expresses mood and social intent, key to bonding and communication.  
Energetically, it represents emotional flow, empathy, and connection.

###Location###
Arises from the medulla and travels through the inner ear region, branching across the face.

###Parts###
Auriculopalpebral branch – moves ears and eyelids.  
Buccal branches – control lips, whiskers, and cheeks.  
Cervical branch – moves the lower jaw and skin around the neck.

###Function###
Controls facial and ear movement for communication and emotion.  
Energetically, it encourages openness, emotional balance, and connected expression.
