#Olfactory Nerve (I)#

The dog’s olfactory nerve provides its extraordinary sense of smell, capable of detecting scents over great distances and depths.  
It connects directly to the brain’s emotion and memory centers, linking scent to instinct, trust, and emotion.  
Energetically, it symbolizes intuition, awareness, and the connection between body and spirit.

###Location###
Arises from the nasal epithelium and passes through the cribriform plate into the olfactory bulbs at the front of the brain.

###Parts###
Olfactory epithelium – specialized sensory cells detecting scent molecules.  
Olfactory bulbs – interpret scent signals.  
Olfactory tracts – carry information to higher brain centers.

###Function###
Enables scent recognition for tracking, identification, and emotional bonding.  
Energetically, it represents instinctual knowing, awareness, and connection to memory and emotion.
