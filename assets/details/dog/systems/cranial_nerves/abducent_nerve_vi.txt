#Abducent Nerve (VI)#

The dog’s abducent nerve moves the eye outward, expanding peripheral vision and spatial awareness.  
It allows for balance between alertness and relaxation.  
Energetically, it represents openness, curiosity, and exploration.

###Location###
Originates in the medulla and travels to the orbit, controlling the lateral rectus muscle.

###Parts###
Single branch – controls lateral eye movement.

###Function###
Moves the eye laterally to expand awareness of surroundings.  
Energetically, it promotes flexibility, observation, and openness.
