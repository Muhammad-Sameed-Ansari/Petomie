#Pineal Gland#

The pineal gland is a small, cone-shaped gland within the brain that produces melatonin.  
It regulates sleep cycles and seasonal rhythms.  
Energetically, it represents awareness, intuition, and the natural rhythm of rest and renewal.

###Parts###

##Pinealocytes##  
Cells that secrete melatonin in response to light cycles.  
Energetically, symbolize calm perception and flow.

###Function###  
Controls sleep-wake cycles and synchronizes circadian rhythm.  
Energetically, aligns instinct and awareness with the cycles of nature.

###Common Imbalances###  
Sleep disruption: loss of rhythm or restlessness.  
Overactivity: hypersensitivity to stimulation.  
Low function: fatigue or disconnection.
