#Hypothalamus#

The hypothalamus lies at the base of the brain, linking the nervous and endocrine systems.  
It regulates hunger, thirst, temperature, and hormonal control through the pituitary.  
Energetically, it represents balance between instinct and awareness â€” the bridge of body wisdom.

###Parts###

##Supraoptic and Paraventricular Nuclei##  
Produce oxytocin and antidiuretic hormone.  
Energetically, symbolize compassion, bonding, and trust.

##Arcuate Nucleus##  
Regulates hunger and metabolism.  
Energetically, reflects instinctive knowing and satisfaction.

###Function###  
Maintains internal stability, coordinates hormone release, and integrates emotion with physical response.  
Energetically, supports intuition, trust, and equilibrium.

###Common Imbalances###  
Appetite imbalance: emotional emptiness or overattachment.  
Stress reactivity: overdrive or fear.  
Disconnection: confusion or fatigue.
