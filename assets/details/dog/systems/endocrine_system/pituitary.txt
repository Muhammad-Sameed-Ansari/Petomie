#Pituitary Gland#

The pituitary gland lies beneath the brain in the sella turcica and governs other endocrine glands.  
It releases hormones controlling growth, reproduction, and metabolism.  
Energetically, it represents coordination, trust, and balanced leadership.

###Parts###

##Anterior Lobe (Adenohypophysis)##  
Produces growth hormone, TSH, ACTH, LH, FSH, and prolactin.  
Energetically, symbolizes purpose, cooperation, and nurturing leadership.

##Posterior Lobe (Neurohypophysis)##  
Stores oxytocin and vasopressin from the hypothalamus.  
Energetically, reflects loyalty and connection.

###Function###  
Coordinates hormonal signals to maintain growth and stability.  
Energetically, governs trust and balance between action and rest.

###Common Imbalances###  
Underactivity: fatigue or apathy.  
Overactivity: tension or anxiety.  
Disconnection: imbalance between giving and receiving.
