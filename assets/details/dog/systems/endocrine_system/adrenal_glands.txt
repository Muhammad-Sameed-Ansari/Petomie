#Adrenal Glands#

The adrenal glands are small, paired organs situated above each kidney.  
They produce hormones that control metabolism, stress response, and electrolyte balance.  
Energetically, they represent courage, instinctive strength, and resilience in times of challenge.

###Parts###

##Cortex##  
Outer region producing cortisol, aldosterone, and sex hormones.  
Energetically, symbolizes stability, endurance, and adaptability.

##Medulla##  
Inner section releasing adrenaline and noradrenaline.  
Energetically, reflects readiness, alertness, and protection.

###Function###  
Regulate stress response, maintain water and mineral balance, and support energy metabolism.  
Energetically, they provide balance between calm awareness and instinctive action.

###Common Imbalances###  
Fatigue: long-term stress or emotional strain.  
Overactivity: restlessness or anxiety.  
Weakness: loss of drive or confidence.
