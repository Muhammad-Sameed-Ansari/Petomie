#Gonads#

The gonads consist of the ovaries in females and testes in males.  
They produce reproductive hormones that influence behavior, growth, and vitality.  
Energetically, they represent creation, connection, and the instinctive joy of life.

###Parts###

##Ovaries##  
Located near the kidneys, producing estrogen and progesterone.  
Energetically, symbolize nurturing, renewal, and intuition.

##Testes##  
Located in the scrotum, producing testosterone and sperm.  
Energetically, represent strength, confidence, and drive.

###Function###  
Regulate reproduction, hormonal balance, and secondary sexual traits.  
Energetically, support vitality, motivation, and emotional expression.

###Common Imbalances###  
Hormonal fluctuation: behavioral or emotional changes.  
Infertility: blocked creative flow.  
Overactivity: dominance or restlessness.
