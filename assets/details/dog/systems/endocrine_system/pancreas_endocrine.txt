#Pancreas (Endocrine)#

The endocrine portion of the pancreas, known as the Islets of Langerhans, regulates blood sugar and metabolism.  
Energetically, it represents balance, nourishment, and the joy of giving and receiving.

###Parts###

##Alpha Cells##  
Produce glucagon to raise blood sugar.  
Energetically, symbolize drive and motivation.

##Beta Cells##  
Produce insulin to lower blood sugar.  
Energetically, reflect receptivity, peace, and cooperation.

##Delta Cells##  
Regulate both alpha and beta functions.  
Energetically, embody moderation and adaptability.

###Function###  
Maintains stable glucose levels and energy balance.  
Energetically, harmonizes action and rest, strength and gentleness.

###Common Imbalances###  
Diabetes: imbalance between giving and receiving.  
Low sugar: depletion or overexertion.  
Fatigue: lack of sweetness or satisfaction in life.
