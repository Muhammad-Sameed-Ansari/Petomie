#Thyroid Gland#

The thyroid gland lies along the trachea and regulates metabolism, energy, and temperature.  
Energetically, it represents communication, flow, and expression of inner truth.

###Parts###

##Lobes##  
Two sides joined by an isthmus.  
Energetically, symbolize harmony between giving and receiving.

##Follicular Cells##  
Produce thyroxine (T4) and triiodothyronine (T3).  
Energetically, reflect transformation and steady flow.

##Parafollicular Cells##  
Secrete calcitonin to balance calcium levels.  
Energetically, embody calmness and balance.

###Function###  
Controls metabolic rate and energy balance.  
Energetically, maintains flow between action and rest, grounding and expression.

###Common Imbalances###  
Hyperthyroidism: excess activity or restlessness.  
Hypothyroidism: fatigue, low motivation, or emotional dullness.  
Imbalance: disconnection between action and awareness.
