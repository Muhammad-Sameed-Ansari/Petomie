#Thymus (Endocrine)#

The thymus is located in the upper chest behind the sternum.  
It produces hormones important for immune function and development.  
Energetically, it represents loyalty, love, and vitality from the heart.

###Parts###

##Lobules##  
Clusters of lymphoid and epithelial cells.  
Energetically, symbolize protection and connection.

###Function###  
Supports immune development and bridges the endocrine and immune systems.  
Energetically, strengthens emotional trust and compassion.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weak immunity: emotional depletion or sadness.  
Atrophy: aging or loss of playfulness.  
Overactivity: tension or internal guarding.
