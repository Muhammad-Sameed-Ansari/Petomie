#Peyer's Patches#
Part of the Lymphatic System: Lymphatic tissue in the intestines monitoring gut bacteria and immunity.  
In the dog, they link digestion and immune regulation.  
Energetically, they represent intuition, integration, and harmony within nourishment.

###Parts###

##Clusters##  
Located in the ileum region of the small intestine.  
Energetically, symbolize inner awareness and intuitive defense.

###Function###  
Detect and respond to intestinal microbes, promoting balance.  
Energetically, bridge nourishment and protection.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: inner conflict or imbalance.  
Weakness: fatigue or poor digestion.  
Energetic stagnation: loss of trust in gut intuition.
