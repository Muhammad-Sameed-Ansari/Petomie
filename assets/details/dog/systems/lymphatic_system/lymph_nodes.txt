#Lymph Nodes#
Part of the Lymphatic System: Small glands that filter lymph and house immune cells.  
In the dog, they are vital for immune defense and detoxification.  
Energetically, they represent discernment, protection, and purification.

###Parts###

##Structure##  
Bean-shaped filters located along lymph vessels.  
Energetically, symbolize checkpoints of awareness and truth.

##Location##  
Clustered in the neck, armpits, groin, and abdomen.  
Energetically, reflect conscious boundaries and clarity.

###Function###  
Filter pathogens, store lymphocytes, and initiate immune response.  
Energetically, strengthen self-protection and emotional purification.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling: overload or suppressed energy.  
Pain: resistance or tension.  
Energetic stagnation: blocked processing or overprotection.
