#Tonsils#

##Parts##

###Tonsillar Tissue###
Lymphatic tissue at the back of the throat near the opening of the airway and mouth.
Energetically, it represents protection and communication, guarding both physical and energetic entry points.

###Tonsillar Crypts###
Small surface folds that capture bacteria and debris for immune recognition.
Energetically, they reflect discernment and boundaries, recognizing what to accept or reject.

###Lymphocytes###
Immune cells that respond to foreign particles.
Energetically, they symbolize awareness and vigilance, maintaining safety through balanced defense.

###Lymphatic Connections###
Link the tonsils with lymph nodes in the neck and throat.
Energetically, they represent integration and flow, allowing harmony between speech, breath, and emotion.

##Function##
Tonsils provide early immune defense and protect against airborne or ingested pathogens.
Energetically, they maintain communication and safety, keeping the dog open yet protected.
