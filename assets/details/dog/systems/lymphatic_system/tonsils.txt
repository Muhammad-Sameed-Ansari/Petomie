#Tonsils#
Part of the Lymphatic System: Lymphoid tissue at the entrance of the throat guarding against infection.  
In the dog, they act as first-line immune defenders.  
Energetically, they represent communication, protection, and trust in expression.

###Parts###

##Palatine and Pharyngeal Tonsils##  
Located in the throat region.  
Energetically, symbolize gateways of truth and purification.

###Function###  
Trap pathogens entering through the mouth and nose.  
Energetically, purify communication and maintain trust in interaction.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: suppressed expression or conflict.  
Swelling: overload or emotional resistance.  
Energetic stagnation: fear of speaking truth.
