#Thoracic Duct#
Part of the Lymphatic System: The main lymphatic channel returning lymph to the bloodstream.  
In the dog, it drains lymph from most of the body into the venous system.  
Energetically, it represents integration, release, and harmony between cleansing and nourishment.

###Parts###

##Origin##  
Begins at the cisterna chyli in the abdomen.  
Energetically, symbolizes collection and grounding.

##Pathway##  
Ascends through the thorax to join the venous system near the neck.  
Energetically, reflects transformation and integration.

###Function###  
Returns filtered lymph to the circulatory system.  
Energetically, balances purification with replenishment.

###Common Imbalances###  
Blockage: stagnation or withheld emotion.  
Inflammation: irritation or resistance to release.  
Energetic imbalance: difficulty integrating life flow.
