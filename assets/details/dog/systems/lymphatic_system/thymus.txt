#Thymus#
Part of the Lymphatic System: A gland producing T-cells for immunity, located in the upper thorax.  
In the dog, it is active in youth and shrinks with age.  
Energetically, it represents trust, love, and resilience.

###Parts###

##Lobes##  
Two main sections in the upper chest.  
Energetically, symbolize faith and courage.

##Tissue##  
Rich in lymphocytes for immune education.  
Energetically, reflects growth and adaptation.

###Function###  
Develops immune strength and regulates defense.  
Energetically, strengthens trust and heart-centered vitality.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weakness: lack of resilience.  
Overactivity: anxiety or hyper-vigilance.  
Energetic imbalance: distrust or emotional withdrawal.
