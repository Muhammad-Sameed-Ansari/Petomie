#Right Lymphatic Duct#
Part of the Lymphatic System: A short vessel that drains lymph from the right upper body.  
In the dog, it supports local immune drainage.  
Energetically, it represents direction, balance, and conscious release.

###Parts###

##Origin##  
Formed by merging lymph vessels from the right thorax, neck, and forelimb.  
Energetically, symbolizes harmony and balance.

##Connection##  
Joins the venous system near the right subclavian vein.  
Energetically, reflects equal exchange and flow.

###Function###  
Drains lymph from the right head, neck, and limb.  
Energetically, assists focused cleansing and balance.

###Common Imbalances###  
Blockage: tension or imbalance in expression.  
Weak flow: fatigue or disconnection.  
Energetic stagnation: resistance to localized release.
