#Spleen#
Part of the Lymphatic System: A major organ filtering blood, recycling red cells, and supporting immune defense.  
In the dog, it plays a central role in vitality and blood balance.  
Energetically, it represents nourishment, grounding, and clarity.

###Parts###

##Red Pulp##  
Filters old red cells.  
Energetically, symbolizes renewal and letting go.

##White Pulp##  
Houses lymphocytes for immune defense.  
Energetically, reflects discernment and protection.

###Function###  
Cleans blood, stores immune cells, and regulates energy.  
Energetically, maintains equilibrium and strength.

###Common Imbalances###  
Enlargement: stagnation or overprotection.  
Weakness: depletion or low vitality.  
Energetic stagnation: holding emotional burdens.
