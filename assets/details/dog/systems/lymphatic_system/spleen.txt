#Spleen#

##Location##
The spleen is located on the left side of the abdomen, just below the ribs and beside the stomach.  
It is attached to the stomach by the gastrosplenic ligament and lies along the body wall.

##Shape##
A long, flat, tongue-shaped organ with a smooth dark red to purple surface.  
The spleen can change size depending on how much blood it stores.  
It has an outer capsule and soft inner tissue filled with blood vessels.

##Function##
- Filters and cleans the blood by removing old or damaged red blood cells.  
- Stores extra blood and releases it during activity, stress, or injury.  
- Produces and stores white blood cells (lymphocytes) that fight infection.  
- Supports immune defense and helps maintain healthy blood composition.  
- Plays a role in recycling iron and other components from red blood cells.
