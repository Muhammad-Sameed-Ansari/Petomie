#Heart#

The **dog’s heart** is the central pump of the circulatory system, driving life through its rhythmic contractions.  
It is strong, resilient, and deeply symbolic of loyalty and love.  
Energetically, the dog’s heart radiates **devotion, courage, and emotional truth**, harmonizing instinct with affection.

###Parts###

##Atria##
Receive blood returning from the body and lungs.  
Energetically, symbolize **openness and trust in connection**.

##Ventricles##
Pump blood to the lungs and the rest of the body.  
Energetically, reflect **courage, action, and purpose**.

##Valves##
Ensure proper flow and separation of chambers.  
Energetically, symbolize **boundaries, faith, and trust in rhythm**.

###Function###
The heart pumps blood throughout the body, maintaining oxygen supply and systemic rhythm.  
It supports every organ, sustaining energy and vitality.  

Energetically, the heart governs **love, loyalty, and emotional harmony**.

###Common Imbalances###
Arrhythmia: Emotional turbulence or imbalance.  
Weak pulse: Exhaustion or lack of enthusiasm.  
Heart murmur: Energy leakage or sadness.  
Energetic closure: Fear of loss or withholding affection.  
Energetic excess: Overattachment or overexertion.
