###Parts###

##Vena Cava##
Composed of cranial and caudal sections that return blood from the body to the right atrium.  
Energetically, represents **returning home to center and integration of experience**.

##Pulmonary Veins##
Carry oxygenated blood from the lungs to the left atrium.  
Energetically, symbolize **renewal and restoration of breath and spirit**.

##Jugular Veins##
Drain blood from the head and neck.  
Energetically, reflect **release of mental and emotional tension**.

##Femoral and Saphenous Veins##
Return blood from the limbs.  
Energetically, embody **grounding, rest, and regeneration**.

###Function###
Veins return deoxygenated blood to the heart under low pressure.  
They regulate blood storage and maintain equilibrium.  

Energetically, veins express **receptivity, rest, and the healing flow of return**.

###Common Imbalances###
Venous stasis: Slow or congested flow.  
Varicosities: Weak walls or poor elasticity.  
Energetic stagnation: Emotional heaviness or fatigue.  
Energetic depletion: Lack of recovery or reflection.
