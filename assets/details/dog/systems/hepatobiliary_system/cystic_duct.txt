#Cystic Duct#
Part of the Hepatobiliary System: Connects the gallbladder to the bile ducts for bile movement.
Energetically, it represents flexibility, trust in timing, and smooth transition between phases of storage and release.

###Parts###

##Connection to Gallbladder##
Allows bile to move in and out of the gallbladder.  
Energetically, symbolizes balance between holding and letting go.

###Function###
Regulates bile exchange between gallbladder and ducts.  
Ensures efficient storage and release during digestion.

###Common Imbalances###
Obstruction: tension or delayed action.  
Weak flow: hesitation or emotional stagnation.  
Energetic blockage: fear of release.  
Energetic excess: impatience or overreaction.
