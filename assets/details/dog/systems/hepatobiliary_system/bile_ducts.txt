#Bile Ducts#
Part of the Hepatobiliary System: Transport bile from the liver to the intestine to aid digestion and detoxification.
Energetically, the ducts represent purification, flow, and the healthy release of stored energy.

###Parts###

##Intrahepatic Ducts##
Small channels within the liver collecting bile from cells.  
Energetically, symbolize inner organization and movement.

##Hepatic Ducts##
Merge to form the common hepatic duct.  
Energetically, reflect communication and harmony between systems.

##Common Bile Duct##
Extends toward the duodenum, guiding bile into the intestine.  
Energetically, embodies flow, transition, and renewal.

###Function###
Carry bile from the liver and gallbladder to the small intestine.  
Support digestion of fats and elimination of waste.

###Common Imbalances###
Obstruction: resistance or poor digestion.  
Sluggish flow: fatigue or buildup.  
Energetic blockage: emotional holding or suppression.  
Energetic weakness: lack of motivation or inner stagnation.
