#Kupffer Cells#
Part of the Hepatobiliary System: Specialized immune cells that clear debris and pathogens from the blood.
Energetically, they represent discernment, protection, and emotional purification.

###Parts###

##Sinusoids##
Vascular channels where Kupffer cells reside.  
Energetically, symbolize guardianship and alertness.

###Function###
Remove bacteria, toxins, and worn-out cells from blood passing through the liver.  
Support immune defense and purification.

###Common Imbalances###
Overactivity: tension or excessive defensiveness.  
Weak function: vulnerability or fatigue.  
Energetic stagnation: holding old pain.  
Energetic depletion: low resilience.
