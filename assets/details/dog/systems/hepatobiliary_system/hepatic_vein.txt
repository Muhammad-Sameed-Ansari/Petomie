##Right, Middle, and Left Veins##

###Function###

###Common Imbalances###
Congestion: internal buildup or emotional tension.  
Weak flow: fatigue or exhaustion.  
Energetic stagnation: holding onto the past.  
Energetic depletion: low emotional energy.
