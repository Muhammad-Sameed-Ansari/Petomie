#Hepatic Lobules#
Part of the Hepatobiliary System: Microscopic functional units of the liver.
Energetically, they represent harmony, structure, and cellular cooperation within the system.

###Parts###

##Central Vein##
Receives processed blood from liver cells.  
Energetically, symbolizes alignment and inner order.

##Portal Triad##
Includes a branch of the hepatic artery, portal vein, and bile duct.  
Energetically, reflects integration and teamwork.

###Function###
Facilitates detoxification, metabolism, and bile secretion at the cellular level.  
Coordinates vital chemical exchanges for health and balance.

###Common Imbalances###
Overload: toxin accumulation or fatigue.  
Degeneration: depletion or disorganization.  
Energetic imbalance: inner disharmony.  
Energetic depletion: reduced vitality.
