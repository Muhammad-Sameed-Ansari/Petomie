#Hepatic Ducts#
Part of the Hepatobiliary System: Drain bile from the liver into the biliary system.
Energetically, they represent structure and harmony, linking inner processes with external flow.

###Parts###

##Right and Left Hepatic Ducts##
Collect bile from each liver lobe.  
Energetically, symbolize duality and cooperation.

##Common Hepatic Duct##
Formed by merging of the right and left ducts.  
Energetically, reflects unity and alignment.

###Function###
Channel bile from liver lobes to the gallbladder or duodenum.  
Ensure smooth bile flow for digestion.

###Common Imbalances###
Blockage: stagnation or frustration.  
Inflammation: inner conflict or irritation.  
Energetic restriction: fear of expression.  
Energetic depletion: low energy flow.
