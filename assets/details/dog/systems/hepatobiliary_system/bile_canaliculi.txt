#Bile Canaliculi#
Part of the Hepatobiliary System: Tiny channels between liver cells that collect bile and lead to intrahepatic ducts.
Energetically, they represent inner flow, subtle communication, and renewal through cleansing.

###Parts###

##Intercellular Channels##
Connect hepatocytes and direct bile movement.  
Energetically, symbolize delicate flow and awareness.

###Function###
Carry bile from hepatocytes to the intrahepatic ducts.  
Support detoxification and lipid metabolism.

###Common Imbalances###
Blockage: sluggish energy or emotional holding.  
Inflammation: inner irritation or tension.  
Energetic blockage: suppressed expression.  
Energetic depletion: fatigue or dullness.
