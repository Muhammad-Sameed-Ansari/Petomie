#Spinal Nerves#
Spinal nerves emerge from the spinal cord and connect it to the body.  
In the dog, they coordinate motor and sensory communication.  
Energetically, they represent flow, awareness, and connection through the whole being.

###Parts###

##Dorsal and Ventral Roots##  
Carry sensory and motor fibers.  
Energetically, symbolize dual flow and unity.

###Function###  
Transmit signals between the CNS and body.  
Energetically, maintain integration of physical and energetic systems.

###Common Imbalances###  
Nerve compression: inner tension.  
Pain: blocked flow or emotional rigidity.  
Energetic stagnation: loss of direction or vitality.
