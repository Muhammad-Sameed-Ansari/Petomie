#Liver (Immune Role)#

##Location##
Large organ occupying much of the front abdomen, just behind the diaphragm.

###Shape###
Multiple smooth, reddish-brown lobes.

###Parts###

##Kupffer Cells##
Specialized immune cells that line liver sinusoids and filter blood.  
Energetically, represent **cleansing, transformation, and letting go**.

###Function###
Detoxifies blood, filters pathogens and toxins, and supports immune balance and metabolism.  
Energetically, helps with **releasing stored emotion, renewal, and steady flow of energy**.

###Common Imbalances###
Toxin overload, infection, inflammation, or liver enzyme elevation.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Deep green, teal  
Crystals: Malachite, bloodstone  
Element: Wood  
Affirmation: “I release what no longer serves my body and spirit.”
