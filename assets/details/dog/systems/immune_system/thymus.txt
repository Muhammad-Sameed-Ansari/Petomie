#Thymus#

##Location##
Situated in the upper chest in front of the heart, most prominent in puppies and gradually shrinking with age.

###Shape###
Soft, lobed gland with multiple segments.

###Parts###

##Cortex##
Outer area where immature T-cells begin development.  
Energetically, symbolizes **growth, learning, and forming safe boundaries**.

##Medulla##
Inner region where T-cells mature and become specialized defenders.  
Energetically, represents **clarity, courage, and refined protection**.

##Thymic Lobules##
Small compartments that organize stages of T-cell maturation.  
Energetically, reflect **guided evolution and inner structure**.

###Function###
Creates and trains T-lymphocytes, shaping early immune competence and long-term immune memory.  
Energetically, supports **bravery, emotional grounding, and a sense of inner safety**.

###Applies to##
All dogs (especially vital in early life).

###Common Imbalances###
Weak immunity in young dogs, increased infections, or age-related loss of thymic tissue.

Crystals: Amazonite, aquamarine  
Element: Air
