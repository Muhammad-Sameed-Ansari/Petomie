#Harderian Gland#

##Location##
Situated behind the eye within the orbit, associated with the third eyelid.

###Shape###
Soft, multilobed gland.

###Parts###

##Secretory Lobules##
Produce lipid and immune-rich secretions for the eye surface.  
Energetically, symbolize **clarity, subtle protection, and inner sight**.

##Duct System##
Carries secretions toward the third eyelid and eye surface.  
Energetically, reflects **flow, cleansing, and emotional release**.

###Function###
Supports tear film quality, lubricates the eye, and contributes to local immune defense.  
Energetically, enhances **intuition, perception, and safe connection with the outer world**.

###Applies to##
All dogs.

###Common Imbalances###
Eye discharge, irritation, third eyelid swelling, or infection.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Indigo, deep blue  
Crystals: Lapis lazuli, sodalite  
Element: Light  
Affirmation: “I see clearly while remaining protected.”
