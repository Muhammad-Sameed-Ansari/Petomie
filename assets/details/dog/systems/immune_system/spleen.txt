#Spleen#

##Location##
Lies along the greater curvature of the stomach in the left side of the abdomen.

###Shape###
Elongated, flat, dark-red organ.

###Parts###

##White Pulp##
Lymphoid tissue that produces immune cells and detects blood-borne pathogens.  
Energetically, symbolizes **clarity, insight, and protective awareness**.

##Red Pulp##
Filters blood, removes old cells, and stores red blood cells and platelets.  
Energetically, represents **renewal, grounding, and resilience**.

###Function###
Filters blood, supports immune responses, and acts as a reserve for blood cells.  
Energetically, maintains **centeredness, stamina, and steady strength**.

###Applies to##
All dogs.

###Common Imbalances###
Splenomegaly, infection, anemia, or rupture from trauma.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Yellow, gold  
Crystals: Citrine, amber  
Element: Earth  
Affirmation: “I remain centered, strong, and supported from within.”
