#Peyer's Patches#

##Location##
Found in the wall of the small intestine, especially the ileum.

###Shape###
Flat or slightly raised patches of lymphoid tissue.

###Parts###

##Follicular Centers##
Sites where immune cells respond to gut antigens.  
Energetically, symbolize **processing, adaptation, and integration**.

###Function###
Monitor gut contents, activate B-cells, and support digestive immune balance.  
Energetically, help with **emotional digestion, flexibility, and ease in change**.

###Applies to##
All dogs.

###Common Imbalances###
Digestive infections, chronic diarrhea, inflammatory bowel issues, or food sensitivities.
