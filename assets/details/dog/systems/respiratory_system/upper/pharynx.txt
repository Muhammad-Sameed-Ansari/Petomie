#Pharynx#
The pharynx in the dog connects the nasal cavity and mouth to the larynx and esophagus.  
It serves as a shared passage for air and food.  
Energetically, it represents communication, coordination, and transition between senses and action.

###Parts###

##Nasopharynx, Oropharynx, Laryngopharynx##  
Guide airflow and food movement.  
Energetically, symbolize unity between intention and expression.

###Function###  
Directs air to the respiratory tract and food to the digestive system.  
Energetically, harmonizes breath and expression with instinct.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: irritation or frustration.  
Blockage: internal conflict or tension.  
Energetic stagnation: difficulty expressing instinctive truth.
