#Bronchi#
The bronchi are main branches from the trachea that direct air into each lung.  
They distribute and regulate airflow throughout the respiratory system.  
Energetically, they represent direction, purpose, and clarity in movement.

###Parts###

##Primary and Secondary Bronchi##  
Divide air between right and left lungs.  
Energetically, symbolize organization and communication between pathways.

###Function###  
Direct air to each lung and maintain efficient airflow.  
Energetically, channel determination and flow toward balance.

###Common Imbalances###  
Bronchitis: irritation or emotional inflammation.  
Constriction: fear or guardedness.  
Energetic stagnation: difficulty in moving forward freely.
