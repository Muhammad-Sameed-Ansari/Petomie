#Radial Nerve#
Part of the Nervous System: The main extensor nerve of the forelimb, originating from C7–T2.  
Energetically, it represents empowerment, action, and expansion — the ability to move forward with strength and confidence.

###Parts###

##Origin##  
Formed from spinal nerves C7–T2.  
Energetically, symbolizes courage and purposeful direction.

##Pathway##  
Curves around the humerus and continues along the lateral forelimb.  
Energetically, reflects motion, endurance, and confidence.

##Branches##  
Motor to triceps and extensors; sensory to the dorsal paw.  
Energetically, unites action with awareness.

###Function###  
Extends the elbow, carpus, and digits; provides sensation to the dorsal paw.  
Energetically, empowers confident, balanced movement.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weak extension: loss of drive or purpose.  
Nerve injury: self-doubt or restraint.  
Energetic blockage: resistance to change.
