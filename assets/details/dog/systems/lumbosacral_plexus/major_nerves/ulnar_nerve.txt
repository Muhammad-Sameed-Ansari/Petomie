#Ulnar Nerve#
Part of the Nervous System: A branch of the brachial plexus (C8–T2) that supplies flexor muscles and sensory input to the caudal paw.  
Energetically, it represents sensitivity, compassion, and letting go — emotional balance in connection and release.

###Parts###

##Origin##  
Emerges from spinal nerves C8–T2.  
Energetically, symbolizes flexibility and sensitivity in motion.

##Pathway##  
Runs medially to the carpus and fifth digit.  
Energetically, reflects graceful control and emotional intelligence.

##Branches##  
Motor to flexors, sensory to caudal paw.  
Energetically, connects giving with receiving.

###Function###  
Controls flexion and supplies sensation to the caudal paw.  
Energetically, harmonizes physical action with emotional flow.

###Common Imbalances###  
Numbness: disconnection or self-protection.  
Pain: emotional strain or overholding.  
Energetic stagnation: reluctance to let go or express.
