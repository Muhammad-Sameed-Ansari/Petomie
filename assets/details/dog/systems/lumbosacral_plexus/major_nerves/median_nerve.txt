#Median Nerve#
Part of the Nervous System: A branch of the brachial plexus (C7–T1) that controls flexion of the carpus and digits and sensation of the paw’s palmar surface.  
Energetically, it represents precision, focus, and dexterity — balanced control in giving and receiving.

###Parts###

##Origin##  
Arises from spinal nerves C7–T1.  
Energetically, symbolizes communication and detailed awareness.

##Pathway##  
Runs medially along the forearm to the paw.  
Energetically, reflects directed flow and purpose.

##Branches##  
Motor to flexors; sensory to the palmar paw.  
Energetically, connects touch with consciousness.

###Function###  
Flexes the carpus and digits; provides sensation to the palmar paw.  
Energetically, enhances tactile expression and awareness.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weakness: indecision or disconnection.  
Pain: restriction or blocked creativity.  
Energetic stagnation: loss of sensitivity or expression.
