#Lateral Cutaneous Femoral Nerve#
Part of the Nervous System: A sensory nerve from the lumbar plexus (L4–L5) that supplies the skin of the outer thigh.  
Energetically, it represents sensitivity, protection, and the balance between openness and personal space.

###Parts###

##Origin##  
Arises from the ventral branches of L4–L5.  
Energetically, symbolizes awareness and outer boundary sensing.

##Pathway##  
Courses along the iliacus muscle to reach the lateral thigh.  
Energetically, reflects adaptability and intuitive awareness of surroundings.

##Distribution##  
Supplies cutaneous sensation to the skin of the outer thigh.  
Energetically, enhances perception and comfort within movement.

###Function###  
Provides sensation and temperature awareness to the lateral thigh.  
Supports proprioception and balance in limb movement.

###Common Imbalances###  
Numbness: emotional disconnection.  
Tingling: overstimulation or energetic strain.  
Energetic blockage: hypersensitivity or unclear boundaries.
