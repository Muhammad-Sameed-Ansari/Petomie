#Vagina#

##Location##
Extends from the **cervix** to the **vestibule**, lying within the pelvic cavity.

###Shape###
A **long, distensible muscular tube** lined with stratified epithelium.  
Adapted to receive the penis and to pass puppies during birth.

##Parts##

###1. Cranial Vagina###
Region closest to the cervix; site of semen deposition.

###2. Middle Vagina###
Main passageway with longitudinal folds allowing expansion.

###3. Vestibule###
**Caudal continuation** shared with the urinary tract; contains the **urethral opening** on a small **urethral tubercle**.

###4. Hymen/Transitional Fold###

###5. Vulva###
External opening forming the entrance to the vestibule.

###Function###
Serves as the **copulatory organ** and **birth canal**.  
Allows drainage of uterine/cervical secretions.  
Protects internal organs via mucosal defenses and normal flora.
