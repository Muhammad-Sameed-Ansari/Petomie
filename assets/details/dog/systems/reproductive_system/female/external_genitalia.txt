#External Genitalia#

##Location##
Situated beneath the **anus** at the rear of the body, forming the visible part of the reproductive tract.

###Shape###
Composed of soft **labia** forming a vertical slit (vulvar cleft), with subtle changes during estrus and pregnancy.

##Parts##

###1. Vulva###
Two **labia** that close to protect internal organs; the entrance to the vestibule.

###2. Clitoris###
Small **sensory organ** within the **clitoral fossa** at the floor of the vulva.

###3. Vestibule###
Shared passage for reproductive and urinary systems; houses the **urethral opening** on the urethral tubercle.

###4. Dorsal and Ventral Commissures###
Upper and lower junctions of the vulvar lips maintaining closure.

###5. Perineum###
Skin and muscle between the vulva and anus supporting the pelvic floor.

###Function###
Provides **protection** and a **gateway** for mating, urination, and birth.  
Maintains a **tight seal** to prevent contamination and preserve reproductive health.
