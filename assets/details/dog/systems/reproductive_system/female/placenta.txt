#Placenta#

##Location##
Forms only during **pregnancy** within the **uterus**, attaching along bands on each uterine horn where fetuses develop.

###Shape###
A **zonary, endotheliochorial placenta** forming a **beltâ€‘like zone** around each fetus.  
Includes fetal membranes: **chorion**, **allantois**, and **amnion**.

##Parts##

###1. Maternal Portion###
Modified **endometrium** forming contact areas for exchange.

###2. Fetal Portion###
Develops from **chorion and allantois**, containing fetal vessels connected to the **umbilical cord**.

###3. Umbilical Cord###
Connects fetus to placenta, carrying **umbilical arteries and vein**.

###4. Placental Membranes###
**Chorion, allantois, amnion** enclosing and protecting the fetus.

###Function###
Provides **gas, nutrient, and waste exchange** between dam and fetuses.  
Secretes hormones that **support and maintain pregnancy**.  
Is expelled as **afterbirth** following delivery.
