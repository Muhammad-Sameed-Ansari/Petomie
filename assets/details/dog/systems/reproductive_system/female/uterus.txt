#Uterus#
The uterus is the center of gestation in the dog.  
It nurtures and protects developing embryos.  
Energetically, it represents creation, transformation, and sacred protection.

###Parts###

##Uterine Horns##  
Extend from the body to each ovary.  
Energetically, symbolize expansion and balance.

##Endometrium##  
Inner lining supporting embryo implantation.  
Energetically, reflects receptivity and growth.

###Function###  
Provides a nourishing environment for fetal development.  
Energetically, anchors creative and emotional life force.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: tension or suppressed emotion.  
Weak tone: fatigue or low creative flow.  
Energetic imbalance: unexpressed nurturing energy.
