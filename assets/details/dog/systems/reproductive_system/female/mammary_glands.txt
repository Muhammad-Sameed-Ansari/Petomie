#Mammary Glands#
The mammary glands of the dog produce and secrete milk for newborn nourishment.  
Energetically, they symbolize nurturing, care, and the giving of life energy.

###Parts###

##Teats and Ducts##  
Deliver milk from the glands to the exterior.  
Energetically, represent flow of love and nourishment.

##Glandular Tissue##  
Produces and stores milk.  
Energetically, reflects giving and connection.

###Function###  
Provides nutrition and antibodies for offspring.  
Energetically, supports nurturing, generosity, and maternal strength.

###Common Imbalances###  
Mastitis: blocked care or overburden.  
Engorgement: emotional holding.  
Energetic imbalance: imbalance between giving and receiving.
