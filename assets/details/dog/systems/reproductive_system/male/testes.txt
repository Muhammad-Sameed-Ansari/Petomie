#Testes#
The testes in the dog produce sperm and testosterone.  
Energetically, they represent vitality, creation, and core masculine strength.

###Parts###

##Seminiferous Tubules##  
Produce and mature sperm.  
Energetically, symbolize creativity and renewal.

##Leydig Cells##  
Secrete testosterone for vitality and drive.  
Energetically, reflect confidence and motivation.

###Function###  
Produce sperm and hormones for reproduction and strength.  
Energetically, sustain vitality, courage, and grounded energy.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling: inner stress or overactivity.  
Pain: vulnerability or emotional strain.  
Energetic imbalance: depletion, fear, or loss of confidence.
