#Sheath (Prepuce)#

##Location##
A protective fold of skin located along the underside of the belly, just in front of the scrotum.
It covers and encloses the penis when it is not extended.

###Shape###
Soft, flexible tubular covering made of skin and connective tissue, with a smooth, moist inner lining.

##Parts##

###1. Outer Fold###
Hair-covered external skin that blends with the surrounding abdominal area.

###2. Inner Fold###
Smooth internal lining that lies directly against the penis, keeping it clean and lubricated.

##Function##
Protects the penis from dirt, debris, and injury.
Maintains a clean and moist environment around the penis.
Allows smooth extension and retraction during erection.
Secretes natural oils that aid lubrication and provide protection.
