#Epididymis#

##Location##
Attached along the back edge of each testis, extending from the upper to the lower pole within the scrotum.
It connects the testes to the sperm duct (vas deferens).

###Shape###
A long, narrow, and coiled tube forming a curved structure on the posterior side of the testis.

##Parts##

###1. Head (Caput)###
Receives immature sperm from the testis.

###2. Body (Corpus)###
Region where sperm mature and gain the ability to swim.

###3. Tail (Cauda)###
Stores mature sperm until ejaculation.

##Function##
Provides a site for sperm maturation and storage.
Transports sperm from the testes to the vas deferens during ejaculation.
Ensures sperm achieve motility and fertilizing capacity before release.
