#Prepuce#

##Location##
Covers and protects the **penis** when not erect.  
Located along the **ventral abdominal wall**, just behind the umbilical region.

###Shape###
A **fold of skin** forming a sheath around the penis.  
The external surface is covered in hair, while the inner lining is smooth and moist.

##Parts##

###1. External Fold###
The outer, hairy portion that merges with the surrounding abdominal skin.

###2. Internal Fold###
The inner mucosal layer that lies against the penis and provides lubrication.

###3. Preputial Orifice###
The **opening** at the tip of the prepuce, allowing the penis to extend during mating.

###Function###
Protects the penis from **injury and contamination**.  
Maintains **moisture and cleanliness** of the penile surface.  
Retracts during mating to permit **extension of the penis**.  
Prevents debris and pathogens from entering the preputial cavity.
