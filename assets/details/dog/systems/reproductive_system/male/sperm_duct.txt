#Sperm Duct (Vas Deferens)#

##Location##
Extends from the **tail of the epididymis** through the **spermatic cord** into the **pelvic cavity**, ending at the urethra.  
It joins ducts from the accessory glands near the prostate before entering the urethra.

###Shape###
A **narrow, muscular tube** with a thick wall and small lumen.  
Its course is slightly curved as it travels toward the prostate and urethra.

##Parts##

###1. Proximal Segment###
Carries sperm from the epididymis into the pelvic cavity.

###2. Ampulla###
An **enlarged terminal region** that serves as a reservoir for sperm before ejaculation.

###3. Terminal Segment###
Joins with ducts of the accessory glands to deliver sperm into the urethra.

###Function###
Transports **mature sperm** during ejaculation using muscular contractions.  
Acts as a **temporary storage site** for sperm.  
Coordinates with accessory glands to mix sperm with seminal fluid for ejaculation.
