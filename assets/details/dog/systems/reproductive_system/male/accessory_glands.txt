#Accessory Glands#

##Location##
Located within the **pelvic cavity**, surrounding the **urethra** near its junction with the bladder.  
In dogs, only the **prostate gland** is fully developed.

###Shape###
A **lobulated, glandular organ** surrounding the neck of the bladder and upper urethra.  
Its surface is smooth and firm, and size can vary with age and hormonal activity.

##Parts##

###1. Prostate Gland###
Encircles the urethra and secretes fluid that nourishes and protects sperm.  
It is the **only significant accessory gland** in dogs.

###2. Bulbourethral Glands###
Absent in dogs, unlike in some other species.

###Function###
Produces **seminal fluid** that supports sperm survival and motility.  
Secretes substances that **neutralize acidity** in the urethra.  
Provides lubrication to facilitate sperm transport during ejaculation.
