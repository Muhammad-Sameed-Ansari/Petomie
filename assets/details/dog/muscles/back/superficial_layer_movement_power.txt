The superficial back muscles form the **outer layer** responsible for broad, visible movement and strength.  
They link the spine to the limbs, coordinating posture and motion.  
Energetically, they represent **action, expression, and dynamic flow**.

###Parts###

##Latissimus Dorsi##  
Large, triangular muscle from thoracic spine to humerus.  
Pulls forelimb back during stride.  
Energetically, symbolizes **confidence and freedom in motion**.

##Thoracolumbar Fascia##  
Fibrous sheet connecting upper and lower body strength.  
Energetically, reflects **unity, protection, and integrated power**.

###Function###  
Controls forelimb retraction, spinal extension, and power transmission.  
Energetically, connects **willpower with expressive motion**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tightness: restricted reach or short stride.  
Weakness: poor topline or lack of engagement.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **tension between control and freedom**.
