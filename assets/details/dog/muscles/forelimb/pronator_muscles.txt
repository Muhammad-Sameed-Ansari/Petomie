###Parts###

##Pronator Teres##  
Superficial muscle from the humerus to the radius, rotates the forearm inward.  
Energetically, symbolizes **adaptability and direction**.

##Pronator Quadratus##  
Deep muscle connecting the radius and ulna, ensuring stability.  
Energetically, reflects **connection and calm precision**.

###Function###  
Facilitates rotation and fine control of the forearm and paw.  
Energetically, supports **orientation and awareness of environment**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tightness: stiffness in forearm rotation.  
Weakness: instability or poor positioning.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **uncertainty or hesitation in movement**.
