#Pes Muscles#

##Distal limb below the hock##
###1. Interossei (Plantar Muscles)###
Located between metatarsal bones.
Flex digits and stabilize the metatarsophalangeal joints.
###2. Lumbricales###
Small muscles associated with the tendons of the flexor digitorum profundus.
Flex the metatarsophalangeal joints and extend the distal joints.
###3. Short Digital Flexors###
Flex the digits and support the paw during weight-bearing.
###4. Extensor Tendons (Common & Lateral)###
Extend the digits, stabilize the toes, and aid in balance.
###5. Intertarsal and Intermetatarsal Muscles###
Small stabilizing muscles of the paw.
Maintain foot arch and assist in fine positioning.

##Function##
Provide fine motor control of the paw and digits.
Support the limb during stance, maintain joint stability, and aid in traction and propulsion.
