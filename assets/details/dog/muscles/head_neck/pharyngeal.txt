#Pharyngeal Muscles#

The dog’s pharyngeal muscles form the throat’s muscular wall, controlling swallowing and guiding food to the esophagus.  
They coordinate with the tongue and larynx for breathing and vocalization.  
Energetically, they represent **trust, surrender, and flow between expression and nourishment**.

###Parts###

##Constrictor Muscles##  
Circular muscles that push food toward the esophagus.  
Energetically, symbolize **flow and trust in life’s process**.

##Stylopharyngeus##  
Elevates and opens the pharynx during swallowing.  
Energetically, reflects **release and openness**.

###Function###  
Coordinates swallowing, breathing, and vocalization.  
Energetically, harmonizes **control with surrender**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tightness: difficulty swallowing or tension in throat.  
Weakness: gag reflex or food retention.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **difficulty letting go or emotional blockage**.
