###Parts###

##Orbicularis Oris##  
Encircles the mouth, controlling lip movement.  
Energetically, symbolizes **expression and communication**.

##Zygomaticus##  
Pulls the lips and corners of the mouth backward.  
Energetically, reflects **joy and social connection**.

##Frontalis##  
Raises the brows and wrinkles the forehead.  
Energetically, represents **awareness and perception**.

##Platysma##  
Thin superficial muscle extending along the neck and jaw.  
Energetically, embodies **vulnerability and openness**.

###Function###  
Controls facial expressions, assists with vocalization, and enhances communication.  
Energetically, supports **emotional awareness and empathy**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tightness: stiffness or reduced mobility in the face.  
Weakness: drooping or loss of expression.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **emotional suppression or disconnection**.
