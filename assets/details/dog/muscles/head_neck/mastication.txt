###Parts###

##Masseter##  
Main chewing muscle, closes the jaw.  
Energetically, symbolizes **strength and persistence**.

##Temporalis##  
Broad muscle over the skull, assists in jaw elevation.  
Energetically, reflects **focus and mental power**.

##Pterygoid Muscles##  
Control side-to-side movement of the jaw.  
Energetically, represent **adaptation and balance**.

###Function###  
Enable chewing, biting, and tension maintenance of the jaw.  
Energetically, they link **sustenance with determination**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tightness: jaw clenching or tension.  
Weakness: difficulty chewing or uneven bite.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **suppressed anger or resistance**.
