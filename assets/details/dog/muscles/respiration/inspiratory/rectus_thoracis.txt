#Rectus Thoracis#

##Location##
Situated along the **ventral thoracic wall**, extending from the **first rib** to approximately the **fourth rib**.  
Runs parallel to the sternum, continuing into the rectus abdominis muscle.

###Shape###
Narrow, strap-like muscle with longitudinal fibers.  
Lies adjacent to the sternum and intercostal spaces.

##Function##
Assists **inspiration** by pulling the ribs forward to increase thoracic volume.  
Supports ventral thoracic wall stability and rib movement during respiration.
