#Serratus Dorsalis Cranialis#

##Location##
Located on the **dorsolateral thoracic wall**, beneath the rhomboideus and trapezius.  
Extends from the **thoracolumbar fascia** to the **first several ribs**.

###Shape###
Thin, fan-shaped muscle with **fibers running caudoventrally** (downward and forward).  
Covers the dorsal aspect of the first few ribs near the vertebral column.

##Function##
Draws the cranial ribs forward and outward to expand the thoracic cavity.  
Acts as an **inspiratory muscle**, particularly during deep or forced breathing.  
Helps maintain rib alignment and thoracic flexibility.
