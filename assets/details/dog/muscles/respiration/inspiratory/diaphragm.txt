#Diaphragm#

##Location##
Forms the **muscular partition between the thoracic and abdominal cavities**, extending from the **sternum, ribs, and lumbar vertebrae** to the **central tendon**.  
Separates the lungs and heart from the abdominal viscera.

###Shape###
A **broad, dome-shaped muscle** with a central tendinous portion and muscular periphery.  
The convex surface faces the thoracic cavity; the concave surface faces the abdomen.

##Function##
Principal muscle of **inspiration**.  
Contracts and flattens to enlarge the thoracic cavity, reducing intrathoracic pressure and drawing air into the lungs.  
Assists in abdominal pressure increase during defecation, urination, and parturition.
