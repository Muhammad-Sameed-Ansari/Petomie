#Scalenus#

##Location##
Situated along the **lateral neck**, connecting the **cervical vertebrae** to the **first few ribs**.  
Lies deep to the brachiocephalicus and omotransversarius muscles.

###Shape###
Long, strap-like muscle divided into dorsal, middle, and ventral portions.  
Fibers run obliquely from the neck downward and backward.

##Function##
Elevates the first ribs during **inspiration** to expand the upper thorax.  
Stabilizes the neck and thoracic inlet during respiration.  
Assists in neck flexion and stabilization when both sides contract simultaneously.
