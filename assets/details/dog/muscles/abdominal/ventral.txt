##Location##
Found along the **underside of the abdomen**, between the ribs and the pelvis.  
These muscles form the ventral belly floor that supports the viscera.

###Shape###
Broad, flat sheets of muscle with longitudinal fibers.  
Provides firm but flexible abdominal support.

##Parts##

###1. Rectus Abdominis###
- Main ventral belly muscle.  
- **Function:** Supports trunk, aids flexion, and stabilizes movement.  

###2. Transversus Abdominis###
- Deep layer beneath other abdominal muscles.  
- **Function:** Compresses abdomen and maintains internal pressure.  

###3. Abdominal Fascia###
- Connective tissue covering ventral region.  
- **Function:** Adds strength, flexibility, and tension control.  

##Function##
- **Support:** Holds organs and maintains tone.  
- **Stability:** Provides base for posture and motion.  
- **Respiration:** Works with diaphragm for breathing control.  
- **Protection:** Guards abdomen from impact.
