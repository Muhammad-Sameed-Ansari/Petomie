#Abdominal Central Muscles#

The dog’s central abdominal muscles form the supportive **core of the body**, linking the chest and pelvis.  
They stabilize the trunk, protect internal organs, and maintain posture during movement.  
Energetically, they represent **balance, inner power, and the harmony between control and flow**.

###Parts###

##Central Line##  
Runs along the body’s midline, anchoring both sides of the abdomen.  
Energetically, symbolizes **alignment and inner strength**.

##Core Sheets##  
Broad muscle layers wrapping around the abdomen, giving tone and support.  
Energetically, reflect **stability and centered awareness**.

##Support Fibers##  
Connect upper and lower body movement.  
Energetically, embody **fluid unity and balance**.

###Function###  
Stabilizes the spine, assists with breathing, and coordinates body movement.  
Energetically, grounds **core confidence and balanced power**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tightness: rigidity or shallow breathing.  
Weakness: poor posture or instability.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **loss of balance or inner insecurity**.
