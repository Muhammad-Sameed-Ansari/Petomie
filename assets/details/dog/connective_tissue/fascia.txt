#Fascia#

In dogs, fascia is a continuous web of connective tissue surrounding muscles, bones, and organs.  
It integrates body movement and transmits energy.  
Energetically, it represents **connection, unity, and fluid communication**.

###Parts###

##Superficial Fascia##  
Beneath the skin, storing fat and supporting movement.  
Energetically, it symbolizes **sensitivity and protection**.

##Deep Fascia##  
Encases muscles, enhancing posture and coordination.  
Energetically, it reflects **alignment and inner flow**.

##Visceral Fascia##  
Anchors and supports internal organs.  
Energetically, it embodies **harmony and grounding**.

###Function###  
Fascia maintains structural balance, assists healing, and distributes movement forces.  
Energetically, it sustains **connection, awareness, and flow**.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Tightness: emotional tension or restriction.  
- Adhesions: trapped trauma or energy.  
- Weakness: lack of grounding or disconnection.
