#Lymph#

In dogs, lymph is a clear fluid circulating through vessels and nodes that cleanses the body and supports immunity.  
It carries white blood cells and removes waste.  
Energetically, it represents **cleansing, flow, and release**.

###Parts###

##Lymph Fluid##  
Carries immune cells and toxins.  
Energetically, it symbolizes **purification and clarity**.

##Lymph Nodes##  
Filter pathogens and activate immunity.  
Energetically, they embody **protection and discernment**.

##Lymph Vessels##  
Transport lymph fluid throughout the body.  
Energetically, they reflect **emotional flow and release**.

###Function###  
Lymph supports detoxification, fluid balance, and immunity.  
Energetically, it governs **renewal, flow, and lightness**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling: emotional congestion or stagnation.  
Inflammation: overreaction or stress.  
Weak flow: fatigue or unprocessed emotion.
