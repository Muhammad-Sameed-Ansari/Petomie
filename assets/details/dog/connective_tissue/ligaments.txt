#Ligaments#

In dogs, ligaments are fibrous connective tissues connecting bones, providing stability and support.  
They prevent overextension and maintain joint alignment.  
Energetically, they represent **trust, stability, and structure**.

###Parts###

##Joint Ligaments##  
Stabilize bones within joints.  
Energetically, they symbolize **security and integrity**.

##Spinal Ligaments##  
Support the vertebral column, balancing flexibility and strength.  
Energetically, they reflect **support and inner strength**.

##Suspensory Ligaments##  
Anchor organs and maintain posture.  
Energetically, they represent **balance and grounding**.

###Function###  
Ligaments control range of motion, protect joints, and provide stability.  
Energetically, they uphold **trust and resilience in movement**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Laxity: lack of support or direction.  
Tension: control issues or fear.  
Weakness: instability or loss of trust.
