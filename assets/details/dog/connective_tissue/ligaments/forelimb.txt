#Ligaments - Forelimb#

##Location##
Shoulder, elbow, carpus, and digits.

##Shape##
Fibrous cords or bands stabilizing joints.

##Parts##
Glenohumeral Ligaments – Stabilize shoulder joint.
Collateral Ligaments of Elbow – Medial and lateral; prevent abnormal motion.
Collateral Ligaments of Carpus – Medial and lateral; stabilize carpal joint.
Collateral Ligaments of Digits – Stabilize interphalangeal joints.
Annular Ligaments – Hold flexor tendons against bones at digits.

##Function##
Stabilize forelimb joints, prevent dislocation, assist weight-bearing.
