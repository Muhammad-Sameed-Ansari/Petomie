#Cervical Intervertebral Discs#

##Location##
Between each cervical vertebra from C2–C7, supporting head movement, orientation, and full-range neck mobility.

###Shape###
Small, curved fibrocartilage pads adapted for precision, flexibility, and stability during motion.

###Parts###

##Annulus Fibrosus##
Fibrocartilage ring providing stability while allowing flexibility.
Energetically represents boundaries, containment, and emotional steadiness.

##Nucleus Pulposus##
Soft cushioning core absorbing shock and ensuring smooth movement.
Energetically reflects adaptability and emotional fluidity.

Transitions neck to shoulder movement. Energetically supports grounding and responsiveness.
