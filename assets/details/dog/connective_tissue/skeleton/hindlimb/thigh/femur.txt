#Femur#

##Location##  
Found in the **hind limb**, connecting the **hip** to the **stifle (knee)**.  
It is the longest and most powerful bone in the dog’s body.  

###Shape###  
A **long bone** with rounded ends, a thick shaft, and prominent trochanters for muscular attachment.  

###Parts###  

##Head##  
Articulates with the hip socket.  
Energetically, represents **connection and stability**.  

##Neck##  
Transitions from the head to the shaft.  
Energetically, symbolizes **adaptability and flow**.  

##Shaft##  
Provides leverage and strength for motion.  
Energetically, reflects **purpose and strength**.  

##Distal End##  
Forms the stifle joint with the tibia and patella.  
Energetically, connects **drive with balance**.  

###Function###  
Supports body weight, enables movement, and provides strength for propulsion.  
Energetically, governs **willpower, balance, and courage**.
