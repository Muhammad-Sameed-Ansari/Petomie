#Pelvis#

##Location##  
Situated at the **base of the spine**, connecting the **sacrum** to the **hind limbs**.  
It forms the framework of the hip region and supports the attachment of the femurs.  

###Shape###  
A **large, basin-like structure** composed of three bones — the ilium, ischium, and pubis — fused together to form a strong, stable girdle.  

###Parts###  

##Ilium##  
The broad, upper part that articulates with the sacrum.  
Energetically, symbolizes **strength and direction**.  

##Ischium##  
The posterior portion supporting body weight during sitting.  
Energetically, represents **stability and endurance**.  

##Pubis##  
The ventral and anterior section forming the pelvic floor.  
Energetically, reflects **connection, flow, and grounding**.  

###Function###  
Supports internal organs, connects hind limbs, and provides leverage for locomotion.  
Energetically, maintains **balance, security, and power of movement**.

**Emotional Meaning:** Security and foundation.  
**Balanced Energy:** Grounded presence and trust in stability.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Pelvic tilt, joint stiffness, or trauma.  
Energetically, **loss of stability, insecurity, or blocked flow**.
