#Sesamoids#

##Location##  
Small bones embedded within tendons near the **joints of the digits and paws**.  
They are located around the metatarsophalangeal and interphalangeal joints.  

###Shape###  
**Tiny, rounded bones** that vary in number, reducing friction and aiding tendon glide.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal Sesamoids##  
Found near the base of the digits, guiding tendon movement.  
Energetically, symbolize **support and stability**.  

##Distal Sesamoids##  
Located near the distal phalanges, assisting smooth claw motion.  
Energetically, reflect **flow and adaptability**.  

###Function###  
Reduce tendon stress, enhance leverage, and stabilize motion.  
Energetically, sustain **fluidity and balanced coordination**.
