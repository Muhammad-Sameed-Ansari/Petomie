#Metatarsals#

##Location##  
Located in the **hind paw**, between the **tarsal bones** and the **phalanges**.  
These long bones form the supportive framework of the paw, enabling strength and propulsion.  

###Shape###  
A group of **five elongated bones (I–V)**, arranged parallel, with the third and fourth being the most robust.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal Base##  
Articulates with the distal tarsals to stabilize the hind paw.  
Energetically, symbolizes **foundation and balance**.  

##Shaft##  
Supports the paw and transfers energy during stride.  
Energetically, represents **movement and persistence**.  

##Distal Head##  
Connects to the phalanges, forming flexible joints for motion.  
Energetically, reflects **adaptation and forward progress**.  

###Function###  
Provide strength, leverage, and balance during movement and jumping.  
Energetically, sustain **momentum and stable progression**.
