#Premolar 4#

##Location##
Located behind the third premolar on the lower left jaw, working with the upper carnassial tooth.  
A powerful slicing and crushing tooth.

###Shape###
Large, multi-pointed tooth designed for strong cutting.

###Function###
Slices and crushes dense food.  
Energetically represents power, transformation, and grounding.
