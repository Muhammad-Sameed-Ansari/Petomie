#Sphenoid Bone#

##Location##  
Located at the **base of the skull**, between the frontal and temporal bones, forming part of the cranial floor.  

###Shape###  
A **butterfly-shaped bone** with a central body and wing-like extensions.  

###Parts###  

##Body##  
Houses the sphenoid sinus.  
Energetically, represents **core balance and central strength**.  

##Greater and Lesser Wings##  
Extend outward to form the skull’s base.  
Energetically, symbolize **balance between expansion and focus**.  

##Pterygoid Processes##  
Serve as attachment points for jaw muscles.  
Energetically, reflect **action and power**.  

###Function###  
Supports the brain, stabilizes cranial structure, and anchors chewing muscles.  
Energetically, balances **willpower and clarity of thought**.
