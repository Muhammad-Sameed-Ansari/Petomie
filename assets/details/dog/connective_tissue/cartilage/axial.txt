#Axial#

##Location##
In the head, neck, thorax, vertebral column.

##Shape##
Smooth, resilient tissue (mostly hyaline, fibrocartilage, elastic).

##Parts##
Nasal Cartilages – Shape nostrils and support airway.
Laryngeal Cartilages (thyroid, cricoid, arytenoid, epiglottis) – Airway protection, phonation.
Tracheal Cartilage Rings – Maintain airway.
Costal Cartilage – Connect ribs to sternum, expand thorax.
Intervertebral Disc Cartilage – Fibrocartilage for shock absorption.
