#Appendicular#

##Location##
Joint surfaces of forelimbs and hindlimbs.

##Shape##
Hyaline cartilage at articular surfaces; fibrocartilage pads at stifle.

##Parts##
Articular Cartilage of Shoulder, Elbow, Carpus.
Articular Cartilage of Hip, Stifle, Hock.
Menisci of Stifle (fibrocartilage).

##Function##
Reduce friction, absorb impact, allow smooth joint movement.
