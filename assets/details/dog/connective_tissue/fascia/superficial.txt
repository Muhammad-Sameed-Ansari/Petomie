
##Location##
Under the skin, continuous throughout the canine body.

##Shape##
Loose, elastic connective tissue with fat, vessels, nerves.

##Parts##
Cervical Superficial Fascia – Covers neck, connects skin to underlying muscles.
Thoracic and Abdominal Superficial Fascia – Includes panniculus carnosus (twitch muscle).
Limb Superficial Fascia – Continuous with trunk fascia.

##Function##
Allows skin mobility over muscles.
Provides insulation and energy storage.
Protects superficial vessels and nerves.
Distributes tension during movement.
