#Fascia – Deep (Myofascia)#

##Location##
Surrounds and penetrates muscles, tendons, ligaments, and organs.

##Shape##
Dense, fibrous sheets that compartmentalize and connect deeper structures.

##Parts##
Thoracolumbar Fascia – Key aponeurotic fascia along spine.
Deep Cervical Fascia – Encloses trachea, esophagus, carotid sheath.
Deep Limb Fascia – Forms sleeves around muscle groups.
Visceral Fascia – Peritoneum, mesenteries, pleura, pericardium.

##Function##
Transmits muscular forces across regions.
Stabilizes posture and distributes mechanical stress.
Provides organ support and pathways for vessels/nerves.
