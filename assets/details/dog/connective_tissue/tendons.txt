#Tendons#

In dogs, tendons are dense connective tissues that connect muscles to bones.  
They transfer muscular energy into motion.  
Energetically, they represent **strength, resilience, and direction**.

###Parts###

##Flexor Tendons##  
Control paw flexion and grip.  
Energetically, they symbolize **focus and control**.

##Extensor Tendons##  
Enable limb extension and forward reach.  
Energetically, they reflect **action and progress**.

##Elastic Tendons##  
Aid in jumping and quick movement.  
Energetically, they embody **agility and enthusiasm**.

###Function###  
Tendons facilitate motion, coordination, and strength transmission.  
Energetically, they uphold **determination, flow, and purpose**.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Tightness: stress or control issues.  
- Inflammation: frustration or overuse.  
- Weakness: lack of motivation or drive.
