#Blood#

In dogs, blood is a fluid connective tissue that circulates through vessels, carrying oxygen, nutrients, and immune cells.  
It supports life, vitality, and internal communication.  
Energetically, it represents **flow, vitality, and emotional expression**.

###Parts###

##Red Blood Cells##  
Deliver oxygen and remove carbon dioxide.  
Energetically, they symbolize **life force and purpose**.

##White Blood Cells##  
Protect against disease and support immune balance.  
Energetically, they represent **protection and cleansing**.

##Platelets##  
Assist in clotting and healing of wounds.  
Energetically, they embody **restoration and resilience**.

##Plasma##  
Transports nutrients, hormones, and waste.  
Energetically, it reflects **fluidity and emotional adaptability**.

###Function###  
Blood distributes nourishment, removes waste, and regulates temperature.  
Energetically, it sustains **circulation of vitality and emotional flow**.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Anemia: fatigue, sadness, or depletion.  
- Stagnant blood: emotional blockages.  
- Weak immunity: lack of resilience or energy.
