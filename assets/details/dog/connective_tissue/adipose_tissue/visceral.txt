#Visceral#

##Location##
Surrounding and cushioning internal organs in thoracic and abdominal cavities.

##Shape##
Loose fat layers encasing organs, vessels, and mesenteries.

##Parts##
Perirenal Fat – Surrounds kidneys, cushions against trauma.
Omental Fat – Within greater omentum, draped over abdominal organs.
Mediastinal/Pericardial Fat – Around heart and great vessels.
Mesenteric Fat – Associated with intestines, vessels, and lymph nodes.

##Function##
Protects organs against mechanical trauma.
Provides thermal insulation in body cavities.
Serves as long-term energy storage.
Acts as an endocrine organ (adipokines, hormone regulation).
