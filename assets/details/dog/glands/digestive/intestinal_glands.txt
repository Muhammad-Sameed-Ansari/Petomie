#Intestinal Glands#

The dog’s intestinal glands, or crypts of Lieberkühn, line the small and large intestines.  
They secrete mucus, enzymes, and renewal cells to maintain digestion and protection.  
Energetically, they represent flow, renewal, and integration of life’s nourishment.

###Parts###

##Crypts of Lieberkühn##  
Tiny pits producing digestive enzymes and new epithelial cells.  
Energetically, they symbolize regeneration and openness to new experiences.

##Goblet Cells##  
Specialized cells that secrete mucus to protect the intestine.  
Energetically, they represent emotional comfort and softness.

##Enteroendocrine Cells##  
Release hormones that regulate intestinal activity.  
Energetically, they reflect balance and harmony in communication.

###Function###  
Intestinal glands renew the intestinal lining, secrete mucus, and regulate digestion.  
They maintain smooth absorption and defense.  
Energetically, they promote adaptability, balance, and continuous renewal.

###Common Imbalances###  
Irritation: stress or emotional sensitivity.  
Diarrhea: over-release or restlessness.  
Constipation: tension or resistance.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect difficulty integrating or letting go.
