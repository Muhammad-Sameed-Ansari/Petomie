#Perianal (Circumanal) Glands#

##Location##
The perianal glands are located in the skin surrounding the anus, forming a circular group beneath the surface under the tail.
They lie within the outer skin layer, not deep inside the body.

###Shape###
Small lobed glands resembling sebaceous (oil) glands, arranged in clusters around the anal area.

##Function##
Produce scented secretions used for territorial marking and identification.
Contribute to each dog’s unique scent signature, recognized by other dogs.
Help maintain skin health and moisture around the anal opening.
