#Anal Sacs (Anal Glands)#

##Location##
Anal sacs are found just beneath the skin on either side of the anus, typically at the 4 o’clock and 8 o’clock positions.
Each sac has a small duct that opens into the anus.

###Shape###
Two small oval sacs, each about the size of a pea to a grape depending on the dog’s size, lined with glands that produce a strong-smelling fluid.

##Function##
Store and release a thick, oily secretion with a powerful scent used for territorial marking and communication.
Express naturally during defecation or emotional excitement.
Help signal identity and emotional state to other dogs.
