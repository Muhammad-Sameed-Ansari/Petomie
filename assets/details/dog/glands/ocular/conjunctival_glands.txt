#Conjunctival Glands#

##Location##
These are found in the lining of the eyelids and the surface of the eye, within the thin pink membrane called the conjunctiva.

###Shape###
Microscopic clusters of mucous glands scattered beneath the eyelid lining.

##Function##
Produce mucus that keeps the eye’s surface smooth and moist.
Help remove dust, dirt, and small particles.
Support eye comfort and protection.
