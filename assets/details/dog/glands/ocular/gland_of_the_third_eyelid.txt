#Third Eyelid Gland#

The dog’s third eyelid gland, also called the nictitating membrane gland, is found at the inner corner of the eye.  
It produces a large portion of the tear film, protecting and nourishing the eye.  
Energetically, it represents intuition, inner vision, and unseen protection.

###Parts###

##Glandular Body##  
Located at the base of the third eyelid, producing watery tears.  
Energetically, it symbolizes inner awareness and emotional strength.

##Third Eyelid Membrane##  
A movable fold that covers and shields the eye.  
Energetically, it reflects self-protection and instinctive wisdom.

##Duct Openings##  
Tiny channels through which tears reach the eye surface.  
Energetically, they represent flow, release, and trust in intuition.

###Function###  
The third eyelid gland contributes up to half of the tear volume, keeping the eye moist and healthy.  
It defends the eye from dust, infection, and dryness.  
Energetically, it supports protection, inner clarity, and trust in one’s instincts.

###Common Imbalances###  
Prolapse (cherry eye): emotional vulnerability or exposure.  
Inflammation: irritation or strain from suppressed emotion.  
Swelling: pressure or imbalance in protection energy.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect exhaustion, fear of exposure, or loss of intuitive balance.
