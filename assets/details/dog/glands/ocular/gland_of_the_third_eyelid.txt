#Gland of the Third Eyelid#

##Location##
Found at the base of the third eyelid in the inner corner of each eye, near the nose.
It lies under a pink fold that slides across the eye for protection.

###Shape###
A small, rounded gland surrounded by connective tissue.

##Function##
Produces tears and protective fluids to keep the eye moist.
Supports the immune defense of the eye.
Helps prevent irritation from dust, wind, or debris.
