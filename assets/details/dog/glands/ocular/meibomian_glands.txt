#Meibomian (Tarsal) Glands#

##Location##
These glands line the edges of the upper and lower eyelids, opening at the edge where eyelashes grow.

###Shape###
Numerous tiny, tube-shaped glands arranged in straight rows.

##Function##
Produce oily secretions that prevent tears from drying out.
Keep eyelids smooth and protect the eye’s moisture layer.
Support comfort and clear vision.
