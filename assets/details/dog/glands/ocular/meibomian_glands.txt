#Meibomian Sebaceous Glands#

The dog’s Meibomian sebaceous glands are oil-secreting glands at the edge of the eyelids.  
They work alongside the tarsal glands to form the tear film’s protective lipid layer.  
Energetically, they represent clarity, focus, and the protection of perception.

###Parts###

##Sebaceous Lobules##  
Clusters producing oily sebum.  
Energetically, they symbolize nourishment and smooth vision.

##Ducts##  
Open at the eyelid margin to coat the tear surface.  
Energetically, they reflect the flow of awareness and insight.

##Eyelid Margin##  
Supports gland openings and lash protection.  
Energetically, it represents healthy boundaries and perspective.

###Function###  
Meibomian sebaceous glands prevent evaporation of tears and keep the eye’s surface smooth.  
Energetically, they protect emotional clarity and balanced perception.

###Common Imbalances###  
Blockage: styes or swelling at eyelid edges.  
Dryness: lack of oil or emotional strain.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect blurred insight or suppressed vision.
