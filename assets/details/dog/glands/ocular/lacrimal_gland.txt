#Lacrimal Glands#

##Location##
The lacrimal glands sit above and to the outer side of each eye, tucked beneath the upper eyelid near the outer corner.

###Shape###
Two oval glands, pale and soft with small lobes.

##Function##
Produce tears that keep the eyes clean and moist.
Protect against dust, dryness, and infection.
Nourish and protect the cornea for clear vision.
