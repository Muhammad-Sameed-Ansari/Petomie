#Perianal Glands#

The dog’s perianal glands, also called circumanal glands, surround the anus within the skin.  
They produce oils that protect the skin and carry individual scent markers.  
Energetically, they represent identity, confidence, and grounded presence.

###Parts###

##Sebaceous Lobules##  
Produce protective oils around the anus.  
Energetically, they symbolize stability and individuality.

##Apocrine Units##  
Add pheromone elements for scent marking.  
Energetically, they represent communication and self-assurance.

##Skin Surround##  
Supports secretion flow and protection.  
Energetically, it reflects comfort with self and territory.

###Function###  
Perianal glands protect the skin and contribute to the dog’s personal scent signature.  
Energetically, they support emotional security and confidence in self-expression.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling: blocked emotion or overstimulation.  
Infection: irritation or poor hygiene.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect self-doubt or defensiveness.
