#Mandibular Glands#

##Location##
Found beneath the lower jaw (mandible), close to the throat area, on both sides of the neck.
They drain saliva into the floor of the mouth through small ducts behind the lower incisors.

###Shape###
Two oval glands, smooth and soft in texture, surrounded by connective tissue.

##Function##
Produce mixed saliva containing both water and mucus.
Keep the mouth moist and lubricated.
Aid in swallowing, speaking, and maintaining oral comfort.
