#Parotid Glands#

##Location##
The parotid glands are located just below and in front of each ear, between the upper jaw and the neck.
They connect to the mouth through the parotid duct, which opens near the upper cheek teeth.

###Shape###
Two large, lobed glands with a yellowish tone and firm texture.

##Function##
Produce watery saliva that helps moisten food and begin digestion.
Contain enzymes that start breaking down starch.
Support chewing, swallowing, and oral hygiene.
