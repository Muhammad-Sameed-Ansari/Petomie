#Zygomatic Glands#

##Location##
The zygomatic glands lie beneath the eyes, within the cheek area, near the upper molars.
They drain into the mouth through small ducts that open near the last upper teeth.

###Shape###
Two oval glands with a compact structure, located deep in the cheek tissues.

##Function##
Produce mucous saliva to lubricate and moisten the mouth and cheeks.
Protect oral tissues and assist with chewing and swallowing.
Support tear drainage through nearby connections.
