#Sublingual Glands#

##Location##
The sublingual glands are located beneath the tongue, along the floor of the mouth.
Their ducts open directly into the mouth under the tongue.

###Shape###
Two elongated, soft glands with small lobes, embedded within connective tissue.

##Function##
Produce mucus-rich saliva that lubricates the tongue and mouth.
Facilitate smooth swallowing and speech.
Protect the oral lining and prevent dryness or irritation.
