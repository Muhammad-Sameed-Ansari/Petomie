#Sweat (Apocrine) Glands#

##Location##
Apocrine glands are found throughout the skin, close to hair follicles, especially in the neck, back, and paws.

###Shape###
Coiled tubular glands that release sweat into the hair follicles.

##Function##
Produce a thick, milky sweat that helps with scent communication and temperature regulation.
Release pheromones important for social interaction and identification.
Aid in maintaining skin moisture and resilience.
