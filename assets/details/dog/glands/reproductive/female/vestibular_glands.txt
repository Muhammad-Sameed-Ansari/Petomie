#Vestibular Glands (Bartholin’s Glands)#

They secrete mucus during mating to provide lubrication, comfort, and protection.  
Energetically, they represent feminine flow, receptivity, and ease in intimate connection.

##Mucous Cells##

Energetically, they symbolize fluidity, softness, and emotional openness.

##Gland Ducts##  
Small openings releasing mucus into the vestibule.  
Energetically, they reflect expression, movement, and readiness.

##Supportive Tissue##  
Anchors and cushions the glands within the vaginal walls.  
Energetically, it represents grounding, stability, and nurturing support.

###Function###  
Vestibular glands lubricate the vaginal vestibule, reduce friction, and support reproductive comfort.  
Energetically, they enhance receptivity, feminine expression, and balanced connection.

###Common Imbalances###

Energetically, imbalance may reflect guardedness or discomfort with vulnerability.
