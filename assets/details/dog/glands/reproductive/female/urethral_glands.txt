#Urethral Glands (Littré’s Glands)#

The dog’s urethral glands are small mucus-secreting glands lining the urethra.  
They keep the urinary passage moist and protected.  
Energetically, they represent purification, release, and flow.

###Parts###

##Mucous Cells##  
Produce protective mucus along the urethra.  
Energetically, they symbolize gentle release and comfort.

##Ducts##  
Small openings into the urethral lumen.  
Energetically, they represent freedom and balance in flow.

##Surrounding Tissue##  
Supports gland activity and elasticity.  
Energetically, it reflects support and adaptability.

###Function###  
Urethral glands lubricate and protect the urinary tract from friction and infection.  
Energetically, they help cleanse and maintain harmony in physical and emotional release.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: irritation or suppressed expression.  
Dryness: tension or difficulty letting go.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect resistance to release or emotional stagnation.
