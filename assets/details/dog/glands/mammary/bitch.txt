##Thoracic Glands##

##Abdominal Glands##  
Situated along the midline of the body, often the most functional during nursing.

##Inguinal Glands##  
Found near the groin, typically the largest pair.

###Function###  
Mammary glands produce and secrete milk after birth under the influence of hormones such as prolactin and oxytocin.  
They nourish newborn puppies and strengthen the maternal bond.
