Located in the throat region, extending through the neck, jaw, and upper airway.

###Color###

###Element###
Ether / Sound

###Signs of Balance###
Clear, appropriate vocalization
Healthy communication with humans and other animals

Calm responsiveness and expressive behavior

###Signs of Imbalance###

Excessive barking or complete silence
Difficulty expressing needs or emotions
Frustration, whining, or confusion
Mixed signals or unclear communication

##Physical##
Throat irritation or coughing
Jaw, neck, or upper airway tension
Respiratory strain
Difficulty swallowing

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle neck massage and stretching
Calm training that encourages clear communication
Allowing healthy, natural vocal expression
Speaking gently and reassuringly to the dog
