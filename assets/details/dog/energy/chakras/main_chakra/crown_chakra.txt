#Crown Chakra (Sahasrara)#

###Location###
Located at the top of the head, extending upward into the energetic field around the skull.

###Color###
Violet / White

###Element###
Spirit / Universal Energy

###Meaning & Function###
The Crown Chakra governs spiritual connection, higher awareness, and a sense of universal safety.
It helps dogs feel guided, calm, and connected to their environment and caregivers.
This chakra supports trust, inner peace, and the ability to feel supported by life itself.

##Connection##
Top of the head, skull, and upper nervous system
Energetic connection to higher guidance
Calmness, spiritual awareness, and intuitive understanding
Deep emotional trust and serenity

###Signs of Balance###
Calm, peaceful presence
Confidence in new situations
Strong intuitive awareness and gentle responsiveness
Relaxed, trusting behavior

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Disconnection or confusion
Anxiety without clear cause
Difficulty trusting or settling
Over-sensitivity to subtle energies

##Physical##
Head sensitivity or tension
Neurological imbalance
Sleep disruptions
Fatigue or restlessness

###Balancing Activities###
Quiet environments and gentle reassurance
Peaceful bonding time
Soft touch on or near the top of the head
Meditative calmness from the caregiver
Violet crystals nearby (amethyst, lepidolite)
