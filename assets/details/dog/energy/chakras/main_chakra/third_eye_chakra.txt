#Third Eye Chakra (Ajna)#

###Location###
Located on the forehead between the eyes, extending into the brow and upper skull.

###Color###
Indigo

###Element###
Light

###Meaning & Function###
The Third Eye Chakra governs intuition, perception, awareness, and mental clarity.
It supports a dog’s ability to read energy, understand intentions, and respond with insight.
This chakra enhances focus, emotional intelligence, and inner guidance.

##Connection##
Forehead, brow, upper skull
Pineal and pituitary energetic centers
Intuition, awareness, and perception
Mental focus and emotional processing

###Signs of Balance###
Calm attentiveness and awareness
Strong intuition and responsiveness to subtle cues
Balanced focus and mental clarity
Confidence in navigating new environments

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Confusion or difficulty focusing
Overreactivity or misreading situations
Appearing “spaced out” or disconnected
Anxiety when processing new stimuli

##Physical##
Eye strain or brow tension
Head sensitivity
Sleep cycle irregularities
Overstimulation or fatigue

###Balancing Activities###
Quiet environments for mental clarity
Mindful training that encourages focus
Gentle touch on forehead or between eyes
Indigo crystals placed nearby (amethyst, lapis lazuli)
