#Brahmarandra Chakra#

###Location###
Located at the highest point of the skull, slightly behind the crown chakra.

###Color###
White / Violet / Pale Gold

###Element###
Light / Ether

###Meaning & Function###
The Brahmarandra Chakra is the sacred spiritual opening to divine energy.
It governs spiritual awareness, transcendence, intuitive purity, and sensitivity to subtle energies.

##Connection##
Crown and upper intuitive centers
Nervous system
Divine energetic channels

###Signs of Balance###
Deep calm and serenity
Heightened intuitive awareness
Strong spiritual connection with caretaker
Soft, peaceful expression

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Energetic overwhelm
Sensitivity to subtle vibrations
Withdrawn or reactive behavior

##Physical##
Head tension
Neck or poll discomfort
Sleep disturbances

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle head strokes
Calm, quiet bonding time
Light visualization above the head
Serene resting environments
