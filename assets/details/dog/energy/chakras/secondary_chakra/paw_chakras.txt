#Paw Chakras (4)#

##Location##
Center of each paw pad

##Color##
Dark Brown or Black

##Function##
Provide grounding and environmental sensing
Help dogs pick up vibrational cues from surfaces and earth energy

##Imbalances##
Reluctance to walk on certain surfaces
Nervous paw licking, chewing, or sore paws
Hyperactivity, restlessness

##Balancing##
Walks on natural terrain (grass, dirt, sand)
Gentle paw massage or acupressure
Regular paw care (trimming nails, moisturizing pads)
