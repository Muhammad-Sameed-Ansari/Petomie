#Whorl Chakra#

###Location###
Located at the forehead whorl or the energetic center of the brow area.

###Color###
Indigo / White

###Element###
Light

###Meaning & Function###
The whorl chakra blends Third Eye and intuitive centers, supporting insight, emotional intelligence, and deep connection.
It helps dogs read energy, emotions, and intentions clearly.

##Connection##
Forehead, brow, upper skull
Intuitive and emotional centers

###Signs of Balance###
Calm, wise expression
Strong intuitive connection
Clear emotional communication

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Misreading social cues
Difficulty focusing
Over-sensitivity

##Physical##
Forehead tension
Eye strain
Poll or brow tightness

###Balancing Activities###
Forehead massage or gentle strokes
Quiet bonding time
Soft lighting and calm spaces
Light-based energy work
