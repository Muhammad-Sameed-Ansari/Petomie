#Brachial Chakra#

###Location###
Located in front of the shoulder, where the neck meets the body.
Extends into the shoulder joint, upper forelimb muscles, and surrounding energetic field.

###Color###
Turquoise / Aquamarine

###Element###
Ether / Air

###Meaning & Function###
The Brachial Chakra is the primary human–animal connection chakra in dogs.
It governs bonding, emotional partnership, communication, and relational trust.
Through this chakra, dogs interpret intention, sense emotional states, and form deep attachments.
It also influences willingness, responsiveness, and the emotional quality of the human–dog bond.

##Connection##
Shoulder, scapula, upper forelimb
Front limb meridians
Bonding, emotional connection, and relational awareness
Trust, communication, and energetic attunement

###Signs of Balance###
Strong, healthy bonding with caretaker
Willingness to engage and follow
Soft, receptive body language
Calm responsiveness and emotional clarity
Relaxed shoulders and forelimbs

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Difficulty bonding or trusting
Avoidance of touch near shoulders
Clinginess or insecurity
Overreactivity to human emotion
Lack of engagement or withdrawal

##Physical##
Shoulder stiffness or restricted movement
Tension in upper forelimbs
Sensitivity in neck–shoulder junction
Uneven gait or reluctance to step forward

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle shoulder massage and slow range-of-motion exercises
Calm bonding time with heart-to-shoulder contact
Soft verbal communication and intention setting
Energy work or gentle hands-on presence near the shoulder
Turquoise crystals placed near resting area
Trust-building exercises and predictable routines
