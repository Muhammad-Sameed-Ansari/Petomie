#Spleen Chakra#

###Location###
Located on the left side behind the ribs over the spleen region.

###Color###
Yellow / Gold

###Element###
Earth / Fire

###Meaning & Function###
The spleen chakra governs vitality, immunity, emotional nourishment, and recovery from stress.
It supports physical strength, resilience, and emotional stability.

##Connection##
Spleen, lymphatic system, immune function
Blood vitality and emotional energy
Stress recovery

###Signs of Balance###
Strong vitality
Healthy immune response
Calm emotional energy
Stable mood and stamina

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Fatigue or low motivation
Emotional fragility
Stress sensitivity

##Physical##
Low immunity
Lymphatic congestion
Slow recovery

###Balancing Activities###
Warm compresses along ribs
Gentle massage
Stress-free environment
Yellow or gold crystals nearby
