#Nose / Muzzle Chakra#

###Location###
Located at the nose, muzzle, and whisker region extending into sinuses.

###Color###
Light Blue / Silver

###Element###
Air

###Meaning & Function###
This chakra governs scent perception, sensory awareness, curiosity, and emotional communication.
It helps dogs interpret the world, connect socially, and process information through smell.

##Connection##
Nostrils, muzzle, whiskers
Respiratory system and sinuses
Sensory interpretation and emotional reading

###Signs of Balance###
Curiosity and healthy sniffing
Comfort with muzzle touch
Soft, relaxed nose and lips
Balanced sensory processing

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Avoidance of muzzle touch
Hyper-vigilance or fear reactions
Loss of curiosity or interest

##Physical##
Nasal irritation or sensitivity
Sinus congestion
Tension around lips or whiskers

###Balancing Activities###
Soft muzzle massage
Scent-based enrichment
Gentle breathing relaxation
Calm bonding through touch
