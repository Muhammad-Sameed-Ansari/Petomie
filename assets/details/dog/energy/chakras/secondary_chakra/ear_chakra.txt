#Ear Chakras (2)#

##Location##
Just behind the ears

##Color##
Lavender or Light Violet

##Function##
Tune into sound frequencies and energetic cues
Help dogs respond appropriately to human tone and environment

##Imbalances##
Noise sensitivity, fear of thunderstorms/fireworks
Ear infections or frequent head shaking
Over-alertness or hypervigilance

##Balancing##
Calm sound therapy, desensitization to noise
Massage at base of ears and along head
Gentle, reassuring voice during stressful times
