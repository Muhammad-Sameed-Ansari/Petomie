#Ear Chakra#

###Location###
Located in and around each ear, extending outward into auditory and energetic fields.

###Color###
Light Blue / Lavender

###Element###
Air / Ether

###Meaning & Function###
The ear chakras govern auditory perception, intuition, awareness, and emotional sensitivity.
They help dogs interpret sound, intention, and subtle environmental cues.

##Connection##
Inner and outer ears
Vestibular system
Intuition and alertness

###Signs of Balance###
Calm, attentive ear movement
Appropriate sound responsiveness
Relaxed expression and awareness

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Overreacting to noise
Anxiety or confusion
Difficulty focusing

##Physical##
Ear irritation or sensitivity
Vestibular imbalance
Head shaking or stiffness

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle ear stroking
Quiet, predictable environments
Soft vocal reassurance
Energetic work around ears
