#Flank Chakras#

###Location###
Located on each side of the lower ribcage and abdomen.

###Color###
Orange / Gold

###Element###
Fire / Water

###Meaning & Function###
The flank chakras support emotional processing, sensitivity, body awareness, and movement flexibility.
They help dogs regulate emotional tension stored in the sides of the body.

##Connection##
Lateral abdomen, ribs, muscles
Emotional centers (solar plexus and sacral chakras)
Movement fluidity

###Signs of Balance###
Relaxed response to touch
Soft lateral movement
Comfort during grooming
Smooth flexibility

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Flinching or guarding sides
Emotional tension or irritability
Avoidance of contact

##Physical##
Side stiffness
Ribcage tension
Lateral movement limitation

###Balancing Activities###
Soft brushing along flank
Slow stretching and bending
Belly lifts and core engagement
Energetic clearing along ribs
