#Liver Meridian (LV)#

##Location##
Begins at the medial side of the second digit of the hind paw (LV-1).
Ascends the medial leg in front of the hock, through groin, abdomen, and up along the ribs to the chest (LV-14).
Internally connects with liver, gall bladder, and diaphragm.

##Shape##
A deep Yin line regulating Blood and emotion.

##Function##
Stores Blood and ensures smooth Qi flow
Supports tendons, ligaments, and eyes
Regulates reproductive and detox functions
Encourages patience and inner harmony

##Signs of Imbalance##
Irritability, anger
Muscle stiffness, cramps
Eye disorders
Hormonal imbalance or digestive upset
