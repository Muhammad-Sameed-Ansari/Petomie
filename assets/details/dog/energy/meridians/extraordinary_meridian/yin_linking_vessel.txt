#Yin Linking Vessel (Yin Wei Mai)#

##Location##
Begins at Kidney 9 (KI-9) on the medial side of the hind limb.
Ascends the inner thigh into the abdomen, running deep through the belly and chest.
Continues up through the sternum to the throat region (near CV-22).
Internally, it links to the Heart, Pericardium, Liver, and Conception Vessel.

##Shape##
A deep inner channel connecting all Yin meridians of the body.
It integrates emotions, hormones, and the circulation of Blood and fluids.
In dogs, it supports calm focus, emotional balance, and healthy bonding with humans and pack members.

##Function##
Links all Yin meridians and harmonizes internal energy
Balances hormonal, reproductive, and emotional cycles
Nourishes the Heart and supports deep relaxation
Promotes resilience and stable temperament

##Signs of Imbalance##
Emotional exhaustion, sadness, or anxiety
Chest tension, irregular heartbeat, or shallow breathing
Hormonal irregularities or loss of appetite
Clinginess, over-sensitivity, or lack of calm focus
