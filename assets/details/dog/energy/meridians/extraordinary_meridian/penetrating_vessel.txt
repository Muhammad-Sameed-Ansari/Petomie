#Penetrating Vessel (Chong Mai)#

The **Extraordinary Meridians** are deeper energy channels described in **Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM)**.  
They act as reservoirs of **Qi and Blood**, regulating and balancing the flow within the Primary Meridians.  
In dogs, they form the energetic foundation that connects physical vitality with emotional strength, supporting endurance, reproduction, and stability.  
Each Extraordinary Vessel harmonizes Yin and Yang forces, maintaining internal balance and supporting the dog’s adaptability and vitality.  

The **Penetrating Vessel** governs the **core life force, circulation, and reproductive vitality**.  
It represents the deep source of energy that sustains life and links physical power with emotional depth.  
It embodies resilience, continuity, and the rhythm of existence.  

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins in the lower abdomen, travels upward through the chest and along the inner side of the body, branching through the abdomen and chest to nourish the organs.  
This deep vertical channel distributes Qi and Blood throughout the core of the body.  

###Function###  
Physically, it regulates blood flow, fertility, and organ nourishment.  
It supports the uterus, stomach, and heart, ensuring proper circulation and vitality.  
Energetically, it governs ancestral energy, endurance, and the connection between generations — sustaining deep physical and emotional reserves.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Reproductive weakness, circulatory problems, fatigue, or digestive stagnation.  
Energetic disharmony such as fear of depletion, lack of vitality, or difficulty grounding.  
The dog may appear tired, emotionally distant, or lacking in stamina.  

##Type##  
Single (Yin)  
Connected System: Circulatory and Reproductive Systems  
Element: Earth  
Time: 4PM - 6PM
