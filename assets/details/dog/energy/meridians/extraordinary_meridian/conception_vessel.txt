#Conception Vessel (Ren Mai)#

##Location##
Begins at the perineum (CV-1), runs along ventral midline through genital area, abdomen, chest, throat, ending at the chin (CV-24).
Internally links to bladder, stomach, and heart.

##Shape##
Ventral midline “Sea of Yin.”

##Function##
Nourishes all Yin meridians
Supports respiration, digestion, reproduction
Strengthens emotional nurturing and trust
Balances cycles and feminine energy

##Signs of Imbalance##
Urinary/reproductive weakness
Respiratory shallowness
Digestive sluggishness
Emotional depletion
