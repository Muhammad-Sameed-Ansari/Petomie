#Conception Vessel (Ren Mai)#

The **Extraordinary Meridians** are deeper energy channels described in **Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM)**.  
They act as reservoirs of **Qi and Blood**, regulating and balancing the flow within the Primary Meridians.  
In dogs, they form the energetic foundation that links body, mind, and spirit, influencing breathing, digestion, reproduction, and emotional stability.  
Each Extraordinary Vessel harmonizes Yin and Yang forces, maintaining internal balance and supporting the dogâ€™s adaptability and vitality.  

The **Conception Vessel** represents the **Yin aspect** of the body and runs along the midline of the underside.  
It connects and nourishes all Yin meridians, supporting internal strength, restoration, and emotional harmony.  
It embodies nurturing, receptivity, and the essence of self-care and trust.  

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins near the lower lip, travels down the underside of the neck, chest, and abdomen, and ends between the hind limbs near the perineum.  
This downward pathway supports calmness, connection, and the grounding of emotional and physical energy.  

###Function###  
Physically, it regulates the reproductive organs, breathing, and digestion.  
It influences hydration, hormone balance, and the flow of nourishment within the body.  
Energetically, it governs emotional security, compassion, and the ability to receive and restore.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Reproductive or hormonal issues, digestive upset, weakness in the abdomen, or shallow breathing.  
Energetic disharmony such as emotional detachment, hypersensitivity, or lack of nurturing energy.  
The dog may appear anxious, withdrawn, or disconnected from comfort and affection.  

##Type##  
Single (Yin)  
Connected System: Reproductive and Digestive Organs  
Element: Water  
Time: 2PM - 4PM
