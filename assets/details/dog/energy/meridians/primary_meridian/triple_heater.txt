#Triple Heater (San Jiao) Meridian#

##Location##
Starts on the lateral side of the fourth digit of the front paw (TH-1).
Ascends the lateral forelimb, over the carpus, elbow, and shoulder.
Travels up the side of the neck behind the ear, curves around it, and ends at the outer corner of the eye (TH-23).
Internally connects to the three “burners”: upper (respiratory), middle (digestive), and lower (reproductive/urinary).

##Shape##
A lateral pathway integrating all functional zones.

##Function##
Regulates temperature, moisture, and energy distribution
Supports lymphatic and endocrine systems
Coordinates organ communication
Balances adaptability and vitality

##Signs of Imbalance##
Swelling, fluid retention, ear issues
Forelimb/neck stiffness
Digestive sluggishness
Irritability, restlessness
