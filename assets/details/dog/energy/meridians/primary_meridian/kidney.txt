#Kidney Meridian (KI)#

##Location##
Begins on the plantar side of the hind paw between the pads of the third and fourth digits (KI-1).
Ascends along the medial side of the hock and tibia, through the groin, into the abdomen.
Internally connects with kidneys, bladder, and reproductive organs; externally continues along the ventral abdomen to the chest (KI-27).

##Shape##
A deep medial line of vitality from hind paw to chest.

##Function##
Stores Essence (Jing); supports growth and reproduction
Regulates water metabolism
Strengthens back, bones, and willpower
Anchors Qi and supports longevity

##Signs of Imbalance##
Weak hindquarters or back pain
Urinary or hormonal imbalance
Cold extremities, fatigue, hair loss
Fear, insecurity, exhaustion
