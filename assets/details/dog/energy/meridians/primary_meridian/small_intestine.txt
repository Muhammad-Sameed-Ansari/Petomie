#Small Intestine Meridian (SI)#

##Location##
Starts at the lateral side of the fifth digit of the front paw (SI-1).
Ascends the caudolateral side of the forelimb, crossing the carpus and elbow, over the shoulder and neck.
Curves around the base of the ear and terminates in front of the ear canal (SI-19).
Internally connects with the small intestine, heart, and stomach.

##Shape##
A superficial outer-forelimb path linking digestion and perception.

##Function##
Separates pure from impure in digestion
Assists absorption and assimilation
Balances heat, supports hearing and jaw
Promotes discernment and clarity

##Signs of Imbalance##
Abdominal discomfort, poor absorption
Stiff shoulder/neck
Ear infection or head shaking
Confusion or hypersensitivity
