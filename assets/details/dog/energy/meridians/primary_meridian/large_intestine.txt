#Large Intestine Meridian (LI)#

##Location##
Begins on the medial side of the second digit of the front paw (LI-1).
Ascends the craniolateral side of the forelimb, crossing the lateral carpus and elbow, and passes over the shoulder and side of the neck.
Curves around the lower cheek and ends beside the nostril (LI-20), connecting internally with the nose and large intestine.

##Shape##
A superficial, lateral line from the front paw to the face.
Works in partnership with the Lung Meridian to regulate exterior and interior balance.

##Function##
Eliminates waste and regulates bowel movement
Clears exterior heat and supports immune response
Benefits nose, mouth, and teeth
Assists in emotional release and “letting go”

##Signs of Imbalance##
Constipation, diarrhea, or irregular stool
Sinus or nasal congestion
Dental pain or gum inflammation
Skin eruptions or itching
Irritability or difficulty releasing attachments
