#Pericardium Meridian (PC)#

##Location##
Begins in the pericardial sac, emerges near the thoracic inlet (PC-1).
Runs down the medial forelimb between flexor muscles, across the carpus, ending at the tip of the middle digit (PC-9).

##Shape##
A protective line mirroring the Heart Meridian.

##Function##
Guards and regulates the Heart
Promotes healthy circulation and calm mind
Relieves chest tension and emotional overload
Supports balance between empathy and boundaries

##Signs of Imbalance##
Palpitations, anxiety
Chest tightness, shortness of breath
Forelimb stiffness
Emotional fatigue or over-sensitivity
