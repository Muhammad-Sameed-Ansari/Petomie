#Heart Meridian (HT)#

##Location##
Begins deep in the chest, connects with the heart and small intestine, then emerges in the axilla (HT-1).
Travels down the medial side of the forelimb in the bicipital groove, crosses the medial elbow and forearm.
Ends at the tip of the fifth digit on the front paw (HT-9).

##Shape##
A narrow inner-forelimb pathway aligned with circulation and emotion.

##Function##
Governs Blood and spirit (Shen)
Supports joy, vitality, and consciousness
Regulates heart rhythm and sleep
Encourages compassion and connection

##Signs of Imbalance##
Restlessness, insomnia, anxiety
Palpitations or irregular heartbeat
Tongue/mouth ulcers
Medial forelimb tremor or weakness
Emotional agitation or sadness
