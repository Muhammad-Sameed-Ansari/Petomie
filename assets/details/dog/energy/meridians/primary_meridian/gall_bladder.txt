#Gall Bladder Meridian (GB)#

##Location##
Begins at the outer canthus of the eye (GB-1), runs around the ear and lateral neck, over the shoulder and thoracic wall, along the flank and hip.
Continues down the lateral hind limb, over stifle and hock, ending at the fourth digit of the hind paw (GB-44).

##Shape##
A long, lateral meridian of motion and coordination.

##Function##
Stores and excretes bile; assists digestion
Supports decision-making, courage, and flexibility
Smooths flow of Liver Qi and releases tension
Promotes balance and coordination

##Signs of Imbalance##
Lateral body stiffness
Eye irritation or dizziness
Digestive issues with fats
Indecision, frustration
