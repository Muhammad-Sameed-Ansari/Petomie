#Spleen Meridian (SP)#

##Location##
Begins on the medial side of the hind paw at the caudal edge of the second digit (SP-1).
Ascends the medial hind limb in front of the hock and behind the tibia.
Continues up the inner thigh and through the groin into the abdomen, following near the ventral midline.
Passes the flank and ends on the lateral chest wall near the sixth intercostal space (SP-21).

##Shape##
A deep, nourishing meridian running from the hind paw to the chest.
Closely aligned with vessels and lymphatic routes.

##Function##
Transforms and transports nutrients and fluids
Regulates digestion and Blood production
Strengthens muscles and limbs
Balances worry and overthinking with stability

##Signs of Imbalance##
Loss of appetite, bloating, loose stool
Muscle weakness or fatigue
Fluid retention or edema
Anxiety, lethargy, or poor focus
