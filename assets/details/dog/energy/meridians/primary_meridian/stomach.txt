#Stomach Meridian (ST)#

##Location##
Begins below the eye (ST-1), descends along the face and jaw, through the throat and ventral neck.
Runs down the cranial chest and abdomen near the mammary line, then over the flank to the cranial thigh.
Travels along the cranial aspect of the hind limb, over the stifle and down the front of the tibia, ending at the dorsolateral second digit of the hind paw (ST-45).

##Shape##
A long, bilateral pathway traveling from the head to the hind paw.
Superficial through most of its length and associated with digestive organs internally.

##Function##
Transforms and transports food and fluids
Regulates digestion, appetite, and energy
Supports muscle tone and limb vitality
Balances emotions linked to nourishment and security

##Signs of Imbalance##
Poor appetite, vomiting, or bloating
Weakness in hind limbs
Excess salivation or dental sensitivity
Worry, anxiety, or overthinking
