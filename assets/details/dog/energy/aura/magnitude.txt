#Aura Magnitude#

###Location###
Surrounds the entire dog, radiating outward from the skin and coat in a warm, responsive energy field.
Because dogs are social, emotionally expressive, and deeply bonded to their pack (human or canine), their aura tends to expand widely during positive interaction and contract during stress, fear, or uncertainty.

###Shape###
Oval, flowing, and emotionally expressive.
The aura expands during excitement, play, affection, and curiosity, and contracts during fear, confusion, or overstimulation.

###Meaning & Function###
The aura’s magnitude reflects the dog’s physical vitality, emotional expression, and social openness.

A strong aura magnitude indicates:
High energy and vitality
Emotional balance
Social confidence
Strong connection to humans and environment

A contracted magnitude may indicate:
Fear or withdrawal
Stress or overstimulation
Illness or fatigue
Uncertainty or lack of grounding

###Signs of Disturbance###
Pacing or restlessness
Avoiding eye contact
Clinginess or hiding
Startle responses
Difficulty relaxing

###Balancing Activities###
Calm routine and structure
Grounding walks
Slow petting or calm touch
Energetic clearing in the environment
Predictable emotional communication
