#Mandible#

##Location##
Forms the lower jaw and articulates with the temporal bone at the temporomandibular joint (TMJ).

##Shape##
Consists of two halves (hemimandibles) joined at the mandibular symphysis; each half has a horizontal body and vertical ramus.

##Parts##
Body – Horizontal part housing lower teeth.
Ramus – Vertical ascending part forming jaw joint.
Condylar Process – Articulates with temporal bone at TMJ.
Coronoid Process – Dorsal projection providing attachment for temporalis muscle.
Mandibular Symphysis – Joint connecting left and right halves rostrally.
