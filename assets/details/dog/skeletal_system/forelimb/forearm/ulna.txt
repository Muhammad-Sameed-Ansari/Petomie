#Ulna#

##Location##
Lies parallel and lateral to the radius in the forearm. Extends from the elbow to the carpus.

##Shape##
Long, slender bone. Its proximal olecranon process forms the point of the elbow and serves as a major muscle attachment site.

##Function##
Provides lever arm for triceps brachii (elbow extension)
Completes elbow joint articulation with humerus
Supports radius and stabilizes forearm
