#Radius#

##Location##
Located in the forearm (antebrachium), medial to the ulna. It runs from the elbow to the carpus.

##Shape##
Long, slightly curved bone. Proximal end articulates with humerus (forming part of the elbow), distal end articulates with the carpal bones.

##Function##
Primary weight-bearing bone of the forearm
Transmits forces from humerus to carpus
Provides stable base for forelimb movement
