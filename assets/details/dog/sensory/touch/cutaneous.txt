#Cutaneous Hearing Structures#

##Location##
Located on either side of the head, the outer and middle ear include the pinna, ear canal, and eardrum.

###Shape###
Pinna: Large, movable flap that captures sound waves.
Ear Canal: Long, L-shaped passage protecting the eardrum.
Eardrum and Ossicles: Vibrate and transfer sound to the inner ear.

##Function##
Collects and amplifies sound.
Protects deeper ear parts from debris.
Transfers vibrations efficiently to the inner ear.
Supports breed-specific hearing range and directionality.
