###Parts###

##Eyelids##
Protect the eye from injury, debris, and excessive light while spreading tears evenly.
Energetically, symbolize boundaries, discernment, and emotional protection.

##Eyelashes##
Prevent dust, insects, and foreign particles from entering the eye.
Energetically, reflect alertness and subtle defense.

##Conjunctiva##
A thin mucous membrane lining the eyelids and covering the sclera.
Provides lubrication and immune defense.
Energetically, represents adaptability and gentle protection.

##Nictitating Membrane (Third Eyelid)##
Provides additional protection and tear distribution, especially during movement or stress.
Energetically, symbolizes instinctive protection and subconscious awareness.

##Lacrimal Glands##
Produce tears to lubricate, nourish, and cleanse the eye.
Energetically, represent emotional release, cleansing, and self-soothing.

##Nasolacrimal Duct (Tear Ducts)##
Drain excess tears from the eye into the nasal cavity.
Energetically, reflect emotional flow and release.

##Cornea##
The transparent outer layer that allows light to enter the eye and contributes to focus.
Energetically, embodies clarity, openness, and perception of truth.

##Sclera##
The white outer layer providing structure and protection to the eye.
Energetically, symbolizes stability, grounding, and resilience.

##Iris##
Controls pupil size and regulates the amount of light entering the eye.
Energetically, represents adaptation, balance, and responsiveness to surroundings.

##Lens##
Focuses light onto the retina for clear vision.
Energetically, reflects focus, intention, and perspective.

##Ciliary Body##
Adjusts lens shape for near and far vision and produces aqueous humor.
Energetically, symbolizes flexibility and adjustment to change.

##Extraocular Muscles##
Control eye movement and alignment.
Energetically, represent direction, attention, and coordinated action.

###Function###
Protect the eye, maintain moisture and cleanliness, regulate light entry, and support accurate visual focus and movement.
Energetically, these structures foster clear perception, emotional safety, adaptability, and trust in what is seen.

###Common Imbalances###
Dry eye, excessive tearing, conjunctivitis, corneal irritation, eyelid disorders, or eye strain.
Energetically, imbalance may reflect fear of seeing clearly, emotional overload, guardedness, or difficulty processing visual experiences.
