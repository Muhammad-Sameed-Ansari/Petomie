#Accessory Structures#

The dog’s accessory structures support the ear’s health and hearing ability.  
They include protective tissues and glands that maintain balance, cleanliness, and sound transmission.  
Energetically, they represent **support, protection, and the harmony of form and function**.

###Parts###

##Ear Cartilage##  
Gives shape and flexibility to the ear.  
Energetically, symbolizes **adaptability and structural balance**.

##Ear Muscles##  
Allow movement and positioning for sound direction.  
Energetically, reflect **focus, responsiveness, and alertness**.

##Ceruminous and Sebaceous Glands##  
Produce earwax to protect against debris and moisture.  
Energetically, represent **cleansing and gentle boundaries**.

##Hair and Skin Lining##  
Provide defense and maintain inner ear health.  
Energetically, embody **awareness and sensitivity to environment**.

###Function###  
Protect and support the ear canal, regulate cleanliness, and enhance auditory precision.  
Energetically, they foster **balance, attentiveness, and comfort within sensitivity**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Excess wax, irritation, or infection.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **blocked communication or overstimulation**.
