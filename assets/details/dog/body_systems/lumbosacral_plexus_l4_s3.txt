#Lumbosacral Plexus (L4–S3)#

##Parts##
- Sciatic Nerve – Hamstrings; branches to tibial/peroneal
- Femoral Nerve – Quadriceps; saphenous sensory
- Obturator Nerve – Adductor muscles
- Tibial Nerve – Caudal crus; plantar sensation
- Common Peroneal (Fibular) Nerve – Craniolateral crus; dorsal paw

##Function##
Motor and sensory innervation to the hindlimb.
