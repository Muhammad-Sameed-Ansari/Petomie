#Pancreas#

##Location##
Elongated organ situated in the abdomen, adjacent to the duodenum and stomach.

##Parts##
Exocrine Portion produces digestive enzymes (amylase, lipase, proteases) secreted into the duodenum.
Endocrine Portion (Islets of Langerhans) secretes hormones:
Alpha cells produce glucagon.
Beta cells produce insulin.
Delta cells produce somatostatin.

##Function##
Dual function as an exocrine gland for digestion and endocrine gland for glucose regulation and metabolic homeostasis.
