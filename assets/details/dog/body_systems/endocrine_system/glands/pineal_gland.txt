#Pineal Gland#

##Location##
Small, cone-shaped endocrine organ located in the epithalamus, between the cerebral hemispheres, attached to the roof of the third ventricle.

##Parts##
Pinealocytes secrete melatonin.
Supporting Glial Cells provide structural support.

##Function##
Regulates circadian rhythms, seasonal reproductive cycles, and influences sleep-wake patterns through melatonin secretion.
