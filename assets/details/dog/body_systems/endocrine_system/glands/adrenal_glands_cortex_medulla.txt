#Adrenal Glands#

##Location##
Paired glands located cranial to each kidney, enclosed in fat and connective tissue.

##Parts##
Cortex produces steroid hormones:
Zona glomerulosa secretes mineralocorticoids (aldosterone).
Zona fasciculata secretes glucocorticoids (cortisol).
Zona reticularis produces sex steroids (androgens).
Medulla secretes catecholamines (epinephrine and norepinephrine).

##Function##
Regulate stress response, metabolism, electrolyte balance, and fight-or-flight reactions.
