#Parathyroid Glands#

##Location##
Small glands embedded in or near the thyroid lobes, usually two pairs in dogs.

##Parts##
Chief Cells produce parathyroid hormone (PTH).
Oxyphil Cells present but less prominent in dogs.

##Function##
Maintain calcium and phosphate homeostasis by increasing blood calcium levels (stimulating bone resorption, kidney reabsorption, and vitamin D activation).
