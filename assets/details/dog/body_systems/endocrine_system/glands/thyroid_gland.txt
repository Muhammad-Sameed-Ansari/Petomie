#Thyroid Gland#

##Location##
Bilobed gland located in the ventral neck, lateral to the trachea just caudal to the larynx.

##Parts##
Follicles contain colloid and secrete thyroid hormones (T3 and T4).
Parafollicular Cells (C cells) produce calcitonin.

##Function##
Regulates metabolism, growth, development, and calcium balance.
