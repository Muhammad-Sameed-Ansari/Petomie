#Cerebrum (Frontal, Parietal, Temporal, Occipital Lobes)#

##Location##
Largest part of the brain, occupying the cranial cavity above the brainstem and cerebellum. Divided into left and right hemispheres.

##Parts##
Frontal Lobe – Governs voluntary movement, reasoning, planning, personality, and speech.
Parietal Lobe – Processes sensory input such as touch, pressure, temperature, and spatial orientation.
Temporal Lobe – Involved in hearing, memory, and language comprehension.
Occipital Lobe – Primary center for visual processing.

##Function##
Integrates higher brain functions including thought, decision-making, voluntary motor control, sensory perception, memory, language, and vision.
