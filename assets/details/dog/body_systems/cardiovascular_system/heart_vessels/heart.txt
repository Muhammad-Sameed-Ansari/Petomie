#Heart#

##Location##
Located in the thoracic cavity between the lungs within the mediastinum.

##Parts##
- Right Atrium – Receives systemic venous blood
- Right Ventricle – Pumps blood to lungs
- Left Atrium – Receives oxygenated pulmonary blood
- Left Ventricle – Pumps blood to body

##Function##
Pumps blood to maintain circulation and oxygen delivery to tissues.
