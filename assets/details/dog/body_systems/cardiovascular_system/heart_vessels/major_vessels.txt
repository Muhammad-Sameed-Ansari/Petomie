#Major Vessels#

##Location##
Connected to heart and distributed throughout body.

##Parts##
- Aorta – Main systemic artery
- Cranial & Caudal Vena Cava – Main systemic veins
- Carotid Arteries – Supply head/brain
- Jugular Veins – Drain head/neck
- Subclavian/Brachial Arteries – Supply forelimbs
- Femoral/External Iliac Arteries – Supply hindlimbs
- Renal Arteries/Veins – Supply kidneys
- Hepatic Portal Vein – Drains GI to liver

##Function##
Transport blood to and from body regions.
