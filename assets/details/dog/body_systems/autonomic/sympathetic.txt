#Sympathetic Nervous System#

##Location##

Chain of ganglia runs parallel to the vertebral column.

##Parts##
- Sympathetic Chain & Paravertebral Ganglia
- Cervicothoracic (stellate) ganglion
- Middle & Cranial Cervical ganglia
- Prevertebral ganglia (celiac, cranial/caudal mesenteric)

##Function##
""Fight or Flight"" response:
- Increases heart rate and blood pressure
- Dilates pupils and bronchi
- Redirects blood flow to muscles
- Inhibits digestion and bladder contraction
