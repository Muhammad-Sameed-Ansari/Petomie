#Parasympathetic Nervous System#

##Location##
Craniosacral outflow: Preganglionic cell bodies in brainstem (CN III, VII, IX, X) and sacral spinal cord (S1–S3).

##Parts##
- Vagus Nerve (CN X) – Thoracic & abdominal viscera
- Pelvic Nerves – Pelvic viscera, bladder, colon

##Function##
""Rest and Digest"" response:
- Slows heart rate
- Stimulates digestion and salivation
- Contracts bladder for urination
- Promotes energy storage and recovery
