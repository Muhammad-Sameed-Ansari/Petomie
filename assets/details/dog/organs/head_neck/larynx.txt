#Larynx#

##Location##
Located in the neck, between the pharynx and trachea.

##Parts##
Thyroid cartilage, cricoid cartilage, arytenoid cartilages, epiglottis.

##Function##
Airway protection during swallowing (epiglottis closes over glottis).
Phonation (sound production) via vocal folds.
Controls airflow to lower respiratory tract.
