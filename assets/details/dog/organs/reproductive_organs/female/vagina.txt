A **muscular, elastic tube** with mucous lining that can expand during mating or birth.  
Its walls are flexible and well-supplied with nerves and blood vessels.

##Function##
- Serves as the **copulatory canal** during mating.  
- Provides a **birth canal** during parturition.  
- Drains natural secretions from the uterus.  
- Protects internal organs by maintaining pH and immune defense.
