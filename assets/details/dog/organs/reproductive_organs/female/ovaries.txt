#Ovaries#

##Location##  
Located near the **kidneys**, within the abdominal cavity, attached by the **suspensory ligament** and supported by the broad ligament.  
One on each side of the midline.

###Shape###  
Small, oval, and smooth structures that vary in texture through the reproductive cycle.  
Contain follicles, ova, and hormone-producing tissues.

###Parts###  

##Cortex##  
Houses follicles and corpus luteum.  
Energetically, represents **creativity and renewal**.

##Medulla##  
Contains blood vessels and connective tissue.  
Energetically, symbolizes **nourishment and support**.

###Function###  
Produce ova (eggs) and secrete estrogen and progesterone.  
Regulate reproductive cycles and fertility.  
Energetically, govern **creative flow and feminine strength**.
