#Ovaries#

##Location##
The ovaries are located in the **abdominal cavity**, near the **kidneys**, on either side of the midline.  
They are supported by the **suspensory ligament** and connected to the **uterine tubes (oviducts)**.

###Shape###
Small, **oval-shaped organs** that vary slightly in size and texture depending on the dog's age and reproductive cycle.

##Function##
- Produce **eggs (ova)** for fertilization.  
- Secrete **estrogen and progesterone** to regulate the estrous cycle.  
- Maintain **reproductive health** and hormonal balance.  
- Support overall **vitality and energy**.
