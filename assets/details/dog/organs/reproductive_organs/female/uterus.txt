#Uterus#

##Location##
Located in the **pelvic cavity**, between the **urinary bladder** and **rectum**.  
It consists of **two long uterine horns**, a **body**, and a **cervix** leading into the vagina.

###Shape###
A **Y-shaped, muscular organ** with long horns to accommodate multiple puppies during pregnancy.  
It expands and contracts depending on reproductive stage.

##Function##
- Provides the **site for embryo implantation and development**.  
- Nourishes and protects developing fetuses.  
- Contracts during **birth** to deliver puppies.  
- Regulates hormones during the reproductive cycle.
