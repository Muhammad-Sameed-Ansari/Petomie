#Cervix#

##Location##  
The cervix lies between the **uterus** and the **vagina**, forming the passage that connects these two structures.  
It sits within the pelvic cavity and acts as the barrier between the external and internal reproductive tract.

###Shape###  
A **short, thick-walled tube** with firm muscle and connective tissue.  
Its opening (the cervical os) changes in size and texture during different reproductive stages.

###Parts###  

##External Os##  
Opens into the vagina.  
Energetically, symbolizes **boundaries and transitions**.

##Cervical Canal##  
Contains mucous glands that adjust secretions during heat or pregnancy.  
Energetically, represents **flow and adaptation**.

##Internal Os##  
Connects to the uterus.  
Energetically, reflects **communication and receptivity**.

###Function###  
Regulates the passage of sperm and prevents entry of pathogens into the uterus.  
Opens during estrus and parturition, closes during pregnancy.  
Energetically, governs **balance, trust, and rhythm**.
