#Cervix#

##Location##
Located between the **uterus** and **vagina**, forming the **lower part of the uterus**.  
It acts as a gateway regulating the passage between internal and external reproductive tracts.

###Shape###
A **short, firm, cylindrical structure** with a central canal.  
Its tone and texture vary with hormonal cycles and pregnancy.

##Function##
- Controls **entry of sperm** and **exit of fluids**.  
- Produces **mucus** that changes consistency throughout the cycle.  
- Dilates during **birth** to allow delivery.  
- Serves as a **protective barrier** against infections.
