#Uterine Tubes (Oviducts / Fallopian Tubes)#

##Location##
Extend from the **ovaries** to the **uterine horns**, lying within the **mesosalpinx** in the upper pelvic cavity.  
Each tube connects an ovary to its respective uterine horn.

###Shape###
Thin, **coiled tubes** lined with cilia that guide the eggs toward the uterus.  
They are delicate and flexible, designed to support fertilization and early embryo development.

##Function##
- Transport **eggs** from the ovary to the uterus.  
- Serve as the **site of fertilization**.  
- Provide nourishment for early embryos.  
- Regulate communication between ovaries and uterus.
