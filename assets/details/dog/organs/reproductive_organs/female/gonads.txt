#Gonads#

##Location##  
The ovaries are located in the **abdominal cavity**, positioned near the **kidneys**, and attached by the **suspensory ligament**.  
They connect to the uterus via the **oviducts (fallopian tubes)**.

###Shape###  
Small, oval organs with a smooth surface in young females, becoming textured with natural reproductive cycles.  
Contain follicles that mature into eggs.

###Parts###  

##Follicles##  
Eggs in different stages of development.  
Energetically, represent **potential, renewal, and creative cycles**.

##Corpus Luteum##  
Forms after ovulation, producing progesterone to support pregnancy.  
Energetically, symbolizes **nurturing, grounding, and emotional stability**.

##Tunica Albuginea##  
Outer connective layer protecting the ovary.  
Energetically, reflects **inner safety, resilience, and boundaries**.

###Function###  
Produce eggs and secrete hormones (estrogen and progesterone) that regulate heat cycles, reproductive readiness, and emotional rhythm.  
Energetically, maintain **creativity, intuition, and balanced flow**.
