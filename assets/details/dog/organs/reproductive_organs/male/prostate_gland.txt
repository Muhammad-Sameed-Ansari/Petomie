#Prostate Gland#  

##Location##  
Located in the **pelvic cavity**, just behind the **urinary bladder**, surrounding the **proximal urethra** (the first part of the urethra).  
In the dog, it sits on the midline and is closely associated with the urinary and reproductive tract.

###Shape###  
A **bilobed, rounded gland** (often described as heart-shaped in cross-section), with two lobes joined around the urethra.  
Size can vary with age and hormonal status (it may enlarge in intact males).

###Parts###  

##Right Lobe##  
One half of the gland that produces prostatic fluid.  
Energetically, reflects **protective structure and steadiness**.

##Left Lobe##

Energetically, represents **harmony and alignment in creation**.

##Prostatic Urethra Region##  
The urethral segment that passes through the prostate.  
Energetically, symbolizes **clear flow, expression, and release**.

###Function###  
Produces **prostatic fluid**, a key component of semen that supports sperm transport and viability.  
Helps **flush and protect** the urethra by contributing fluids during ejaculation.  
Energetically, governs **grounded masculine energy, healthy boundaries, and confident expression**.
