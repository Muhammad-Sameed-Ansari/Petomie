#Gonads#

##Location##  
The testes are housed in the **scrotum**, outside the abdominal cavity, connected by the **spermatic cords**.  
In dogs, they usually descend through the inguinal canal shortly after birth.

###Shape###  
Oval, firm organs with smooth surfaces, enclosed by a fibrous capsule.  
Contain microscopic tubules where sperm cells are formed.

###Parts###  

##Seminiferous Tubules##  
Produce sperm cells.  
Energetically, represent **vitality and creation**.

##Interstitial (Leydig) Cells##  
Produce testosterone, influencing fertility, muscle tone, and behaviour.  
Energetically, symbolize **drive and grounded strength**.

##Tunica Albuginea##  
Fibrous outer protective layer.  
Energetically, reflects **structure, security, and boundaries**.

###Function###  
Produce sperm and testosterone to regulate reproductive function and physical vitality.  
Energetically, maintain **strength, confidence, and creative force**.
