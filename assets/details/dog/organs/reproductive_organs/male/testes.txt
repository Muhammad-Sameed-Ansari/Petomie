#Testes#

##Location##  
The testes are housed in the **scrotum**, outside the abdominal cavity, connected by the **spermatic cords**.  
They descend through the inguinal canal shortly after birth.

###Shape###  
Oval, firm organs with smooth surfaces, enclosed by a fibrous capsule.  
Contain microscopic tubules where sperm are formed.

###Parts###  

##Seminiferous Tubules##  
Produce sperm cells.  
Energetically, represent **vitality and creation**.

##Interstitial (Leydig) Cells##  
Produce testosterone, controlling fertility and strength.  
Energetically, symbolize **drive and grounded energy**.

##Tunica Albuginea##  
Dense outer layer providing protection.  
Energetically, reflects **structure and security**.

###Function###  
Produce sperm and testosterone to regulate reproductive and physical vitality.  
Energetically, maintain **strength, confidence, and creative force**.
