#Epididymis#

##Location##
Attached to the **back (dorsal surface)** of each testis, extending along its length.  
It connects the testis to the **vas deferens**.

###Shape###
A **coiled, elongated tube** divided into three sections:  
- **Head (Caput):** Receives sperm from the testis.  
- **Body (Corpus):** Where sperm mature.  
- **Tail (Cauda):** Stores sperm until ejaculation.

##Function##
- Stores and **matures sperm cells**.  
- Transports sperm from the testis to the vas deferens.  
- Adds fluids and nutrients to enhance sperm vitality.  
- Regulates temperature for optimal sperm health.
