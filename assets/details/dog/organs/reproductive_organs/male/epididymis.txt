#Epididymis#

##Location##  
Located along the **posterior border of each testis**, within the scrotum.  
It connects the testis to the sperm duct (vas deferens).

###Shape###  
A **curved, coiled tube** forming three sections — head, body, and tail — compactly folded against the testis.

###Parts###  

##Head (Caput)##  
Receives sperm from the testis.  
Energetically, reflects **receptivity and preparation**.

##Body (Corpus)##  
Site of sperm maturation and fluid absorption.  
Energetically, represents **growth and refinement**.

##Tail (Cauda)##  
Stores sperm until ejaculation.  
Energetically, symbolizes **readiness and control**.

###Function###  
Stores, matures, and transports sperm to the sperm duct.  
Maintains a healthy environment for sperm preservation.  
Energetically, governs **patience, rhythm, and preparation for action**.
