#Penis#

##Location##  
The penis is positioned along the lower abdomen, enclosed within the **prepuce (sheath)**.  
It extends externally during urination or mating and retracts afterward.

###Shape###  
A **muscular and vascular organ** with a unique structure — the **os penis (baculum)** — providing rigidity even without full erection.

###Parts###  

##Root##  
Anchored to the pelvis by muscles and connective tissue.  
Energetically, represents **grounding and foundation**.

##Body##  
Contains erectile tissue and the urethra.  
Energetically, symbolizes **strength and controlled flow**.

##Glans Penis##  
Expanded tip with the **bulbus glandis**, which swells during mating to form a tie with the female.  
Energetically, reflects **union and endurance**.

###Function###  
Used for urination and mating, transporting semen and forming a copulatory tie.  
Energetically, governs **connection, endurance, and balance of physical drive**.
