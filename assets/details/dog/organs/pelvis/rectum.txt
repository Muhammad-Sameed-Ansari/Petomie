#Rectum#

##Location##
The rectum lies in the caudal abdomen, continuing from the large intestine and ending at the anus.
It sits within the pelvic cavity, just above the tail base.

###Shape###
A straight, muscular tube lined with mucous membrane, designed for storage and passage of fecal material.

##Function##
Stores feces before elimination.
Expels waste through coordinated muscle contractions.
Helps regulate body detoxification and water balance.
###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Root Chakra (Muladhara).
Meridian Connection: Large Intestine Meridian.
Emotional Meaning: Symbolizes release and grounding.
Imbalance may appear as constipation, tension, or fear of change.
Balanced Energy: Smooth elimination, emotional stability.
Energetic Support: Calm environment and regular exercise help maintain flow.
