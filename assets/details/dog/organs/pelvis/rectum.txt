#Rectum#

##Location##  
The **final portion of the large intestine**, extending from the descending colon to the anus.  

###Shape###  
A **straight muscular tube** with mucosal lining and smooth muscle layers.  

###Parts###  

##Upper Rectum##  
Stores feces before elimination.  
Energetically, represents **preparation and patience**.  

##Middle Rectum##  
Senses fullness and signals need for release.  
Energetically, symbolizes **awareness and readiness**.  

##Lower Rectum##  
Connects to the anal canal.  
Energetically, reflects **transition and release**.  

###Function###  
Stores feces and assists in controlled elimination.  
Supports pressure balance during defecation.  
Energetically, governs **completion and acceptance**.
