#Anus#

##Location##
The anus is the external opening of the digestive tract, located beneath the tail.
It marks the end of the rectum and is surrounded by strong muscles.

###Shape###
A circular muscular opening (sphincter) that controls the release of feces.

##Function##
Controls elimination through voluntary and involuntary muscle contraction.
Protects from infection by sealing the rectal passage.
Houses anal glands, which produce scent-marking secretions.
###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Root Chakra (Muladhara).
Meridian Connection: Bladder and Large Intestine Meridians.
Emotional Meaning: Represents boundaries and release of holding patterns.
Imbalance may appear as anxiety, fear, or difficulty letting go.
Balanced Energy: Healthy elimination and grounded presence.
Energetic Support: Calm handling and gentle massage near the base of the tail can support relaxation.
