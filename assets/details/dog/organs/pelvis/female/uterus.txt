#Uterus#

##Location##
The uterus lies in the pelvic cavity, between the bladder and rectum.
It consists of two long uterine horns that join at a short uterine body leading to the cervix.

###Shape###
A Y-shaped, muscular organ with flexible walls that can expand greatly during pregnancy.

##Function##
Provides a nurturing environment for embryos during development.
Contracts to expel pups during birth.
Plays a vital role in hormonal cycles and reproductive health.
