#Uterus#

##Location##  
Located within the **pelvic and abdominal cavities**, between the bladder and colon.  
It is Y-shaped, consisting of a body and two long uterine horns.

###Shape###  
A **hollow, muscular organ** that can expand significantly during pregnancy.  
Composed of smooth muscle and a glandular lining.

###Parts###  

##Uterine Horns##  
Extend toward the ovaries.  
Energetically, symbolize **nurturing and growth**.

##Uterine Body##  
Central portion leading to the cervix.  
Energetically, represents **unity and stability**.

##Endometrium and Myometrium##  
Lining and muscle layers for nourishment and contraction.  
Energetically, reflect **support and creation**.

###Function###  
Provides environment for embryo development and fetal nourishment.  
Contracts during birth to deliver puppies.  
Energetically, governs **nurturing, rhythm, and creation**.

**Emotional Meaning:** Compassion and emotional grounding.  
**Balanced Energy:** Calm nurturing and trust in life.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation (pyometra) or tension.  
Energetically, **overextension or suppressed nurturing**.
