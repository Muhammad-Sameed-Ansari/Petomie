#Ovaries#

##Location##
The ovaries are located in the dorsal abdomen, near the kidneys, one on each side.
They are supported by ligaments that attach them to the body wall and connect them to the uterus via the uterine tubes.

###Shape###
Two small, oval glands, each about the size of a small bean.
They are pale pink and covered with a smooth surface membrane.

##Function##
Produce eggs (ova) for reproduction.
Secrete hormones such as estrogen and progesterone that regulate the heat cycle and reproductive behavior.
Influence growth, mood, and fertility.
