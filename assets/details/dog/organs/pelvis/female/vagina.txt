#Vagina#

##Location##
The vagina extends from the cervix to the vulva, lying between the urethra and rectum.
It forms the lower portion of the female reproductive tract.

###Shape###
A muscular, elastic tube that can expand during mating or birth.
Its lining produces moisture for comfort and protection.

##Function##
Serves as the birth canal during whelping.
Acts as the copulatory organ during mating.
Provides a passage for the release of reproductive secretions.
