#Uterine Tubes (Oviducts)#

##Location##
The uterine tubes extend from the ovaries to the uterine horns, lying within the dorsal abdomen.
Each tube is suspended by a thin fold of tissue called the mesosalpinx.

###Shape###
Two delicate, coiled tubes with a narrow passage.
The ends near the ovaries have fringed edges (fimbriae) that help guide the egg.

##Function##
Transport eggs from the ovary to the uterus.
Provide the site for fertilization of the egg by sperm.
Secrete fluids that nourish and support early embryo development.
