#Uterine Tubes (Oviducts)#

##Location##  
Extend from each **ovary** to the **uterine horns**, enclosed within the broad ligament.  
They capture released ova and guide them toward the uterus.

###Shape###  
Slender, coiled tubes lined with cilia that aid egg movement.  
Site of fertilization in the female dog.

###Parts###  

##Infundibulum##  
Funnel-shaped opening near the ovary.  
Energetically, symbolizes **receptivity and openness**.

##Ampulla##  
Wider midsection where fertilization usually occurs.  
Energetically, represents **union and creation**.

##Isthmus##  
Narrow section leading to the uterus.  
Energetically, reflects **flow and direction**.

###Function###  
Transport ova, provide conditions for fertilization, and move embryos to the uterus.  
Energetically, govern **connection, harmony, and new beginnings**.
