#Urethra#

##Location##  
A **narrow tube** that runs from the urinary bladder to the external opening.  
Passes through the pelvic region and differs slightly in males and females.  

###Shape###  
A **slender muscular tube** lined with mucous membrane.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal Segment##  
Connects to the bladder.  
Energetically, represents **containment and security**.  

##Pelvic Segment##  
Runs through the pelvic floor muscles.  
Energetically, symbolizes **support and stability**.  

##Distal Segment##  
Ends at the external opening for urine release.  
Energetically, reflects **freedom and release**.  

###Function###  
Carries urine from the bladder to the outside of the body.  
Maintains flow and urinary control.  
Energetically, governs **purification, flow, and emotional release**.
