#Urethra#

##Location##
The urethra runs from the urinary bladder through the pelvis, exiting the body through the penis (male) or vulva (female).

###Shape###
A narrow, flexible tube surrounded by smooth and skeletal muscle layers.

##Function##
Transports urine from the bladder to the outside.
In males, also carries semen during ejaculation.
Plays a role in fluid regulation and elimination.
