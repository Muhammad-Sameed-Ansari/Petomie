#Sperm Duct#

##Location##
The sperm duct (vas deferens) extends from the tail of the epididymis, travels through the spermatic cord, and connects with the urethra near the prostate.

###Shape###
A firm, muscular tube lined with smooth muscle and supported by connective tissue.
It runs alongside blood vessels and nerves within the spermatic cord.

##Function##
Transports mature sperm from the epididymis to the urethra during ejaculation.
Controls the direction and release of reproductive cells.
Coordinates with muscles and glands during mating.
