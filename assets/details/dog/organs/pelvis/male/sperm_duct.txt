#Sperm Duct (Vas Deferens)#

##Location##  
Extends from the tail of the **epididymis** through the **inguinal canal** into the pelvic cavity, joining the urethra near the prostate.

###Shape###  
A **narrow, muscular tube** with smooth muscle walls that contract to propel sperm forward.

###Parts###  

##Ampulla##  
Enlarged region near the urethra that stores sperm.  
Energetically, represents **focus and readiness**.

##Muscular Wall##  
Contracts during ejaculation.  
Energetically, reflects **direction and movement**.

##Opening to Urethra##  
Where sperm enter the urethral passage.  
Energetically, symbolizes **flow and transition**.

###Function###  
Transports sperm from the epididymis to the urethra.  
Coordinates with prostate secretions for semen delivery.  
Energetically, governs **direction, strength, and expression**.
