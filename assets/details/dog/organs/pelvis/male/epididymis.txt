#Epididymis#

##Location##
The epididymis is attached along the top and back of each testis within the scrotum.
It connects the testes to the sperm ducts and acts as a link between sperm production and transport.

###Shape###
A narrow, coiled tube forming a curved structure over each testis.
It appears as a soft ridge along the dorsal surface of the testes.

##Function##
Stores and matures sperm before ejaculation.
Provides a controlled environment for sperm mobility and quality.
Plays a key role in regulating fertility and timing.
