#Urinary Bladder#

Situated in the **lower abdomen**, behind the pubic bone.  
Connects to the kidneys through the ureters.  

###Shape###  
A **hollow muscular sac** that expands and contracts to store urine.  

###Parts###

Energetically, represents **adaptability and patience**.  

##Body##

##Neck##  
Narrow section leading to the urethra.

###Function###  
Collects and stores urine until voluntary release.  
Regulates water balance and excretion.  
Energetically, governs **release, safety, and flow**.
