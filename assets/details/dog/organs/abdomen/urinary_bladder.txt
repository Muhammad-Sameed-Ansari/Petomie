#Urinary Bladder#

##Location##
The bladder lies in the caudal abdomen, just before the pelvis.
It connects to the urethra, which leads outside the body.

###Shape###
A flexible, muscular sac that expands and contracts as urine is stored and released.

##Function##
Stores urine from the kidneys.
Empties through the urethra during urination.
Maintains fluid balance.
Assists in toxin elimination.
###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Root Chakra (Muladhara).
Meridian Connection: Bladder Meridian.
Emotional Meaning: Represents flow, adaptability, and emotional release.
Imbalance may show as urinary tension or fear.
Balanced Energy: Relaxed flow and calm grounding.
Energetic Support: Hydration, gentle walks, and relaxation support urinary balance.
