#Spleen#

##Location##
The spleen lies on the left side of the abdomen, near the stomach and under the ribs.

###Shape###
A flat, elongated, dark-red organ that can enlarge or shrink depending on blood storage.

##Function##
Stores and filters blood, removing worn-out red blood cells.
Produces and releases white blood cells to fight infection.
Acts as a blood reserve in emergencies.
Supports immune and circulatory systems.
###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura).
Meridian Connection: Spleen Meridian.
Emotional Meaning: Represents stability, care, and trust.
Imbalance may appear as fatigue, overthinking, or worry.
Balanced Energy: Grounded calmness and vitality.
Energetic Support: Routine, warmth, and balanced meals restore energy.
