#Kidneys#

##Location##
The kidneys lie on the dorsal wall of the abdomen, just below the spine.
The right kidney is positioned slightly forward of the left.

###Shape###
Smooth, bean-shaped organs with a reddish-brown color.

##Function##
Filter blood to remove waste and toxins.
Maintain fluid and electrolyte balance.
Regulate blood pressure and red blood cell production.
Produce urine to excrete waste.
###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Root and Sacral Chakras (Muladhara & Svadhisthana).
Meridian Connection: Kidney Meridian.
Emotional Meaning: Represents vitality, fear, and resilience.
Imbalance may appear as anxiety or fatigue.
Balanced Energy: Calm courage and stable vitality.
Energetic Support: Warmth, rest, and hydration nurture kidney energy.
