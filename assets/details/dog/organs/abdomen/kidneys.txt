#Kidneys#

##Location##  
Located in the **upper abdomen** on either side of the spine, just below the last ribs.  
The right kidney lies slightly ahead of the left, nestled near the liver.  

###Shape###  
Paired **bean-shaped organs** with smooth surfaces and distinct inner structures.  

###Parts###  

##Cortex##  
Outer layer containing nephrons for filtration.  
Energetically, represents **structure and purification**.  

##Medulla##  
Inner section with pyramids and tubules concentrating urine.  
Energetically, symbolizes **flow and transformation**.  

##Renal Pelvis##  
Collects filtered urine before it enters the ureter.  
Energetically, reflects **release and letting go**.  

###Function###  
Filters waste and toxins from the blood, balances electrolytes, and regulates water levels.  
Supports blood pressure and hormone production.  
Energetically, governs **resilience, grounding, and life force**.

**Chakra:** Root Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Kidney Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Fear, trust, and vitality.  
**Balanced Energy:** Strength, courage, and emotional stability.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Kidney disease, dehydration, or toxin overload.  
Energetically, **fear, exhaustion, or imbalance in flow**.
