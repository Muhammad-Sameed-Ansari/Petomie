#Gallbladder#

##Location##
The gallbladder lies beneath the right central lobes of the liver and connects to the duodenum through the bile duct.

###Shape###
A small, greenish, pear-shaped sac that expands and contracts with bile storage and release.

##Function##
Stores and concentrates bile from the liver.
Releases bile into the small intestine to digest fats.
Coordinates digestion with liver and pancreas.
Helps maintain metabolic and digestive balance.
