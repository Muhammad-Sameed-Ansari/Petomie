#Gallbladder (Dog)#

##Location##  
Nestled on the **underside of the liver**, between its lobes.  
Positioned near the stomach and duodenum, where it can easily release bile into the digestive tract.

###Shape###  
A **small, pear-shaped, fluid-filled sac** with a smooth, elastic wall that expands and contracts based on bile storage.

###Parts###

##Fundus (Base)##  
The rounded end where bile is stored.  
Energetically, represents **holding, patience, and emotional containment**.

##Body##  
The central chamber that maintains bile volume.  
Energetically, symbolizes **balance, steadiness, and digestion of experiences**.

##Neck##  
Connects to the cystic duct for bile release.  
Energetically, reflects **decision-making, direction, and flow of action**.

###Function###  
Stores and concentrates bile produced by the liver.  
Releases bile into the small intestine to aid in fat digestion and nutrient absorption.  
Energetically, governs **clarity, direction, courage, and processing of emotional “fat” or heaviness**.

**Chakra:** Solar Plexus Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Gallbladder Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Courage, assertiveness, and the ability to move forward.  
**Balanced Energy:** Smooth digestion, emotional clarity, confident decision-making.

###Common Imbalances###  
Gallstones, inflammation, bile blockages, or digestive discomfort.  
Energetically, **indecision, frustration, or suppressed assertiveness**.
