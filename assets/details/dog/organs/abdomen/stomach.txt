#Stomach#

##Location##  
Located in the **upper left abdomen**, between the esophagus and duodenum.  

###Shape###  
A **J-shaped muscular sac** with expandable walls.  

###Parts###  

##Cardia##  
Entrance from the esophagus.  
Energetically, represents **reception and readiness**.  

##Body##  
Main chamber mixing food with digestive fluids.  
Energetically, symbolizes **nurturing and transformation**.  

##Pylorus##  
Exit leading to the small intestine.  
Energetically, embodies **timing and trust**.  

###Function###  
Stores, mixes, and breaks down food with acid and enzymes.  
Begins digestion and regulates flow to the intestine.  
Energetically, governs **emotional digestion and balance**.

**Meridian:** Stomach Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Comfort and openness.  
**Balanced Energy:** Calm digestion and emotional ease.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Vomiting, bloat, or ulcers.  
Energetically, **anxiety or over-responsibility**.
