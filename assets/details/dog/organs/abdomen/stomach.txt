#Stomach#

##Location##
The stomach lies in the upper left part of the abdomen, just behind the liver and beneath the diaphragm.
It connects the esophagus to the small intestine (duodenum).

###Shape###
A J-shaped, muscular sac that expands and contracts with food intake.
Its lining contains both glandular and non-glandular regions to begin digestion.

##Function##
Mixes food with stomach acid and enzymes for digestion.
Begins the breakdown of proteins.
Regulates the passage of food into the small intestine.
Acts as a temporary storage chamber.

Chakra: Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura).
Meridian Connection: Stomach Meridian.
Emotional Meaning: Reflects how one accepts nourishment and experiences.
Imbalance may cause nausea, worry, or tension.
