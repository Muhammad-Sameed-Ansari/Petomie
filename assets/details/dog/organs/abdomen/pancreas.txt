#Pancreas#

##Location##
The pancreas lies behind the stomach, between the duodenum and spleen.

###Shape###
A flat, pale pink organ with a soft texture.
It has two parts:
Exocrine portion – produces digestive enzymes.
Endocrine portion – releases insulin and glucagon.

##Function##
Produces enzymes to digest fats, carbohydrates, and proteins.
Releases insulin to control blood sugar levels.
Regulates metabolism and energy use.
Supports overall digestive harmony.
###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura).
Meridian Connection: Spleen and Stomach Meridians.
Emotional Meaning: Represents trust, balance, and nourishment.
Imbalance may appear as fatigue or sugar imbalance.
Balanced Energy: Vitality and calm digestion.
Energetic Support: Rest after eating and emotional calm aid pancreas function.
