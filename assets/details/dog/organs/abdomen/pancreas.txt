#Pancreas#

##Location##  
Situated between the **stomach and duodenum**, near the liver and spleen.  

###Shape###  
A **long, soft, lobulated organ** with both digestive and hormonal functions.  

###Parts###  

##Head##  
Connects to the duodenum for enzyme release.  
Energetically, represents **nourishment and flow**.  

##Body##  
Main section producing hormones and enzymes.  
Energetically, symbolizes **balance and sweetness**.  

##Tail##  
Extends toward the spleen.  
Energetically, reflects **completion and harmony**.  

###Function###  
Secretes digestive enzymes and regulates blood sugar via insulin and glucagon.  
Energetically, governs **nourishment, balance, and contentment**.
