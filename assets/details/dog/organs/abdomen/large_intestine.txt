#Large Intestine#

##Location##  
Extends from the **small intestine** to the **anus**, forming the final section of the digestive tract.  

###Shape###  
A **wide tubular structure** that frames the abdominal cavity.  

###Parts###  

##Cecum##  
Small pouch receiving undigested material.  
Energetically, represents **adaptation and preparation**.  

##Colon##  
Ascending, transverse, and descending sections absorb water and electrolytes.  
Energetically, symbolize **cleansing and renewal**.  

##Rectum##  
Stores waste before elimination.  
Energetically, embodies **release and completion**.  

###Function###  
Absorbs water, forms and stores feces, and maintains fluid balance.  
Supports the body’s detoxification process.  
Energetically, governs **release, cleansing, and emotional freedom**.
