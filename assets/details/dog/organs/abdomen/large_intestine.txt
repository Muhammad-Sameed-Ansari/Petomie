#Large Intestine#

##Location##
The large intestine is found in the caudal abdomen, connecting the small intestine to the rectum.
It includes the cecum, colon, and rectum.

###Shape###
A wide, looping tube that forms the final part of the digestive tract.

##Function##
Absorbs water and electrolytes.
Ferments leftover food with gut bacteria.
Forms and stores feces.
Maintains gut health and detoxification.
