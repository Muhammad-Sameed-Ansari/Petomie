#Small Intestine#

##Location##
The small intestine fills the middle abdomen, connecting the stomach to the large intestine.
It is supported by the mesentery, a fold of tissue containing blood vessels and lymph nodes.

###Shape###
A long, coiled tube made up of three parts — duodenum, jejunum, and ileum.

##Function##
Completes digestion using enzymes from pancreas and bile from the liver.
Absorbs nutrients, vitamins, and water.
Supports immune defense through lymphatic tissue.
Transfers digested material to the large intestine.
