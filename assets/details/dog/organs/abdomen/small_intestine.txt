#Small Intestine#

##Location##  
Occupies the **central abdominal cavity**, connecting the stomach to the large intestine.  

###Shape###  
A **long, coiled tube** with a soft mucous lining for absorption.  

###Parts###  

##Duodenum##  
Receives bile and enzymes for digestion.  
Energetically, represents **acceptance and readiness**.  

##Jejunum##  
Middle section absorbing nutrients.  
Energetically, symbolizes **assimilation and vitality**.  

##Ileum##  
Final portion connecting to the large intestine.  
Energetically, reflects **completion and transition**.

Maintains gut immunity and bacterial balance.
