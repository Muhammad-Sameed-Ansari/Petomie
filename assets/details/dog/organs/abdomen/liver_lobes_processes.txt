#Liver (Lobes & Processes)#

##Location##
Cranial abdomen, just caudal to the diaphragm.

##Parts##
Left lateral, left medial, quadrate, right medial, right lateral, caudate lobes, caudate process, papillary process.

##Function##
Produces bile for fat digestion, metabolizes nutrients, stores glycogen, detoxifies blood, synthesizes proteins.
