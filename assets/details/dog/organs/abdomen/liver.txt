#Liver#

##Location##
The liver is located in the cranial abdomen, just below the diaphragm and mostly to the right side.
It touches the stomach and small intestine.

###Shape###
A large, reddish-brown organ divided into multiple lobes.
The gallbladder lies between its lobes.

##Function##
Produces bile to digest fats.
Detoxifies harmful substances in the blood.
Stores glycogen, vitamins, and minerals.
Plays a central role in metabolism and hormone balance.
###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura).
Meridian Connection: Liver Meridian.
Emotional Meaning: Symbolizes patience, adaptability, and emotional flow.
Imbalance may appear as irritability or frustration.
Balanced Energy: Clear emotions and steady vitality.
Energetic Support: Calm movement, hydration, and rest support liver balance.
