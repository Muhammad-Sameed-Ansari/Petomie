#Liver#

##Location##  
Lies beneath the **diaphragm** in the front of the abdomen, spanning across the stomach and intestines.  

###Shape###  
A **large, multi-lobed organ** with smooth reddish-brown tissue.  

###Parts###  

##Left and Right Lobes##  
Handle metabolism and detoxification.  
Energetically, represent **balance and strength**.  

##Gallbladder Fossa##  
Depression housing the gallbladder.  
Energetically, symbolizes **stored energy and patience**.  

##Hepatic Vessels##  
Transport nutrients and detoxified blood.  
Energetically, embody **flow and renewal**.  

###Function###  
Processes nutrients, detoxifies chemicals, and stores glycogen.  
Produces bile for fat digestion and supports metabolism.  
Energetically, governs **transformation, direction, and courage**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Solar Plexus Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Liver Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Anger, drive, and planning.  
**Balanced Energy:** Motivation and harmony.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Liver overload, poor digestion, or inflammation.  
Energetically, **frustration, anger, or stagnation**.  

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###  
Support detox with greens and gentle exercise.  
**Colors:** Green and gold inspire renewal.  
**Crystals:** Bloodstone and aventurine balance flow.  
**Elements:** Wood and fire enhance transformation.
