#Thymus#

##Location##  
Located in the **upper thoracic cavity**, in front of the heart and behind the sternum.  
Most active in young dogs, shrinking as they mature.

###Shape###  
A **soft, lobed gland** composed of lymphatic tissue.  
Light pink and delicate in young animals.

###Parts###  

##Lobules##  
Produce immune cells (T-cells).  
Energetically, represent **growth and protection**.

##Capsule and Septa##  
Support and divide gland structures.  
Energetically, symbolize **structure and learning**.

###Function###  
Develops and regulates the immune system.  
Supports the body’s defense and adaptation to stress.  
Energetically, governs **trust, courage, and healing capacity**.
