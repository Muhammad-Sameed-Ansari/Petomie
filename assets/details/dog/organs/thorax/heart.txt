#Heart#

##Location##
The heart lies in the center of the chest, slightly to the left, between the lungs and above the diaphragm.
It is enclosed within a protective membrane called the pericardium and shielded by the ribs.

###Shape###
A cone-shaped muscular organ with four chambers — two atria and two ventricles.
It contracts rhythmically to pump blood through the lungs and the rest of the body.

##Function##
Pumps oxygenated blood to all body tissues.
Regulates circulation, rhythm, and pressure balance.
Maintains vital energy and communication between organs.
Reflects both physical health and emotional balance.
###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Heart Chakra (Anahata) — love, connection, and inner balance.
Meridian Connection: Heart and Pericardium Meridians.
Emotional Meaning: Represents love, trust, and courage to connect.
Imbalance may cause nervousness, sadness, or emotional withdrawal.
Balanced Energy: Calm heart rhythm, open emotional flow, and peaceful behavior.
Energetic Support: Gentle chest contact, calm surroundings, and balanced breathing foster coherence and healing.
