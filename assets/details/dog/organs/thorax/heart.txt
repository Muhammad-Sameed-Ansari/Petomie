#Heart#

##Location##  
The heart lies in the **thoracic cavity**, between the lungs and slightly to the left of the midline, protected by the ribs.

###Shape###  
A **cone-shaped muscular organ** surrounded by the pericardium.  
Divided into four chambers — two atria and two ventricles.

###Parts###  

##Atria##  
Receive returning blood.  
Energetically, represent **acceptance and receptivity**.

##Ventricles##  
Pump blood to lungs and body.  
Energetically, symbolize **strength and vitality**.

##Valves##  
Maintain one-way blood flow.  
Energetically, reflect **balance and trust**.

###Function###  
Pumps blood throughout the body, delivering oxygen and nutrients.  
Maintains circulation, temperature, and energy distribution.  
Energetically, governs **love, courage, and emotional vitality**.

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Heart Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Heart and Pericardium Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Connection, joy, and life force.  
**Balanced Energy:** Calm, steady heartbeat and open heart.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weakness, stress, or irregular pulse.  
Energetically, **closed-off feelings or overexertion**.
