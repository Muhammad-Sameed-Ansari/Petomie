#Lungs#

##Location##  
Occupy most of the **thoracic cavity**, surrounding the heart and extending from the diaphragm to the thoracic inlet.

###Shape###  
Spongy, elastic, and lobed organs.  
The right lung has four lobes; the left has two.

###Parts###  

##Bronchi and Bronchioles##  
Branching airways distributing air.  
Energetically, represent **communication and adaptability**.

##Alveoli##  
Tiny sacs for gas exchange.  
Energetically, symbolize **renewal and inspiration**.

##Pleura##  
Protective membrane covering the lungs.  
Energetically, reflects **ease and expansion**.

###Function###  
Exchange oxygen and carbon dioxide, regulate pH, and support voice production.  
Energetically, govern **release, inspiration, and emotional freedom**.

**Chakra:** Heart and Throat Chakras.  
**Meridian:** Lung and Large Intestine Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Letting go and openness.  
**Balanced Energy:** Calm, full breathing and peace.

###Common Imbalances###  
Coughing, shallow breathing, or congestion.  
Energetically, **grief or emotional tightness**.
