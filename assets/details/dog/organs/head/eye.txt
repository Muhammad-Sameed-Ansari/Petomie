#Eye#

##Location##  
Located at the **front of the skull** within the bony orbits, connected to the brain by the optic nerves.  

###Shape###  
A **round organ** protected by eyelids and surrounded by muscles for movement.  

###Parts###  

##Cornea##  
Transparent front layer focusing incoming light.  
Energetically, represents **clarity and openness**.  

##Lens and Iris##  
Control light entry and focus.  
Energetically, symbolize **adaptability and vision**.  

##Retina and Optic Nerve##  
Convert light into signals for the brain.  
Energetically, embody **insight and awareness**.  

###Function###  
Detects light, motion, and color for vision and environmental awareness.  
Energetically, governs **perception, insight, and clarity**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Third Eye Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Liver and Gallbladder Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Intuition and focus.  
**Balanced Energy:** Clear sight and perspective.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Eye irritation, infection, or cloudy vision.  
Energetically, **confusion or avoidance of truth**.  

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###  
Support with good lighting and calm environments.  
**Colors:** Indigo and turquoise cleanse vision.  
**Crystals:** Fluorite and lapis lazuli enhance clarity.  
**Elements:** Light and air balance awareness.
