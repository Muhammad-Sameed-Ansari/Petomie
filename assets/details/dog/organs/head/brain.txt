#Brain#

##Location##  
The brain is located within the **cranial cavity**, protected by the skull and cushioned by cerebrospinal fluid.  

###Shape###  
A **soft, rounded organ** composed of several lobes and hemispheres with a smooth outer surface.  

###Parts###  

##Cerebrum##  
Controls voluntary movement, behavior, and learning.  
Energetically, represents **awareness and intelligence**.  

##Cerebellum##  
Regulates balance and coordination.  
Energetically, symbolizes **harmony and rhythm**.  

##Brainstem##  
Connects to the spinal cord and regulates vital functions such as breathing and heartbeat.  
Energetically, embodies **life force and stability**.  

###Function###
