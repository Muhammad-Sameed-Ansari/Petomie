#Ear#

##Location##  
Positioned on each side of the **head**, consisting of external, middle, and inner structures.  

###Shape###  
The **external pinna** is a mobile flap that directs sound into a curved canal leading to deeper chambers.  

###Parts###  

##External Ear##  
Collects sound waves.  
Energetically, represents **receptivity and awareness**.  

##Middle Ear##

Energetically, symbolizes **communication and harmony**.  

##Inner Ear##  
Houses the cochlea and vestibular system for hearing and balance.  
Energetically, reflects **intuition and equilibrium**.

Energetically, governs **listening, focus, and perception**.
