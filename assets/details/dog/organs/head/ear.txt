#Ear#

##Location##
Situated on either side of the head.

##Parts##
- Pinna (Auricle) – Collects sound
- Ear Canal – Conducts sound to tympanum
- Tympanum (Eardrum) – Vibrates with sound waves
- Ossicles (Malleus, Incus, Stapes) – Amplify sound
- Cochlea – Converts sound to neural signals
- Vestibule & Semicircular Canals – Detect position and motion (balance)

##Function##
Hearing and balance (equilibrium).
