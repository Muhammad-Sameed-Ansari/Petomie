#Esophagus#

##Location##  
The esophagus extends from the **pharynx** down the **left side of the neck**, passing through the thoracic cavity to connect with the **stomach**.  

###Shape###  
A **long, muscular tube** lined with mucous membrane, adapted for smooth transport of food.  

###Parts###  

##Cervical Portion##  
Runs along the neck beside the trachea.  
Energetically, represents **expression and flow**.  

##Thoracic Portion##  
Passes through the chest behind the heart and lungs.  
Energetically, symbolizes **transition and trust**.  

##Abdominal Portion##  
Short section entering the stomach at an oblique angle.  
Energetically, reflects **readiness and transformation**.  

###Function###  
Transports food and water from the mouth to the stomach through peristaltic motion.  
Prevents reflux due to a strong lower sphincter.  
Energetically, governs **acceptance, flow, and nourishment**.

**Emotional Meaning:** Trust and openness in receiving nourishment.  
**Balanced Energy:** Smooth, easy swallowing and calm digestion.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Choke, obstruction, or inflammation.  
Energetically, **fear of swallowing or resistance to receiving**.
