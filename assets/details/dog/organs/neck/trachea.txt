#Trachea#

##Location##  
Extends from the **larynx** through the neck into the **thoracic cavity**, where it divides into the bronchi leading to the lungs.  

###Shape###  
A **flexible tube** supported by C-shaped rings of cartilage and lined with cilia and mucous glands.  

###Parts###  

##Cartilaginous Rings##  
Keep the airway open and maintain structure.  
Energetically, represent **support and resilience**.  

##Tracheal Muscle##  
Completes the dorsal part of the ring, aiding in airflow control.  
Energetically, symbolizes **adaptability and rhythm**.  

##Mucosal Lining##  
Traps dust and particles for removal.  
Energetically, reflects **cleansing and purity**.  

###Function###  
Provides a clear airway for air exchange and filters inhaled particles.  
Energetically, governs **flow of breath and expression of life force**.
