#Larynx#

##Location##  
Located in the **upper neck**, between the pharynx and trachea, forming part of the upper airway.  

###Shape###  
A **cartilaginous framework** with muscles and membranes that regulate airflow and sound.  

###Parts###  

##Epiglottis##  
Flexible cartilage that closes during swallowing to protect the airway.  
Energetically, represents **protection and choice**.  

##Vocal Folds##  
Thin membranes producing sound during airflow.  
Energetically, symbolize **expression and voice**.  

##Arytenoid Cartilages##  
Control opening and closing of the airway.  
Energetically, embody **balance and communication**.  

###Function###  
Controls breathing and swallowing while producing vocalization.  
Prevents aspiration of food into the trachea.  
Energetically, governs **truth, self-expression, and clear intention**.
