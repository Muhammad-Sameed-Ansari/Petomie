#Pharynx#

##Location##  
A muscular chamber at the **back of the nasal and oral cavities**, connecting them to the esophagus and larynx.  

###Shape###  
A **funnel-shaped structure** composed of muscular walls lined with mucosa.  

###Parts###  

##Nasopharynx##  
Behind the nasal cavity, allowing airflow.  
Energetically, represents **clarity and breath**.  

##Oropharynx##  
Behind the mouth, guiding food and air.  
Energetically, symbolizes **choice and transition**.  

##Laryngopharynx##  
Leads into the esophagus and larynx.  
Energetically, reflects **integration and direction**.  

###Function###  
Passageway for food and air, assisting in swallowing and phonation.  
Energetically, governs **coordination, trust, and openness**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Coughing, inflammation, or swallowing difficulty.  
Energetically, **tension between expression and intake**.
