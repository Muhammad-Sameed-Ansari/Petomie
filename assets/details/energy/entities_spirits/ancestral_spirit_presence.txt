#Ancestral Spirit Presence#

Ancestral spirit presence refers to the awareness or proximity of ancestral consciousness connected through lineage.
It does not imply attachment, possession, or interference.

Ancestral spirits may remain present out of protection, guidance, loyalty, or unresolved lineage themes.
Their presence is often neutral or supportive.

Both humans and animals can experience ancestral spirit presence.
