###Types of Energetic Parasites###

##Emotional Parasites##
Feed on strong or repetitive emotions
Often linked to fear, anxiety, guilt, or anger
May intensify emotional reactions

##Thought-Form Parasites##
Sustained by repetitive thoughts or belief loops
Can amplify self-doubt, worry, or negative self-talk
May feel autonomous over time

##Environmental Parasites##
Picked up from places with dense or chaotic energy
Common in shelters, hospitals, trauma sites, or crowded spaces
Often released once removed from the environment

##Ancestral or Lineage Parasites##
Passed through family or herd lines
Linked to survival, scarcity, or trauma patterns
May persist until consciously addressed

##Trauma-Based Parasites##
Attach during moments of shock or overwhelm
Feed on unresolved trauma energy
Common in rescue animals or post-accident situations

##Collective Energy Parasites##
Linked to collective fear, panic, or stress
Often activated during large-scale events or crises
May affect many individuals simultaneously
