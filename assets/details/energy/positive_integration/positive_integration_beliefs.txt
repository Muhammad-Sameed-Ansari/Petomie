#Positive Integration Beliefs#

Positive integration beliefs are stabilizing internal truths that the system adopts after release, clearing, or resolution.
They support safety, regulation, autonomy, and healthy connection in the present moment.

##Positive Integration Anchors the Present##

###Positive Beliefs###

##Upper##
###TABLE_START###
No. | SET 1 | SET 2 | SET 3
1. | I am safe | I am protected | Nothing is threatening me
2. | I can relax | My body can soften | I can let go
3. | I belong | I have a place | I fit here
4. | I am capable | I can respond | I can manage situations
5. | It is allowed to rest | Pausing is safe | There is no urgency
6. | I can trust life | Support exists | I am guided
7. | I am present | I am here now | I am grounded in this moment
8. | I am valued | I matter | My presence is welcome
9. | I am stable | Things are steady | The ground holds me
10. | Change is manageable | I can adapt | I remain intact
###TABLE_END###

##Lower##
###TABLE_START###
No. | SET 1 | SET 2 | SET 3
1. | I am resourced | I have what I need | Inner support is available
2. | I can process slowly | There is enough time | I do not need to rush
3. | Orientation is possible | I can find direction | I can make sense of this
4. | I have choice | I can decide | I am powerful
5. | It is safe to be open | Vulnerability is allowed | I remain protected
6. | Ease is available | Flow can return | Effort is not required
7. | Being seen is neutral | Attention is safe | Focus does not harm me
8. | I am included | I am part of the group | I am not excluded
9. | Connection remains | Bonds persist | Distance does not equal loss
10. | Familiarity can shift | New can be safe | Stability is internal
###TABLE_END###

##A Positive Integration May Show As##
Relaxed posture and breathing. Improved settling and rest. Balanced attachment without distress. Reduced anticipatory stress. Curiosity and calm engagement. Stable behavior during separation or change
