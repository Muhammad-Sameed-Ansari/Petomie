#Lotus Flower#

##Location##
The Lotus Flower resonates strongly within the heart, sacral, and crown chakras, supporting emotional healing, purity, and spiritual awakening.
Its energy flows through the aura, especially in areas where release, renewal, and gentle transformation are needed.
It also stabilizes the emotional body, helping the animal rise above past trauma, fear, or heaviness.

###Shape###
The Lotus Flower is depicted as layered petals radiating outward from a central point.
Its form symbolizes unfolding, purity, and emergence from darkness into light.
The closed bud represents potential and inner growth.
The open petals symbolize enlightenment, expansion, and the blossoming of the soul.
Its symmetry reflects harmony, resilience, and the ability to rise above challenges.

##Function##
Supports emotional release by encouraging old patterns to dissolve gently.
Calms the nervous system through soft, high-vibrational frequencies.
Promotes clarity, self-healing, and inner peace.
Encourages the animal to rise out of fear or trauma into a more balanced and open state.
Strengthens the connection between grounded presence and elevated awareness.

##Energetic Function##
Clears stagnant emotional energy with softness and compassion
Strengthens the heart chakra and opens the field to love
Supports sacral healing related to trust, relationships, and emotional safety
Encourages upward energy movement through chakras
Brings harmony to the aura through gentle expansion
Helps release trauma without overwhelm
Promotes emotional resilience and peaceful growth
Stabilizes the energy field during transitions or healing processes

##Spiritual Connection##
The Lotus Flower symbolizes spiritual awakening, purity, and the journey of rising from darkness into light.
When visualized in the aura, it deepens the connection to inner truth, soul purpose, and divine guidance.
It teaches that healing is a process of unfolding, that growth happens layer by layer, and that beauty emerges from depth.
Through this resonance, the animal radiates serenity, openness, and spiritual grace, embodying the peaceful evolution of the soul.
