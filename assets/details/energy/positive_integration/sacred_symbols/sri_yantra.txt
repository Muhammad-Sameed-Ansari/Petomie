###Shape###
The Sri Yantra is composed of nine interlocking triangles radiating from a central point.
Four triangles point upward, representing divine masculine energy, direction, and expansion.
Five triangles point downward, symbolizing divine feminine energy, creation, and infinite potential.
Together they create a multidimensional geometric mandala expressing perfect balance, unity, and the structure of the universe.
A surrounding circle and lotus petals represent protection, evolution, and the unfolding of consciousness.
