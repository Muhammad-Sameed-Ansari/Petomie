#Flower of Life#

##Location##
Exists as a universal geometric pattern found in all living beings, cells, and energetic structures.
In the animal energy system, it resonates within the aura and subtle body, especially around the heart and solar plexus chakras, acting as a blueprint of balance and creation.
It also connects the animal’s field to the Earth’s sacred geometry and the greater web of life.

###Shape###
A symmetrical pattern composed of multiple evenly spaced, overlapping circles that form a flower-like structure.
This sacred geometry represents perfect harmony, unity, and interconnectedness between all aspects of existence — physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual.

##Function##
Serves as an energetic template that maintains coherence and order in the energy field.
Supports natural regeneration, alignment, and communication between cells, organs, and energy centers.
Acts as a stabilizing frequency that harmonizes the flow of life force and restores balance when distortion or fragmentation occurs.
Encourages connection between the individual being and the universal patterns of creation.

##Energetic Function##
Aligns all layers of the aura and chakras into a unified frequency of balance
Enhances energy circulation through meridians and subtle pathways
Amplifies the body’s ability to hold light and higher vibration
Resonates with the geometry of DNA, crystals, and planetary grids, creating coherence across all scales of life
Helps integrate healing frequencies and transmit energy more efficiently throughout the body
Promotes harmony between the animal’s energy and its surroundings, reinforcing equilibrium and peace

##Spiritual Connection##
The Flower of Life symbolizes divine order and unity consciousness.
When this pattern is activated or visualized within the aura, it strengthens the animal’s connection to source energy and the sacred geometry of creation.
It brings awareness of oneness: that every living being is part of a single, harmonious design.
Through this resonance, the animal radiates balance, clarity, and wholeness, embodying the universal rhythm of life itself.
