#Positive Integration Emotions#

Positive integration emotions are regulated emotional states that emerge after release, clearing, or resolution.
They support nervous system balance, emotional coherence, and a felt sense of safety in the present moment.

Positive Emotions are stable emotional qualities that allow the system to remain open, settled, and responsive.
