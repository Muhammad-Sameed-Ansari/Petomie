###Types of Entity & Spirit Attachments###

##Earthbound Spirits##
Consciousness that has not fully transitioned
May be confused, attached to places, people, or animals
Often seek safety, familiarity, or resolution

##Nature or Elemental Energies##
Land-based or environmental consciousness
Often neutral and connected to specific locations
May attach through proximity or resonance

##Protective or Guardian Attachments##
Energies attached to protect during trauma
May stay long after protection is no longer needed

##Ancestral or Lineage Spirits##
Consciousness connected to family or herd lines
May carry unresolved experiences, duties, or emotions

##Fragmented or Lost Consciousness##
Fragments created during shock or trauma
May appear as external attachments

##Collective or Group Consciousness##
Energies linked to collective fear, grief, or trauma
Often picked up in shelters, hospitals, battle sites, or high-stress environments

###Possible Effects###
Startle responses
Over-bonding or withdrawal
Fear without clear cause
Unexplained physical or behavioral changes

##Energetic Insight##
Entity and spirit attachments are not punishments.
They indicate that the system experienced a moment of vulnerability or openness.
