#Tree of Life#

##Location##
The Tree of Life resonates throughout the entire energy field, linking the animal’s roots (lower chakras) with its branches (upper chakras and aura).
It is especially active in the root, sacral, heart, and crown chakras, where grounding, growth, connection, and higher guidance meet.
Energetically, it anchors the being into Earth’s wisdom while opening the field to universal consciousness.

###Shape###
The Tree of Life is depicted as a tree with strong roots, a stable trunk, and expansive branches reaching upward into light.
This form symbolizes the connection between Earth and sky, body and spirit, physical life and higher awareness.
The roots represent grounding, ancestry, and stability.
The branches symbolize expansion, growth, and limitless potential.
The trunk acts as the central channel through which energy flows between both realms.

##Function##
Provides grounding and stability to the entire energetic system.
Supports emotional growth, resilience, and the ability to adapt to change.
Enhances vitality by strengthening the flow of life force from Earth to the body.
Encourages natural regeneration and the development of inner strength.
Helps integrate experiences by connecting past, present, and future growth.

##Energetic Function##
Strengthens grounding and root chakra connection
Supports upward expansion through the central channel
Balances Earth energy and spiritual energy within the system
Encourages stable, nourishing flow of life force
Promotes emotional resilience and steady growth
Helps release old patterns through root clearing
Enhances aura coherence through vertical alignment
Connects the animal to the cycles and rhythms of nature

##Spiritual Connection##
The Tree of Life represents unity between Earth and Spirit, reminding all beings that they are rooted in the physical world while reaching toward higher consciousness.
When visualized in the aura, it deepens connection to Earth’s wisdom, animal instinct, and divine guidance.
It teaches growth through grounded presence and expansion through openness.
Through this resonance, the animal radiates balance, strength, and soulful connection, embodying the harmony of life’s natural evolution.
