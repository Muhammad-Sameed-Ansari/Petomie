#Merkaba#

##Location##
The Merkaba exists within the highest layers of the energy field, surrounding the animal in a multidimensional light structure.
It resonates strongly at the heart, third eye, and crown chakras, as well as the central vertical channel, connecting the physical body with the light body.
Energetically, it serves as a vehicle of ascension, expansion, and energetic protection, linking the animal to higher consciousness.

###Shape###
The Merkaba is formed by two interlocking tetrahedrons, one pointing upward and the other pointing downward.
The upward tetrahedron represents spiritual energy and higher guidance.
The downward tetrahedron represents grounding, embodiment, and physical life force.
Together they form a rotating star tetrahedron, symbolizing the union of body, mind, spirit, and the flow of energy in all directions.

Activates the light body and enhances the flow of higher consciousness.
Strengthens the communication between physical and energetic systems.
Supports deep healing by restoring multidimensional coherence.
Creates strong energetic protection, stabilizing the field and blocking disruptive frequencies.
Enhances spiritual awareness, clarity, and intuitive perception.

##Spiritual Connection##
The Merkaba symbolizes the divine vehicle of light, the body of ascension and spiritual evolution.
When visualized in the aura, it awakens deep connection to higher consciousness, soul memory, and the universal field of light.
It teaches unity between the physical and spiritual, grounding and ascension, matter and energy.
Through this resonance, the animal radiates clarity, protection, and elevated presence, embodying the harmony of higher dimensional awareness.
