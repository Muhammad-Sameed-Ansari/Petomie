#Kidney Meridian (KI)#

##Location##
Inner hind toe → inner leg → groin → abdomen → chest → terminates near sternum.

##Function##
Governs kidneys, adrenal glands, bones, reproduction, willpower.

##Imbalance signs##
Weak bones/joints, arthritis
Fearfulness, lack of stamina
Infertility, coat dullness, hearing loss
