#Large Intestine Meridian (LI)#

##Location##
Starts at outer claw → lateral forelimb → shoulder → neck → face → nostril.

##Function##
Eliminates waste, regulates fluids, supports nose/sinuses.

##Imbalance signs##
Constipation/diarrhea
Nasal discharge, sinus congestion
Toothache, jaw tension, stubbornness
