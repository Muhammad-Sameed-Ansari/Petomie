#Bladder Meridian (BL)#

##Location##
Begins at inner eye → head → along spine (two parallel lines) → sacrum → hindlimb → outer hind toe.

##Function##
Governs bladder, back, nervous system, stores ancestral Qi.

##Imbalance signs##
Back pain, hind-end weakness
Urinary tract issues, incontinence
Nervousness, sensitivity to touch
