#Yin Wei Mai (Yin Linking Vessel)#

##Pathway##
Runs along ventral trunk, connecting all Yin meridians, ending near throat.

##Functions##
Harmonizes Yin energy, emotional processing
Strengthens deep inner resources (Jing)
Helps animal cope with chronic illness or grief

##Imbalances##
Chronic fatigue, failure to thrive
Worry, sadness, introversion
Poor appetite, cold extremities
