#Liver Meridian (LR)#

##Location##
Inner hind toe → medial leg → abdomen → chest → terminates near sternum.

##Function##
Stores blood, regulates Qi flow, affects eyes, anger.

##Imbalance signs##
Eye issues, aggression, irritability
Tendon stiffness, laminitis (horses)
Hormonal imbalance, PMS-like symptoms
