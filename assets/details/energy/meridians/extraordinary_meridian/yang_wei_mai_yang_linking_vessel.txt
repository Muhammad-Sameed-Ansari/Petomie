#Yang Wei Mai (Yang Linking Vessel)#

##Pathway##
Runs along lateral body, connecting all Yang meridians, ending near head.

##Functions##
Harmonizes Yang energy, immune response, vitality
Helps animal cope with stress or infection
Balances temperature and activity levels

##Imbalances##
Chronic fevers, infections
Muscle rigidity, flank soreness
Anxiety, irritability, over-reactivity
