#Yang Qiao Mai (Yang Heel Vessel)#

##Pathway##
Runs along outer legs, hip, flank, up neck to lateral head and outer eye.

##Functions##
Balances Yang postural tone (outer muscles, activity, readiness)
Keeps animal alert, agile, coordinated
Affects balance of eyes and nervous system

##Imbalances##
Hyperactivity, restlessness, insomnia
Stifle/hock pain, lateral body tension
Aggression, startle response
