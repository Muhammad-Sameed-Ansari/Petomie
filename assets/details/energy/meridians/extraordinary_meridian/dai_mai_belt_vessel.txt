#Dai Mai (Belt Vessel)#

##Pathway##
Encircles the waist like a belt, connecting front and back.

##Functions##
The only horizontal meridian
“Holds” all vertical meridians in place, supports spine, hips, abdominal organs
Helps coordinate hind and fore body movement

##Imbalances##
Hip or lumbar weakness, poor topline
Abdominal bloating, sluggish digestion
Feeling “stuck,” difficulty moving forward energetically
