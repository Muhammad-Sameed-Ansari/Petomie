
##Pathway##
Runs along the ventral midline (belly/chest), from the genital area up the abdomen, chest, to the mouth.

##Physical function##
Governs the Yin meridians (nourishment, rest, receptivity).
Strongly influences reproductive organs, digestion, and respiratory system.
Helps regulate fluids and supports growth and healing.

##Energetic/emotional meaning##
Represents nurturing, receptivity, self-care, compassion.
Balances emotional sensitivity and maternal/paternal instincts.
Imbalances may show as digestive weakness, reproductive issues, or emotional withdrawal.
