#Third Eye Chakra (Ajna)#

##Location##
Between the eyes, slightly above

##Color##
Indigo

##Element##
Light

##Meaning & Function##
Governs intuition, perception, and awareness
Influences pineal gland, brain, and eyes
Supports telepathic communication and sensing danger

##Signs of Imbalance##
Disorientation, fear of unseen threats, inability to focus
Eye issues, seizures, neurological imbalance
Overly anxious or “spaced out” behavior

##Balancing Activities##
Quiet meditation time with the animal
Energy work over the forehead
Games that encourage problem solving and mental stimulation
