#Crown Chakra (Sahasrara)#

##Location##
Top of the head

##Color##
Violet or White

##Element##
Spirit

##Meaning & Function##
Governs connection to the divine and universal energy
Connects animals to their spiritual purpose
Supports deep peace, trust, and life transitions

##Signs of Imbalance##
Detachment, lack of connection with humans or herd
Chronic stress, inability to settle
Spiritual or emotional trauma after loss

##Balancing Activities##
Gentle energy healing (Reiki, crystal therapy)
Allowing space for rest and introspection
Spending time in calm, spiritually charged environments
