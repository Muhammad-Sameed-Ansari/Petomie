#Heart Chakra (Anahata)#

##Location##
Center of the chest

##Color##
Green (sometimes pink)

##Element##
Air

##Meaning & Function##
Governs love, compassion, and relationships
Regulates heart, lungs, circulation, and thymus
Supports emotional healing and bonding with humans/other animals

##Signs of Imbalance##
Emotional withdrawal, lack of affection, anxiety
Respiratory issues, heart irregularities, immune problems
Difficulty trusting humans after trauma

##Balancing Activities##
Heart-to-heart bonding, slow grooming sessions
Gentle touch therapy (Reiki, TTouch)
Quiet time in nature
