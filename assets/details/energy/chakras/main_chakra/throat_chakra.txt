#Throat Chakra (Vishuddha)#

##Location##
Base of the throat

##Color##
Blue

##Element##
Ether / Sound

##Meaning & Function##
Governs communication, expression, and truth
Influences voice, thyroid, throat, and jaw
Helps animals communicate needs (barking, neighing, meowing)

##Signs of Imbalance##
Excessive vocalization or silence
Thyroid issues, neck tension, coughing
Difficulty expressing discomfort or pain

##Balancing Activities##
Encouraging natural vocal expression (safe howling, neighing)
Singing, soothing sounds, music therapy
Clear, calm verbal cues from humans
