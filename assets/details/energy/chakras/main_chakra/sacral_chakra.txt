#Sacral Chakra (Svadhisthana)#

##Location##
Just in front of the pelvis / lower abdomen

##Color##
Orange

##Element##
Water

##Meaning & Function##
Governs emotions, creativity, and social bonds
Influences reproductive organs, bladder, and lower spine
Supports healthy expression of feelings and playfulness

##Signs of Imbalance##
Aggression or frustration, lack of interest in play
Reproductive issues, urinary problems, hormonal imbalance
Overly submissive or overly dominant behavior

##Balancing Activities##
Play sessions, positive interaction with other animals
Gentle massage over hips/belly
Healthy social environment with safe companions
