#Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)#

##Location##
Middle of the abdomen, behind the diaphragm

##Color##
Yellow

##Element##
Fire

##Meaning & Function##
Governs personal power, confidence, and digestion
Regulates stomach, liver, pancreas, and metabolism
Affects assertiveness and ability to trust leadership

##Signs of Imbalance##
Fearful or overly dominant behavior
Digestive upset, liver stress, weight problems
Difficulty respecting boundaries or following commands

##Balancing Activities##
Confidence-building training
Gentle leadership and clear communication
Sunshine exposure and balanced nutrition
