#Brachial Chakra (Key Animal Chakra)#

In the shoulder area, near where the neck meets the forelegs
Black or sometimes Turquoise
Known as the “Master Chakra” — it links all other chakras and is key for human–animal connection.
Trauma, rescue animals, distrust, or reluctance to be touched can indicate blockage here.
Gentle shoulder massage, energy work, calm presence builds trust.
