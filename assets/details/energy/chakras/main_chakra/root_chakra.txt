#Root Chakra (Muladhara)#

##Location##
Base of the tail / perineum

##Color##
Red

##Element##
Earth

##Meaning & Function##
Governs survival instincts, grounding, and sense of safety
Connects the animal to the earth and its environment
Regulates elimination organs, hind legs, bones, and adrenal glands

##Signs of Imbalance##
Nervousness, restlessness, fearfulness, lack of trust
Digestive issues, weakness in hind legs, hip problems
Overactive fight-or-flight response

##Balancing Activities##
Regular exercise and turnout
Physical touch, grooming, and bonding
Stable environment and consistent routines
