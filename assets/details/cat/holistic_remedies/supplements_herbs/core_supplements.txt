#Core Supplements#

Core supplements focus on foundational support and are commonly used repeatedly in holistic and integrative care. For cats, these supplements are chosen very conservatively and are often best assessed through energetic testing before physical use.

For best outcomes, it is very important to choose supplements that are free from alcohol, artificial additives, fillers, colors, and flavors.

##Important Cat Safety Note##
Many supplements suitable for dogs or humans are not appropriate for cats. Energetic testing is strongly recommended, and physical use should always be supervised by a qualified veterinarian.
