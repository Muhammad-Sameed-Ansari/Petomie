#Deep Layer Core Stability#

The cat’s deep muscular layer forms the foundation of core stability, lying closest to the spine and internal structures.  
These muscles support posture, protect vital organs, and allow controlled, precise movement.  
Energetically, they represent **inner strength, resilience, and grounded presence**.

###Parts###

##Spinal Stabilizers##  
Small muscles along the vertebrae that maintain alignment.  
Energetically, symbolize **support, integrity, and inner guidance**.

##Deep Abdominal Fibers##  
Wrap horizontally to secure the core.  
Energetically, reflect **containment, safety, and emotional steadiness**.

##Pelvic Support Muscles##  
Anchor the pelvis and sacrum.  
Energetically, embody **rootedness and trust in foundation**.

###Function###  
Provide postural support, protect organs, and stabilize every movement from within.  
Energetically, they anchor the cat’s **center of power and calm**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weakness: poor posture, compensation, or instability.  
Tension: stress, guarding, or unresolved trauma.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **shaken foundations or inner insecurity**.
