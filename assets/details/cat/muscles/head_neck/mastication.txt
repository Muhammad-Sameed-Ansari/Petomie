#Mastication Muscles#

The cat’s mastication muscles control jaw movement for biting, chewing, and vocalization.  
They are strong and coordinated, supporting feeding and defense.  
Energetically, they represent **decision, strength, and emotional processing** — the power to take in and let go.

###Parts###

##Masseter##  
Elevates the jaw for chewing.  
Energetically, symbolizes **power, stability, and control**.

##Temporalis##  
Closes the jaw with strength and precision.  
Energetically, reflects **determination and focus**.

##Pterygoid Muscles##  
Aid in side-to-side and fine jaw movements.  
Energetically, represent **adaptation and balance**.

###Function###  
These muscles manage food intake, defense, and vocal expression.  
Energetically, they transform **physical nourishment into emotional and energetic strength**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tightness: teeth grinding or tension.  
Pain: imbalance in bite alignment.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **anger suppression or inner resistance**.
