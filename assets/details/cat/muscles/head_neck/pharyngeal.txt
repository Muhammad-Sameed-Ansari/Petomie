##Constrictor Muscles##

##Stylopharyngeus##

###Function###  
These muscles guide food safely to the esophagus and coordinate with the larynx and tongue.  
Energetically, they create harmony between **taking in and letting go**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Difficulty swallowing: muscular tightness or inflammation.  
Weakness: aspiration or feeding issues.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **fear of transition or resistance to flow**.
