##Soleus##  
Assists in extending the hock and maintaining posture.  
Energetically, reflects **stability and endurance**.

##Flexor and Extensor Groups##  
Control precise movement of the hock and digits.  
Energetically, represent **balance between action and rest**.

##Tibialis Cranialis##

Energetically, embodies **clarity, direction, and lightness**.

###Function###  
The crus muscles provide coordinated power and control during running, jumping, and landing.  
Energetically, they maintain **rhythm and fluidity between motion and grounding**.
