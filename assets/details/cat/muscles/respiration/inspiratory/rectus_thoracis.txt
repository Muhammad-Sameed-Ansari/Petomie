#Rectus Thoracis#

##Location##
Situated along the **ventral thoracic wall**, extending from the **first rib** to approximately the **fourth rib**.  
Lies adjacent to the sternum and continues with the rectus abdominis muscle.

###Shape###
A narrow, strap-like muscle running parallel to the sternum with longitudinal fibers.

##Function##
Assists **inspiration** by pulling the ribs forward to increase thoracic volume.  
Provides ventral support and stability to the thoracic wall.
