#Rectus Thoracis#

The rectus thoracis is a thin muscle extending from the first rib to the second or third costal cartilage.  
It aids in inspiration by elevating the ribs and stabilizing the thoracic wall.  
Energetically, it represents initiative, alertness, and inner readiness.

###Parts###

##Upper Portion##  
Attaches to the first rib for lift.  
Energetically, symbolizes readiness and new beginnings.

##Lower Portion##  
Connects to the upper ribs for coordinated expansion.  
Energetically, reflects harmony and cooperative action.

###Function###  
The rectus thoracis helps expand the upper chest during inhalation.  
Energetically, it awakens awareness and presence in each breath.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tightness: limited chest motion.  
Weakness: poor upper rib expansion.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect hesitation or emotional suppression.
