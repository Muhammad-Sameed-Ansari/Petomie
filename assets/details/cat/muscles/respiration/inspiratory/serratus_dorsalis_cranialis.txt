#Serratus Dorsalis Cranialis#

##Location##
Found on the **upper thoracic wall**, beneath the trapezius and rhomboideus.  
Extends from the **thoracolumbar fascia** to the **first few ribs**.

###Shape###
Thin, fan-shaped muscle with **fibers running caudoventrally**.  
Covers the upper portion of the ribs near the vertebral column.

##Function##
Draws the cranial ribs forward and upward, expanding the thoracic cavity.  
Assists in **inspiration** and supports rib movement during breathing.
