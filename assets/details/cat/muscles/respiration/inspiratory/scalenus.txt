#Scalenus#

##Location##
Located along the **lateral neck**, connecting the **cervical vertebrae** to the **first few ribs**.  
Lies deep to the brachiocephalicus and omotransversarius muscles.

###Shape###
A long, strap-like muscle composed of dorsal, middle, and ventral portions.  
Fibers run obliquely downward and backward.

##Function##
Assists **inspiration** by elevating the first ribs.  
Stabilizes the neck and thoracic inlet during respiration.  
Helps in neck flexion when both sides contract together.
