#External Intercostal Muscles#

##Location##
Situated between adjacent ribs, extending from the **caudal border of one rib** to the **cranial border of the next**.  
Found throughout the intercostal spaces except near the sternum.

###Shape###
Flat sheets of muscle with **fibers running caudoventrally** (downward and forward).  
Lie superficial to the internal intercostal muscles.

##Function##
Aid **inspiration** by elevating the ribs and expanding the thoracic cavity.  
Work in unison with the diaphragm to increase lung volume and maintain rib spacing.
