#Diaphragm#

##Location##
Forms the muscular partition between the **thoracic and abdominal cavities**, attaching to the **sternum, ribs, and lumbar vertebrae**.  
Separates the lungs and heart from the abdominal organs.

###Shape###
A **thin, dome-shaped muscle** with a central tendon and muscular periphery.  
The convex surface faces the thoracic cavity, while the concave side faces the abdomen.

##Function##
Primary muscle of **inspiration**.  
Contracts and flattens to expand the thoracic cavity and draw air into the lungs.  
Also assists with abdominal pressure during defecation, urination, and parturition.
