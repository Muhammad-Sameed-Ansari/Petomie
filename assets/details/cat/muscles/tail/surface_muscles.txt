###Parts###

##Superficial Dorsal Muscles##  
Lift and arch the tail upward.  
Energetically, symbolize **confidence and alertness**.

##Superficial Ventral Muscles##  
Pull the tail downward and guide emotional tone.  
Energetically, reflect **surrender and relaxation**.

##Lateral Fibers##  
Create side-to-side movements.  
Energetically, embody **adaptability and communication**.

###Function###  
The surface muscles provide visible motion and emotional expression.  
Energetically, they allow **the flow of communication through movement and sensitivity**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tension: emotional suppression or stress.  
Weakness: reduced expressiveness.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **conflict between control and release**.
