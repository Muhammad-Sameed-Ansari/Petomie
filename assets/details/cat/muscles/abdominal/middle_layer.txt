##Location##
Between the deep and superficial layers, covering the flanks and sides of the abdomen.  
Acts as the bridge between internal support and external movement.

###Shape###
Made of **broad, flat muscles** running diagonally across the abdomen.  
These layers overlap to provide both flexibility and strength.

##Parts##

###1. Internal Abdominal Oblique###
- Lies beneath the external oblique.  
- **Function:** Rotates trunk, assists side bending, and compresses abdominal cavity.  

###2. Iliocostalis###
- Extends from pelvis to ribs.  
- **Function:** Aids posture and movement along the body axis.  

##Function##
- **Coordination:** Transfers energy between trunk and limbs.  
- **Flexibility:** Allows smooth bending and twisting.  
- **Support:** Reinforces deep layers.  
- **Stability:** Helps keep torso balanced during motion.
