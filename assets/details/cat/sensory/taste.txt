#Taste System#

The cat’s taste system is unique, adapted for detecting protein and amino acids rather than sweetness.  
It helps ensure safety in food selection and influences appetite.  
Energetically, it represents **discernment, nourishment, and self-awareness** — knowing what truly sustains body and soul.

###Parts###

##Tongue##  
Covered with papillae that assist in grooming and tasting.  
Energetically, symbolizes **expression and sensory awareness**.

##Taste Buds##  
Located mainly on the sides and back of the tongue, detecting savory and bitter tastes.  
Energetically, reflect **discernment and intuitive nourishment**.

##Soft Palate##  
Supports swallowing and taste perception.  
Energetically, embodies **adaptation and receptivity**.

##Nerves (Facial, Glossopharyngeal, and Vagus)**  
Transmit taste signals to the brain.  
Energetically, symbolize **communication between senses and spirit**.

###Function###  
The taste system allows the cat to identify safe and nutritious food, influencing feeding behavior and digestion.  
Energetically, it encourages **trust in inner guidance and connection to true nourishment**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Loss of taste: illness or medication side effects.  
Selective appetite: emotional imbalance or environmental stress.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **resistance, disinterest, or blocked enjoyment of life**.
