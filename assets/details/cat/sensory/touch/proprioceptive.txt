#Proprioceptive Hearing Structures#

##Location##
Located in the inner ear within the temporal bone, connected to the brain through the auditory nerve.

###Shape###
- Cochlea: Spiral structure that interprets sound frequencies.  
- Vestibular System: Three semicircular canals and vestibule controlling balance.  
- Auditory Nerve: Pathway carrying auditory signals to the brain.

##Function##
- Converts sound vibrations into electrical impulses.  
- Provides precise orientation and spatial awareness.  
- Integrates hearing with balance and movement control.  
- Supports hunting and rapid coordination.
