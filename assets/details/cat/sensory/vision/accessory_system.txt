###Parts###

##Eyelids##
Protect the eye from injury, debris, and excessive light while spreading tears evenly.
Energetically, symbolize boundaries, discernment, and emotional protection.

##Eyelashes##
Help prevent dust and foreign particles from entering the eye.
Energetically, reflect alertness and subtle defense.

##Conjunctiva##
A thin mucous membrane lining the eyelids and covering the sclera.
Provides lubrication and immune protection.
Energetically, represents adaptability and gentle protection.

##Nictitating Membrane (Third Eyelid)##
Provides additional protection and moisture, especially during movement or stress.
Energetically, symbolizes instinctive protection and subconscious awareness.

##Lacrimal Glands##
Produce tears to lubricate, cleanse, and nourish the eye.
Energetically, represent emotional release, cleansing, and regulation.

##Nasolacrimal Duct (Tear Ducts)##
Drain excess tears into the nasal cavity.
Energetically, reflect emotional flow and release.

##Cornea##
Transparent outer layer that allows light to enter the eye and aids focus.
Energetically, embodies clarity and perception.

##Sclera##
The firm outer layer providing structure and protection.
Energetically, symbolizes stability and resilience.

##Iris##
Controls pupil size and regulates light intake.
Energetically, represents adaptability and balance.

##Lens##
Focuses light onto the retina.
Energetically, reflects focus, precision, and perspective.

##Ciliary Body##
Adjusts lens shape and produces aqueous humor.
Energetically, symbolizes flexibility and adjustment.

##Extraocular Muscles##
Control eye movement and alignment.
Energetically, represent direction, coordination, and attention.

###Function###
Protect the eye, maintain moisture and cleanliness, regulate light entry, and support sharp vision and eye movement.
Energetically, these structures foster awareness, safety, intuition, and confidence in perception.

###Common Imbalances###
Eye discharge, conjunctivitis, corneal irritation, dry eye, or light sensitivity.
Energetically, imbalance may reflect overstimulation, guardedness, or heightened vigilance.
