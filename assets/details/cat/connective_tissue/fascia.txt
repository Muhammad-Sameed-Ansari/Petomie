#Fascia#

In cats, fascia is a continuous web of connective tissue that surrounds muscles, organs, and bones.  
It maintains body structure and transmits energy between systems.  
Energetically, it represents **connection, unity, and flow**.

###Parts###

##Superficial Fascia##  
Lies beneath the skin and stores fat and fluid.  
Energetically, it symbolizes **protection and sensitivity**.

##Deep Fascia##  
Encases muscles, aiding in coordination and strength.  
Energetically, it reflects **alignment and flow of energy**.

##Visceral Fascia##  
Supports organs and maintains internal balance.  
Energetically, it represents **inner harmony and communication**.

###Function###  
Fascia integrates all body parts, transmits mechanical and energetic forces, and aids healing.  
Energetically, it governs **connection, awareness, and emotional coherence**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tightness: stress or emotional tension.  
Adhesions: trapped emotions or stagnation.  
Weakness: disconnection or energy leakage.
