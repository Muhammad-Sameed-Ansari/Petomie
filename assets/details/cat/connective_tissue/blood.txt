#Blood#

In cats, blood is a vital connective tissue that transports oxygen, nutrients, hormones, and immune cells throughout the body.  
It flows through a closed circulatory system, maintaining balance and vitality.  
Energetically, it represents **life force, flow, and emotional vitality**.

###Parts###

##Red Blood Cells##  
Carry oxygen from the lungs to tissues.  
Energetically, they symbolize **action and vitality**.

##White Blood Cells##  
Defend against infection and maintain immune strength.  
Energetically, they reflect **protection and purification**.

##Platelets##  
Aid in clotting and healing of wounds.  
Energetically, they embody **restoration and resilience**.

##Plasma##  
The liquid portion carrying nutrients and waste.  
Energetically, it represents **fluidity and emotional balance**.

###Function###  
Blood sustains tissues, supports temperature regulation, and enables healing.  
Energetically, it governs **circulation of life, vitality, and emotional flow**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Anemia: energy depletion or sadness.  
Thick blood: stagnation or emotional suppression.  
Immune weakness: vulnerability or energetic exhaustion.
