#Lymph#

In cats, lymph is a clear fluid circulating through vessels and nodes, carrying immune cells and removing waste.  
It supports detoxification and defense.  
Energetically, it represents **cleansing, flow, and emotional release**.

###Parts###

##Lymph Fluid##  
Carries immune cells and collects toxins.  
Energetically, it symbolizes **purification and clarity**.

##Lymph Nodes##  
Filter harmful substances and produce immune cells.  
Energetically, they reflect **discernment and boundary strength**.

##Lymph Vessels##  
Transport lymph fluid throughout the body.  
Energetically, they represent **pathways of emotional flow**.

###Function###  
The lymphatic system maintains immunity, fluid balance, and detoxification.  
Energetically, it governs **release, renewal, and cleansing of emotions**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling: emotional holding or stagnation.  
Infection: overload of toxins or stress.  
Weak drainage: fatigue or lack of release.
