#Tendons#

In cats, tendons are dense connective tissues that attach muscles to bones.  
They transmit muscular force to create movement.  
Energetically, they represent **strength, resilience, and willpower**.

###Parts###

##Flexor Tendons##  
Enable curling and gripping of paws.  
Energetically, they symbolize **control and expression**.

##Extensor Tendons##  
Straighten limbs and extend movement.  
Energetically, they reflect **expansion and assertiveness**.

##Elastic Tendons##  
Assist in jumping and flexibility.  
Energetically, they embody **spring, readiness, and adaptability**.

###Function###  
Tendons support movement, coordination, and power transfer.  
Energetically, they uphold **purpose, courage, and flow of strength**.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Tightness: control issues or emotional strain.  
- Inflammation: overuse or frustration.  
- Weakness: lack of drive or confidence.
