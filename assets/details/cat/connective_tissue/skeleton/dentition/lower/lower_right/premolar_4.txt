#Premolar 4#

##Location##
Located behind the third premolar on the right lower jaw, working with the upper carnassial tooth for cutting meat.

###Shape###
A strong, multi-cusped tooth with broad cutting edges.

###Function###
Cuts and crushes food effectively.  
Energetically represents endurance, teamwork, and harmony in exchange.
