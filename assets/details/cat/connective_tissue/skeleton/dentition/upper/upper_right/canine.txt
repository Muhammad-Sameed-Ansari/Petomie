#Canine#

##Location##
Located behind the incisors on the upper right jaw, firmly rooted and curved for strength.  
Used for gripping, tearing, and maintaining jaw balance.

###Shape###
A long, pointed tooth with a strong base for power and control.

###Function###
Crucial for catching prey and protecting the mouth structure.  
Energetically represents confidence, will, and self-expression.
