#Costal Cartilage#

##Location##
The costal cartilage connects the ribs to the sternum, forming the flexible part of the cat’s ribcage.
It allows the thoracic cavity to expand and contract during breathing.

###Shape###
Smooth, elastic cartilage that curves inward toward the sternum and blends gradually with each rib.

###Function###
Provides flexibility and elasticity for breathing while protecting the heart and lungs.
Energetically, represents adaptability, openness, and emotional flow.
