#Intercostal Muscles#

##Location##
The intercostal muscles fill the spaces between the ribs, forming part of the cat’s thoracic wall.
They are divided into external and internal layers.

###Shape###
Thin sheets of muscle fibers running diagonally between the ribs, aiding in expansion and contraction of the ribcage.

###Function###
Assist in breathing by lifting and lowering the ribs during inhalation and exhalation.
Energetically, they represent rhythm, flow, and harmony between strength and softness.
