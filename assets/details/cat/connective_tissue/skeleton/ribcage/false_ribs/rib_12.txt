#Rib 12#

##Location##
Rib 12 belongs to the group known as false ribs, which include ribs 10 to 12 in the cat.
These ribs do not attach directly to the sternum but connect to each other through cartilage, forming the flexible rear section of the ribcage.
They provide both protection and mobility to the lower thoracic area.

###Shape###
Curved and slightly flattened bones that taper toward the ventral end, allowing expansion and flexibility during breathing.

###Function###
Protect the lungs and upper abdominal organs while supporting smooth respiratory movement.
Energetically, they symbolize adaptability, emotional flow, and the ability to remain flexible through change.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Heart and Solar Plexus Chakras.
Meridian: Lung and Liver Meridians.
Emotional Meaning: Openness, balance, and freedom of breath.
Balanced Energy: Calm, rhythmic, and emotionally open.

###Common Imbalances###
Tightness or restriction in breathing or side movement.
Energetically, resistance to emotional openness or flexibility.
