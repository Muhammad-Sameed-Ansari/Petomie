#Incisor 2#

##Location##
Positioned next to the first incisor on the upper left jaw.  
Supports the alignment of the front teeth and helps maintain balance during biting.

###Shape###
Slightly larger than the first incisor, with a flat crown and narrow edge.

###Function###
Assists in grooming and guiding food into the mouth.  
Energetically represents adaptability and gentle strength.
