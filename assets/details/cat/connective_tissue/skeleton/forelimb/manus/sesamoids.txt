#Sesamoids#

##Location##  
Small bones embedded within tendons near the **joints of the digits and paws**.  
They are found near the metacarpophalangeal and interphalangeal joints.  

###Shape###  
**Tiny, rounded bones** that vary in number and shape, reducing friction and supporting smooth tendon motion.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal Sesamoids##  
Located near the base of the proximal phalanges, assisting in joint leverage.  
Energetically, symbolize **support and flexibility**.  

##Distal Sesamoids##  
Found near the claws, cushioning and guiding tendon movement.  
Energetically, reflect **adaptability and flow**.  

###Function###  
Reduce friction, protect tendons, and enhance mechanical efficiency during movement.  
Energetically, maintain **fluidity and smooth transitions**.
