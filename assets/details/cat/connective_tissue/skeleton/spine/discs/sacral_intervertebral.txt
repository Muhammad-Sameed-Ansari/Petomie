#Sacral Intervertebral Discs#

##Location##
Between sacral vertebrae before natural fusion occurs; form a single fused sacrum in adult cats.

###Shape###
Small fibrocartilage pads early in life, later becoming part of the solid sacral plate.

###Parts###

##Annulus Fibrosus##
Thin fibrocartilage ring offering stability while maintaining flexibility.
Energetically represents boundaries, containment, and emotional steadiness.

##Nucleus Pulposus##
Soft cushioning core absorbing shock and supporting fluid movement.
Energetically reflects adaptability and emotional flow.

##Individual Discs##
###S1–S2 Region###
Early development cushioning before fusion. Energetically tied to foundational stability.

###S2–S3 Region###
Forms mid-sacral support. Energetically relates to structural balance.

###S3–S4 Region###
Supports early pelvic formation. Energetically aids steady emotional development.


###Function###
Early-life cushioning; fused sacrum provides pelvic and hind-end stability.
