#Tibia#

##Location##  
Located in the **hind limb**, extending from the **stifle (knee)** to the **hock (tarsus)**.  
It lies medial to the fibula and serves as the **primary weight-bearing bone** of the lower leg.  

###Shape###  
A **long, strong bone** with expanded ends for articulation and a slightly curved shaft for strength and flexibility.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal End##  
Forms the stifle joint with the femur.  
Energetically, symbolizes **foundation and initiation of movement**.  

##Shaft##  
Supports and transmits weight along the limb.  
Energetically, reflects **stability and balance**.  

##Distal End##  
Connects with the tarsal bones to form the hock joint.  
Energetically, represents **transition and adaptability**.  

###Function###  
Supports body weight, forms part of major joints, and enables locomotion.  
Energetically, maintains **stability, alignment, and flow of grounded energy**.
