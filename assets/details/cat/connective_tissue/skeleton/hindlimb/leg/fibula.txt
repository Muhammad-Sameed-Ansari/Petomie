#Fibula#

##Location##  
Found in the **hind limb**, running parallel to the **tibia** on the lateral side.  
It extends from the **stifle** to the **hock**, providing structural support and muscle attachment.  

###Shape###  
A **slender, elongated bone** that tapers toward its ends, aiding in flexibility and lightness of motion.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal End##  
Articulates with the tibia near the stifle.  
Energetically, symbolizes **connection and support**.  

##Shaft##  
Serves as an anchor for muscles of the lower leg.  
Energetically, represents **endurance and stability**.  

##Distal End##  
Connects with the tarsal bones, completing the hock structure.  
Energetically, reflects **balance and coordination**.  

###Function###  
Provides muscle attachment, lateral balance, and assists in stabilizing the hock.  
Energetically, governs **resilience, adaptability, and subtle strength**.
