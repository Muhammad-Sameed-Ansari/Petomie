#Ligaments#

In cats, ligaments are strong bands of connective tissue that connect bones and stabilize joints.  
They guide movement while preventing overextension.  
Energetically, they represent **stability, trust, and support**.

###Parts###

##Joint Ligaments##  
Bind bones at the joints for alignment and balance.  
Energetically, they symbolize **security and integrity**.

##Vertebral Ligaments##  
Maintain spinal alignment and flexibility.  
Energetically, they reflect **backbone and strength**.

##Suspensory Ligaments##  
Support organs and muscle attachments.  
Energetically, they embody **support and grounding**.

###Function###  
Ligaments provide stability and coordination in motion.  
Energetically, they maintain **confidence and trust in movement**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Overstretching: lack of boundaries or over-giving.  
Stiffness: rigidity or fear of change.  
Weakness: instability or loss of direction.
