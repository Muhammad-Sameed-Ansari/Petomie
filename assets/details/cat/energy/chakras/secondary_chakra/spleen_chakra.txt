#Spleen Chakra#

##Location##
Left side of the body, near the spleen

##Color##
Golden Yellow

##Function##
Filters external energies, prevents emotional overload
Helps cats adapt to changes in environment

##Imbalances##
Hiding, refusing food during stressful changes
Overly sensitive to household emotions
Digestive upset or stress-related illness

##Balancing##
Gradual introduction to new situations or spaces
Calming music, pheromone therapy, or energy clearing
Gentle reassurance without forcing interaction
