#Spleen Chakra#

###Location###
Located on the left side of the body behind the ribs, over the spleen region.

###Color###
Yellow / Gold

###Element###
Earth / Fire

###Meaning & Function###
The Spleen Chakra governs vitality, immunity, emotional nourishment, and stress recovery.
In cats, it helps regulate blood energy, supports emotional resilience, and stabilizes the nervous system.

##Connection##
Spleen and lymphatic system
Blood vitality and nourishment
Emotional processing and stress balance

###Signs of Balance###
Steady energy levels
Healthy immune response
Calm, centered emotional state
Smooth recovery after stress

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Lethargy or low motivation
Emotional fragility
Stress-induced behaviors or withdrawal

##Physical##
Weak immune system
Lymphatic congestion
Fatigue or poor recovery after illness

###Balancing Activities###
Warm resting spots
Soft touch around rib area (if tolerated)
Low-stress environment
Yellow or gold crystals placed nearby
