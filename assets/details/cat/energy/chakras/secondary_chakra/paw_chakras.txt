#Paw Chakras (4)#

##Location##
Center of each paw pad

##Color##
Dark Brown or Black

##Function##
Provide grounding, balance, and environmental sensing
Essential for climbing, stalking, and navigating territory

##Imbalances##
Clumsiness, hesitation to jump or climb
Paw licking or obsessive cleaning
Sensitivity to surfaces or textures

##Balancing##
Opportunities for climbing, scratching, and safe exploration
Paw massage or gentle acupressure
Ensuring litter and surfaces feel safe and comfortable
