#Tail Chakra#

###Location###
Located at the base of the tail, extending through the entire tail and its energetic field.

###Color###
Deep Red / Black

###Element###
Earth

###Meaning & Function###
The Tail Chakra governs energetic release, grounding, emotional expression, and balance.
In cats, this chakra is essential for emotional communication, territory confidence, and stabilizing the nervous system.

##Connection##
Tail base, sacrum, lower spine
Grounding pathways
Emotional release and boundaries

###Signs of Balance###
Relaxed, expressive tail movement
Clear emotional communication
Stable and grounded energy
Comfort exploring and interacting

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Tail clamping
Sudden agitation
Nervous or defensive tail movement
Difficulty releasing stress

##Physical##
Lower spine tension
Tail stiffness
Sensitivity at the base of the tail

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle energy work near tail base
Safe, quiet rest spaces
Grounding contact or soft presence
Earth-toned crystals in the room
