#Tail Chakra#

##Location##
Tip of the tail

##Color##
Deep Red or Brown

##Function##
Releases excess energy, maintains balance, communicates emotional state
Tail position and movement reflect mood and intention

##Imbalances##
Tail tucked tightly (fear), or puffed up frequently (anxiety)
Overgrooming or biting the tail
Tension or stiffness in hind end

##Balancing##
Play sessions to release pent-up energy
Soft stroking along the tail, energy sweeping
Calming routine and safe hiding spaces
