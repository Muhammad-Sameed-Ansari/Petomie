#Brachial Chakra#

###Location###
Located in front of the shoulder where the neck meets the body.
Extends into the shoulder joint, chest inlet, and surrounding energetic field.

###Color###
Turquoise / Aquamarine

###Element###
Ether / Air

###Meaning & Function###
The Brachial Chakra is the central human–animal bonding chakra in cats.
It governs trust, emotional connection, intuitive communication, and relational safety.
Through this chakra, cats sense human intention, form bonds, and choose how deeply they connect.
It also influences willingness, affection, and comfort with being handled.

##Connection##
Shoulder, scapula, chest inlet
Front limb meridians
Emotional bonding and trust
Intuitive communication and relational awareness

###Signs of Balance###
Soft, receptive body language
Willingness to connect and be close
Calm curiosity toward humans
Balanced independence with affection
Relaxed shoulders and forelimbs

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Avoidance of touch or being picked up
Difficulty bonding or trusting
Inconsistent affection (push–pull behavior)
Irritability near shoulders
Over-sensitivity to human emotions

##Physical##
Shoulder tension or stiffness
Restricted forelimb movement
Sensitivity at neck–shoulder junction
Reduced willingness to climb or jump

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle shoulder strokes and slow, soft touch
Calm bonding time without pressure
Energetic presence near shoulder area
Soft verbal interaction and intention-setting
Turquoise crystals placed in the room (not on the cat)
Providing choice in interaction to build trust
