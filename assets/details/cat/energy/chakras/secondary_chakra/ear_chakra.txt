#Ear Chakra#

###Location###
Located inside and around each ear, extending outward into the auditory and intuitive field.

###Color###
Light Blue / Lavender

###Element###
Air / Ether

###Meaning & Function###
The Ear Chakra governs hearing, intuition, orientation, alertness, and energetic perception.
Cats use this chakra to sense subtle vibrations, emotional tones, and environmental cues.

##Connection##
Inner and outer ear
Vestibular system
Intuitive and sensory pathways

###Signs of Balance###
Calm attentiveness
Smooth ear movement
Clear focus without overstimulation
Balanced reaction to sound

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Hypervigilance
Overreacting to noise
Difficulty focusing
Fear reactions without cause

##Physical##
Ear sensitivity
Vestibular imbalance
Head shaking or tension around ears

###Balancing Activities###
Soft, slow strokes around ears
Quiet, predictable environments
Energetic clearing around head
Lavender or pale blue crystals placed nearby
