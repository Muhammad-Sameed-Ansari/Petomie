#Flank Chakra#

###Location###
Located on each side of the ribcage between the lower ribs and the abdomen.
These are paired chakras, one on the left side and one on the right side.

###Color###
Orange / Gold

###Element###
Fire / Water

###Meaning & Function###
The Flank Chakras govern emotional processing, sensitivity, flexibility, body awareness, and fluid movement.
In cats, these chakras help regulate emotional tension, support smooth motion, and maintain comfort with touch and handling.
They also store emotional experiences related to fear responses, sudden stress, or territorial tension.

##Connection##
Lateral abdomen and ribcage
Oblique muscles and core
Emotional centers (sacral and solar plexus chakras)
Flexibility, movement, and sensory awareness

###Signs of Balance###
Relaxed response to flank touch
Smooth twisting and stretching
Comfortable posture during grooming
Calm, emotionally balanced behavior

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Flinching or guarding on one or both sides
Sensitivity during grooming or lifting
Avoidance of touch or sudden withdrawal
Emotional tension such as irritability or defensiveness

##Physical##
Ribcage or abdominal tightness
Core stiffness
Side-specific movement limitations
Tension stored from past injuries or stress

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle brushing or stroking along the flanks
Slow stretching and natural feline movement
Calm, confidence-building play
Soft energetic clearing along ribcage lines
Warm, safe resting spots to release tension
