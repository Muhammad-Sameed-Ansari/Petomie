#Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)#

###Location###
Located in the upper abdomen, between the ribs and the navel.

###Color###
Yellow

###Element###
Fire

###Meaning & Function###
The Solar Plexus supports the cat’s confidence, independence, alertness, and emotional boundaries.
It governs personal power, self-assurance, and the ability to feel safe while maintaining autonomy.
This chakra fuels curiosity, inner strength, and environmental awareness.

##Connection##
Stomach, liver, pancreas
Upper digestive system
Core body strength and balance
Confidence, independence, and emotional resilience

###Signs of Balance###
Healthy independence with affectionate connection
Strong curiosity and alert yet calm behavior
Good appetite and digestive comfort
Confident movement and body posture

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Timidity, hiding, or low self-confidence
Irritability or sudden defensive behavior
Overcontrol of space or territory
Overreactivity to environmental changes

##Physical##
Digestive disturbances or poor appetite
Liver or pancreatic stress
Tension in abdominal region
Fatigue or overstimulation

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle confidence-building play
Predictable routines and territory stability
Warmth or light touch on the upper abdomen
Safe spaces to observe and retreat
Yellow crystals placed nearby (citrine, tiger’s eye)
