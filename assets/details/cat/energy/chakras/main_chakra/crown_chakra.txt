#Crown Chakra (Sahasrara)#

###Location###
Located at the top of the head, extending upward around the ears and upper skull.

###Color###
Violet / White

###Element###
Spirit / Universal Energy

###Meaning & Function###
The Crown Chakra governs spiritual connection, intuitive perception, and higher awareness.
It helps cats feel safe, guided, and balanced within their inner world.
This chakra enhances serenity, intuitive understanding, and emotional harmony.

##Connection##
Top of head and upper skull
Energetic intuitive network
Spiritual awareness and deep perception
Emotional calm and balance

###Signs of Balance###
Calm, centered presence
Strong intuitive confidence
Smooth emotional regulation
Comfort in quiet or meditative environments

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Nervousness or energetic overstimulation
Appearing disconnected or “far away”
Difficulty grounding or focusing
Startle responses without clear triggers

##Physical##
Head or ear sensitivity
Sleep disturbances
Neurological tension
Restlessness or fatigue

###Balancing Activities###
Quiet resting spaces
Gentle touch on top of head
Soft, calming interaction
Energetic grounding through the environment
Violet crystals placed safely nearby
