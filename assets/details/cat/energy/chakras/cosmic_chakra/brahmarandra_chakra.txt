#Brahmarandra Chakra#

###Location###
Located at the top center of the skull, slightly behind the crown chakra.

###Color###
White / Violet / Pale Gold

###Element###
Light / Ether

###Meaning & Function###
A sacred point of divine connection.
Supports spiritual awakening, heightened intuition, energetic sensitivity, and emotional purity.

##Connection##
Crown and intuitive centers
Nervous system
Spiritual channels

###Signs of Balance###
Deep serenity
Heightened awareness
Calm understanding
Peaceful behavior

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Energetic overwhelm
Withdrawn behavior
Overreactivity to subtle energy

##Physical##
Head sensitivity
Neck or poll tension
Sleep disturbances

###Balancing Activities###
Soft stroking on top of head
Calm bonding time
Light visualization work
Gentle, quiet environment
