##Shape##
A long, lateral meridian running from the head down the side of the body to the outer hind toe.
Closely associated with the muscles of movement and coordination, and energetically linked to decision-making and courage.

Supports liver function and detoxification
Promotes smooth flow of Qi throughout the body
Influences balance, coordination, and muscle tone
