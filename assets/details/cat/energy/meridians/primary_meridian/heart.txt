#Heart Meridian#

##Location##
Begins deep within the chest, connecting with the heart and small intestine before emerging at the axilla (armpit) region (HT-1).
Travels along the medial side of the forelimb, running through the groove between the biceps brachii and triceps brachii muscles.
Passes over the medial aspect of the elbow and forearm, following the flexor muscles.
Continues down the forelimb and ends at the caudomedial side of the front paw, at the tip of the fifth digit (HT-9).
