#Conception Vessel (Ren Mai)#

##Location##
Begins at the perineum, just below the anus (CV-1).
Ascends along the midline of the ventral body, passing through the genital area and the ventral abdomen.
Continues upward along the chest, over the sternum, and through the throat.
Ends at the depression between the lower lip and the chin (CV-24).
Internally, it connects with the uterus, bladder, stomach, and heart.

##Shape##
A single, ventral midline meridian running from the perineum up the underside of the body to the chin.
Known as the “Sea of Yin,” it nourishes all Yin meridians and governs the body’s nurturing, cooling, and restorative functions.

##Function##
Regulates Yin energy and reproductive health
Supports respiration, digestion, and hormonal balance
Nourishes the organs of the lower abdomen and chest
Strengthens emotional stability, trust, and self-care
Balances cycles, fertility, and mothering instincts

##Signs of Imbalance##
Reproductive or urinary problems
Respiratory weakness or shallow breathing
Digestive sluggishness or bloating
Weakness of ventral muscles or loss of tone
Emotional depletion, insecurity, or lack of self-nurture
