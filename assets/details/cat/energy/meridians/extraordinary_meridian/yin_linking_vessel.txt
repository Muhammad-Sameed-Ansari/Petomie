#Yin Linking Vessel (Yin Wei Mai)#

##Location##
Begins near the medial side of the hind limb at Kidney 9 (KI-9).
Ascends along the medial thigh and enters the abdomen, traveling deep through the lower belly.
Continues upward along the ventral chest and through the throat, connecting internally with the Heart, Pericardium, and Conception Vessel.
Ends at the throat region near Conception Vessel 22 (CV-22).

##Shape##

##Function##
Harmonizes and supports the Yin meridians (Lung, Heart, Spleen, Liver, Kidney)
Balances emotions related to inner peace, trust, and compassion
Nourishes the Heart and stabilizes emotional energy
Supports hormonal and reproductive harmony
Encourages introspection, healing, and emotional resilience

Hormonal or reproductive irregularities

A tendency to feel overwhelmed or overly sensitive
