#Yang Linking Vessel (Yang Wei Mai)#

##Location##
Begins near the lateral side of the hind limb at Bladder 63 (BL-63).
Ascends along the outer thigh and flank, connecting with the Gall Bladder Meridian.
Continues upward over the lateral side of the ribs and chest, traveling to the shoulder and neck.
Passes around the back of the ear and temple region, ending at the outer corner of the forehead near Gall Bladder 13 (GB-13).
Internally, it connects with the Gall Bladder, Triple Heater, and all Yang meridians.

##Shape##
A lateral and ascending meridian linking all Yang channels of the body.
Flows along the outer surface, harmonizing external activity, movement, and protective (Wei) energy.

##Function##

Balances external and internal activity and responsiveness
Supports mobility, coordination, and vitality
