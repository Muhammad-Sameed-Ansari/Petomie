#Governing Vessel (Du Mai)#

The **Extraordinary Meridians** are special channels in **Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM)** that act as reservoirs of Qi and Blood.  
They regulate, store, and distribute energy throughout the body, maintaining harmony between the twelve Primary Meridians.  
Unlike the main meridians, they do not correspond to a specific organ but rather balance the entire energetic network.  
In cats, they represent deep instinctive wisdom, energetic protection, and alignment between spirit and body.  

The **Governing Vessel (Du Mai)** governs **Yang energy, vitality, and spinal flow**.  
It supports courage, movement, and spiritual awareness.  

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins at the base of the tail, travels along the midline of the spine, passes over the head, and ends at the upper lip.  
This upward movement symbolizes awakening, strength, and the rising of life force through the spine.  

###Function###  
Physically, it influences the spinal cord, brain, and nervous system.  
It regulates posture, coordination, and energy flow along the back.  
Energetically, it unites all Yang meridians, empowering the cat with alertness, confidence, and spiritual connection.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Back pain, stiffness, or neurological sensitivity.  
Energetic disharmony such as hyperactivity, tension, or detachment from instinct.  
Emotional signs include over-alertness, defensiveness, or disconnection from trust.
