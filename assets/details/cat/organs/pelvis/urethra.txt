#Urethra#

##Location##  
A narrow tube leading from the **urinary bladder** to the **external opening**, running through the pelvic region.  
Differs slightly in length and shape between males and females.  

###Shape###  
A **slender, flexible tube** lined with mucous membrane and surrounded by smooth muscle.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal Section##  
Connected to the bladder, controlling urine flow.  
Energetically, represents **containment and trust**.  

##Pelvic Section##  
Passes through the pelvic floor.  
Energetically, symbolizes **strength and flow**.  

##Distal Section##  
Ends at the external opening for urine release.  
Energetically, reflects **release and freedom**.  

###Function###  
Carries urine from the bladder to the outside of the body.  
Regulates pressure and direction of flow.  
Energetically, governs **release, purity, and flow of emotions**.
