#Uterine Tubes (Oviducts)#

##Location##  
Extend from the **ovaries** to the **uterine horns**, suspended in the broad ligament.  
Each tube lies close to the ovary, capturing released ova.

###Shape###  
A **narrow, coiled tube** with ciliated lining that moves the ova toward the uterus.

###Parts###  

##Infundibulum##  
Funnel-shaped opening near the ovary.  
Energetically, symbolizes **receptivity and connection**.

##Ampulla##  
Wider region where fertilization occurs.  
Energetically, represents **union and creation**.

##Isthmus##  
Narrow portion leading to the uterus.  
Energetically, reflects **transition and flow**.

###Function###  
Transport ova, provide a site for fertilization, and support early embryo movement.  
Energetically, govern **connection, cooperation, and trust in timing**.
