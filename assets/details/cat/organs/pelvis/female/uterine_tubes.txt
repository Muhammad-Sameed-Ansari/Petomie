#Uterine Tubes (Oviducts)#

##Location##
Extend from each ovary toward the uterine horns, lying within the dorsal abdomen and suspended by connective tissue.

###Shape###
Two slender, coiled tubes with fringed openings (fimbriae) that catch and guide the released eggs.

##Function##
Serve as the passage for eggs from the ovaries to the uterus and as the site where fertilization occurs.
