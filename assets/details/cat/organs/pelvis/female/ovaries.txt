#Ovaries#

##Location##  
The ovaries are located in the **abdominal cavity** near the **kidneys**, supported by the **broad ligament** on either side.  
They are small and oval, suspended close to the ends of the uterine tubes.

###Shape###  
Smooth, oval organs that vary in size with the reproductive cycle.  
Covered by a thin tunic and containing follicles at different stages of development.

###Parts###  

##Cortex##  
Contains follicles and corpus luteum.  
Energetically, represents **creation and renewal**.

##Medulla##  
Houses blood vessels and connective tissue.  
Energetically, symbolizes **nourishment and support**.

###Function###  
Produce eggs and secrete hormones such as estrogen and progesterone.  
Control reproductive cycles and fertility.  
Energetically, govern **creativity, cycles, and feminine rhythm**.
