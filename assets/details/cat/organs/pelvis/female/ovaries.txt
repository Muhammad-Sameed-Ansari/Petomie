#Ovaries#

##Location##
The ovaries are located in the dorsal abdomen, near the kidneys, one on each side of the spine.

###Shape###
Two small, oval-shaped organs enclosed in thin membranes and supported by ligaments connecting them to the uterus.

##Function##
Produce eggs (ova) and secrete the hormones estrogen and progesterone that regulate the reproductive cycle and behavior.
