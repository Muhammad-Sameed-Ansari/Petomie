#Uterus#

##Location##
Lies within the pelvic cavity between the bladder and rectum, consisting of two long uterine horns and a short body connected to the cervix.

###Shape###
A Y-shaped, muscular organ capable of great flexibility and expansion during pregnancy.

##Function##
Provides nourishment and protection for developing embryos and contracts during birth to deliver kittens.
