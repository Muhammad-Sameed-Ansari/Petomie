#Rectum#

##Location##  
The **final segment of the large intestine**, extending from the descending colon to the anus.  

###Shape###  
A **straight muscular tube** lined with mucous membrane and smooth muscle layers.  

###Parts###  

##Upper Rectum##  
Stores fecal material before excretion.  
Energetically, represents **holding and preparation**.  

##Middle Rectum##  
Senses pressure and signals the urge to defecate.  
Energetically, symbolizes **awareness and readiness**.  

##Lower Rectum##  
Leads into the anal canal.  
Energetically, reflects **transition and release**.  

###Function###  
Stores feces, regulates elimination, and maintains internal pressure.  
Energetically, governs **timing, readiness, and completion**.
