#Rectum#

##Location##
Located in the pelvic cavity, following the large intestine and leading to the anus.

###Shape###
A short, muscular passage for storage of feces before elimination.

##Function##
Stores feces and expels waste through muscle contractions.

###Energetic Connection###
- **Chakra:** Root Chakra (Muladhara)
- **Meridian Connection:** Large Intestine Meridian
- **Emotional Meaning:** Represents grounding and ability to let go physically and emotionally.
- **Balanced Energy:** Smooth elimination and calm presence.
- **Energetic Support:** Comfortable space and calm energy support healthy elimination.
