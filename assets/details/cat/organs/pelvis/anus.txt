#Anus#

##Location##  
Located at the **end of the digestive tract**, just below the base of the tail.  
Forms the external opening for elimination of feces.  

###Shape###  
A **circular muscular structure** surrounded by internal and external sphincters.  

###Parts###  

##Internal Sphincter##  
Smooth muscle maintaining involuntary closure.  
Energetically, represents **control and inner security**.  

##External Sphincter##  
Skeletal muscle allowing voluntary release.  
Energetically, symbolizes **choice and letting go**.  

##Anal Glands##  
Located near the opening, secrete scent for communication.  
Energetically, embody **identity and expression**.  

###Function###  
Regulates excretion and maintains control during defecation.  
Supports communication through scent marking.  
Energetically, governs **release, boundaries, and grounding**.

**Emotional Meaning:** Safety, trust, and release.  
**Balanced Energy:** Groundedness, relaxation, and healthy elimination.  

###Common Imbalances###

Energetically, **fear of loss of control or resistance to letting go**.
