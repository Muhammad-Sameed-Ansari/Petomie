#Pancreas#

##Location##
The pancreas lies behind the **stomach**, nestled between the **duodenum** and **spleen**.  

###Shape###
A **flat, pale pink organ** with two main portions:  
- **Exocrine part:** produces digestive enzymes.  
- **Endocrine part:** releases insulin and glucagon to regulate blood sugar.

##Function##
- Produces **enzymes** that digest proteins, fats, and carbohydrates.  
- Secretes **insulin** to balance blood glucose levels.  
- Supports metabolism and energy conversion.  
- Works closely with the **liver and small intestine**.

- **Chakra:** Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura).  
- **Meridian Connection:** Spleen and Stomach Meridians.  
- **Emotional Meaning:** Reflects **trust in nourishment and sweetness of life**.  
  - Imbalance may cause fatigue, worry, or poor sugar regulation.

- **Energetic Support:** Gentle feeding rhythm, gratitude, and rest after meals.
