#Pancreas#

##Location##  
Lies between the **stomach and duodenum**, near the liver.  
It is elongated and partially hidden within connective tissue.  

###Shape###  
A **soft, lobulated organ** with both endocrine and exocrine functions.  

###Parts###  

##Head##  
Adjacent to the duodenum, releasing digestive enzymes.  
Energetically, symbolizes **nourishment and flow**.  

##Body##  
Central portion producing hormones.  
Energetically, represents **balance and distribution**.  

##Tail##  
Reaches toward the spleen.  
Energetically, reflects **completion and harmony**.  

###Function###  
Produces digestive enzymes and regulates blood sugar via insulin and glucagon.  
Supports nutrient absorption and metabolism.  
Energetically, governs **balance, sweetness, and acceptance**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Solar Plexus Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Spleen and Stomach Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Nourishment and self-worth.  
**Balanced Energy:** Stability, comfort, and inner sweetness.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Digestive upset, enzyme deficiency, or diabetes.  
Energetically, **bitterness, overthinking, or lack of joy**.

Eat slowly, rest after meals, and express gratitude.  
**Colors:** Soft yellow and peach bring warmth.
