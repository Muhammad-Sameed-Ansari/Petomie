#Urinary Bladder#

##Location##  
Situated in the **lower abdomen**, behind the pubic bone.  
Connected to the kidneys through the ureters.  

###Shape###  
A **hollow, muscular sac** that expands and contracts to store and release urine.  

###Parts###  

##Apex##  
Rounded top that expands as the bladder fills.  
Energetically, represents **adaptability and flow**.  

##Body##  
Main part storing urine.  
Energetically, symbolizes **containment and patience**.  

##Neck##  
Narrow lower part leading to the urethra.  
Energetically, reflects **timing and trust**.  

###Function###  
Collects and stores urine until elimination.  
Regulates internal pressure and water balance.  
Energetically, governs **release, control, and adaptability**.
