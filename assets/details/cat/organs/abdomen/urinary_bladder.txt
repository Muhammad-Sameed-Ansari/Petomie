#Urinary Bladder#

##Location##
The urinary bladder lies in the **caudal abdomen**, just in front of the pelvis.  
It connects to the **urethra**, which leads outside the body.

###Shape###
A **hollow, muscular sac** that expands as it fills with urine and contracts during urination.

##Function##
- **Stores urine** from the kidneys.  
- **Releases urine** when full.  
- Maintains **fluid balance and toxin removal**.  
- Works with kidneys to regulate waste excretion.
