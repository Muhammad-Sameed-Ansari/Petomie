#Small Intestine#

##Location##  
Extends from the **stomach** to the **large intestine**, filling most of the lower abdomen.  

###Shape###  
A **long, coiled tube** with a narrow diameter and soft walls.  

###Parts###  

##Duodenum##  
First section receiving bile and enzymes.  
Energetically, represents **reception and readiness**.  

##Jejunum##  
Middle portion absorbing nutrients.  
Energetically, symbolizes **assimilation and vitality**.  

##Ileum##  
Final section connecting to the large intestine.  
Energetically, embodies **completion and flow**.  

###Function###  
Digests and absorbs nutrients from food.  
Maintains immune defense and microbiome balance.  
Energetically, governs **discernment and assimilation of experiences**.
