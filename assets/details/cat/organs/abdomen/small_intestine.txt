#Small Intestine#

##Location##
The small intestine fills the **central abdomen**, connecting the **stomach** to the **large intestine**.  
It is suspended by the **mesentery**, which contains blood vessels and lymph nodes.

###Shape###
A **long, coiled tube** divided into three parts: **duodenum**, **jejunum**, and **ileum**.

##Function##
- Completes **digestion** using enzymes from the pancreas and bile from the liver.  
- **Absorbs nutrients** and water into the bloodstream.  
- Supports **immune defense** through intestinal lymph tissue.
