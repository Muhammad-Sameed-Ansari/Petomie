#Kidney#

##Location##  
Located on either side of the **spine**, just behind the last ribs in the upper abdominal cavity.  
The right kidney sits slightly more forward than the left.  

###Shape###  
A **bean-shaped organ** with a smooth outer capsule and rich internal structure.  

###Parts###  

##Cortex##  
Outer layer containing filtering units (nephrons).  
Energetically, represents **structure and stability**.  

##Medulla##  
Inner region with pyramids and collecting ducts.  
Energetically, symbolizes **flow and purification**.  

##Renal Pelvis##  
Funnel-like cavity collecting urine before it moves to the ureter.  
Energetically, reflects **release and letting go**.  

###Function###  
Filters blood to remove waste and balance water, minerals, and pH.  
Regulates blood pressure and produces hormones like erythropoietin.  
Energetically, governs **fear, resilience, and vitality**.

**Chakra:** Root Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Kidney Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Trust in life, courage, and grounding.  
**Balanced Energy:** Strength, flow, and emotional steadiness.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Kidney weakness, dehydration, or toxin buildup.  
Energetically, **fear, fatigue, or lack of grounding**.
