#Kidneys#

##Location##
The kidneys are found on the **dorsal wall of the abdomen**, just below the spine.  
The **right kidney** lies slightly more forward than the **left**.

###Shape###
Smooth, **bean-shaped organs** with a reddish-brown color.  

##Function##
- **Filter blood** to remove waste and toxins.  
- Maintain **fluid and electrolyte balance**.  
- Regulate **blood pressure** and **red blood cell production**.  
- Produce urine to excrete metabolic waste.

###Energetic Connection###
- **Chakra:** Root and Sacral Chakras (Muladhara and Svadhisthana).  
- **Meridian Connection:** Kidney Meridian.  
- **Emotional Meaning:** Symbolizes **vitality, courage, and resilience**.  
  - Imbalance may cause fear or fatigue.  
- **Balanced Energy:** Calm strength, grounded confidence.  
- **Energetic Support:** Hydration, warmth, and emotional security strengthen kidney energy.
