#Large Intestine#

##Location##  
Extends from the **end of the small intestine** to the **anus**, forming a frame around the abdominal cavity.  

###Shape###  
A **wide tubular structure** divided into several segments.  

###Parts###  

##Cecum##  
Short pouch at the junction with the small intestine.  
Energetically, represents **transition and adaptation**.  

##Colon##  
Ascending, transverse, and descending parts absorb water and minerals.  
Energetically, symbolize **assimilation and cleansing**.  

##Rectum##  
Terminal section storing feces before elimination.  
Energetically, embodies **release and closure**.  

###Function###  
Absorbs water, forms stool, and eliminates waste.  
Supports microbial balance and hydration.  
Energetically, governs **release of the old to welcome the new**.
