#Large Intestine#

##Location##
The large intestine occupies the **caudal abdomen**, connecting the **small intestine** to the **rectum**.  
It includes the **cecum**, **colon**, and **rectum**.

###Shape###
A **wider, shorter tube** than the small intestine, forming loops in the lower abdomen.

##Function##
- **Absorbs water and electrolytes** from digested material.  
- Forms and stores **feces**.  
- Maintains **healthy gut bacteria**.  
- Plays a role in immune defense and detoxification.

###Energetic Connection###
- **Chakra:** Root Chakra (Muladhara).  
- **Meridian Connection:** Large Intestine Meridian.  
- **Emotional Meaning:** Represents **release and letting go**.  
  - Imbalance may lead to constipation or emotional holding.  
- **Balanced Energy:** Regular elimination, grounded calmness.  
- **Energetic Support:** Hydration, fiber, and movement enhance natural flow.
