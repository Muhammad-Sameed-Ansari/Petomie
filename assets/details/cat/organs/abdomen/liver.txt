#Liver#

Occupies the **front of the abdominal cavity**, below the diaphragm, spanning the upper abdomen.  

###Shape###  
A **large, multi-lobed organ** with smooth reddish-brown tissue.  

###Parts###

Major divisions performing metabolic and detox functions.

Regulates metabolism and supports immune defense.
