#Liver#

##Location##
The liver lies in the **cranial abdomen**, mostly on the right side, just beneath the diaphragm.  
It sits close to the stomach and small intestine.

###Shape###
A **large, reddish-brown organ** divided into multiple lobes.  
It has a smooth surface and soft texture.  
The **gallbladder** is located beneath its right lobe.

##Function##
- Produces **bile** for fat digestion.  
- **Detoxifies** the blood and processes nutrients.  
- Stores **glycogen** and releases it as glucose when needed.  
- Regulates **metabolism, hormones, and vitamins**.  

###Energetic Connection###
- **Chakra:** Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura).  
- **Meridian Connection:** Liver Meridian.  
- **Emotional Meaning:** Represents **patience, flexibility, and emotional flow**.  
  - Imbalance may appear as irritability or frustration.  
- **Balanced Energy:** Calm focus and clear emotions.  
- **Energetic Support:** Rest, hydration, and peaceful surroundings assist liver balance.
