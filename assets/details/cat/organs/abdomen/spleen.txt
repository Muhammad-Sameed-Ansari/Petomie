#Spleen#

##Location##  
Located on the **left side of the abdomen**, near the stomach and pancreas.  

###Shape###  
A **flat, elongated organ** with a dark red color and smooth surface.  

###Parts###  

##Red Pulp##  
Filters blood and removes old red cells.  
Energetically, represents **cleansing and renewal**.  

##White Pulp##  
Contains immune cells for defense.  
Energetically, symbolizes **protection and awareness**.  

##Hilum##  
Entry point for blood vessels and nerves.  
Energetically, embodies **connection and coordination**.  

###Function###  
Filters blood, supports immune response, and stores iron and red cells.  
Energetically, governs **empathy, balance, and vitality**.
