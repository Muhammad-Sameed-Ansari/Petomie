#Spleen#

##Location##
The spleen lies along the **left side of the abdomen**, near the **stomach**.  
It is attached to the stomach by connective tissue and supported by the omentum.

###Shape###
A **flat, elongated, dark-red organ** that can change size depending on blood volume and activity.

##Function##
- **Filters and stores blood**, removing damaged red cells.  
- Produces **white blood cells** for immune defense.  
- Acts as a **blood reservoir** during stress or activity.  
- Helps regulate the immune system.

###Energetic Connection###
- **Chakra:** Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura).  
- **Meridian Connection:** Spleen Meridian.  
- **Emotional Meaning:** Symbolizes **care, trust, and emotional grounding**.  
  - Imbalance may appear as fatigue or overthinking.  
- **Balanced Energy:** Stability and vitality.  
- **Energetic Support:** Regular meals, rest, and gentle emotional nurturing restore balance.
