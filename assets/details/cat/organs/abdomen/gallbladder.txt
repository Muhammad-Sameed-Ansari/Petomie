#Gallbladder#

##Location##
The gallbladder is located beneath the **right lobe of the liver** in the upper abdomen.  
It connects to the **duodenum** through the **bile duct**.

###Shape###
A **small, pear-shaped sac** that stores and concentrates bile produced by the liver.  
It expands when filled with bile and contracts during digestion.

##Function##
- **Stores bile** produced by the liver.  
- **Releases bile** into the duodenum to help digest fats.  
- Regulates bile flow during meals.  
- Assists the **liver and pancreas** in digestive harmony.
