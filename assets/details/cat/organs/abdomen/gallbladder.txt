#Gallbladder#

##Location##  
Situated beneath the **right lobe of the liver**, nestled in a shallow depression.  
It connects to the liver and duodenum via the bile ducts.  

###Shape###  
A **small, pear-shaped sac** with a thin muscular wall and a smooth inner lining.  

###Parts###  

##Fundus##  
Rounded end that stores bile.  
Energetically, represents **patience and calm holding**.  

##Body##  
Middle section that contracts to expel bile.  
Energetically, symbolizes **release and movement**.  

##Neck and Cystic Duct##  
Connect to the bile duct, regulating bile flow.  
Energetically, embodies **communication and timing**.  

###Function###  
Stores and concentrates bile produced by the liver.  
Releases bile into the small intestine to aid fat digestion.  
Energetically, governs **decision-making and emotional flow**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Solar Plexus Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Gallbladder Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Decisiveness, flexibility, and courage.  
**Balanced Energy:** Clear choices, emotional ease, and smooth digestion.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Gallstones, bile congestion, or sluggish digestion.  
Energetically, **anger, frustration, or indecision**.

Support with gentle detox, hydration, and movement.  
**Colors:** Green and gold bring balance and renewal.
