#Stomach#

##Location##
The stomach lies in the **upper left side of the abdomen**, just behind the diaphragm and beneath the liver.  
It connects the **esophagus** to the **small intestine (duodenum)**.

###Shape###
A **curved, sac-like organ** with expandable walls that can hold food after meals.  
It contains both **glandular** and **non-glandular** regions to digest food efficiently.

##Function##
- Mixes food with **stomach acid** and digestive enzymes.  
- Begins **protein digestion** through the enzyme **pepsin**.  
- Regulates the release of food into the **small intestine**.  
- Provides an acidic environment to kill harmful bacteria.
