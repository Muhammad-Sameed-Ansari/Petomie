#Stomach#

##Location##  
Located in the **upper left abdomen**, between the esophagus and duodenum.  

###Shape###  
A **J-shaped muscular sac** with an elastic wall and glandular lining.  

###Parts###  

##Cardia##  
Entry from the esophagus.  
Energetically, symbolizes **reception and readiness**.  

##Fundus and Body##  
Main sections storing and mixing food.  
Energetically, represent **nurturing and transformation**.  

##Pylorus##  
Exit to the duodenum controlling food passage.  
Energetically, reflects **timing and trust**.  

###Function###  
Stores, mixes, and digests food with acids and enzymes.  
Begins protein digestion and regulates release to the intestine.  
Energetically, governs **emotional digestion and openness**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Solar Plexus Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Stomach Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Openness, acceptance, and balance.  
**Balanced Energy:** Comfort and steady digestion.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Vomiting, ulcers, or loss of appetite.  
Energetically, **overwhelm or anxiety**.  

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###  
Eat calmly, avoid tension, and cultivate gratitude.  
**Colors:** Yellow and coral promote calm digestion.  
**Crystals:** Carnelian and citrine warm the center.  
**Elements:** Earth and fire restore flow.  
**Affirmation:** “I digest life with calm and confidence.”
