#Diaphragm#

##Location##  
The diaphragm separates the **thoracic cavity** from the **abdominal cavity**, attaching to the ribs, sternum, and lumbar vertebrae.  
It acts as the main muscle of breathing, expanding and contracting during respiration.

###Shape###  
A **thin, dome-shaped muscle** that flattens as it contracts, drawing air into the lungs and rises as it relaxes to expel air.

###Parts###  

##Central Tendon##  
Fibrous center connecting muscle fibers.  
Energetically, represents **connection and balance**.

##Muscular Portions##  
Costal, sternal, and lumbar regions working in unison.  
Energetically, symbolize **strength and rhythm**.

###Function###  
Regulates breathing and supports circulation by changing thoracic pressure.  
Assists in vomiting, defecation, and posture.  
Energetically, governs **flow and harmony between upper and lower energies**.
