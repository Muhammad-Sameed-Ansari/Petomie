#Thymus#

##Location##  
Located in the **upper thoracic cavity**, in front of the heart and behind the sternum.  
Prominent in kittens and diminishes with age.

###Shape###  
A **soft, pale gland** composed of lymphatic tissue divided into lobes.

###Parts###  

##Lobules##  
Contain developing T-cells.  
Energetically, represent **growth and protection**.

##Capsule and Septa##  
Divide and support the gland.  
Energetically, symbolize **structure and stability**.

###Function###  
Produces immune cells and hormones supporting the immune system.  
Vital for young cats’ resistance and development.  
Energetically, governs **trust, courage, and vitality**.
