#Lungs#

##Location##  
Situated in the **thoracic cavity** on both sides of the heart, extending from the diaphragm to the thoracic inlet.

###Shape###  
Spongy, elastic, lobed organs covered by the pleura.  
Adapted for quick, light respiration in cats.

###Parts###  

##Bronchi and Bronchioles##  
Branching tubes that carry air into the lungs.  
Energetically, represent **communication and adaptability**.

##Alveoli##  
Tiny air sacs where oxygen exchange occurs.  
Energetically, symbolize **renewal and life-force**.

##Pleura##  
Protective membrane surrounding the lungs.  
Energetically, reflects **comfort and movement**.

###Function###  
Performs gas exchange, regulating oxygen and carbon dioxide levels.  
Supports vocalization and temperature control.  
Energetically, governs **release, inspiration, and emotional balance**.

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Heart and Throat Chakras.  
**Meridian:** Lung and Large Intestine Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Acceptance and letting go.  
**Balanced Energy:** Calm breathing and peace.

###Common Imbalances###  
Coughing, shortness of breath, or congestion.  
Energetically, **grief or tightness in expression**.

**Colors:** White and pale blue restore purity.
