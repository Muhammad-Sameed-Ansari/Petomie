#Heart#

##Location##  
Located within the **thoracic cavity**, between the lungs, slightly to the left of the midline, protected by the ribs and sternum.

###Shape###  
A **small, cone-shaped muscular organ** enclosed in a fibrous pericardium.  
Composed of four chambers — two atria and two ventricles.

###Parts###  

##Atria##  
Upper chambers that receive blood.  
Energetically, symbolize **acceptance and trust**.

##Ventricles##  
Lower chambers that pump blood to the lungs and body.  
Energetically, represent **action and strength**.

##Valves##  
Maintain one-way blood flow.  
Energetically, reflect **order and flow**.

###Function###  
Circulates blood, oxygen, and nutrients through the body.  
Supports all organs and tissues.  
Energetically, governs **love, trust, and emotional connection**.

**Chakra:** Heart Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Heart and Pericardium Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Love, connection, and courage.  
**Balanced Energy:** Calm pulse and open-heartedness.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weak pulse, fatigue, or stress.  
Energetically, **guardedness or emotional strain**.
