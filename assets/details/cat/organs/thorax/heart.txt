#Heart#

##Location##
The heart sits in the **middle of the chest**, between the **lungs**, slightly to the **left side**, and above the **diaphragm**.  
It is enclosed in a protective sac called the **pericardium**.

###Shape###
A **cone-shaped muscular organ** about the size of a small walnut in adult cats.  
It consists of **four chambers**: two atria and two ventricles.  
Blood flows in a rhythmic pattern, circulating oxygen throughout the body.

##Function##
- Pumps **oxygenated blood** throughout the body.  
- Maintains **blood pressure and circulation**.  
- Supplies all organs and tissues with nutrients and energy.  
- Works closely with the lungs to oxygenate and purify the blood.
