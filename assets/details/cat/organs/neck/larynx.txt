#Larynx#

##Location##  
Situated in the **neck**, between the pharynx and trachea, forming the upper part of the airway.  

###Shape###  
A **cartilaginous structure** composed of interconnected plates supporting vocal folds and airway opening.  

###Parts###  

##Epiglottis##  
Leaf-shaped cartilage preventing food from entering the airway.  
Energetically, represents **protection and choice**.  

##Vocal Folds##  
Vibrate to produce sound.  
Energetically, symbolize **expression and truth**.  

##Arytenoid and Thyroid Cartilages##  
Provide movement and shape for vocalization.  
Energetically, embody **flexibility and communication**.  

###Function###  
Regulates airflow to the lungs and enables vocalization (purring, meowing).  
Protects the lower respiratory tract during swallowing.  
Energetically, governs **self-expression and safe communication**.
