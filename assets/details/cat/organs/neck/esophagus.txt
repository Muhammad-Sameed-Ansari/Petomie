#Esophagus#

##Location##  
A muscular tube extending from the **pharynx** in the throat down through the neck and chest, connecting to the **stomach**.  
It passes behind the trachea and heart within the thoracic cavity.  

###Shape###  
A **long, flexible tube** with muscular walls lined by mucous membrane.  

###Parts###  

##Cervical Portion##  
Upper section in the neck region.  
Energetically, represents **expression and flow of nourishment**.  

##Thoracic Portion##  
Middle section running through the chest cavity.  
Energetically, symbolizes **movement through transition**.  

##Abdominal Portion##  
Short final section entering the stomach.  
Energetically, reflects **reception and readiness**.  

###Function###  
Carries food and water from the mouth to the stomach using rhythmic contractions (peristalsis).  
Prevents reflux through sphincter control.  
Energetically, governs **flow, transition, and acceptance**.

Regurgitation, inflammation, or blockage.
