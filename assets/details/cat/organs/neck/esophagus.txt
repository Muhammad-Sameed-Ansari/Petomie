#Esophagus#

##Location##
The esophagus is a **muscular tube** running from the **pharynx** to the **stomach**, located behind the trachea and alongside the spine.  
It passes through the diaphragm before entering the stomach.

###Shape###
A **long, flexible tube** lined with smooth muscles that move food downward by rhythmic contractions called **peristalsis**.

##Function##
- Transports **food and water** from mouth to stomach.  
- Prevents reflux through a **valve-like sphincter** at the stomachâ€™s entrance.  
- Works automatically through reflexive muscle movement.  
- Begins the process of **digestion and nourishment**.
