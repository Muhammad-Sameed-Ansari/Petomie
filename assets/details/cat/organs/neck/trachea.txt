#Trachea#

##Location##
The trachea, or **windpipe**, runs from the **larynx** down the **neck** into the **chest cavity**, dividing into two bronchi that lead to the lungs.

###Shape###
A **tube-like structure** made of **C-shaped cartilage rings** connected by muscle.  
The rings keep the airway open while allowing flexibility for movement and breathing.

##Function##
- Carries **air to and from the lungs**.  
- Filters and moistens air through mucous and cilia inside the lining.  
- Cough reflex helps **remove irritants** or blockages.  
- Allows **smooth airflow** during breathing, meowing, and purring.
