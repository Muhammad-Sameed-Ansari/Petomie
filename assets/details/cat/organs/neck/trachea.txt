#Trachea#

##Location##  
Extends from the **larynx** to the **bronchi** within the neck and thoracic cavity.  
Sits in front of the esophagus and leads air directly to the lungs.  

###Shape###  
A **flexible tube** supported by C-shaped rings of cartilage, open at the back.  

###Parts###  

##Cartilaginous Rings##  
Prevent collapse during breathing.  
Energetically, represent **support and resilience**.  

##Tracheal Muscle##  
Closes the dorsal side and adjusts airflow.  
Energetically, symbolizes **adaptability and rhythm**.  

##Mucous Lining##  
Protects and filters air.  
Energetically, embodies **cleansing and clarity**.  

###Function###  
Conducts air to the bronchi and lungs.  
Filters particles and maintains open airflow.  
Energetically, governs **flow of life force and communication**.
