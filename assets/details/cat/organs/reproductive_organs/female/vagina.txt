#Vagina#

##Location##  
The vagina extends from the **cervix** to the **vulva**, within the pelvic cavity beneath the rectum.  
It forms part of the birth canal and passage for mating.

###Shape###  
A **short, muscular canal** lined with folds that expand during mating or birth.  
Moist mucous membranes protect against friction and infection.

###Parts###  

##Anterior Portion##  
Connects to the cervix.  
Energetically, symbolizes **acceptance and transition**.

##Posterior Portion##  
Leads to the vulva and external opening.  
Energetically, represents **connection and trust**.

###Function###  
Acts as a passage for mating and birthing, protects internal organs, and supports microbial balance.  
Energetically, governs **trust, openness, and receptive communication**.
