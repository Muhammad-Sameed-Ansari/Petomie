#Gonads#

##Location##  
The ovaries in female cats are located in the **abdominal cavity**, positioned just below the kidneys.  
They are attached by the **suspensory ligament** and connect to the uterus through the **oviducts**.

###Shape###  
Small, oval organs that are smooth in young queens and become more textured with age and cycling.  
Contain multiple follicles at various stages of development.

###Parts###  

##Follicles##  
Egg cells developing in fluid-filled structures.  
Energetically, represent **potential, renewal, and creative rhythm**.

##Corpus Luteum##  
Forms after ovulation and produces progesterone.  
Energetically, symbolizes **nurturing, emotional grounding, and stability**.

##Tunica Albuginea##  
Protective connective layer surrounding the ovary.  
Energetically, reflects **safety, inner strength, and boundaries**.

###Function###  
Produce eggs and regulate hormonal cycles (estrogen and progesterone).  
Queens are **induced ovulators**, meaning ovulation occurs through mating stimulus.  
Energetically, maintain **creative flow, emotional cycles, and feminine balance**.
