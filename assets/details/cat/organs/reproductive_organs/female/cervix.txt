#Cervix#

##Location##  
The cervix lies between the **uterus** and **vagina**, within the pelvic cavity.  
It serves as the narrow muscular passage connecting the two, acting as a gateway between internal and external reproductive environments.

###Shape###  
A **short, firm, tubular structure** composed of dense smooth muscle and fibrous connective tissue.  
The feline cervix remains tightly closed except during heat or birth.

###Parts###  

##External Os##  
Opening into the vagina.  
Energetically, symbolizes **boundaries and protection**.

##Cervical Canal##  
Central passage containing mucous glands that change secretions with hormonal cycles.  
Energetically, represents **flow and adaptation**.

##Internal Os##  
Opens into the uterus.  
Energetically, reflects **communication and trust**.

###Function###  
Regulates passage of sperm, prevents infection, and protects the uterus.  
Dilates during mating or birth.  
Energetically, governs **transitions, boundaries, and readiness to open when safe**.
