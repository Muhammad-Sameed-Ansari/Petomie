#Uterus#

##Location##  
The uterus lies within the **pelvic and abdominal cavities**, between the bladder and colon.  
It is Y-shaped with two long **uterine horns** that extend toward the ovaries.

###Shape###  
A **bicornuate organ** composed of smooth muscle and glandular lining.  
Adaptable and capable of expansion during pregnancy.

###Parts###  

##Uterine Horns##  
Extend from the body toward the ovaries.  
Energetically, represent **nurturing and growth**.

##Uterine Body##  
Joins the horns to the cervix.  
Energetically, reflects **union and stability**.

##Endometrium and Myometrium##  
Lining and muscular layers supporting embryo development.  
Energetically, symbolize **protection and creation**.

###Function###  
Provides environment for conception, implantation, and fetal growth.  
Contracts during birth to deliver kittens.  
Energetically, governs **nurturing and patient creation**.

**Emotional Meaning:** Care, fertility, and grounding.  
**Balanced Energy:** Emotional warmth and stability.

Inflammation or infection.  
Energetically, **overextension or emotional suppression**.
