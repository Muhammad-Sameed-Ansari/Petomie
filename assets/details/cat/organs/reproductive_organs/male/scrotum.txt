#Scrotum#

##Location##  
The scrotum is located below the tail and between the hind limbs, containing the **testes**, **epididymides**, and **spermatic cords**.

###Shape###  
A **thin, elastic skin pouch** with smooth muscle that contracts or relaxes to regulate testicular temperature.

###Parts###  

##Skin and Dartos Muscle##  
Adjust tension and temperature.  
Energetically, represent **adaptability and grounding**.

##Spermatic Cord##  
Contains vessels and nerves supplying the testes.  
Energetically, symbolizes **connection and strength**.

##Tunica Vaginalis##  
A protective membrane around each testis.  
Energetically, reflects **comfort and security**.

###Function###  
Protects and regulates temperature for sperm production.  
Provides flexibility and movement.  
Energetically, governs **protection and grounded security**.
