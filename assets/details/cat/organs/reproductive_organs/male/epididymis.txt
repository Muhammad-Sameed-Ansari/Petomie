#Epididymis#

##Location##  
Attached to the upper and posterior surface of each **testis**, within the scrotum.  
It connects the testis to the sperm duct (vas deferens).

###Shape###  
A **narrow, coiled tube** divided into head, body, and tail.  
Compact and finely structured to conserve space while storing sperm.

###Parts###  

##Head (Caput)##  
Receives sperm from the testis.  
Energetically, symbolizes **reception and preparation**.

##Body (Corpus)##  
Site of sperm maturation and fluid absorption.  
Energetically, represents **development and balance**.

##Tail (Cauda)##  
Stores mature sperm before ejaculation.  
Energetically, reflects **readiness and patience**.

###Function###  
Stores and matures sperm, maintaining optimal temperature and nutrition.  
Provides a vital link between production and release.  
Energetically, governs **preparation, patience, and focused energy**.
