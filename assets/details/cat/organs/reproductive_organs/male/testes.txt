#Testes#

##Location##  
Housed within the **scrotum**, outside the abdominal cavity, suspended by the **spermatic cords**.  
This positioning ensures an optimal temperature for sperm production.

###Shape###  
Small, oval organs enclosed in a tough capsule (tunica albuginea), with a soft, spongy internal texture.

###Parts###  

##Seminiferous Tubules##  
Microscopic coils producing sperm.  
Energetically, represent **creative spark and vitality**.

##Interstitial Cells (Leydig Cells)##  
Produce testosterone to regulate reproduction and strength.  
Energetically, symbolize **drive and motivation**.

##Tunica Albuginea##  
Protective outer capsule providing form.  
Energetically, reflects **structure and containment**.

###Function###

Energetically, govern **strength, vitality, and creative balance**.
