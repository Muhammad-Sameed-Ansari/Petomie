#Accessory Glands#

##Location##  
The accessory glands are located in the **pelvic cavity**, near the neck of the **bladder** and the **urethra**.  
In the cat, the **prostate** and **bulbourethral glands** are present, while the seminal vesicles are absent.

###Shape###  
Small, rounded glands with smooth surfaces and rich vascular supply.  
Each gland contributes unique secretions to semen composition.

###Parts###  

##Prostate Gland##  
Encircles the urethra at the bladder neck.  
Energetically, represents **stability and flow**.

##Bulbourethral Glands##  
Located on either side of the urethra behind the prostate.  
Energetically, symbolize **preparation and ease**.

###Function###  
Produce fluids that lubricate, nourish, and protect sperm during ejaculation.  
Support fertility and healthy semen composition.  
Energetically, govern **balance between drive and gentleness**.
