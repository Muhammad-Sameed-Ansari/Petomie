#Prostate Gland#  

##Location##  
Located in the **pelvic cavity**, just behind the **urinary bladder**, surrounding the **proximal urethra**.  
In the male cat, the prostate lies on the midline and is closely associated with the urinary tract and the beginning of the reproductive outflow pathway.

###Shape###  
A **small, compact gland** (usually less prominent than in dogs), positioned around the urethra.  
It can be difficult to palpate in healthy cats due to its relatively small size.
