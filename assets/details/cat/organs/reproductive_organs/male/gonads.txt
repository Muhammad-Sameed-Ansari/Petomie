
#Gonads#

##Location##  
The testes in male cats are housed in the **scrotum**, located outside the abdominal cavity and connected by the **spermatic cords**.  
They normally descend through the inguinal canal before or shortly after birth.

###Shape###  
Small, firm, oval organs with smooth outer surfaces, enclosed in a fibrous capsule.  
Contain tightly coiled tubules responsible for sperm production.

###Parts###  

##Seminiferous Tubules##  
Produce sperm cells.  
Energetically, represent **vitality and creation**.

##Interstitial (Leydig) Cells##  
Produce testosterone, influencing fertility, muscle tone, and tomcat behaviour.  
Energetically, symbolize **drive, instinct, and grounded strength**.

##Tunica Albuginea##  
Fibrous outer protective layer.  
Energetically, reflects **structure, security, and boundaries**.
