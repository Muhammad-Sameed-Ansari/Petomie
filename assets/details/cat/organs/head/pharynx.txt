#Pharynx#

##Location##  
Located behind the **nasal cavity and mouth**, connecting to both the esophagus and trachea.  
It serves as a shared passage for air and food.  

###Shape###  
A **muscular funnel-shaped chamber** lined with mucous membrane.  

###Parts###  

##Nasopharynx##  
Upper section behind the nasal cavity.  
Energetically, represents **clarity and breath flow**.  

##Oropharynx##  
Middle part behind the mouth.  
Energetically, symbolizes **choice and transition**.  

##Laryngopharynx##  
Lower section leading to the larynx and esophagus.  
Energetically, embodies **integration and direction**.  

###Function###  
Guides air toward the trachea and food toward the esophagus.  
Assists in swallowing and vocal resonance.  
Energetically, governs **coordination and trust in dual pathways**.
