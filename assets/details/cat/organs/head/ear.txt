#Ear#

##Location##  
Located on both sides of the **head**, each ear includes an external flap, canal, middle ear, and inner ear structures.  

###Shape###  
A **triangular external pinna** leading into a curved canal, connecting to delicate internal chambers.  

###Parts###  

##External Ear (Pinna and Canal)##  
Collects and directs sound waves inward.  
Energetically, represents **receptivity and attention**.  

##Middle Ear##  
Contains the eardrum and tiny bones (ossicles) transmitting vibrations.  
Energetically, symbolizes **communication and harmony**.  

##Inner Ear##  
Houses the cochlea and semicircular canals for hearing and balance.  
Energetically, reflects **equilibrium and intuition**.  

###Function###  
Receives and processes sound, maintains balance, and helps with spatial awareness.  
Energetically, governs **listening, receptivity, and awareness of surroundings**.

**Emotional Meaning:** Ability to hear truth and maintain inner balance.  
**Balanced Energy:** Attentive, calm, and in harmony with sound.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Ear infections, hearing loss, or head shaking.  
Energetically, **resistance to hearing or overwhelm from noise**.
