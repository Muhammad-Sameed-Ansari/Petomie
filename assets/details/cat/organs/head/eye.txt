#Eye#

##Location##  
Situated on the **front of the head** within the bony orbits.  
Each eye connects to the brain via the optic nerve.  

###Shape###  
A **spherical organ** with layered membranes and a clear cornea for light entry.  

###Parts###  

##Cornea##  
Transparent outer layer focusing incoming light.  
Energetically, represents **clarity and openness**.  

##Lens and Iris##  
Control focus and light regulation.  
Energetically, symbolize **adaptability and perception**.  

##Retina and Optic Nerve##  
Convert light into nerve signals for vision.  
Energetically, embody **insight and awareness**.  

###Function###  
Provides vision, detects movement, and adjusts to light intensity.  
Energetically, governs **perception, understanding, and intuitive vision**.
