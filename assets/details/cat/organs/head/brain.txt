#Head – Brain#

##Location##
The brain is located inside the **skull**, protected by the **cranial bones** and surrounded by **cerebrospinal fluid**.  
It connects to the **spinal cord** through the **brainstem**, forming the control center for body function and behavior.

###Shape###
A soft, folded structure divided into:  
- **Cerebrum:** controls thought, movement, and learning.  
- **Cerebellum:** manages balance and coordination.  
- **Brainstem:** regulates essential functions like heartbeat, breathing, and reflexes.  
The surface of the brain is covered in grooves (gyri and sulci) that increase its processing area.

##Function##
- Receives and processes **information** from all senses.  
- Controls **movement, coordination, and balance**.  
- Manages **emotion, instinct, and learned behavior**.  
- Regulates **heartbeat, breathing, and digestion** automatically.  
- Enables **memory, problem-solving, and communication** through body language and sound.
