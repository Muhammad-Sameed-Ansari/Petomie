#Brain#

##Location##  
The brain is located within the **cranial cavity** of the skull, protected by the cranial bones and cushioned by cerebrospinal fluid.  

###Shape###  
A **soft, rounded organ** composed of multiple regions and lobes with a convoluted surface.  

###Parts###  

##Cerebrum##  
Controls voluntary actions, memory, and learning.  
Energetically, represents **awareness and consciousness**.  

##Cerebellum##  
Coordinates balance, posture, and fine motor control.  
Energetically, symbolizes **harmony and rhythm**.  

##Brainstem##  
Connects the brain to the spinal cord, regulating vital functions such as heartbeat and breathing.  
Energetically, embodies **stability and life force**.  

###Function###  
Processes sensory input, controls movement, regulates behavior, and maintains internal balance.  
Energetically, governs **clarity, awareness, and connection between mind and body**.
