#Heart#

The **cat’s heart** is a muscular, four-chambered organ that serves as the rhythmic center of circulation.  
It beats continuously to deliver oxygen, nutrients, and warmth throughout the body.  
Energetically, the heart is the seat of **love, balance, and emotional intelligence**, harmonizing body and soul.

###Parts###

##Atria##
The upper chambers that receive blood from veins.  
Energetically, symbolize **receptivity and openness to love**.

##Ventricles##
The lower chambers that pump blood to the lungs and body.  
Energetically, represent **action, will, and expression of love through movement**.

##Valves##
Ensure one-way flow, maintaining rhythm and order.  
Energetically, symbolize **boundaries and trust in flow**.

###Function###
The cat’s heart pumps blood throughout the body, supplying oxygen and nutrients while removing waste.  
It sustains rhythm and vitality in all systems.  

Energetically, the heart governs **love, coherence, and the ability to give and receive freely**.

###Common Imbalances###
Arrhythmia: Disturbed rhythm or emotional disharmony.  
Weak pulse: Fatigue or lack of enthusiasm.  
Heart murmur: Energy leak or unresolved grief.  
Energetic block: Closed heart or fear of connection.  
Energetic excess: Overgiving or emotional strain.
