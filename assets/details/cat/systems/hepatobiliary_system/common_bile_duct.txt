#Common Bile Duct#
Part of the Hepatobiliary System: Final channel delivering bile to the duodenum.
Energetically, it represents release, transition, and the courage to let go of the old and welcome renewal.

###Parts###

##Upper Segment##
Receives bile from hepatic and cystic ducts.  
Energetically, symbolizes cooperation and alignment.

##Lower Segment##
Joins the pancreatic duct near the duodenum.  
Energetically, reflects transition and transformation.

###Function###
Delivers bile into the small intestine to aid digestion and detoxification.  
Ensures bile reaches the gut for fat emulsification and waste elimination.

###Common Imbalances###
Blockage: frustration or rigidity.  
Inflammation: irritation or stored emotion.  
Energetic sluggishness: difficulty in release.  
Energetic overdrive: restlessness or impatience.
