#Portal Vein#
Part of the Hepatobiliary System: Carries nutrient-rich blood from the intestines to the liver.
Energetically, it represents nourishment, sharing, and integration — the flow of life’s lessons into wisdom.

###Parts###

##Tributaries##
Include veins from the stomach, intestines, and spleen.  
Energetically, symbolize collaboration and support.

###Function###
Transports absorbed nutrients to the liver for processing and detoxification.  
Maintains the balance between digestion and purification.

###Common Imbalances###
Congestion: over-absorption or overload.  
Weak flow: fatigue or lack of direction.  
Energetic stagnation: inability to process experience.  
Energetic excess: overwhelm or agitation.
