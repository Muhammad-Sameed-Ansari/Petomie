#Hepatic Ducts#
Part of the Hepatobiliary System: Channel bile from the liver to the common bile duct.
Energetically, they represent communication and orderly flow, uniting inner processes with outward function.

###Parts###

##Right and Left Hepatic Ducts##
Collect bile from individual liver lobes.  
Energetically, symbolize duality and cooperation.

##Common Hepatic Duct##
Formed by the merging of right and left ducts, directing bile outward.  
Energetically, reflects unity and collaboration.

###Function###
Transport bile from the liver lobes toward the gallbladder or duodenum.  
Facilitate efficient digestion through smooth flow.

###Common Imbalances###
Blockage: stagnation or confusion.  
Inflammation: frustration or tension.  
Energetic restriction: difficulty expressing truth or emotion.  
Energetic depletion: loss of motivation or flow.
