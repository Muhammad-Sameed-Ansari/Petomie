#Hepatic Artery#
Part of the Hepatobiliary System: Supplies oxygenated blood to the liver.  
Energetically, it represents vitality, purpose, and the infusion of life force into transformation.

###Parts###

##Right and Left Branches##
Deliver blood to each liver lobe.  
Energetically, symbolize strength and balance in distribution.

###Function###
Carries oxygen and nutrients from the heart to sustain liver cells.  
Supports metabolism and detoxification functions.

###Common Imbalances###
Reduced flow: fatigue or lack of vitality.  
Constriction: emotional tension or resistance.  
Energetic stagnation: blocked creativity.  
Energetic overdrive: restlessness or impatience.
