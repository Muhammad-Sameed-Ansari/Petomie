#Cystic Duct#
Part of the Hepatobiliary System: Connects the gallbladder to the common bile duct.
Energetically, it represents readiness and transition â€” the balance between holding and releasing.

###Parts###

##Neck Connection##
Controls bile movement between gallbladder and ducts.  
Energetically, symbolizes trust in timing and adaptability.

###Function###
Allows bile to flow in and out of the gallbladder during storage and release.  
Maintains equilibrium in bile pressure and storage.

###Common Imbalances###
Obstruction: tension or delayed decision-making.  
Weak flow: hesitation or resistance.  
Energetic blockage: inability to let go or adapt.  
Energetic excess: impulsiveness or reactivity.
