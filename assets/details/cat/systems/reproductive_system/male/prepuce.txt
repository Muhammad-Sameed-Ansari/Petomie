#Prepuce#

##Location##
Covers and protects the **penis** when it is not erect.  
Located along the **ventral abdomen**, just behind the umbilical region.

###Shape###
A **fold of skin** forming a sheath around the penis.  
The inner lining is smooth and moist, while the outer surface is covered with fine hair.

##Parts##

###1. External Fold###
The outer skin layer that blends with the surrounding abdominal surface.

###2. Internal Fold###
The inner mucosal layer that contacts the penis directly and keeps it lubricated.

###3. Preputial Orifice###
The **opening** at the tip of the prepuce where the penis protrudes during mating.

###Function###
Protects the penis from **injury, contamination, and drying**.  
Keeps the penile surface **moist and clean**.  
Retracts easily to allow **extension of the penis** during mating.  
Acts as a barrier to external debris and infection.
