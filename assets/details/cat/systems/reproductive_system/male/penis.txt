#Penis#

##Location##
Located below the anus, extending forward along the **ventral midline** between the hind limbs.  
It lies within a protective fold of skin called the **prepuce** when not erect.

###Shape###
A **slender, retractable organ** with a slightly curved shape.  
The tip (glans) contains **small keratinized spines** that stimulate ovulation during mating.

##Parts##

###1. Root###
Anchors the penis to the pelvis via the **crura** and surrounding muscles.

###2. Body###
The **main portion** containing erectile tissue that fills with blood during arousal.

###3. Glans Penis###
The distal end of the penis containing **penile spines** that trigger the femaleâ€™s ovulatory response.

###4. Urethra###
Runs through the center of the penis, carrying both urine and semen.

###Function###
Serves as the **copulatory organ** during mating.  
Delivers **sperm into the female reproductive tract**.  
Penile spines **induce ovulation** in the female during copulation.  
Also functions as a **urinary passage** when not in reproductive use.
