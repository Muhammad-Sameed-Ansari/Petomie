#Sperm Duct (Vas Deferens)#

##Location##
Extends from the **tail of the epididymis** through the **spermatic cord** into the pelvic cavity.  
It connects to the **urethra** near the prostate region.

###Shape###
A **narrow, muscular tube** with a thick wall and small lumen.  
Its surface is smooth and slightly curved along its course.

##Parts##

###1. Proximal Segment###
Begins at the epididymis and carries sperm into the pelvic region.

###2. Ampulla###
A slightly enlarged portion near the end of the duct that serves as a temporary sperm reservoir.

###3. Terminal Segment###
Joins with ducts from the accessory glands before opening into the urethra.

###Function###
Transports **mature sperm** from the epididymis to the urethra during ejaculation.  
Uses **muscular contractions** to propel sperm forward.  
Acts as a short-term **storage site** before ejaculation.
