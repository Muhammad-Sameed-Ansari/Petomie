#Epididymis#
The epididymis in the cat is a coiled tube attached to each testis where sperm mature and are stored.  
Energetically, it represents patience, preparation, and transformation before action.

###Parts###

##Head, Body, and Tail##  
Sections where sperm mature and are stored.  
Energetically, symbolize growth and readiness.

###Function###  
Stores and matures sperm until ejaculation.  
Energetically, cultivates focus, purpose, and steady readiness.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling: stress or emotional buildup.  
Pain: blocked progress or withheld expression.  
Energetic stagnation: impatience or inner pressure.
