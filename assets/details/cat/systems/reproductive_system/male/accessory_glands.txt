#Accessory Glands#
The accessory glands in the cat produce fluids that nourish and protect sperm.  
They include the prostate and bulbourethral glands.  
Energetically, they represent support, balance, and cooperative function within creation.

###Parts###

##Prostate Gland##  
Produces seminal fluid to nourish sperm.  
Energetically, symbolizes strength and stability.

##Bulbourethral Glands##  
Secrete fluid to clear and prepare the urethra.  
Energetically, reflect clarity and readiness for creative flow.

###Function###  
Provide lubrication and nutrients for sperm transport.  
Energetically, ensure balanced support in the act of creation.

###Common Imbalances###  
Enlargement: overexertion or emotional pressure.  
Infection: internal conflict or imbalance.  
Energetic stagnation: blocked creative support or stress.
