#Cervix#
The cervix in the cat forms the muscular gateway between the uterus and vagina.  
It acts as both a barrier and a passage, controlling reproductive flow.  
Energetically, it represents boundaries, protection, and the transition between inner creation and outer expression.

###Parts###

##Cervical Canal##  
Connects uterus and vagina.  
Energetically, symbolizes flow and control.

##Cervical Rings##  
Muscular folds that tighten or relax with hormonal cycles.  
Energetically, reflect adaptability and trust.

###Function###  
Prevents infection from entering the uterus and opens during birth.  
Energetically, governs openness and protection in creation.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: tension or fear of exposure.  
Blockage: emotional holding or resistance.  
Energetic stagnation: difficulty trusting cycles of change.
