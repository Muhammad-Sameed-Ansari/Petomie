#Ovaries#
The ovaries of the cat are paired organs that produce eggs and hormones.  
Energetically, they represent creativity, fertility, and the cycle of renewal.

###Parts###

##Follicles##  
Contain developing eggs.  
Energetically, symbolize potential and new beginnings.

##Corpus Luteum##  
Temporary gland formed after ovulation.  
Energetically, reflects completion and transformation.

###Function###  
Produce ova and regulate reproductive hormones.  
Energetically, embody cycles of creation and regeneration.

###Common Imbalances###  
Cysts: trapped creativity or stagnation.  
Hormonal imbalance: emotional instability.  
Energetic stagnation: suppressed self-expression or blocked creation.
