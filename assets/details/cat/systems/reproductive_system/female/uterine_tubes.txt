#Uterine Tubes#
The uterine tubes (oviducts) carry eggs from the ovaries to the uterus.  
In the cat, fertilization occurs here.  
Energetically, they represent guidance, flow, and the journey of potential into creation.

###Parts###

##Infundibulum, Ampulla, Isthmus##  
Sections that capture, transport, and guide the egg.  
Energetically, symbolize direction and divine timing.

###Function###  
Transport ova and support fertilization.  
Energetically, channel inspiration into form.

###Common Imbalances###  
Blockage: delay or resistance.  
Inflammation: anger or emotional congestion.  
Energetic stagnation: fear of progress or transition.
