#Thymus#

##Location##
Situated in the upper chest in front of the heart, largest in kittens and shrinks with age.

###Shape###
Soft, lobed gland.

###Parts###

##Cortex##
Outer layer where T-cells begin development.  
Energetically symbolizes **growth, protection, and forming boundaries**.

##Medulla##
Inner region for T-cell maturation.  
Energetically represents **clarity, courage, and inner resilience**.

##Thymic Lobules##
Compartments guiding immune development.  
Energetically reflect **organization and guided evolution**.

###Function###
Develops T-cells, builds immunity in young cats, and strengthens long-term immune memory.  
Energetically supports **emotional protection, bravery, and security**.

###Applies to##
All cats (strongest immune function in kittens).

###Common Imbalances###
Weak immunity, infections in young cats, age-related atrophy.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Light green, blue  
Crystals: Amazonite, chrysocolla  
Element: Air  
Affirmation: “My inner strength grows with every breath.”
