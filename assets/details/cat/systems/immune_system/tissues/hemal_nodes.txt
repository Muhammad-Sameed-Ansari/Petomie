#Hemal Nodes#

##Location##
Small dark-red nodes located along major blood vessels, especially near the abdomen and pelvis.

###Shape###
Tiny, spleen-like nodules.

###Parts###

##Blood-Filled Sinuses##
Filter pathogens directly from blood.  
Energetically symbolize **deep cleansing and inner purification**.

###Function###
Act like miniature spleens, filtering blood and supporting immune responses.  
Energetically support **renewal, grounding, and stability**.

###Applies to##
Cats (present but small and less developed than in ruminants).

###Common Imbalances###
Swelling, infection, inflammation.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Burgundy, deep red  
Crystals: Garnet  
Element: Earth  
Affirmation: “My inner world is clean, grounded, and strong.”
