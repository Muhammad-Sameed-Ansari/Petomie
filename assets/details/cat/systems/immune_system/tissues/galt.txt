#GALT (Gut-Associated Lymphoid Tissue)#

##Location##
Distributed throughout the intestines, including Peyer’s patches and isolated lymphoid follicles.

###Shape###
Clusters or patches of lymphoid tissue.

###Parts###

##Peyer’s Patches##
Monitor and respond to gut pathogens.  
Energetically symbolize **processing and emotional digestion**.

##Isolated Lymphoid Follicles##
Small immune units throughout the gut lining.  
Energetically represent **absorption and internal clarity**.

###Function###
Protects the digestive tract, supports immune responses to food and microbes.  
Energetically governs **flow, nourishment, and adaptation**.

###Common Imbalances###
Digestive inflammation, allergies, infections.
