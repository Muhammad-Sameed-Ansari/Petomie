#CALT (Conjunctiva-Associated Lymphoid Tissue)#

##Location##
Located in the conjunctiva lining of the eye, beneath the eyelids.

###Shape###
Small lymphoid clusters embedded in mucosal tissue.

###Parts###

##Lymphoid Follicles##
Detect pathogens entering through the eyes.  
Energetically symbolize **clarity, awareness, and subtle protection**.

###Function###
Provides immune defense for the eye surface and protects against airborne microbes.  
Energetically supports **insight, openness, and emotional clarity**.

###Applies to##
All cats.

###Common Imbalances###
Eye irritation, conjunctivitis, allergic reactions.

Colors: Indigo, sky blue  
Crystals: Lapis lazuli, celestite  
Element: Light  
Affirmation: “I see clearly and remain protected.”
