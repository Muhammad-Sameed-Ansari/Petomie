#Spleen#

##Location##
Located in the abdomen near the stomach.

###Shape###
Flattened, elongated organ.

###Parts###

##Red Pulp##
Filters blood, removes damaged cells.  
Energetically: **renewal and grounding**.

##White Pulp##
Produces immune cells, activates responses.  
Energetically: **clarity and purification**.

###Function###
Filters pathogens, stores immune cells, and regulates blood.  
Energetically maintains **balance, resilience, and centeredness**.

###Common Imbalances###
Enlargement, infection, anemia.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Yellow, gold  
Crystals: Citrine, amber  
Element: Earth  
Affirmation: “I remain centered, strong, and protected.”
