#Harderian Gland#

##Location##
Located behind the cat’s eye within the orbit, associated with the third eyelid.

###Shape###
Soft, lobed gland.

###Parts###

##Secretory Lobules##
Produce protective secretions that support immune defense.  
Energetically symbolize **clarity, inner sight, and subtle protection**.

##Duct System##
Carries secretions to the eye surface.  
Energetically reflects **flow, cleansing, and emotional release**.

###Function###
Supports eye immunity, lubricates the eye, and contributes to local pathogen defense.  
Energetically enhances **intuition, trust, and clear perception**.

###Applies to##
All cats.

###Common Imbalances###
Eye irritation, infection, or third eyelid swelling.

Colors: Indigo  
Crystals: Lapis lazuli  
Element: Light  
Affirmation: “I see clearly and remain protected.”
