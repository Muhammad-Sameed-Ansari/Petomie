##Location##
Distributed within the dermis, connected to hair follicles and skin pores.

##Shape##
Microscopic glandular structures, each specialized for secretion or communication.

##Parts##

###1. Sebaceous Glands###
- Small glands near hair follicles.  
- **Function:** Produce sebum to lubricate and waterproof fur and skin.  

###2. Sweat Glands###
- Found primarily on paw pads.  
- **Function:** Help with scent marking and minimal temperature control.  

###3. Scent Glands###
- Present on the face, tail base, and paws.  
- **Function:** Release pheromones for marking and communication.  

###4. Mammary Glands###
- Located along the abdomen.  
- **Function:** Produce milk for kittens.  

##Function##
- **Lubrication:** Keeps skin supple and fur healthy.  
- **Communication:** Releases scent signals for identification and marking.  
- **Protection:** Aids in defense and minor temperature regulation.
