#Dermis#

The dermis lies beneath the epidermis and contains connective tissue, nerves, and vessels.  
It provides strength, elasticity, and sensory awareness.  
Energetically, it represents grounding, emotional depth, and responsiveness.

###Parts###

##Papillary Layer##  
Upper region with blood vessels and receptors.  
Energetically, symbolizes sensitivity and perception.

##Reticular Layer##  
Deeper network giving structure and flexibility.  
Energetically, reflects stability and strength.

###Function###  
Supports skin resilience, senses touch and temperature, and nourishes the epidermis.  
Energetically, maintains balance between sensitivity and emotional strength.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: emotional agitation.  
Loss of elasticity: fatigue or rigidity.  
Irritation: hypersensitivity or inner tension.
