#Integumentary System – Dermis (Cat)#

##Location##
The dermis lies **beneath the epidermis**, forming the middle layer of the cat’s skin.

##Shape##
Dense and flexible connective tissue rich in **collagen, blood vessels, glands, and nerve endings**.

##Parts##

###1. Papillary Layer###
- Contains capillaries and touch receptors.  
- **Function:** Nourishes the epidermis and allows sensory detection.  

###2. Reticular Layer###
- Thick layer of collagen and elastin fibers.  
- **Function:** Provides structure, elasticity, and durability.  

###3. Hair Follicles and Glands###
- Embedded throughout the dermis.  
- **Function:** Support hair growth, oil secretion, and sweat regulation.  

##Function##
- **Support:** Provides skin strength and elasticity.  
- **Sensation:** Contains touch, temperature, and pain receptors.  
- **Circulation:** Delivers oxygen and nutrients to the epidermis.
