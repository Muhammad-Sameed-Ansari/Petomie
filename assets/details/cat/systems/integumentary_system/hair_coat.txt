##Location##
Covers nearly the entire body surface, except paw pads, nose, and mucous areas.

##Shape##
A **dense fur covering** composed of fine undercoat hairs and longer guard hairs.  
Highly adapted for thermoregulation and sensory communication.

##Parts##

###1. Guard Hairs###
- Long, coarse outer hairs.  
- **Function:** Provide protection from moisture and physical contact.  

###2. Undercoat###
- Soft, dense layer beneath guard hairs.  
- **Function:** Maintains warmth and insulation.  

###3. Hair Follicle###
- Anchored deep in the dermis and connected to sebaceous glands.  
- **Function:** Supports hair growth and oil secretion.  

##Function##
- **Insulation:** Maintains body temperature balance.  
- **Protection:** Shields skin from UV light and damage.  
- **Sensory input:** Assists in communication and environmental awareness.
