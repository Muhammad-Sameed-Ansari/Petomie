#Whiskers (Vibrissae)#

Whiskers are specialized sensory hairs that detect changes in air and vibration.  
They support navigation and spatial awareness.  
Energetically, they represent perception, boundaries, and intuitive foresight.

###Parts###

##Mystacial Whiskers##  
Located on the muzzle, sensing air flow and distance.  
Energetically, symbolize spatial awareness and confidence.

##Superciliary and Mandibular Whiskers##  
Above eyes and below chin for facial protection.  
Energetically, reflect alertness and emotional intuition.

###Function###  
Provide environmental perception, balance, and communication.  
Energetically, enhance intuition and sensitivity to unseen movement.

###Common Imbalances###  
Trimming or damage: disorientation or emotional shock.  
Overgrowth: overstimulation.  
Breakage: stress or confusion.
