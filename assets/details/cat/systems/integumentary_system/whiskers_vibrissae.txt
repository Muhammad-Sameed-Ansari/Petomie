##Location##
Found on the **muzzle, above eyes, cheeks, and forelegs**.

##Shape##
Long, thick, and deeply rooted hairs surrounded by nerve-rich blood sinuses.

##Parts##

###1. Follicle-Sinus Complex###
- Capsule of nerves, connective tissue, and blood supply.  
- **Function:** Detects vibration, air movement, and contact.  

###2. Whisker Shaft###
- Long keratinized hair projecting from follicle.  
- **Function:** Acts as a sensory antenna to detect obstacles.  

##Function##
- **Navigation:** Guides cats through tight spaces and low light.  
- **Sensation:** Detects air currents and nearby objects.  
- **Communication:** Reflects emotion and orientation.
