#Hypodermis#

The hypodermis anchors the skin to underlying muscles and stores fat for warmth and energy.  
It cushions the body and enables smooth movement.  
Energetically, it represents grounding, safety, and emotional stability.

###Parts###

##Fat Cells##  
Provide insulation and energy reserves.  
Energetically, symbolize nourishment and support.

##Connective Tissue##  
Links skin to muscle layers.  
Energetically, reflects unity and inner balance.

###Function###  
Protects internal structures, stores energy, and insulates heat.  
Energetically, supports comfort, calmness, and steady flow.

###Common Imbalances###  
Thinning: vulnerability or lack of support.  
Inflammation: stress or overprotection.  
Loss of insulation: fatigue or depletion.
