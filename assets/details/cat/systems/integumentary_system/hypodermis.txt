##Location##
Situated **beneath the dermis**, connecting the skin to the underlying muscles and skeleton.

##Shape##
Composed of **loose connective tissue, elastic fibers, and adipose (fat) cells**.  
Thickness varies depending on body condition and age.

##Parts##

###1. Adipose Tissue###
- Clusters of fat cells.  
- **Function:** Stores energy, cushions organs, and provides insulation.  

###2. Connective Fibers###
- Elastic and collagen fibers interwoven throughout.  
- **Function:** Provide skin flexibility and support.  

###3. Blood Vessels###
- Rich network supplying the upper skin layers.  
- **Function:** Maintains skin temperature and nutrient delivery.  

##Function##
- **Protection:** Cushions and supports deeper tissues.  
- **Insulation:** Helps maintain body temperature.  
- **Energy storage:** Acts as a fat and nutrient reserve.
