##Location##
Located at the **base of each hair follicle** within the dermis.

##Shape##
Fine, sensitive nerve endings wrapped around each follicle, forming a sensory network.

##Parts##

###1. Merkel Cells###
- Specialized cells near hair follicles.  
- **Function:** Detect light touch and pressure.  

###2. Nerve Endings###
- Sensory fibers surrounding each follicle.  
- **Function:** Respond to movement and vibration.  

##Function##
- **Sensation:** Provides detailed tactile perception.  
- **Awareness:** Enhances response to environmental stimuli.
