##Location##
Distributed through the **epidermis, hair shafts, and skin markings**.

##Shape##
Varies in color and pattern due to genetics, environment, and breed type.

##Parts##

###1. Melanocytes###
- Pigment cells within the epidermis.  
- **Function:** Produce melanin to protect against UV light and define color.  

###2. Pigmented Hairs###
- Hairs with melanin granules.  
- **Function:** Create distinct fur colors and patterns.  

###3. Markings###
- Spots, stripes, or patches.  
- **Function:** Provide camouflage and breed recognition.  

##Function##
- **Protection:** Prevents UV-induced skin damage.  
- **Camouflage:** Aids hunting and concealment.  
- **Identification:** Indicates health, mood, and genetic traits.
