#Ulnar Nerve#
Part of the Nervous System: A branch of the brachial plexus (C8–T2) that innervates the flexors of the carpus and digits and provides sensation to the caudal paw.  
Energetically, it represents trust, flexibility, and emotional sensitivity — the ability to grasp and release with balance.

###Parts###

##Origin##  
Emerges from spinal nerves C8–T2.  
Energetically, symbolizes balance between strength and receptivity.

##Pathway##  
Travels along the medial side of the limb to the paw.  
Energetically, reflects emotional flow and connection.

##Branches##  
Motor to flexors, sensory to the fifth digit and caudal paw.  
Energetically, relates to empathy and gentle strength.

###Function###  
Provides flexion control and sensation to the caudal paw.  
Energetically, governs balance between holding on and letting go.

###Common Imbalances###  
Numbness: detachment or withdrawal.  
Pain: emotional strain or overcontrol.  
Energetic blockage: resistance to connection or release.
