#Brachial Plexus#
Part of the Nervous System: A network of nerves arising from spinal nerves C6–T2 that supplies the forelimb.  
Energetically, it represents connection, coordination, and communication between thought and action.

###Parts###

##Roots##  
Composed of ventral branches of spinal nerves C6–T2.  
Energetically, symbolizes integration between intellect and motion.

##Trunks and Cords##  
Formed by merging nerve roots that distribute into peripheral nerves.  
Energetically, reflect unity and collaboration in action.

##Major Branches##  
Include radial, median, ulnar, musculocutaneous, and axillary nerves.  
Energetically, represent purpose and fluid communication in expression.

###Function###  
Controls motor and sensory function of the shoulder, forelimb, and paw.  
Coordinates voluntary movement and tactile sensation.  
Energetically, governs harmony between intention and action.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weakness: fear of reaching out or expressing.  
Pain or compression: blocked communication or resistance.  
Energetic stagnation: loss of coordination or creative flow.
