#Pudendal Nerve#
Part of the Nervous System: Arises from spinal nerves S1–S3 and supplies the external genitalia, perineum, and anal sphincter.  
Energetically, it represents boundaries, trust, and the balance of control and release.

###Parts###

##Origin##  
Comes from the ventral branches of S1–S3.  
Energetically, symbolizes security and balance between holding and letting go.

##Pathway##  
Travels caudally through the pelvic canal to reach the perineal region.  
Energetically, reflects privacy, safety, and grounding.

##Branches##  
Includes caudal rectal, perineal, and dorsal nerves of the penis or clitoris.  
Energetically, represent integrity and personal power.

###Function###  
Controls the external anal sphincter and sensory input from the perineal area.  
Supports elimination, reproduction, and emotional boundaries.  
Energetically, governs grounding and the root of security.

###Common Imbalances###  
Pain: tension or fear of vulnerability.  
Numbness: emotional disconnection or avoidance.  
Energetic blockage: repression or boundary confusion.
