#Parathyroid Glands#

##Parts##
###Chief Cells###
Produce **parathyroid hormone (PTH)** to raise calcium levels in the blood.  

###Oxyphil Cells###
Support calcium metabolism and may act as hormone reserves.  

###Small Blood Vessels###
Allow rapid hormone release into circulation.  

##Function##
Regulates calcium and phosphorus balance in the body, ensuring healthy **bones, teeth, muscles, and nerve function**.  
Works with **calcitonin** from the thyroid gland to keep calcium levels stable.  
Prevents low calcium levels that could lead to tremors or weakness.
