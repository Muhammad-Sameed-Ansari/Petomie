#Parathyroid Glands#

The parathyroid glands are small, paired structures located on the thyroid gland’s surface.  
They regulate calcium and phosphorus balance in the blood and tissues.  
Energetically, they represent stability, adaptability, and the fine-tuning of emotional and physical strength.

###Parts###

##Chief Cells##  
Secrete parathyroid hormone (PTH).  
Energetically, symbolize adjustment and internal resilience.

###Function###  
Maintain calcium levels by stimulating bone release and kidney absorption.  
Energetically, balance flexibility and firmness, adapting to life’s changing structure.

###Common Imbalances###  
Calcium imbalance: instability or emotional rigidity.  
Weakness: lack of grounding.  
Overactivity: tension or overstimulation.
