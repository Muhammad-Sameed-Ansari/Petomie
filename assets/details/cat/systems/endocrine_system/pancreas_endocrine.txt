#Pancreas (Endocrine)#

The endocrine portion of the pancreas regulates blood sugar and energy metabolism.  
It contains clusters of cells called the Islets of Langerhans.  
Energetically, it represents nourishment, balance, and the sweetness of life.

###Parts###

##Alpha Cells##  
Produce glucagon to raise blood glucose.  
Energetically, symbolize motivation and action.

##Beta Cells##  
Produce insulin to lower blood glucose.  
Energetically, reflect receptivity and trust in lifeâ€™s flow.

##Delta Cells##  
Release somatostatin to regulate both alpha and beta activity.  
Energetically, embody balance and moderation.

###Function###  
Maintains stable blood sugar and energy levels.  
Energetically, harmonizes giving and receiving, effort and rest.

###Common Imbalances###  
Hypoglycemia: over-giving or depletion.  
Hyperglycemia: holding on or overcontrol.  
Pancreatic fatigue: imbalance in emotional nourishment.
