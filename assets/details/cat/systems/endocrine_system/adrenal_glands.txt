#Adrenal Glands#

The adrenal glands are small, paired organs located above the kidneys.  
They produce hormones that regulate metabolism, stress response, and balance within the body.  
Energetically, they represent adaptability, courage, and the instinctive flow of energy during change.

###Parts###

##Cortex##  
Outer layer producing steroid hormones such as cortisol and aldosterone.  
Energetically, symbolizes strength, endurance, and grounding.

##Medulla##  
Inner core releasing adrenaline and noradrenaline for stress response.  
Energetically, represents alertness, readiness, and instinctive protection.

###Function###  
Regulate stress responses, water balance, and blood pressure.  
Support metabolism and help maintain homeostasis.  
Energetically, they stabilize vitality and the ability to respond with clarity and focus.

###Common Imbalances###  
Fatigue: depletion of energy or prolonged stress.  
Overactivity: anxiety, restlessness, or excessive alertness.  
Hormonal imbalance: instability in emotional grounding.
