#Adrenal Glands (Cortex and Medulla)#

##Parts##
###Adrenal Cortex###
Outer layer that produces steroid hormones:  
- **Cortisol:** Controls stress, inflammation, and metabolism.  
- **Aldosterone:** Balances sodium and potassium, maintaining blood pressure.  
- **Sex Steroids:** Small amounts influence mood and body strength.  

###Adrenal Medulla###
Inner layer that produces adrenaline (**epinephrine**) and noradrenaline (**norepinephrine**). These hormones activate the “fight or flight” response.  

##Function##
Regulates **stress response, metabolism, and water balance**.  
During excitement or fear, the adrenal glands increase heart rate and alertness.  
Prolonged stress can lead to adrenal fatigue, affecting energy and immunity.
