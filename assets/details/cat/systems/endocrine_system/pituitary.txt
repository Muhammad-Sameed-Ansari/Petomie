#Pituitary Gland#

The pituitary gland is a small oval organ at the brain’s base, housed in the sella turcica.  
It is known as the “master gland,” controlling many other endocrine organs.  
Energetically, it represents harmony, direction, and faith in the body’s wisdom.

###Parts###

##Anterior Lobe (Adenohypophysis)##  
Releases growth hormone, ACTH, TSH, LH, FSH, and prolactin.  
Energetically, symbolizes productivity and nurturing leadership.

##Posterior Lobe (Neurohypophysis)##  
Stores and releases oxytocin and vasopressin from the hypothalamus.  
Energetically, reflects trust and emotional bonding.

###Function###  
Coordinates hormonal output across multiple systems.  
Energetically, harmonizes communication and supports purposeful growth.

###Common Imbalances###  
Overactivity: tension, overwhelm, or restlessness.  
Underactivity: fatigue or disconnection.  
Hormonal imbalance: lack of coordination or inner direction.
