#Thyroid Gland#

##Parts##
###Right and Left Lobes###
Located beside the trachea, below the larynx. They are reddish-brown and connected by a thin bridge of tissue.  

###Thyroid Follicles###
Store and release **T3 (triiodothyronine)** and **T4 (thyroxine)**, which control metabolic rate and energy use.  

###Parafollicular Cells###
Secrete **calcitonin**, which lowers calcium levels in the blood by storing calcium in bones.  

##Function##
Regulates **metabolism**, **body temperature**, **growth**, and **energy production**.  
Maintains healthy skin, coat, heart rate, and digestion.  
In cats, overactivity (hyperthyroidism) is common in older individuals, causing weight loss, hyperactivity, and increased appetite.
