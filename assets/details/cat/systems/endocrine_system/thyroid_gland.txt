#Thyroid Gland#

The thyroid gland lies along the trachea in the neck.  
It secretes hormones that regulate metabolism, temperature, and energy use.  
Energetically, it represents expression, flow, and balanced transformation.

###Parts###

##Lobes##  
Two connected lobes flanking the trachea.  
Energetically, symbolize duality and harmony between doing and being.

##Follicular Cells##  
Produce thyroxine (T4) and triiodothyronine (T3).  
Energetically, reflect transformation and life energy.

##Parafollicular Cells##  
Secrete calcitonin for calcium regulation.  
Energetically, embody calmness and stability.

###Function###  
Regulates metabolism, growth, and heat production.  
Energetically, maintains balance between activity and stillness, inner fire and calm.

###Common Imbalances###  
Hyperthyroidism: excess activity or agitation.  
Hypothyroidism: fatigue, coldness, or emotional withdrawal.  
Imbalance: conflict between expression and rest.
