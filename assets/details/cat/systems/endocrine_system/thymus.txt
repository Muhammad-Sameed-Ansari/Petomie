#Thymus#

##Parts##
###Cortex###
Outer layer where young **T-lymphocytes** are created and begin training.  

###Medulla###
Inner region where mature T-cells are released into the bloodstream.  

###Thymic Hormones###
Produce **thymosin**, which helps immune cells recognize harmful invaders.  

##Function##
Supports **immune development** in kittens by producing and training T-cells.  
Strengthens disease resistance early in life and protects against infections.  
Shrinks with age as the immune system matures but remains essential for strong defense.
