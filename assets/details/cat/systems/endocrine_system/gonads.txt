#Gonads (Testes and Ovaries)#

##Parts##
###Testes (Male)###
Located in the scrotum, they produce **sperm** and **testosterone**.  
Testosterone maintains muscle strength, libido, and territorial behavior.  

###Ovaries (Female)###
Found near the kidneys, they release **eggs** and produce **estrogen** and **progesterone**, controlling the heat cycle and pregnancy.  

###Reproductive Ducts###
Carry sperm or eggs and support hormone transport between organs.  

##Function##
Regulate **reproduction, sexual behavior, and fertility**.  
Influence body growth, energy, and emotional balance.  
Hormone changes affect behavior, mood, and coat condition throughout the breeding cycle.
