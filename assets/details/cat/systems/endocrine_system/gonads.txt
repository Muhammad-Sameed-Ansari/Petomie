#Gonads#

The gonads include the ovaries in females and testes in males.  
They produce reproductive hormones and gametes essential for fertility and mating behavior.  
Energetically, they represent creativity, vitality, and the flow of life energy through connection and renewal.

###Parts###

##Ovaries##  
Located near the kidneys, producing estrogen and progesterone.  
Energetically, symbolize intuition, nurturing, and creation.

##Testes##  
Located within the scrotum, producing testosterone and sperm.  
Energetically, reflect strength, instinct, and vitality.

###Function###  
Regulate reproductive cycles, secondary sexual traits, and hormonal balance.  
Energetically, they govern creation, inspiration, and emotional expression.

###Common Imbalances###  
Hormonal fluctuation: mood or behavior shifts.  
Infertility: blocked creative energy.  
Overstimulation: restlessness or dominance imbalance.
