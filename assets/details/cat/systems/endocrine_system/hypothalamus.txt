#Hypothalamus#

The hypothalamus is located at the base of the brain, connecting the nervous and endocrine systems.  
It controls hunger, thirst, body temperature, and hormone release through the pituitary gland.  
Energetically, it represents balance between instinct and awareness, and harmony between body and mind.

###Parts###

##Supraoptic and Paraventricular Nuclei##  
Produce oxytocin and antidiuretic hormone.  
Energetically, symbolize compassion and emotional flow.

##Arcuate and Ventromedial Nuclei##  
Regulate appetite and metabolism.  
Energetically, reflect stability and inner control.

###Function###  
Coordinates endocrine and autonomic functions.  
Maintains body temperature, hunger, thirst, and emotional balance.  
Energetically, governs awareness and intuitive regulation.

###Common Imbalances###  
Appetite changes: imbalance in emotional fulfillment.  
Temperature dysregulation: instability in inner harmony.  
Stress reactivity: overcontrol or exhaustion.
