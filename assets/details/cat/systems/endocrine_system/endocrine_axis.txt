#Endocrine Axis#

The endocrine axis links the hypothalamus, pituitary gland, and target endocrine organs.  
It forms a feedback system that maintains hormonal harmony throughout the body.  
Energetically, it represents integration, communication, and the balance between instinct, emotion, and physical function.

###Parts###

##Hypothalamic Control##  
Regulates hormone release through signals to the pituitary.  
Energetically, symbolizes inner leadership and direction.

##Pituitary Mediation##  
Acts as the master gland, transmitting messages to other organs.  
Energetically, represents harmony and trust between systems.

##Target Glands##  
Include adrenal, thyroid, gonadal, and pancreatic glands.  
Energetically, reflect cooperation and holistic balance.

###Function###  
Maintains hormonal balance through feedback loops.  
Coordinates growth, metabolism, reproduction, and stress response.  
Energetically, aligns intuition, emotion, and body function into unity.

###Common Imbalances###  
Disconnection: fatigue, confusion, or emotional imbalance.  
Overactivation: tension, restlessness, or hyper-reactivity.  
Suppression: lack of motivation or inner alignment.
