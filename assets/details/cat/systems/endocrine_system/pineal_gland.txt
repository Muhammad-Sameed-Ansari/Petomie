#Pineal Gland#

##Parts##
###Pinealocytes###
Specialized cells that produce **melatonin**, a hormone secreted mainly at night.  

###Nerve Fibers###
Receive signals from light-sensitive pathways, helping the gland detect changes in daylight.  

###Blood Capillaries###
Distribute melatonin to all parts of the body through circulation.  

##Function##
Produces **melatonin**, which regulates **sleep-wake cycles**, **daily rhythms**, and **seasonal behavior**.  
Helps cats adjust activity to light and darkness and influences rest, alertness, and mood stability.
