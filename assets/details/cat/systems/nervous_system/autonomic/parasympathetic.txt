#Parasympathetic System#
The parasympathetic system restores calm and recovery.  
In the cat, it governs digestion, rest, and healing.  
Energetically, it represents peace, trust, and emotional harmony.

###Parts###

##Vagus and Pelvic Nerves##  
Major components influencing heart, lungs, and organs.  
Energetically, symbolize connection and surrender.

###Function###  
Slows heart rate and enhances digestion and recovery.  
Energetically, encourages balance and tranquility.

###Common Imbalances###  
Overactivity: lethargy or stagnation.  
Weakness: tension and inability to rest.  
Energetic imbalance: disconnection from calm or faith.
