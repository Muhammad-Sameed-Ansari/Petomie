#Enteric System#
Known as the “second brain,” the enteric nervous system controls gut function.  
In the cat, it maintains digestive rhythm and communication with the brain.  
Energetically, it represents intuition, inner knowing, and emotional processing.

###Parts###

##Myenteric and Submucosal Plexuses##  
Networks embedded in the intestinal wall.  
Energetically, symbolize inner communication and wisdom.

###Function###  
Regulates gut movement, secretion, and interaction with microbes.  
Energetically, connects instinct with emotional flow.

###Common Imbalances###  
Digestive upset: stress or emotional strain.  
Tension: holding back feelings.  
Energetic stagnation: disconnection from intuition or inner truth.
