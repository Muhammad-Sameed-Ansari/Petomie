#Brainstem#
The brainstem connects the brain with the spinal cord in the cat. It regulates vital life functions such as breathing and heart rate.  
Energetically, it represents the rhythm of life and the balance between control and surrender.

###Parts###

##Midbrain, Pons, and Medulla##  
Regulate reflexes, sleep, and automatic rhythms.  
Energetically, symbolize natural flow and unconscious trust.

###Function###  
Maintains breathing, circulation, and reflex control.  
Energetically, supports the instinctual rhythm of life.

###Common Imbalances###  
Respiratory issues: resistance to flow.  
Tension: overcontrol of life processes.  
Energetic stagnation: imbalance between trust and vigilance.
