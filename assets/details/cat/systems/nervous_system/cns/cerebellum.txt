#Cerebellum#
The cerebellum is part of the central nervous system, controlling coordination and precision in the cat.  
It fine-tunes balance and posture, ensuring smooth movement.  
Energetically, it represents grace, flow, and harmony between thought and action.

###Parts###

##Anterior and Posterior Lobes##  
Coordinate voluntary movement.  
Energetically, symbolize refinement and precision.

##Flocculonodular Lobe##  
Assists in balance and spatial orientation.  
Energetically, reflects equilibrium and centeredness.

###Function###  
Maintains coordination, rhythm, and posture.  
Energetically, supports calm movement and internal harmony.

###Common Imbalances###  
Clumsiness: loss of rhythm or harmony.  
Overcontrol: tension or rigidity.  
Energetic disharmony: imbalance between instinct and grace.
