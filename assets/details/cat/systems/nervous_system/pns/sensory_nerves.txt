#Sensory Nerves#
Sensory nerves carry signals from the environment to the brain.  
In the cat, they process touch, temperature, and body awareness.  
Energetically, they represent perception, intuition, and connection with surroundings.

###Parts###

##Receptors and Fibers##  
Include touch, pain, temperature, and proprioception.  
Energetically, symbolize awareness and receptivity.

###Function###  
Transmit external stimuli for interpretation and response.  
Energetically, awaken understanding and sensitivity.

###Common Imbalances###  
Numbness: disconnection or avoidance.  
Pain: heightened emotional alertness.  
Energetic imbalance: hypersensitivity or dullness.
