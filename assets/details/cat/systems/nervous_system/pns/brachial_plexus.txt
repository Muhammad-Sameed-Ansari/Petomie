#Brachial Plexus#
The brachial plexus is a network of nerves in the shoulder that controls the forelimb.  
In the cat, it coordinates muscle strength and limb sensation.  
Energetically, it represents action, communication, and graceful expression.

###Parts###

##Roots and Branches##  
Include radial, median, and ulnar nerves.  
Energetically, symbolize connection and purpose.

###Function###  
Controls limb motion and sensitivity.  
Energetically, allows balanced expression through action.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weakness: hesitation to act.  
Pain: emotional restraint.  
Energetic stagnation: blocked communication through motion.
