#Motor Nerves#
Motor nerves transmit impulses from the brain and spinal cord to muscles.  
In the cat, they allow purposeful movement and strength.  
Energetically, they represent drive, motivation, and willpower in action.

###Parts###

##Upper and Lower Motor Neurons##  
Coordinate voluntary movement and reflexes.  
Energetically, symbolize direction and determination.

###Function###  
Control contraction and motion.  
Energetically, connect intention with physical manifestation.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weakness: lack of motivation or confidence.  
Spasms: tension or over-effort.  
Energetic stagnation: misdirected energy or frustration.
