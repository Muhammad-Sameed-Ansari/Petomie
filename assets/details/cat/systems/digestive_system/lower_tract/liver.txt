#Liver#

##Location##
Situated in the upper abdomen, just behind the diaphragm and in front of the stomach.

##Shape##
A large, dark reddish-brown organ divided into multiple lobes. Unlike horses, cats have a gallbladder that stores bile.

##Parts##
- **Lobes:** Right, left, quadrate, and caudate.  
- **Bile Ducts:** Carry bile to the gallbladder and duodenum.  
- **Blood Vessels:** Filter and supply nutrients.  

##Function##
- Produces bile for fat digestion.  
- Stores vitamins, minerals, and glycogen.  
- Detoxifies waste products and chemicals.  
- Regulates metabolism and nutrient balance.  
- Filters blood and processes absorbed nutrients.
