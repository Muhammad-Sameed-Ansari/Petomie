#Pancreas#

The pancreas lies near the stomach and duodenum, producing enzymes and hormones.  
It plays a key role in digestion and blood sugar regulation.  
Energetically, it represents balance, sweetness of life, and emotional nourishment.

###Parts###

##Exocrine Section##  
Produces digestive enzymes for fat and protein breakdown.  
Energetically, reflects transformation and vitality.

##Endocrine Section##  
Secretes insulin and glucagon to maintain blood sugar balance.  
Energetically, symbolizes harmony, giving, and receiving.

###Function###  
Supports digestion through enzyme secretion and regulates energy with hormones.  
Energetically, aligns nourishment with inner peace and calm strength.

###Common Imbalances###  
Pancreatitis: stress, worry, or over-giving.  
Enzyme deficiency: fatigue or loss of joy.  
Imbalance in blood sugar: emotional highs and lows.
