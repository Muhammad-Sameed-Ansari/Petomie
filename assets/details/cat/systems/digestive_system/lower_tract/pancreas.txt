#Pancreas#

##Location##
Located near the duodenum and stomach within the abdominal cavity.

##Shape##
A soft, flattened gland with both digestive and hormonal functions.

##Parts##
- **Exocrine Portion:** Produces digestive enzymes.  
- **Endocrine Portion:** Contains the islets of Langerhans that secrete insulin and glucagon.  

##Function##
- Produces enzymes to digest fats, proteins, and carbohydrates.  
- Regulates blood sugar levels through insulin and glucagon.  
- Supports nutrient absorption and metabolism.  
- Maintains digestive efficiency and balance.
