#Gallbladder#

##Location##
Nestled between the lobes of the liver.

##Shape##
Small, pear-shaped sac with thin walls.

##Parts##
- **Fundus:** Rounded end where bile accumulates.  
- **Body:** Main section for bile storage.  
- **Neck:** Connects to the cystic duct.  

##Function##
- Stores and concentrates bile from the liver.  
- Releases bile into the duodenum during digestion.  
- Aids in fat breakdown and absorption.
