#Small Intestine#

The small intestine is a long coiled tube where most digestion and nutrient absorption occur.  
It is lined with villi that increase the surface for absorption.  
Energetically, it represents discernment, clarity, and the art of choosing what to keep and what to release.

###Parts###

##Duodenum##  
Receives food from the stomach and mixes it with bile and enzymes.  
Energetically, symbolizes initiation and trust.

##Jejunum##  
Main area of absorption.  
Energetically, reflects integration and wisdom.

##Ileum##  
Final portion connecting to the large intestine.  
Energetically, embodies closure and transition.

###Function###  
Completes digestion, absorbs nutrients, and passes residues to the large intestine.  
Energetically, it teaches balance, sensitivity, and inner knowing.

###Common Imbalances###  
Malabsorption: difficulty integrating experiences.  
Inflammation: emotional sensitivity or reactivity.  
Bloating: holding emotional energy or confusion.
