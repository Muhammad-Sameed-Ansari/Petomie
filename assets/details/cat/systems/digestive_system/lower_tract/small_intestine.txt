#Small Intestine#

##Location##
Extends from the stomach to the large intestine, coiled in the central abdominal cavity.

##Shape##
A long, narrow, flexible tube lined with villi to maximize nutrient absorption.

##Parts##
- **Duodenum:** First section receiving bile and pancreatic enzymes.  
- **Jejunum:** Middle section where most digestion and nutrient absorption occur.  
- **Ileum:** Final section connecting to the large intestine, absorbing remaining nutrients.

##Function##
- Completes digestion of proteins, carbohydrates, and fats.  
- Absorbs nutrients, vitamins, and minerals into the bloodstream.  
- Moves chyme toward the colon with rhythmic contractions.  
- Maintains gut flora balance and supports immune defense.
