#Tongue#

##Location##
Occupies the floor of the mouth, attached to the hyoid bone.

##Shape##
A muscular organ with a rough surface covered in backward-facing papillae. Highly flexible and sensitive.

##Parts##
- **Tip and Body:** Manipulate food and water.  
- **Root:** Anchors tongue to the base of the mouth.  
- **Papillae:** Aid grooming, tasting, and handling food.

##Function##
- Moves food during chewing and swallowing.  
- Mixes food with saliva.  
- Assists grooming with rough papillae.  
- Helps cats lap water efficiently.
