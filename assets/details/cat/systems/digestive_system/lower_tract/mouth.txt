#Mouth#

##Location##
Located at the front of the face, extending from the lips to the pharynx. It serves as the first part of the digestive system.

##Shape##
A small, flexible cavity with soft lips, sharp teeth, and a muscular tongue, lined with moist mucous membranes.

##Parts##
- **Lips:** Hold and guide food into the mouth.  
- **Cheeks:** Keep food between the teeth during chewing.  
- **Palate:** Forms the roof of the mouth.  
- **Teeth and Tongue:** Help chew, move, and taste food.  
- **Salivary Ducts:** Openings for saliva secretion.

##Function##
- Receives food and begins digestion.  
- Mixes food with saliva to form a bolus.  
- Starts digestion of carbohydrates and fats.  
- Prepares food for swallowing.
