#Rectum#

The rectum is the final straight section of the large intestine that stores feces before elimination.  
It connects to the anus and maintains control of release.  
Energetically, it represents patience, readiness, and serenity at closure.

###Parts###

##Upper Section##  
Receives feces from the colon.  
Energetically, symbolizes calm preparation and order.

##Lower Section##  
Leads to the anal canal.  
Energetically, reflects acceptance and peaceful surrender.

###Function###  
Holds waste before defecation, allowing timing and control.  
Energetically, it governs emotional closure and graceful release.

###Common Imbalances###  
Constipation: hesitation or inner holding.  
Inflammation: irritation or restlessness.  
Pain: emotional strain or resistance to release.
