#Rectum#

##Location##
At the end of the large intestine, within the pelvic cavity, connecting to the anus.

##Shape##
A straight, muscular tube lined with mucus-producing glands to ease fecal passage.

##Parts##
- **Upper Section:** Stores feces temporarily.  
- **Lower Section:** Connects to the anal canal.  

##Function##
- Stores fecal material until defecation.  
- Stimulates the defecation reflex.  
- Controls elimination through sphincter coordination.
