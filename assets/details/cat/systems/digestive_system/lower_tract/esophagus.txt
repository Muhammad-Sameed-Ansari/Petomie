#Esophagus#

##Location##
Extends from the pharynx through the neck and chest to the stomach.

##Shape##
A muscular, elastic tube lined with mucous membrane. It passes through the diaphragm into the stomach.

##Parts##
- **Cervical Section:** Runs along the neck.  
- **Thoracic Section:** Passes through the chest cavity.  
- **Cardiac Sphincter:** Prevents reflux into the esophagus.

##Function##
- Moves food to the stomach via peristaltic waves.  
- Prevents regurgitation with strong sphincter control.  
- Transports food safely between mouth and stomach.
