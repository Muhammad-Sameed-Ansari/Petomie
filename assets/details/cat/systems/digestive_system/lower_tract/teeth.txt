#Teeth#

##Location##
Fixed within the upper and lower jaws (maxilla and mandible).

##Shape##
Sharp, pointed teeth adapted for cutting and tearing meat.

##Parts##
- **Incisors:** Small teeth used for nibbling and grooming.  
- **Canines:** Long, pointed teeth for gripping and tearing.  
- **Premolars and Molars:** Slice and crush food.  
- **Roots and Enamel:** Anchor and protect each tooth.

##Function##
- Cut and tear meat into smaller pieces.  
- Support proper jaw alignment.  
- Aid grooming and defense.  
- Begin mechanical breakdown of food.
