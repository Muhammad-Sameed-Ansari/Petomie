###Parts###

##Cecum##  
Small pouch between small and large intestines.  
Energetically, symbolizes transition and readiness.

##Colon##  
Main section for absorption and compaction.  
Energetically, reflects order, purification, and emotional grounding.

##Rectum##  
Final segment leading to the anus.  
Energetically, embodies patience and calm before release.

###Function###  
Reclaims water and electrolytes, compacts waste, and prepares for elimination.  
Energetically, it harmonizes endings and emotional flow.

###Common Imbalances###  
Diarrhea: emotional overstimulation or inability to hold.  
Constipation: attachment or emotional resistance.  
Inflammation: irritation from inner imbalance.
