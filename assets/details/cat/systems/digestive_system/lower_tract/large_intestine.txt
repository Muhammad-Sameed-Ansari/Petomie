#Large Intestine#

##Location##
Located in the posterior part of the abdomen, connecting the small intestine to the anus.

##Shape##
A short, wide tube adapted for water absorption and feces formation.

##Parts##
- **Cecum:** Small pouch that connects the ileum to the colon.  
- **Colon:** Main section that absorbs water and electrolytes.  
- **Rectum:** Terminal portion storing feces before elimination.

##Function##
- Absorbs water and electrolytes.  
- Forms and stores feces.  
- Houses beneficial bacteria that assist in digestion.  
- Supports hydration and waste removal.
