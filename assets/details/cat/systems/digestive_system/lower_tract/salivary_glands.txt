#Salivary Glands#

##Location##
Located around the mouth and throat, opening into the oral cavity through ducts.

##Shape##
Soft, lobulated glands producing watery saliva.

##Parts##
- **Parotid Glands:** Near the ears.  
- **Mandibular Glands:** Beneath the jaw.  
- **Sublingual Glands:** Under the tongue.  
- **Zygomatic Glands:** Near the upper cheek region.

##Function##
- Secrete saliva to moisten food.  
- Begin digestion of starches and fats.  
- Aid swallowing and oral health.  
- Help maintain moisture and comfort in the mouth.
