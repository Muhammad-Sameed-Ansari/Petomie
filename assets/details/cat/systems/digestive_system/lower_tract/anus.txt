#Anus#

##Location##
At the end of the digestive tract, beneath the tail.

##Shape##
Circular muscular opening surrounded by internal and external sphincters.

##Parts##
- **Internal Sphincter:** Involuntary smooth muscle.  
- **External Sphincter:** Voluntary skeletal muscle.  

##Function##
- Controls the release of feces.  
- Maintains continence.  
- Acts as a barrier to prevent bacterial entry.
