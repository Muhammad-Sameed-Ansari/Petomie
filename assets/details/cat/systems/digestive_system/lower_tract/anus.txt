#Anus#

The anus is the final opening of the digestive tract, controlling the release of waste from the body.  
It is surrounded by delicate sphincter muscles that maintain tone and control.  
Energetically, it represents release, emotional cleansing, and the grace of letting go with ease.

###Parts###

##Internal Sphincter##  
Smooth, involuntary muscle maintaining closure.  
Energetically, symbolizes inner control and quiet strength.

##External Sphincter##  
Voluntary muscle for precise control during elimination.  
Energetically, reflects trust and comfort with release.

###Function###  
Regulates elimination and ensures control of waste passage.  
Maintains hygiene and protection through balance and timing.  
Energetically, it supports gentle closure and renewal.

###Common Imbalances###  
Constipation: fear of release or resistance to change.  
Irritation: overstimulation or emotional tension.  
Anal gland issues: holding onto suppressed emotions.
