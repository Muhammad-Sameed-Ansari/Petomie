#Stomach#

##Location##
Located on the left side of the upper abdomen, just behind the diaphragm.

##Shape##
A curved, muscular sac with distinct glandular regions for secretion and digestion.

##Parts##
- **Cardia:** Entry point from the esophagus.  
- **Fundus:** Upper region for food storage.  
- **Body:** Main section for mixing food and gastric juices.  
- **Pylorus:** Exit leading to the small intestine.  

##Function##
- Mixes food with acid and digestive enzymes.  
- Begins protein digestion.  
- Stores and sterilizes food.  
- Controls emptying into the small intestine.
