#Pharynx#

##Location##
Behind the mouth and nasal cavity, connecting to the esophagus and larynx.

##Shape##
A muscular, funnel-shaped passageway used by both the digestive and respiratory systems.

##Parts##
- **Nasopharynx:** Connects to nasal cavity.  
- **Oropharynx:** Receives food from the mouth.  
- **Laryngopharynx:** Leads to the esophagus.  

##Function##
- Guides food from the mouth to the esophagus.  
- Prevents food from entering the airways.  
- Coordinates breathing and swallowing.
