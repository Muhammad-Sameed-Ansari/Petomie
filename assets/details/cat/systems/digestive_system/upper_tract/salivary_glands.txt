#Salivary Glands#

The salivary glands produce saliva to moisten food, begin digestion, and cleanse the mouth.  
Energetically, they represent flow, smooth communication, and ease in receiving nourishment.

###Parts###

##Parotid Glands##  
Located below the ears, secreting watery saliva.  
Energetically, represent lightness and refreshment.

##Mandibular Glands##  
At the angle of the jaw, producing mixed saliva.  
Energetically, symbolize balance and support.

##Sublingual Glands##  
Under the tongue, secreting mucous-rich saliva.  
Energetically, reflect softness and gentle expression.

##Zygomatic Glands##  
Situated near the eye, adding subtle lubrication.  
Energetically, represent subtle support and care.

###Function###  
Moisten and lubricate food, start digestion, and maintain oral health.  
Energetically, they embody fluidity and adaptability.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling or blockage: difficulty letting emotions flow.  
Dry mouth: fear or stress.  
Overproduction: emotional overwhelm.
