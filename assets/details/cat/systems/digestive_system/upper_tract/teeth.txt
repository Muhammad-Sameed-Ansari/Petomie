#Teeth#

The teeth are hard, calcified structures used for grasping, tearing, and grinding food.  
They mark the first mechanical step of digestion.  
Energetically, they symbolize strength, decision, and the power to process life experiences.

###Parts###

##Incisors##  
Front teeth for cutting and grasping food.  
Energetically, represent beginnings and precision.

##Canines##  
Pointed teeth for tearing.  
Energetically, embody willpower and protection.

##Premolars##  
Flattened teeth for grinding and crushing.  
Energetically, reflect integration and adaptability.

##Molars##  
Back teeth for heavy grinding.  
Energetically, signify endurance and completion.

###Function###  
Cut, tear, and grind food into manageable pieces for swallowing.  
Assist in grooming and defense.  
Energetically, they help transform external energy into nourishment.

###Common Imbalances###  
Fractures: stress or internal pressure.  
Wear: overexertion or suppressed emotion.  
Pain: resistance to change or imbalance in grounding.
