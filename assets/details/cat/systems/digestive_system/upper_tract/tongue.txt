#Tongue#

The tongue is a muscular, flexible organ covered in specialized papillae.  
It manipulates food, aids swallowing, and allows tasting and grooming.  
Energetically, it represents communication, sensitivity, and discernment.

###Parts###

##Apex##  
Tip used for licking and tasting.  
Energetically, symbolizes curiosity and awareness.

##Body##  
Central muscular portion controlling movement.  
Energetically, reflects adaptability and expression.

##Root##  
Anchored section connecting to the floor of the mouth.  
Energetically, represents grounding and stability.

##Papillae##  
Tiny projections containing taste buds and rough surfaces.  
Energetically, reflect sensitivity and perception.

###Function###  
Moves and shapes food for swallowing.  
Assists in grooming and tasting.  
Energetically, governs sensory communication and inner truth.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: suppressed expression.  
Injury: conflict in communication.  
Texture changes: energetic imbalance or sensitivity.
