#Stomach#

The stomach is a muscular, expandable organ that receives food from the esophagus.  
It begins chemical digestion through acids and enzymes, turning food into a semi-liquid form.  
Energetically, it represents transformation, emotional processing, and trust in lifeâ€™s nourishment.

###Parts###

##Cardia##  
Entry point where the esophagus connects.  
Energetically, symbolizes acceptance of new experiences.

##Fundus##  
Upper rounded region storing gases and food.  
Energetically, represents potential and expansion.

##Body##  
Main section that mixes food with digestive juices.  
Energetically, reflects transformation and assimilation.

##Pylorus##  
Lower end regulating passage to the small intestine.  
Energetically, embodies control and timing of release.

###Function###  
Stores, mixes, and digests food chemically and mechanically.  
Prepares chyme for further absorption in the intestines.  
Energetically, it processes emotional and energetic experiences.

###Common Imbalances###  
Ulcers: suppressed emotion or self-criticism.  
Nausea: rejection of experience.  
Indigestion: difficulty digesting life.
