#Mouth#

The mouth forms the beginning of the digestive system, where food is received, chewed, and mixed with saliva.  
It allows tasting, vocalizing, and the first step of digestion.  
Energetically, it represents receptivity, nourishment, and how one begins to take in life.

###Parts###

##Lips##  
Form the outer boundary and help grasp and guide food.  
Energetically, they symbolize boundaries and sensitivity.

##Cheeks##  
Muscular walls that keep food within the oral cavity.  
Energetically, they represent containment and inner balance.

##Oral Vestibule##  
Space between lips/cheeks and teeth.  
Energetically, it reflects transition and preparation.

##Oral Cavity Proper##  
Inner region containing tongue, teeth, and salivary openings.  
Energetically, it signifies processing and transformation.

###Function###  
Receives, holds, and prepares food for swallowing.  
Supports communication and sensory awareness.  
Energetically, it aligns with openness to nourishment and experience.

###Common Imbalances###  
Oral injury: resistance to receiving.  
Dryness: lack of flow.  
Pain: blocked expression or unmet need.
