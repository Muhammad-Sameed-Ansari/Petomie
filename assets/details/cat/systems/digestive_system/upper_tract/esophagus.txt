#Esophagus#

The esophagus is a muscular tube connecting the pharynx to the stomach.  
It propels food and water through coordinated muscular waves.  
Energetically, it represents trust, flow, and the ability to let life move forward smoothly.

###Parts###

##Upper Esophageal Sphincter##  
Controls entry of food from the pharynx.  
Energetically, symbolizes permission to receive.

##Esophageal Body##  
Main muscular passage transporting food to the stomach.  
Energetically, represents persistence and directed movement.

##Lower Esophageal Sphincter##  
Guards against backflow from the stomach.  
Energetically, symbolizes protection and appropriate boundaries.

###Function###  
Moves swallowed food toward the stomach through rhythmic contractions.  
Prevents regurgitation by sealing both ends.  
Energetically, it mirrors flow and forward motion in life.

###Common Imbalances###  
Choking: blocked flow or resistance.  
Reflux: reversed energetic direction.  
Spasm: tension or anxiety in transition.
