#Pharynx#

The pharynx is a muscular passage that connects the mouth and nasal cavity to the esophagus.  
It allows both food and air to pass through, coordinating swallowing and breathing.  
Energetically, it represents the point of choice and transition between expression and nourishment.

###Parts###

##Nasopharynx##  
Upper part connecting to the nasal cavity.  
Energetically, reflects clarity and sensory awareness.

##Oropharynx##  
Middle region behind the mouth.  
Energetically, symbolizes acceptance and emotional alignment.

##Laryngopharynx##  
Lower section leading to the esophagus.  
Energetically, represents decision and flow toward nourishment.

###Function###  
Coordinates swallowing and prevents food entering the airway.  
Guides food toward the esophagus while maintaining breathing rhythm.  
Energetically, aligns communication and nourishment in harmony.

###Common Imbalances###  
Irritation: conflict at points of choice.  
Difficulty swallowing: fear of moving forward.  
Tension: resistance or stress.
