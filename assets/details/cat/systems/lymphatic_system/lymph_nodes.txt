#Lymph Nodes#

##Parts##

###Structure###
Small, oval nodes found mainly in the jaw, shoulders, armpits, groin, and behind the knees.
Each node has an outer capsule and inner chambers filled with immune cells.
Energetically, they represent **filters and protectors**, keeping only what supports wellbeing.

###Afferent Vessels###
Carry unfiltered lymph to the node, bringing toxins and immune information from tissues.
Energetically, they symbolize **awareness and receptivity**, drawing in experiences to be purified.

###Cortex###
Contains B lymphocytes that identify foreign substances and begin immune responses.
Energetically, this layer mirrors **alertness and defense**, recognizing imbalance early.

###Medulla###
Inner zone that filters lymph, neutralizing bacteria and waste before release.
Energetically, it reflects **cleansing and renewal**, helping energy return to harmony.

###Efferent Vessels###
Carry cleaned lymph toward the bloodstream.
Energetically, they represent **release and clarity**, letting the purified essence flow forward.
