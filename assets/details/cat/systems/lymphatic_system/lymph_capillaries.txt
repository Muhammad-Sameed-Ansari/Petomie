#Lymph Capillaries#
Part of the Lymphatic System: Microscopic vessels collecting lymph from tissues.  
In the cat, they begin the process of fluid return and purification.  
Energetically, they represent sensitivity, subtle awareness, and receiving life energy.

###Parts###

##Structure##  
Tiny thin-walled vessels in connective tissue.  
Energetically, symbolize openness and receptivity.

##Distribution##  
Found throughout the body, except in the brain and bone marrow.  
Energetically, reflect inclusiveness and adaptability.

###Function###  
Absorb interstitial fluid and channel it into larger lymph vessels.  
Energetically, support awareness and flow at the smallest levels.

###Common Imbalances###  
Obstruction: tension or fear of receiving.  
Leakage: energetic depletion or overgiving.  
Energetic stagnation: emotional block or hypersensitivity.
