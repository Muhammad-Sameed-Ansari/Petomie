#Spleen#

##Parts##

###Splenic Capsule###
Protective outer layer that expands and contracts with blood flow.
Energetically, it symbolizes **adaptability and protection**, allowing emotional and physical flexibility.

###Red Pulp###
Filters and recycles red blood cells, storing blood for emergencies.
Energetically, it represents **transformation and renewal**, releasing what no longer serves.

###White Pulp###
Contains immune cells that detect pathogens and produce antibodies.
Energetically, it mirrors **alertness and awareness**, maintaining harmony between inner strength and sensitivity.

###Splenic Vessels###
Arteries and veins circulate blood through the spleen for filtration and nourishment.
Energetically, they symbolize **connection and flow**, distributing vitality across the body.

##Function##
The spleen filters blood, stores energy, and supports immunity.
Energetically, it represents **grounding and emotional balance**, turning nourishment into calm energy.
When harmonious, the cat feels steady, peaceful, and strong; imbalance may lead to fatigue or emotional sensitivity.
