#Optic Nerve (II)#

The cat’s optic nerve allows clear vision even in low light, supporting its predatory focus and coordination.  
It guides movement with precision and trust in perception.  
Energetically, it symbolizes clarity, foresight, and awareness of path.

###Location###
Originates in the retina and extends to the brain, where the two optic nerves meet at the optic chiasm.

###Parts###
Retinal fibers – gather visual signals.  
Optic nerve trunk – carries signals toward the brain.  
Optic chiasm – crossing point enhancing depth perception.  
Optic tracts – transmit data to visual centers.

###Function###
Enables vision, depth, and awareness of surroundings.  
Energetically, it reflects intuitive sight, awareness, and direction.
