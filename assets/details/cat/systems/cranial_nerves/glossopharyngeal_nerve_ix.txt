#Glossopharyngeal Nerve (IX)#

The cat’s glossopharyngeal nerve controls swallowing and taste at the back of the tongue.  
It connects nourishment and expression with sensory awareness.  
Energetically, it represents receiving, tasting life, and truthful communication.

###Location###
Emerges from the medulla and extends into the throat and tongue.

###Parts###
Lingual branch – detects taste at the back of the tongue.  
Pharyngeal branch – assists in swallowing.  
Tympanic branch – provides inner ear sensation.

###Function###
Regulates taste and swallowing reflex.  
Energetically, it supports acceptance, nourishment, and vocal flow.
