#Facial Nerve (VII)#

The cat’s facial nerve controls expressions, ear movement, and taste sensations.  
It also influences emotional expression through subtle facial and ear responses.  
Energetically, it represents communication, empathy, and expression of emotion.

###Location###
Arises from the medulla, passing through the ear region and branching across the face.

###Parts###
Auriculopalpebral branch – moves ears and eyelids.  
Buccal branches – control lips, whiskers, and nostrils.  
Cervical branch – moves lower jaw muscles.

###Function###
Controls facial movement and sensitivity, enabling expression and connection.  
Energetically, it supports emotional release, listening, and heart-centered communication.
