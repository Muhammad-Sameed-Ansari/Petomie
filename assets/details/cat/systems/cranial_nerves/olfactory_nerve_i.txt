#Olfactory Nerve (I)#

The cat’s olfactory nerve governs the sense of smell, essential for detecting prey, territory, and familiar scents.  
It connects the cat to instinctive behavior and emotional memory through scent.  
Energetically, it represents intuition, awareness, and connection to the unseen.

###Location###
Located within the nasal cavity, originating from the olfactory epithelium and extending through the cribriform plate to the olfactory bulbs.

###Parts###
Olfactory epithelium – detects airborne odor molecules.  
Olfactory bulbs – interpret scent signals.  
Olfactory tracts – carry scent information to the brain.

###Function###
Transmits smell information for hunting, bonding, and orientation.  
Energetically, it reflects inner knowing and sensitivity to subtle emotional and energetic cues.
