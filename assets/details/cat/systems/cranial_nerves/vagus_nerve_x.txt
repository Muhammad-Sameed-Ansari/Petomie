#Vagus Nerve (X)#

The cat’s vagus nerve connects the brain with the heart, lungs, and digestive system.  
It regulates calm states, digestion, and relaxation, balancing stress and rest.  
Energetically, it represents peace, balance, and unity between body and spirit.

###Location###
Originates in the medulla and extends through the neck and thoracic cavity into the abdomen.

###Parts###
Cardiac branches – regulate heart rhythm.  
Pulmonary branches – control breathing.  
Abdominal branches – support digestive activity.

###Function###
Manages involuntary functions like heart rate, breathing, and digestion.  
Energetically, it embodies calm, flow, and trust in life’s rhythm.
