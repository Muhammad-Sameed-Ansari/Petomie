#Vestibulocochlear Nerve (VIII)#

The cat’s vestibulocochlear nerve governs balance and hearing, both extremely refined in cats.  
It allows detection of subtle vibrations and orientation even during silent movement.  
Energetically, it reflects harmony, attunement, and sensitivity to vibration.

###Location###
Arises from the inner ear and connects to the brainstem at the medulla.

###Parts###
Vestibular branch – senses balance and body position.  
Cochlear branch – perceives sound frequencies.

###Function###
Maintains balance, hearing, and spatial orientation.  
Energetically, it promotes harmony, alignment, and peace within movement and sound.
