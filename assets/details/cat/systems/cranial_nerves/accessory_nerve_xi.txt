#Accessory Nerve (XI)#

The cat’s accessory nerve governs neck and shoulder movement, aiding posture and grace.  
It connects head orientation with alertness and readiness.  
Energetically, it symbolizes confidence, alignment, and self-assured expression.

###Location###
Originates partly in the medulla and spinal cord, extending to the neck and shoulder muscles.

###Parts###
Cranial branch – assists with throat and vocal movement.  
Spinal branch – controls neck and shoulder muscles.

###Function###
Allows controlled head and neck motion.  
Energetically, it supports presence, balance, and grounded awareness.
