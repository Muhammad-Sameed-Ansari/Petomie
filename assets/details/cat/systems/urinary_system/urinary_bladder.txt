#Urinary Bladder#

###Location###
Situated in the lower abdomen (pelvic cavity), just behind the pubic bone. It can expand into the abdomen when full.

###Shape###
A hollow, elastic, muscular sac with a smooth inner lining.

###Parts###
- **Apex:** Rounded top portion.  
- **Body:** Main expandable part.  
- **Neck:** Narrow portion leading to the urethra.  
- **Trigone:** Area where ureters and urethra connect.

###Function###
- Collects and stores urine until elimination.  
- Contracts to expel urine through the urethra.  
- Regulates urine release through sphincters.
