#Ureters#
The ureters are muscular tubes that carry urine from the kidneys to the bladder.  
In the cat, they ensure smooth and directed flow.  
Energetically, they represent guidance, transition, and the movement of emotional release.

###Parts###

##Upper and Lower Segments##  
Connect the kidneys to the bladder.  
Energetically, symbolize direction and clarity in transition.

###Function###  
Transport urine through rhythmic contractions.  
Energetically, channel flow and assist emotional movement.

###Common Imbalances###  
Obstruction: blockage or resistance.  
Pain: inner struggle with progress.  
Energetic stagnation: stuck emotions or lack of direction.
