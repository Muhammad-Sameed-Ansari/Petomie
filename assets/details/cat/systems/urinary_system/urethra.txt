#Urethra#

###Location###
Extends from the bladder neck to the external opening.  
In males, it runs through the penis; in females, it is shorter and opens into the vulva.

###Shape###
A narrow, flexible tube lined with mucosa and surrounded by smooth muscle.

###Parts###
- **Internal Sphincter:** At the bladder neck; involuntary control.  
- **External Sphincter:** Near the outlet; voluntary control.  
- **Pelvic Section:** Common to both sexes.  
- **Penile Section:** Found only in males.

###Function###
- Carries urine from bladder to the exterior.  
- Maintains continence via sphincter control.  
- In males, also conducts semen during reproduction.
