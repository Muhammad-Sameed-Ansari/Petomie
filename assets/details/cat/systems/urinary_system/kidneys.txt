#Kidneys#

###Location###
Located in the upper abdominal cavity on either side of the spine, just behind the last ribs.
The right kidney sits slightly forward compared to the left.

###Shape###
Smooth, bean-shaped, reddish-brown organs surrounded by a thin capsule.

###Parts###
- **Cortex:** Outer layer containing nephrons that filter the blood.  
- **Medulla:** Inner region collecting urine from nephrons.  
- **Renal Pelvis:** Funnel-shaped cavity that directs urine into the ureter.

- Produce urine and hormones such as erythropoietin.  
- Regulate blood pressure and hydration levels.
