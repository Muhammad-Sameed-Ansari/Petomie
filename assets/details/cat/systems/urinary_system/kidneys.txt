#Kidneys#
The kidneys in the cat filter waste from the blood and regulate fluid balance.  
They are vital organs of purification and internal stability.  
Energetically, they represent emotional balance, trust, and the ability to release what no longer serves.

###Parts###

##Cortex and Medulla##  
Layers that filter and concentrate urine.  
Energetically, symbolize clarity and depth.

##Nephrons##  
Microscopic units that filter waste and control water retention.  
Energetically, reflect precision and balance.

###Function###  
Filter toxins, regulate electrolytes, and balance fluids.  
Energetically, promote emotional release, grounding, and renewal.

###Common Imbalances###  
Dehydration: emotional rigidity or fear.  
Kidney stones: unresolved tension or resistance to flow.  
Energetic stagnation: inability to let go or trust the process.
