#Pharynx#
The pharynx connects the nasal cavity and mouth to the larynx and esophagus.  
It serves as a shared pathway for air and food.  
Energetically, it represents transition, communication, and the balance between speaking and receiving.

###Parts###

##Nasopharynx, Oropharynx, Laryngopharynx##  
Sections guiding air and food flow.  
Energetically, symbolize integration and harmony.

###Function###  
Directs air to the respiratory tract and food to the digestive tract.

Energetic stagnation: hesitation to communicate truth.
