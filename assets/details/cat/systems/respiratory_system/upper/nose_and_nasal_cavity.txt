#Nose and Nasal Cavity#
The nose and nasal cavity of the cat form the first gateway for air intake.  
They filter, warm, and moisten the air before it reaches the lungs.  
Energetically, they represent intuition, perception, and the breath of awareness.

###Parts###

##Nostrils (Nares)##  
Openings that draw air into the nasal cavity.  
Energetically, symbolize openness and curiosity.

##Nasal Turbinates##  
Scroll-like bones that filter and condition air.  
Energetically, reflect sensitivity and refinement.

###Function###  
Cleans, warms, and humidifies inhaled air; detects scent molecules for communication and survival.  
Energetically, connects the outer world with inner awareness through breath.

###Common Imbalances###  
Congestion: blocked perception or emotional heaviness.  
Dryness: loss of connection or detachment.  
Energetic stagnation: resistance to receiving life fully.
