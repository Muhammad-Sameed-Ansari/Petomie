#Bronchi#
The bronchi are the main airways branching from the trachea into each lung.  
They distribute air and regulate airflow to smaller passages.  
Energetically, they represent direction, clarity, and balanced expansion.

###Parts###

##Primary and Secondary Bronchi##  
Guide air into each lung.  
Energetically, symbolize organization and structure.

###Function###  
Distribute air throughout the lungs.  
Energetically, channel inspiration into action with clarity and purpose.

###Common Imbalances###  
Bronchitis: inflammation from irritation or tension.  
Obstruction: holding onto stress.  
Energetic stagnation: difficulty moving forward.
