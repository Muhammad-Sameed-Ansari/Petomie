#Alveoli#
The alveoli are microscopic air sacs in the cat’s lungs where gas exchange occurs.  
They are the meeting point between air and blood — breath and life.  
Energetically, they represent exchange, trust, and the merging of body and spirit.

###Parts###

##Alveolar Walls and Capillaries##  
Thin membranes for oxygen and carbon dioxide exchange.  
Energetically, symbolize openness and harmony.

###Function###  
Exchange oxygen and carbon dioxide between air and bloodstream.  
Energetically, foster renewal and balanced giving and receiving.

###Common Imbalances###  
Fluid buildup: emotional overwhelm.  
Collapse: exhaustion or despair.  
Energetic stagnation: lack of inspiration or restricted breath.
