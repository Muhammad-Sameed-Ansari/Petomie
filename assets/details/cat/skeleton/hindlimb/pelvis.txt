#Pelvis#

##Location##  
The pelvis forms the **hindmost part of the trunk**, connecting the **spine** to the **hind limbs**.  
It lies between the lumbar vertebrae and the femurs, creating the base of the pelvic cavity and the framework for the hips.  

###Shape###  
A **broad, bowl-shaped bone** composed of three fused parts — the ilium, ischium, and pubis — providing support and protection for internal organs.  

###Parts###  

##Ilium##  
Forms the upper, wing-like portion of the pelvis that connects to the sacrum.  
Energetically, symbolizes **support, direction, and stability**.  

##Ischium##  
The lower, posterior part providing sitting support and muscle attachment.  
Energetically, represents **foundation and endurance**.  

##Pubis##  
The ventral portion forming the front of the pelvic arch.  
Energetically, reflects **connection, grounding, and balance**.  

###Function###  
Supports and connects the hind limbs to the body, transmits weight during movement, and protects reproductive and digestive organs.  
Energetically, the pelvis anchors **strength, balance, and centered power**.
