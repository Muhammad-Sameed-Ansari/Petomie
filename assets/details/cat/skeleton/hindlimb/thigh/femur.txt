#Femur#

##Location##  
Located in the **hind limb**, extending from the **hip joint** to the **stifle (knee)**.  
It is the largest and strongest bone in the cat’s body, connecting the pelvis to the tibia.  

###Shape###  
A **long, cylindrical bone** with rounded ends and a pronounced head and trochanter for muscle attachment.  

###Parts###  

##Head##  
Articulates with the acetabulum of the pelvis to form the hip joint.  
Energetically, symbolizes **connection and initiation of movement**.  

##Neck##  
Connects the head to the shaft, allowing flexibility.  
Energetically, represents **transition and flow**.  

##Shaft##  
Provides strength and leverage for movement.  
Energetically, reflects **power and direction**.  

##Distal End##  
Forms the stifle joint with the tibia and patella.  
Energetically, symbolizes **balance and coordination**.  

###Function###  
Supports the body’s weight, provides leverage for propulsion, and enables locomotion.  
Energetically, governs **power, purpose, and forward drive**.
