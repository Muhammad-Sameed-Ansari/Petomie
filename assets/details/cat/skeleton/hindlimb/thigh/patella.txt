#Patella#

##Location##  
Situated in front of the **stifle joint**, embedded within the tendon of the quadriceps muscle group.  
It acts as a protective shield for the joint.  

###Shape###  
A **small, oval, sesamoid bone**, smooth on its underside to glide over the femoral groove.  

###Parts###  

##Base##  
Attaches to the quadriceps tendon.  
Energetically, represents **strength and guidance**.  

##Apex##  
Connects via the patellar ligament to the tibia.  
Energetically, symbolizes **anchoring and grounding**.  

##Articular Surface##  
Smooth underside articulating with the femur.  
Energetically, reflects **fluidity and protection**.  

###Function###  
Protects the stifle joint and enhances leverage for extension of the hind limb.  
Energetically, balances **strength with flexibility and grace**.
