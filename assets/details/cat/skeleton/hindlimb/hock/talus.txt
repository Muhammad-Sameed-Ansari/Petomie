#Talus#

##Location##  
Situated in the **upper part of the hock**, articulating with the **tibia and fibula above** and the **calcaneus and central tarsal below**.  
It forms a key part of the hock joint.  

###Shape###  
A **rounded, block-like bone** with smooth articular surfaces, allowing hinge-like flexion and extension.  

###Parts###  

##Head##  
Connects with the central tarsal bone for movement.  
Energetically, symbolizes **connection and transition**.  

##Body##  
Supports the tibia and transmits weight downward.  
Energetically, reflects **balance and steadiness**.  

##Trochlea##  
A curved ridge that forms the hinge with the tibia.  
Energetically, represents **flow and guidance of energy**.  

###Function###  
Acts as the pivot point for the hock, transferring weight and allowing smooth limb motion.  
Energetically, maintains **balance, fluidity, and alignment in action**.

**Chakra:** Root Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Bladder Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Trust in movement and stability.  
**Balanced Energy:** Secure, balanced flow of energy.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Joint restriction or instability in the hock.  
Energetically, **loss of trust or imbalance in direction**.
