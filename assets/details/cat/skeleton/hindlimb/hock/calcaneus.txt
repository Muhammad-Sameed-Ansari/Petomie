#Calcaneus#

##Location##  
Located at the **back of the hock (tarsus)**, forming the **heel bone**.  
It connects with the talus and serves as a lever for the muscles of the hind limb.  

###Shape###  
A **long, strong bone** with a prominent projection (the calcaneal tuber) that forms the visible point of the hock.  

###Parts###  

##Calcaneal Tuber##  
The uppermost projection, attachment point for the Achilles tendon.  
Energetically, symbolizes **drive, propulsion, and strength**.  

##Body##  
Forms the main shaft of the bone, transmitting forces through the hock.  
Energetically, reflects **support and direction**.  

##Articular Surfaces##  
Connect with the talus and other tarsal bones for motion stability.  
Energetically, represent **balance and harmony in movement**.  

###Function###  
Acts as a lever for the muscles of extension and propulsion during walking, running, and jumping.  
Energetically, governs **momentum, grounded power, and determination**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Root and Solar Plexus Chakras.  
**Meridian:** Bladder and Kidney Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Endurance and forward momentum.  
**Balanced Energy:** Confident movement and strong support.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Heel pain, tendon strain, or misalignment of the hock.  
Energetically, **fear of progress or imbalance in drive**.  

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###  
Encourage gentle stretching and grounding.  
**Colors:** Red and yellow.  
**Crystals:** Jasper and citrine for vitality and stability.  
**Elements:** Earth and Fire.
