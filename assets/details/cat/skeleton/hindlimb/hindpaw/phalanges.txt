#Phalanges#

##Location##  
Found at the **tips of the limbs**, forming the **digits (toes)** of the cat’s paws.  
Each toe (except the dewclaw) has three phalanges—proximal, middle, and distal.  

###Shape###  
Small, **delicate elongated bones**, designed for precision, grip, and balance.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal Phalanx##  
Connects to the metatarsals, forming the first joint of each toe.  
Energetically, symbolizes **foundation and stability**.  

##Middle Phalanx##  
Acts as a link between the proximal and distal bones.  
Energetically, represents **balance and coordination**.  

##Distal Phalanx##  
Carries the claw, enabling grip, hunting, and climbing.  
Energetically, embodies **expression, agility, and purpose**.  

###Function###  
Allow gripping, balance, and precise motion in walking and climbing.  
Energetically, express **freedom, agility, and focused intent**.

**Chakra:** Root and Throat Chakras.  
**Meridian:** Liver Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Graceful action and control.  
**Balanced Energy:** Purposeful and confident motion.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Claw injury, inflammation, or digit deformity.  
Energetically, **restrained expression or imbalance in focus**.
