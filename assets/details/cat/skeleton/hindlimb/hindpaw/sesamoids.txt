#Sesamoids#

##Location##  
Small bones embedded within tendons around the **joints of the toes and paws**.  
They are found near the metatarsophalangeal and interphalangeal joints.  

###Shape###  
**Tiny, rounded bones** that reduce friction and support smooth tendon motion.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal Sesamoids##  
Situated near the base of the toes, assisting in tendon movement and joint support.  
Energetically, symbolize **stability and adaptability**.  

##Distal Sesamoids##  
Located closer to the claws, aiding in fluid motion and pressure distribution.  
Energetically, reflect **flexibility and precision**.  

###Function###  
Reduce tendon tension, increase leverage, and stabilize limb motion.  
Energetically, promote **fluid transitions and adaptability in movement**.
