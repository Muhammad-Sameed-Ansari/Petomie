
#Lower Left Premolar 4#

##Location##
Behind the third premolar in the lower left jaw, working closely with the upper carnassial for cutting meat.

###Shape###
A strong, multi-pointed tooth for slicing and crushing.

###Function###
Serves as a key chewing surface for processing solid food.  
Energetically, symbolizes strength, cooperation, and balance in exchange.
