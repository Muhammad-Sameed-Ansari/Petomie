#Canine#

##Location##
Located behind the incisors on the right lower jaw, deeply rooted and slightly curved.  
Essential for gripping, tearing, and defense.

###Shape###
A long, pointed tooth with a strong root, built for strength and control.

###Function###
Used to grasp prey and provide protection.  
Energetically represents willpower, courage, and confident expression.

Emotional Meaning: Confidence and assertiveness.  
Balanced Energy: Clarity, calm leadership, and courage.

###Common Imbalances###
Fracture, inflammation, or gum irritation.  
Energetically linked to suppressed anger, indecision, or frustration.
