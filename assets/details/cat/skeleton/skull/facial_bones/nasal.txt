#Nasal Bone#

##Location##
Located on the midline of the face, forming the bridge of the nose.  
Extends from the frontal bone to the nasal cartilages.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Flat, elongated plate meeting at the midline with its opposite pair.  
   Covers the nasal cavity and provides support to the nasal cartilage.

2. **Caudal End** – Articulates with the frontal bone, forming part of the nasal roof.  
   Supports soft tissues of the muzzle and nasal openings.

##Function##
 Forms the upper surface of the nasal cavity.  
 Protects internal nasal structures.  
 Shapes the profile of the nose and supports breathing pathways.
