#Incisive Bone#

##Location##
Located at the front of the upper jaw, forming the anterior portion of the face and part of the hard palate.  
It lies in front of the maxilla and supports the upper incisor teeth.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Houses the sockets for the upper incisor teeth.  
   Contributes to the structure of the upper lip and muzzle.  
   Supports the front of the nasal opening.

2. **Palatine Process** – Extends backward to form part of the hard palate.  
   Joins with the maxilla and palatine bones, separating the nasal and oral cavities.

3. **Nasal Process** – Rises to meet the nasal bone.  
   Helps define the upper border of the nasal aperture.

##Function##
 Supports the upper incisors and contributes to the formation of the upper jaw.  
 Forms the front section of the hard palate and nasal opening.  
 Provides structure for the muzzle and assists in food prehension.
