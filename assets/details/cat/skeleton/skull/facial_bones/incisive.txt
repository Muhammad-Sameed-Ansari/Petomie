#Incisive Bone#

##Location##  
The incisive bone lies at the **front of the upper jaw**, supporting the **incisor teeth** and forming part of the nasal opening.  

###Shape###  
A **small paired bone** that is delicate yet strong, shaped to curve along the upper dental arch.  

###Parts###  

##Alveolar Process##  
Houses the incisor teeth.  
Energetically, represents **expression and initiation**.  

##Palatine Process##  
Forms part of the hard palate.  
Energetically, symbolizes **foundation of communication**.  

##Nasal Process##  
Connects with nasal bones to shape the muzzle.  
Energetically, reflects **balance between breath and voice**.  

###Function###  
Supports the upper incisor teeth, aids in facial structure, and participates in the formation of the nasal and oral cavities.  
Energetically, governs **articulation, communication, and self-expression**.
