#Palatine Bone#

##Location##
Situated at the back of the nasal cavity, forming part of the hard palate and nasal passages.  
Lies between the maxilla and the pterygoid bones.

###Parts###
1. **Horizontal Plate** – Forms the caudal section of the hard palate.  
   Fuses with the opposite palatine bone along the midline.  
   Separates nasal and oral cavities.

2. **Perpendicular Plate** – Extends vertically upward into the nasal cavity.  
   Contributes to the side wall of the nasal passage and orbital floor.

##Function##
 Forms part of the hard palate, separating nasal and oral cavities.  
 Provides structural support for the nasal passage.  
 Supports soft tissues involved in respiration and swallowing.
