#Lacrimal Bone#

##Location##
A small, thin bone located on the medial wall of the orbit near the nasal cavity.  
Forms part of the eye socket and connects to the maxilla and frontal bones.

###Parts###
1. **Orbital Surface** – Forms a portion of the medial wall of the orbit.  
   Smooth and concave, providing space for the eye and surrounding tissues.

2. **Lacrimal Fossa** – Small depression housing the lacrimal sac.  
   Leads into the nasolacrimal canal, which drains tears into the nasal cavity.

##Function##
 Supports the eye and the tear drainage system.  
 Forms part of the orbit and nasal wall.  
 Assists in tear drainage and ocular protection.
