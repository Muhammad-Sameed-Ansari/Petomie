#Lacrimal Bone#

##Location##  
Located at the **inner corner of the eye socket**, forming part of the orbit and supporting the lacrimal sac.  

###Shape###  
A **small, thin bone** forming the front portion of the medial orbital wall.  

###Parts###  

##Lacrimal Fossa##  
Houses the lacrimal sac for tear drainage.  
Energetically, represents **release and cleansing**.  

##Orbital Plate##  
Forms part of the eye socket wall.  
Energetically, symbolizes **clarity and protection**.  

###Function###  
Supports tear flow from the eye to the nasal cavity, maintaining eye health and lubrication.  
Energetically, aids **emotional release and gentle vision**.
