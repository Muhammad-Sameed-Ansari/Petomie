#Vomer Bone#

##Location##  
Forms the **lower portion of the nasal septum**, dividing the nasal cavity into left and right halves.

A **thin, flat bone** forming part of the midline of the skull.

Articulates with the maxilla and palatine bones.  
Energetically, represents **division and clarity**.  

##Posterior Edge##
