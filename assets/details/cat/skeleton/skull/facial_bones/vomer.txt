#Vomer#

##Location##
A thin, midline bone located within the nasal cavity.  
Forms the lower part of the nasal septum, dividing the nasal cavity into two passages.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Long, narrow plate forming the ventral part of the nasal septum.  
   Extends from the sphenoid to the incisive region.  
   Lies between the two halves of the nasal cavity.

2. **Wings (Alae)** – Small lateral extensions that articulate with the sphenoid and palatine bones.  
   Provide structural reinforcement within the nasal cavity.

##Function##
 Divides the nasal cavity into left and right chambers.  
 Supports the septal cartilage.  
 Helps guide airflow and stabilize nasal passages.
