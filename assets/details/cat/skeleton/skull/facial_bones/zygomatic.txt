#Zygomatic Bone#

##Location##
Also known as the cheekbone, found on the lateral side of the face.  
Forms the prominence of the cheek and part of the orbit.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Central portion forming part of the orbit.  
   Provides surface for facial muscle attachment.

2. **Temporal Process** – Extends backward to meet the zygomatic process of the temporal bone.  
   Together they form the zygomatic arch.

3. **Orbital Margin** – Contributes to the outer rim of the orbit.  
   Provides structural support and protection for the eye.

##Function##
 Forms part of the orbit and zygomatic arch.  
 Provides muscle attachment for mastication.  
 Contributes to facial contour and protects the eye region.
