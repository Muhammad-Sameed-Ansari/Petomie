#Parietal Bone#

##Location##
Situated on the dorsolateral aspects of the skull, forming much of the cranial vault roof.  
Meets its fellow at the sagittal suture and articulates with frontal and occipital bones.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Plate-like bone forming the dorsum of the cranium.  
   Protects the superior surfaces of the brain.

2. **Sagittal Crest** – Midline ridge (variable prominence).  
   Provides attachment for temporal muscles.

##Function##
 Protects the brain dorsally and laterally.  
 Provides muscular attachment for mastication and neck stabilization.  
 Contributes to skull shape and strength.
