#Sphenoid Bone#

##Location##  
Located at the **base of the skull**, in front of the occipital bone and beneath the brain.  

###Shape###  
A **butterfly-shaped bone** with wings extending outward to connect multiple skull bones.  

###Parts###  

##Body##  
Houses the sphenoid sinus.  
Energetically, symbolizes **core strength and harmony**.  

##Greater and Lesser Wings##  
Extend laterally to connect cranial bones.  
Energetically, represent **balance between expansion and focus**.  

##Pterygoid Processes##  
Provide attachment for chewing muscles.  
Energetically, reflect **action and purpose**.  

###Function###  
Forms part of the cranial floor and orbits, supports the brain, and serves as muscle attachment for mastication.  
Energetically, aligns **strength, balance, and inner stability**.
