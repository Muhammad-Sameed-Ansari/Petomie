#Occipital Bone#

##Location##  
Located at the **back and base of the skull**, surrounding the **foramen magnum**, through which the spinal cord passes.  

###Shape###  
A **large, curved bone** that articulates with the first cervical vertebra (atlas).  

###Parts###

Allows connection between brain and spinal cord.  
Energetically, symbolizes **communication between mind and body**.  

##Occipital Condyles##  
Form joints with the atlas for head movement.  
Energetically, reflect **balance and adaptability**.  

##External Occipital Protuberance##  
Serves as muscle attachment point.  
Energetically, represents **strength and grounding**.
