#T6#

##Location##  
Located in the upper and mid-back region of the cat, the thoracic vertebrae form part of the spine connected to the ribs.  
T6 supports the rib cage, protects internal organs, and contributes to posture and breathing.  

###Shape###  
A **medium-sized vertebra** with a tall spinous process and facets for rib articulation.  
Each thoracic vertebra connects to a rib pair, forming the thoracic cage.
