#Cervical Intervertebral Discs#

##Location##
Between each cervical vertebra from C2–C7, supporting neck flexibility, grooming movements, and predatory motion.

###Shape###
Small, slightly curved fibrocartilage pads designed for high mobility and precision in neck rotation.

###Parts###

##Annulus Fibrosus##
Thin fibrocartilage ring offering stability while maintaining flexibility.
Energetically represents boundaries, containment, and emotional steadiness.

##Nucleus Pulposus##
Soft cushioning core absorbing shock and supporting fluid movement.
Energetically reflects adaptability and emotional flow.

##Individual Discs##

###C4–C5 Disc###

###C5–C6 Disc###
Stabilizes lower neck motion. Energetically linked to releasing tension from vigilance.

###C6–C7 Disc###
Connects neck to shoulder girdle for graceful movement. Energetically supports grounded responsiveness.


###Function###
Absorb shock, allow flexible neck motion, support grooming, hunting posture, and sensory responsiveness.
