#Premolar 3#

##Location##
Located in the middle of the right lower jaw, between the canine and the larger premolar.  
Important for cutting and shearing food.

###Shape###
A sharp, multi-pointed tooth designed for slicing.

###Function###
Cuts and divides meat into smaller pieces.
