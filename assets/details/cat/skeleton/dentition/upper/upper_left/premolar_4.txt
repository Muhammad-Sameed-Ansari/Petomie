#Premolar 4#

##Location##
Located behind the third premolar on the upper left jaw, forming the carnassial tooth with the lower molar.

###Shape###
A large, powerful tooth with broad cutting edges and deep roots.

###Function###
Slices meat and tough food efficiently.  
Energetically represents strength, transformation, and the release of old patterns.
