#Canine#

##Location##
Located behind the incisors on the upper left jaw, deeply anchored and slightly curved.  
Used for gripping, tearing, and defense.

###Shape###
A long, pointed tooth with a deep root for power and precision.

###Function###
Essential for catching prey, tearing food, and maintaining jaw alignment.  
Energetically represents confidence, willpower, and direction.

Emotional Meaning: Courage and decision-making.
