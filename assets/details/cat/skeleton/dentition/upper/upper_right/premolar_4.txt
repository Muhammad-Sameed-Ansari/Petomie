#Premolar 4#

##Location##
Located behind the third premolar on the upper right jaw, forming the upper carnassial tooth for slicing meat.

###Shape###
Large, multi-pointed tooth with broad cutting edges.

###Function###
Slices and crushes dense food.  
Energetically represents power, transformation, and cooperation.

###Energetic Connection###

Chakra: Solar Plexus Chakra.  
Meridian: Spleen Meridian.  
Emotional Meaning: Balance in giving and receiving.  
Balanced Energy: Strength and harmony.

###Common Imbalances###
Wear, cracks, or sensitivity.  
Energetically: Fatigue or imbalance from overexertion.
