#Rib 13#

##Location##
Rib 13 is one of the floating ribs, typically ribs 13 in the cat.
This rib does not attach to the sternum or to the other ribs. It connects only to the thoracic vertebra, allowing flexibility and freedom of movement at the rear of the ribcage.

###Shape###
Short, slender, and slightly curved toward the abdomen, ending freely without a ventral connection.

###Function###
Protects the posterior organs such as the kidneys while maintaining flexibility in the flank area.
Energetically, represents freedom, emotional release, and fluid movement.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Solar Plexus and Sacral Chakras.
Meridian: Liver and Gallbladder Meridians.
Emotional Meaning: Flow, openness, and letting go.
Balanced Energy: Free, light, and emotionally at ease.

###Common Imbalances###
Tightness or sensitivity in the flank region.
Energetically, resistance to change or suppressed emotions.
