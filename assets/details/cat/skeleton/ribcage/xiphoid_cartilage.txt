#Xiphoid Cartilage#

##Location##
The xiphoid cartilage, or xiphoid process, is the small cartilaginous extension at the lower end of the cat’s sternum.
It marks the transition between the thoracic and abdominal cavities and provides attachment for abdominal muscles and the diaphragm.

###Shape###
A soft, flexible cartilage that gradually tapers toward the abdomen and may ossify slightly with age.

###Function###
Supports the lower end of the sternum, connects to the abdominal wall, and aids in breathing and movement.
Energetically, represents grounding, calmness, and emotional integration.
