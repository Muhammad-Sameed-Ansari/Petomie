#Rib 4#

##Location##
Rib 4 belongs to the group known as true ribs, which include ribs 1 to 9 in the cat.
These ribs attach directly to the sternum through costal cartilage, forming the strong and stable front section of the ribcage.
They protect vital organs and provide the main structure for the thoracic cavity.

###Shape###
Curved, elongated bones that arch from the thoracic vertebrae toward the sternum with flexibility for chest expansion.

###Function###
Protect the heart and lungs, support respiration, and stabilize the chest wall during movement.
Energetically, they symbolize security, openness, and strength in balance.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Heart and Solar Plexus Chakras.
Meridian: Lung and Stomach Meridians.
Emotional Meaning: Calmness, courage, and balanced openness.
Balanced Energy: Deep breathing and steady emotional rhythm.

###Common Imbalances###
Rib tightness or strain from tension or restricted movement.
Energetically, guarded emotions or overprotection.
