#Radius#

##Location##  
Located in the **forearm**, extending from the **elbow to the wrist (carpus)**.  
It lies closely beside the ulna, forming the main weight-bearing bone of the feline forelimb.  

###Shape###  
A **slender, cylindrical bone** that slightly flattens toward the distal end for joint articulation.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal End##  
Forms the elbow joint with the humerus.  
Energetically, represents **foundation and initiation of movement**.  

##Shaft##  
Supports stability and coordination of the forearm.  
Energetically, symbolizes **direction, purpose, and focus**.  

##Distal End##  
Articulates with the carpus, allowing smooth limb flexion.  
Energetically, reflects **transition and flexibility**.  

###Function###  
Supports locomotion and assists in weight transfer from upper limb to paw.  
Energetically, governs **stability, precision, and flow in action**.
