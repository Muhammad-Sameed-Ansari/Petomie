#Phalanges#

##Location##  
Located at the **tips of the limbs**, forming the **digits (toes)** of the paw.  
Each toe (except the dewclaw) consists of three phalanges—proximal, middle, and distal.  

###Shape###  
Small **elongated bones** designed for flexibility, grip, and precise motion.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal Phalanx##  
Connects to the metacarpal bones for joint flexibility.  
Energetically, represents **foundation and beginning of expression**.  

##Middle Phalanx##  
Provides support and transmits force to the distal bone.  
Energetically, reflects **balance and adaptation**.  

##Distal Phalanx##  
Forms the claw-bearing bone at the tip.  
Energetically, embodies **expression, precision, and release**.  

###Function###  
Enable grasping, claw extension, and balance during movement.  
Energetically, govern **creativity, action, and completion**.

**Meridian:** Liver Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Precision and self-expression through motion.  
**Balanced Energy:** Grace and controlled strength.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Claw fractures, inflammation, or digit deformity.  
Energetically, **blocked expression or emotional restraint**.
