#Metacarpals#

##Location##  
Located in the **forepaw**, between the **carpal bones** and the **phalanges**.  
These long bones form the main body of the paw and provide structure and support for movement.  

###Shape###  
A series of **slender, elongated bones** numbered I–V, gradually decreasing in size medially.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal Base##  
Articulates with the carpal bones for stability.  
Energetically, symbolizes **foundation and connection to movement**.  

##Shaft##  
Long and supportive, transmitting weight and force.  
Energetically, represents **balance and purpose**.  

##Distal Head##  
Connects to the proximal phalanges, forming the knuckles.  
Energetically, reflects **transition and coordination**.  

###Function###  
Support weight, provide leverage, and allow flexibility during walking or jumping.  
Energetically, govern **balance, coordination, and connection to forward motion**.
