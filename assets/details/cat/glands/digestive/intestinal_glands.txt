#Intestinal Glands#

The cat’s intestinal glands, or crypts of Lieberkühn, line the small intestine.  
They secrete enzymes and mucus for digestion and absorption.  
Energetically, they represent **assimilation, renewal, and flow**.

###Parts###

##Crypts##  
Tubular structures producing secretions.  
Energetically, they symbolize transformation and integration.

##Enterocytes##  
Absorb nutrients and water.  
Energetically, they reflect receptivity and flow.

##Stem Cells##  
Regenerate intestinal lining.  
Energetically, they represent renewal and vitality.

###Function###  
Intestinal glands support digestion, absorption, and tissue renewal.  
Energetically, they assist in integrating nourishment and experiences.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: stress or irritation.  
Malabsorption: depletion or imbalance.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **difficulty receiving or processing nourishment**.
