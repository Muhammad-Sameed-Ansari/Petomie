#Brunner’s Glands#

The cat’s Brunner’s glands are located in the duodenum.  
They secrete mucus and alkaline fluid to neutralize stomach acid.  
Energetically, they represent **protection, balance, and calm digestion**.

###Parts###

##Mucous Cells##  
Produce alkaline secretions.  
Energetically, they symbolize harmony and peace.

##Ducts##  
Deliver secretions into the intestine.  
Energetically, they represent pathways of flow and release.

##Gland Clusters##  
Embedded in the intestinal wall.  
Energetically, they reflect grounding and balance.

###Function###  
Brunner’s glands protect the intestinal lining and maintain pH balance.  
Energetically, they create calmness and flow in digestion and emotion.

###Common Imbalances###  
Irritation: stress or acidity.  
Blockage: tension or rigidity.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **inability to soothe or process emotions**.
