#Von Ebner’s Glands#

The cat’s Von Ebner’s glands are small serous glands located near the tongue’s taste buds.  
They secrete fluid that helps dissolve food particles and cleanse taste pores.  
Energetically, they represent **perception, clarity, and appreciation of life**.

##Serous Units##  
Produce watery secretions.  
Energetically, they symbolize freshness and flow.

##Ducts##  
Deliver fluids to taste buds.  
Energetically, they represent communication and clarity.

##Lingual Tissue##  
Supports taste function.  
Energetically, it reflects sensitivity and enjoyment.

###Function###  
Von Ebner’s glands cleanse taste buds and assist in digestion.  
Energetically, they enhance sensory awareness and appreciation.
