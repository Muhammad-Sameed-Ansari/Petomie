#Molar Glands#

The cat’s molar glands are small salivary glands located on the inside of the cheeks near the back teeth.  
They provide mucous-rich saliva for lubrication and oral comfort.  
Energetically, they represent comfort, adaptability, and gentle strength.

###Parts###

##Gland Clusters##  
Small lobules inside the cheek tissue.  
Energetically, they symbolize subtle awareness and inner support.

##Tiny Ducts##  
Open directly into the mouth near the molars.  
Energetically, they represent precision and balance.

##Surrounding Tissue##  
Supports glands during chewing.  
Energetically, it reflects harmony between strength and sensitivity.

###Function###  
Molar glands help lubricate food and protect the mouth’s lining.  
Energetically, they sustain comfort and ease in expression and nourishment.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling: stress or irritation.  
Dryness: dehydration or lack of care.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect emotional fatigue or withdrawal.
