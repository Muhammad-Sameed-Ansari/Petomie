#Sublingual Glands#

The cat’s sublingual glands are elongated glands beneath the tongue.  
They secrete mucous saliva that keeps the mouth moist and aids grooming.  
Energetically, they represent fluid communication, adaptability, and emotional softness.

###Parts###

##Glandular Body##  
Thin gland extending under the tongue.  
Energetically, it symbolizes flexibility and gentle awareness.

##Ducts##  
Open near the tongue’s underside.  
Energetically, they reflect expression and emotional clarity.

##Support Tissue##  
Holds the gland in position and protects it.  
Energetically, it represents calm stability.

###Function###  
Sublingual glands maintain oral moisture and smooth tongue movement.  
Energetically, they encourage ease in emotional expression and communication.

###Common Imbalances###  
Blockage: tension or communication issues.  
Inflammation: stress or overheating.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect suppressed softness or emotional overload.
