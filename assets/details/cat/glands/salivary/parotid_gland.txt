#Parotid Glands#

##Location##
Located just below and in front of each ear, between the upper jaw and the neck. Their ducts open near the upper cheek teeth.

###Shape###
Two flat, lobed glands with a light yellow tone and firm consistency.

##Function##
Produce watery saliva to moisten food, start digestion, and clean the mouth.
