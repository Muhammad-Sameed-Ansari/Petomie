#Zygomatic Glands#

##Location##
Located beneath the eyes inside the cheek region near the last upper molars, draining into the mouth near the upper teeth.

###Shape###
Two small, oval glands embedded deep within the cheek muscles.

##Function##
Produce mucous saliva that lubricates the mouth and protects tissues during eating or grooming.
