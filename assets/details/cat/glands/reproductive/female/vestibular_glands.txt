#Vestibular Glands (Bartholin’s Glands)#

The dog’s vestibular glands lie in the vaginal vestibule near the vulva.  
They secrete mucus during mating, providing lubrication and comfort.  
Energetically, they represent readiness, flowing femininity, and supportive intimacy.

Produce slippery mucus for lubrication.  
Energetically, they symbolize fluidity and receptive energy.

##Gland Ducts##

Energetically, they reflect expression and openness.

##Supportive Tissue##  
Anchors and protects the glands.  
Energetically, it represents grounding and steady support.

###Function###  
Vestibular glands ease mating, reduce friction, and support reproductive comfort.  
Energetically, they enhance feminine flow, receptivity, and ease in connection.

###Common Imbalances###

Low secretion: stress or hormonal change.
