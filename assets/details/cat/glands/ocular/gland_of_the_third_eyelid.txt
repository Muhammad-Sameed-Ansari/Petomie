#Gland of the Third Eyelid#

##Location##
Located at the base of the third eyelid, near the inner corner of each eye by the nose, beneath the nictitating membrane.

###Shape###
A small, rounded gland beneath the inner eyelid fold.

##Function##
Produces tears and lubricating fluids to protect the eye from dust, wind, and injury, working with the lacrimal gland to maintain moisture.
