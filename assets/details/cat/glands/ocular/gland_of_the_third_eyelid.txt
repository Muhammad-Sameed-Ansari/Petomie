#Third Eyelid Gland#

The cat’s third eyelid gland, also called the nictitating membrane gland, lies beneath the inner corner of the eye.  
It produces a significant portion of tear fluid and provides immune protection.  
Energetically, it represents intuition, protection, and inner vision.

###Parts###

##Glandular Body##  
Produces watery and mucous tear components.  
Energetically, it symbolizes nourishment and clarity.

##Cartilage Support##  
Gives the third eyelid structure and motion.  
Energetically, it reflects balance and strength.

##Lymphoid Tissue##  
Provides immune defense.  
Energetically, it represents spiritual and emotional protection.

###Function###  
The third eyelid gland lubricates the eye and contributes to tear stability.  
Energetically, it safeguards perception and nurtures intuitive awareness.

###Common Imbalances###  
Prolapse (“cherry eye”): energy imbalance or stress.  
Swelling: fatigue or irritation.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect overexposure or loss of intuitive protection.
