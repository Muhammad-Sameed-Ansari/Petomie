#Conjunctival Glands#

##Location##
Located in the inner lining of the eyelids and over the white part of the eye, within the thin pink conjunctiva membrane.

###Shape###
Many microscopic mucous glands spread evenly under the eyelid lining.

##Function##
Produce mucus that mixes with tears to maintain smoothness, trap irritants, and support eye comfort.
