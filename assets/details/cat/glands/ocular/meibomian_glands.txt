#Tarsal (Meibomian) Glands#

##Location##
Found along the edges of the upper and lower eyelids, where the eyelashes meet the eye surface.

###Shape###
Tiny tube-like glands arranged in rows under the eyelid margins.

##Function##
Secrete oils that prevent tears from evaporating, keeping the eye surface moist and comfortable.
