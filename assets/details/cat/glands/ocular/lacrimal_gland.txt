#Lacrimal Glands#

##Location##
Located above and to the outer side of each eye, beneath the upper eyelid near the outer rim of the eye socket.

###Shape###
Two small, oval glands with soft lobes and a light pink color.

##Function##
Produce tears to clean, moisten, and protect the eyes from dust and infection, while nourishing the cornea for clear vision.
