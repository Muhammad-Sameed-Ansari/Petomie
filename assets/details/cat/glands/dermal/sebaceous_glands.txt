#Sebaceous Glands#

##Location##
Located throughout the skin, especially around the face, back, tail base, and paws. They open into hair follicles.

###Shape###
Tiny, sac-like glands that produce oily secretions (sebum).

##Function##
Produce sebum that keeps the skin soft, the coat glossy, and acts as a natural barrier against bacteria and dryness.
