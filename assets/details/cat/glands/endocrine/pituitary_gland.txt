#Pituitary Gland#

The cat’s pituitary gland, located at the brain’s base, is the master endocrine organ.  
It controls hormone secretion across multiple systems.  
Energetically, it represents **leadership, regulation, and harmony**.

###Parts###

##Anterior Lobe (Adenohypophysis)##  
Releases growth, thyroid, and reproductive hormones.  
Energetically, it symbolizes action and responsibility.

##Posterior Lobe (Neurohypophysis)##  
Stores and releases oxytocin and vasopressin.  
Energetically, it reflects connection and emotional flow.

##Infundibulum##  
Links the gland to the hypothalamus.  
Energetically, it represents communication between mind and body.

###Function###  
The pituitary gland regulates metabolism, growth, and reproduction.  
Energetically, it harmonizes physical and emotional cycles of creation and rest.

###Common Imbalances###  
Hormonal excess: overstimulation, dominance.  
Deficiency: fatigue, imbalance.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **misaligned control or lack of direction**.
