#Adrenal Glands#

The catâ€™s adrenal glands are small, triangular organs located above each kidney.  
They produce hormones that regulate stress, metabolism, and electrolyte balance.  
Energetically, they represent **resilience, survival, and adaptability**.

###Parts###

##Cortex##  
Produces cortisol and aldosterone for stress response and salt balance.  
Energetically, it symbolizes grounding and endurance.

##Medulla##  
Releases adrenaline and noradrenaline for fight-or-flight reactions.  
Energetically, it represents activation, courage, and vitality.

##Capsule##  
Protective layer around the gland.  
Energetically, it reflects stability and containment.

###Function###  
The adrenal glands regulate stress, metabolism, and immune responses.  
Energetically, they sustain life-force under pressure and restore equilibrium.

###Common Imbalances###  
Overactivity: anxiety, restlessness, or overdrive.  
Underactivity: fatigue, weakness, or emotional depletion.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **burnout or chronic stress**.
