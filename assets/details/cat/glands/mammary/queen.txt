#Mammary Glands (Queen)#

The queen’s mammary glands are paired glands extending along the abdomen, usually forming four pairs.  
They produce milk to nourish kittens after birth and reflect the essence of nurturing and maternal connection.  
Energetically, they represent love, nourishment, and creative giving.

###Parts###

##Gland Lobes##  
Clusters of secretory tissue that produce milk.  
Energetically, they symbolize abundance, devotion, and life-giving energy.

##Milk Ducts##  
Transport milk to the nipple for release.  
Energetically, they represent generosity and emotional flow.

##Nipples##  
External openings for milk delivery.  
Energetically, they embody giving, tenderness, and connection.

###Function###  
Mammary glands secrete milk rich in nutrients and antibodies for kittens’ health.  
Energetically, they channel the queen’s heart-centered energy into nourishment and care.

###Common Imbalances###  
Mastitis: blocked giving or emotional overload.  
Swelling: hormonal imbalance or tension.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect over-nurturing, self-neglect, or emotional depletion.
