#Spleen#

The cat’s spleen lies along the stomach’s curve in the abdominal cavity.  
It filters blood, removes old red cells, and stores immune cells.  
Energetically, it represents **stability, nourishment, and emotional processing**.

###Parts###

##Red Pulp##  
Destroys worn-out red blood cells.  
Energetically, it symbolizes transformation and renewal.

##White Pulp##  
Contains immune cells to fight infection.  
Energetically, it reflects awareness and defense.

##Capsule and Vessels##  
Protect and supply the spleen.  
Energetically, they represent containment and support.

###Function###  
The spleen maintains blood quality and immune readiness.  
Energetically, it processes emotional energy and grounds vitality.

###Common Imbalances###  
Enlargement: infection or energetic burden.  
Weakness: anemia or depletion.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect **overthinking, worry, or fatigue**.
