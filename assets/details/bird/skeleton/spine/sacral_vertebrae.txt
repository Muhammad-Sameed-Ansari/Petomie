#Sacral Vertebrae#

##Location##
Located within the **synsacrum**, fused with **lumbar, thoracic, and caudal** elements.  
Form the connection between the **spine** and the **pelvic girdle**.

###Shape###
Completely **fused** and indistinguishable from the lumbar and some thoracic vertebrae.  
Broad, compact structure linking to the ilium.

##Parts##
- **Fused Sacral Segments:** Integrated with synsacrum.  
- **Lateral Plates:** Attach to the ilium.  
- **Central Canal:** Houses the spinal cord.

##Function##
- **Stability:** Rigidly connects the pelvis and spine.  
- **Force Transmission:** Distributes weight from hindlimbs to the trunk.  
- **Protection:** Provides a sturdy base for flight and perching.
