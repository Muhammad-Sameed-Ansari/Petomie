#T1#

##Location##  
T1 is one of the thoracic vertebrae of the bird’s chest region.  
It connects to the ribs and supports the wings, lungs, and heart.  

###Shape###  
Strong, compact vertebra designed to anchor ribs and support flight muscles.  

###Parts###  

##Body##  
Provides structural support for the chest cavity.  
Energetically, represents **strength and stability**.  

##Processes##  
Anchor ribs and powerful wing and breathing muscles.  
Energetically, symbolize **support and expansion**.  

###Function###  
Supports breathing, wing movement, and protection of vital organs.  
Energetically, governs **power, breath, and emotional expression**.
