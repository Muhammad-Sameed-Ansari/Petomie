#Syn1#

##Location##  
Syn1 is one of the fused vertebrae forming the bird’s synsacrum.  
It connects the spine to the pelvis and supports weight, balance, and landing forces.  

###Shape###  
Broad, flattened vertebra fused with neighboring bones for strength and stability.  

###Parts###  

##Body##  
Provides structural support for the pelvis and hind limbs.  
Energetically, represents **grounding and security**.  

##Processes##  
Anchor powerful leg and pelvic muscles.  
Energetically, symbolize **support and endurance**.  

###Function###  
Transfers forces between wings, legs, and spine during movement and landing.  
Energetically, governs **stability, survival, and rootedness**.
