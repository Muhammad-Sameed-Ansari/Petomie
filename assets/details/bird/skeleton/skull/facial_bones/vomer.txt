#Vomer#

##Location##
The vomer lies along the midline of the bird’s skull, forming part of the nasal septum.  
It helps divide the nasal passages.

###Shape###
A thin, elongated bone running lengthwise through the nasal cavity.

###Parts###

##Vomerine Plate##

##Anterior Extension##
The front portion guiding airflow.  
Energetically, represents flow, guidance, and ease.

###Function###
Supports the nasal septum, directs airflow, and assists the olfactory system.
