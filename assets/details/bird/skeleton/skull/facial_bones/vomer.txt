#Vomer Bone#

##Location##
Located along the **midline of the skull base**, forming part of the **nasal septum** and the **floor of the nasal cavity**.  
Extends between the **palatine** and **pterygoid** bones.

###Shape###
Thin, elongated, and flat bone that varies in size and shape among species.  
May be reduced or fused with surrounding bones in some birds.

##Parts##

###1. Anterior Portion###
- Connects to the premaxilla and nasal septum.  
- **Function:** Supports the upper beak internally.  

###2. Posterior Portion###
- Joins with the palatine and pterygoid bones.  
- **Function:** Helps stabilize the skull base.  

##Function##
- **Support:** Forms the central axis of the palate.  
- **Respiration:** Assists in nasal cavity separation.  
- **Structure:** Contributes to beak rigidity and stability.
