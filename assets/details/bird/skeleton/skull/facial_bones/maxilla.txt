#Maxilla#

##Location##
Forms the **upper jaw region** of the beak, extending from the **nasal openings** to the **premaxilla** and **palatine bones**.  
Articulates with the **premaxilla**, **nasal**, **lacrimal**, and **zygomatic bones**.

###Shape###
Elongated and lightweight **paired bones**, often fused anteriorly.  
Part of the **upper beak** covered by keratin (rhamphotheca).

##Parts##

###1. Maxillary Process###
- Projects toward the premaxilla.

###2. Infraorbital Process###
- Extends beneath the orbit.  
- **Function:** Supports the cheek region and muscle attachment.  

###3. Palatine Articulation###
- Posterior connection to palatine bones.  
- **Function:** Stabilizes the upper jaw and palate.  

##Function##
- **Feeding:** Provides structure and support for the beak and mouth cavity.  
- **Protection:** Forms part of the nasal and oral walls.  
- **Attachment:** Serves as a base for facial and palatal muscles.
