#Nasal#

##Location##
The nasal bones lie at the top of the beak, forming the upper part of the bird’s snout.  
They connect with the frontal and maxillary bones.

###Shape###
Small, elongated bones shaping the beak’s upper ridge.

###Parts###

##Dorsal Ridge##
The upper surface forming the beak’s top contour.  
Energetically, symbolizes perception and forward awareness.

##Lateral Borders##
Edges connecting to surrounding facial bones.  
Energetically, represent integration and balance.

###Function###
Shapes the upper beak, supports the nasal cavity, and assists with airflow.  
Energetically, governs perception, sensing, and movement direction.
