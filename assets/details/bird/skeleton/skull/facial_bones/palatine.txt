#Palatine Bone#

##Location##
Situated on the **ventral side of the skull**, forming the **posterior part of the hard palate**.  
Connects the **maxilla**, **pterygoid**, and **vomer** bones.

###Shape###
Paired, **elongated, thin plates** that may fuse or curve toward the midline.  
Highly adapted to different feeding styles in birds.

##Parts##

###1. Maxillary Process###
- Anterior connection with the maxilla.  
- **Function:** Supports the palate and beak base.  

###2. Pterygoid Process###
- Posterior connection to the pterygoid bone.  
- **Function:** Transmits motion during cranial kinesis.  

###3. Medial Plate###
- Forms part of the midline palate.  
- **Function:** Separates nasal and oral cavities.  

##Function##
- **Feeding Mechanics:** Aids beak movement and tongue coordination.  
- **Separation:** Divides nasal from oral passageways.  
- **Support:** Strengthens the roof of the mouth.
