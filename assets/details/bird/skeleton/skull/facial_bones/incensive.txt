#Incisive#

##Location##
The incisive bone forms the front portion of the upper beak, located just behind the tip of the maxilla.  
It helps define the beak’s shape and supports the upper palate.

###Shape###
A lightweight, elongated bone forming part of the upper jaw structure.

###Parts###

##Incisive Plate##
The central portion supporting the upper palate.
