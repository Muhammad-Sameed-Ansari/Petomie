#Sphenoid#

##Location##
The sphenoid bone lies at the base of the bird’s skull, forming part of the floor and supporting key cranial structures.

###Shape###
A complex, lightweight bone providing central cranial support.

###Parts###

##Body##
The central portion forming the skull’s internal base.  
Energetically, symbolizes grounding and inner stability.

##Wings##
Lateral extensions that support other cranial bones.  
Energetically, represent expansion, balance, and flow.

###Function###
Supports cranial structure, protects internal tissues, and anchors muscles.  
Energetically, governs inner stability, integration, and strength.
