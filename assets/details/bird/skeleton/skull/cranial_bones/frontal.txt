#Frontal#

##Location##
The frontal bone forms the top and front portion of the bird’s skull, sitting above the eyes.  
It shapes the forehead and contributes to the upper beak region.

###Shape###
A broad, curved plate that provides protection and anchoring areas for facial structures.
