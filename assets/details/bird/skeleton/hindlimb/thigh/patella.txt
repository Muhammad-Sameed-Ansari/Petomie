#Patella#

##Location##
The patella, or kneecap, is a small bone within the tendon of the knee joint.  
In birds, it varies by species—present in some and absent or reduced in others.  
When present, it lies between the femur and the tibiotarsus.

###Shape###
A small, rounded or slightly elongated sesamoid bone.  
Its form helps direct tendon movement and enhance joint efficiency.

###Parts###

##Articular Surface##
The smooth area that glides with the femur.  
Energetically, symbolizes ease, fluidity, and harmony.

##Tendon Attachment##
Where strong tendons connect and transmit force.  
Energetically, represents connection, strength, and guided action.

###Function###
Improves knee extension efficiency and protects tendons during movement.  
Enhances leverage for powerful takeoff and landing.  
Energetically, supports fluid motion, coordination, and aligned effort.
