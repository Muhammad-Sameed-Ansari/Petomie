#Sesamoids#

##Location##
Bird sesamoid bones lie near tendons around the joints of the toes and lower limbs.  
They are embedded within tendons to reduce friction and enhance movement efficiency.

###Shape###
Small, rounded or oval bones that vary in number depending on the species.  
Their lightweight form provides support without adding unnecessary mass.

###Parts###

##Digital Sesamoids##
Located near the toe joints, assisting with tendon glide.

##Metatarsal Sesamoids##
Found near the tarsometatarsal joint.  
Energetically, represent cushioning and balanced movement.

###Function###
Reduce friction at joints, support tendon motion, and increase limb efficiency.  
Help absorb shock during landing or jumping.  
Energetically, encourage smoothness, resilience, and ease of movement.
