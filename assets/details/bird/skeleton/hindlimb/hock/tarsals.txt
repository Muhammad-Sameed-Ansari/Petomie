#Tarsals#

##Location##
The bird’s tarsal bones lie in the ankle region, forming the connection between the tibiotarsus and the fused tarsometatarsus.  
This cluster of bones forms a lightweight but strong support system for movement.

###Shape###
Small, irregularly shaped bones adapted for strength, articulation, and reduced weight.

###Parts###

##Upper Tarsals##
Bones that articulate with the lower leg.  
Energetically, symbolize support and upward flow.

##Lower Tarsals##
Bones connecting toward the metatarsal region.  
Energetically, represent grounding, balance, and direction.

###Function###
Create the flexible but sturdy ankle joint, guide tendon movement, and distribute forces during walking, flying takeoff, or landing.  
Energetically, support alignment, adaptability, and fluid motion.
