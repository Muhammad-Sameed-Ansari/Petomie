#Uncinate Processes#

##Location##
The uncinate processes are small, hook-shaped projections extending backward from the bird’s ribs.  
They strengthen the ribcage and aid in respiration.

###Shape###
Slender, curved bony projections varying in size between species.

###Parts###

##Costal Hooks##
The main projections extending from each rib.  
Energetically, symbolize protection, integration, and supportive flow.

###Function###
Strengthen the ribcage, improve breathing mechanics, and support flight muscles.  
Energetically, govern protection, rhythmic breath, and structural balance.
