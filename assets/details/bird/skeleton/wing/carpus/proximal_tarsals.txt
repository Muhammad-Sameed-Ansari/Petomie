#Proximal Tarsals#

##Location##
The proximal tarsal bones lie between the lower end of the tibiotarsus and the distal tarsals in the bird’s ankle region.  
They participate in forming the upper portion of the ankle joint.

###Shape###
Compact, strong bones shaped for articulation and force absorption.

###Parts###

##Articular Surfaces##
Upper surfaces where the tibiotarsus attaches.  
Energetically, symbolize support, strength, and upward stability.

##Connecting Facets##
Joint regions linking to distal tarsals.  
Energetically, represent balance, cooperation, and structural integration.

###Function###
Absorb impact, support body weight, and guide motion between leg and foot.  
Energetically, govern alignment, strength, and supported transition.
