#Phalanges#

##Location##
The phalanges are located at the distal portion of the bird’s wing, forming the skeletal structure of the wingtip.  
They support feathers and help guide flight.

###Shape###
Small, elongated bones adapted for lightweight stability.

###Parts###

##Proximal Phalanges##
The first bones nearest the metacarpals.  
Energetically, symbolize connection, intention, and beginning motion.

##Distal Phalanges##
Bones closest to the wingtip that anchor primary feathers.  
Energetically, represent precision, direction, and refined expression.

###Function###
Hold flight feathers in place, assist with maneuvering, and influence wing shape.  
Energetically, govern precision, control, and graceful expression.
