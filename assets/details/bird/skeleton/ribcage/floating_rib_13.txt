#Floating Ribs#

##Location##
Found at the **posterior end of the ribcage**, attached only dorsally to the **last thoracic vertebrae**.  
They do **not connect** to the sternum or adjacent ribs.

###Shape###
Small, delicate, and often rudimentary bones.  
Present in some species as one or two pairs that end freely within the body wall.

##Parts##

###1. Rib Head###
- Articulates with the last thoracic vertebra.  
- **Function:** Provides limited attachment for muscles.  

###2. Free End###
- Ventral tip without sternal connection.  
- **Function:** Allows slight flexibility and expansion of posterior thorax.  

##Function##
- **Flexibility:** Permits movement in the lower thoracic region.  
- **Protection:** Shields posterior lungs and air sacs.  
- **Adaptation:** Contributes to lightweight skeletal structure.
