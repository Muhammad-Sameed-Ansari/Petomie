##Cutaneous Colli##

##Cutaneous Trunci##  
A widespread sheet over the trunk that helps lift and twitch the skin to reposition feathers.  
Energetically, it reflects protection, alertness, and boundary awareness.

##Feather Control Fibers##

###Function###  
Cutaneous muscles adjust feather orientation for insulation, display, and flight stability.

###Common Imbalances###
