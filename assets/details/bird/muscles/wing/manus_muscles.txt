#Manus Muscles#

##Location##
Distal part of the wing beyond the wrist joint, forming the **hand region** that supports the primary flight feathers.  
Includes the **carpometacarpus and digits**.

##Shape##
Small, delicate, and highly tendinous muscles responsible for **fine feather and wingtip control**.

##Parts##

###1. Extensor Digitorum Communis###
- Dorsal surface of the manus.  
- **Function:** Extends the digits and spreads the primary feathers during flight.

###2. Flexor Digitorum Profundus###
- Ventral surface of the manus.  
- **Function:** Flexes the digits and aids in feather folding.

###3. Abductor Pollicis Longus###
- Arises from the radius and ulna.  
- **Function:** Abducts the first digit and adjusts feather position during turns.

###4. Intrinsic Feather Muscles###
- Small muscles along feather shafts.  
- **Function:** Fine-tune feather angles for steering and braking.  

---

##Function##
- **Feather Movement:** Controls spread, rotation, and folding of primary feathers.  
- **Flight Precision:** Adjusts wingtip orientation for steering and braking.  
- **Maintenance:** Assists in preening and feather alignment.
