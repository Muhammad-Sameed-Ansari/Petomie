#Abdominal Ventral Layer#

The ventral abdominal layer forms the horizontal, deep core support of the bird’s trunk.  
It stabilizes internal organs, maintains posture, and provides firmness during flight and perching.  
Energetically, it represents grounding, centered power, and inner stillness.

###Parts###

##Transversus Abdominis##  
The deepest abdominal muscle wrapping horizontally around the torso.  
Energetically, it symbolizes inner strength and calm stability.

##Deep Fibers##  
Support compression and core integrity.  
Energetically, they reflect resilience and grounded focus.

##Ventral Fascia##  
Provides structural support to internal organs.  
Energetically, it represents containment, protection, and inner harmony.

###Function###  
This layer stabilizes the core, supports breathing, and protects the organs during movement.  
Energetically, it anchors the bird’s center and strengthens inner balance.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weakness: reduced core stability or fatigue.  
Tension: shallow breathing or stress.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect instability or loss of centeredness.
