#M. Sternohyoideus#

The sternohyoideus extends from the sternum to the hyoid bones.  
It depresses the hyoid and tongue during swallowing and breathing movements.  
Energetically, it represents humility, release, and grounding.

###Parts###

##Sternal Origin Fibers##  
Provide downward pull on the hyoid.  
Energetically, they symbolize grounding and gentle release.

##Hyoid Insertion Bands##  
Depress the hyoid for swallowing motion.  
Energetically, they reflect flow and letting go.

##Midline Stabilizing Slips##  
Ensure balanced motion along the throat.  
Energetically, they represent alignment and calm.

###Function###  
This muscle depresses the hyoid and tongue, aiding swallowing and airway movements.  
Energetically, it supports release, grounding, and calm transitions.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tightness: swallowing restriction.  
Weakness: reduced tongue depression.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect tension or difficulty releasing.
