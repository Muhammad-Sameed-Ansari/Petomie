#Ischiotrochantericus Muscle#

The ischiotrochantericus muscle helps rotate and extend the femur, contributing to strong backward and downward leg motion.  
It plays a key role in climbing, perching adjustments, and controlled landing.  
Energetically, it represents grounding, support, and stabilizing force.

###Parts###

##Ischial Origin Fibers##  
Anchor to the lower pelvis for strong leverage.  
Energetically, they symbolize grounding and security.

##Trochanteric Rotator Slips##  
Rotate the femur for backward or stabilizing motions.  
Energetically, they reflect direction, awareness, and supportive flow.

##Posterior Support Bands##  
Provide stability during powerful leg extension.  
Energetically, they represent strength, rootedness, and balanced power.

###Function###  
The ischiotrochantericus stabilizes and rotates the hip, supporting strong movements and balanced posture.  
Energetically, it enhances grounding, stability, and confident presence.
