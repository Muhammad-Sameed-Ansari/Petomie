##Location##
Short muscle spanning the first rib and cranial sternum along the ventrolateral thorax.

###Shape###
Narrow, longitudinal band linking the 1st costal arch to the sternum.

##Parts##

###1. First-Rib Attachment###
- Originates on the cranial costal arch.  
- **Function:** Pulls the first rib cranially during inspiration.

###2. Sternal Attachment###
- Inserts on the manubrial/cranial sternum region.  
- **Function:** Couples first-rib motion to sternal excursion.

##Function##
Assists cranial rib elevation and sternal excursion, augmenting inspiratory expansion of the thorax.
