#Serratus Dorsalis Caudalis – Avian (Expiratory)#

##Location##
Dorsolateral thorax, arising from thoracolumbar fascia and inserting on caudal ribs.

###Shape###
Oblique slips running cranioventrally to ribs in the caudal thorax.

##Parts##

###1. Caudal Rib Slips###
- Digitations to ribs in the caudal half of the cage.  
- **Function:** Draw ribs caudally to reduce thoracic volume.

###2. Dorsal Fascia Origin###
- From thoracolumbar fascia.  
- **Function:** Anchors muscle for effective rib retraction.

##Function##
Retracts caudal ribs to assist active expiration and air‑sac compression.
