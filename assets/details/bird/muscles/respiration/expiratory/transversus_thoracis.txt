#Transversus Thoracis – Avian (Expiratory)#

##Location##
Inner ventral thoracic wall, radiating from the sternum to costal cartilages.

###Shape###
Thin, fan‑shaped muscle deep to costal cartilages.

##Parts##

###1. Sternal Origin###
- From inner surface of sternum.  
- **Function:** Pulls costal cartilages caudomedially.

###2. Costal Cartilage Slips###
- Inserts onto several cranial costal cartilages.  
- **Function:** Aids sternal retraction and rib depression.

##Function##
Assists expiration by drawing costal cartilages toward the sternum and reducing thoracic circumference.
