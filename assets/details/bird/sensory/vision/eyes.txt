#Eyes#

Birds have some of the most advanced eyes in the animal kingdom, adapted for exceptional clarity, motion detection, and color perception.  
Energetically, the eyes represent **vision, awareness, and the ability to see truth and direction**.

###Parts###

##Cornea##
The clear outer surface that helps focus incoming light.  
Energetically, symbolizes clarity and protection.

##Lens##
Adjusts shape to sharpen vision at various distances.  
Energetically, reflects adaptability and focus.

##Iris##
Controls the amount of light entering the eye.  
Energetically, represents boundaries and regulation.

##Sclera##
The supportive outer layer maintaining eye structure.  
Energetically, embodies stability and resilience.

###Function###
The eyes allow birds to detect movement, navigate flight paths, locate food, and communicate visually.  
Energetically, they support insight, perception, and purposeful direction.
