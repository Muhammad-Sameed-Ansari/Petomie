#Sesamoids#

##Location##
Sesamoid bones in the bird’s wing lie within tendons near joints, particularly around the wrist and wing tip.  
They protect tendons and enhance wing motion.

###Shape###
Small, rounded or oval bones adapted to reduce friction and increase leverage.

###Parts###

##Proximal Sesamoids##
Located near the wing’s wrist joint.  
Energetically, symbolize support, structure, and flow.

##Distal Sesamoids##
Located closer to the phalanges.  
Energetically, represent ease, precision, and guided movement.

###Function###
Reduce friction, protect tendons, and enhance wing motion efficiency.  
Energetically, govern flow, resilience, and smooth expression.
