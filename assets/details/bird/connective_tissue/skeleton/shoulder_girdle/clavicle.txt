#Clavicle#

##Location##
The bird’s clavicles form the fused structure known as the furcula or “wishbone.”  
Located at the front of the chest, it connects both shoulder joints and plays a major role in flight mechanics.

###Shape###
A V-shaped, lightweight yet strong bone created by the fusion of the left and right clavicles.  
Its spring-like design allows it to flex during wing beats.
