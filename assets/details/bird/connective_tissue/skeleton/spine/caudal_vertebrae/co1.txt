#Co1#

##Location##  
Located at the base of the bird’s tail, directly after the synsacrum.  
Co1 forms the transition between the rigid pelvic spine and the flexible tail.  

###Shape###  
Short, strong vertebra designed to support tail base movement.  

###Parts###

Energetically, represents **stability and grounding**.

Anchor tail muscles and ligaments.  
Energetically, symbolize **support and structural strength**.

Initiates tail movement and supports balance during walking and flight.  
Energetically, governs **grounding, security, and physical control**.
