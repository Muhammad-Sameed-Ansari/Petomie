#Lymph#

In birds, the lymphatic system supports immune defense, fluid balance, and waste removal.  
It includes lymph vessels, sinuses, and nodes adapted for avian physiology.  
Energetically, it represents **cleansing, renewal, and lightness**.

###Parts###

##Lymph Fluid##  
Carries immune cells and removes toxins.  
Energetically, it symbolizes **purification and flow**.

##Lymph Nodes##  
Filter harmful substances and generate immune response.  
Energetically, they reflect **clarity and resilience**.

##Lymph Vessels##  
Distribute lymph through the body, aiding detoxification.  
Energetically, they represent **movement and release**.

###Function###  
Lymph maintains health, removes waste, and supports healing.  
Energetically, it governs **renewal and emotional clarity**.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Swelling: emotional congestion or stagnation.  
- Inflammation: overreaction or stress.  
- Fatigue: slow flow or emotional heaviness.
