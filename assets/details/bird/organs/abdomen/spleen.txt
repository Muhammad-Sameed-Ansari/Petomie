#Spleen#

##Location##  
Situated near the **junction of the proventriculus and gizzard**, close to the pancreas and liver.  

###Shape###  
A **small, rounded dark-red organ** encased in smooth tissue.  

###Parts###  

##Red Pulp##  
Filters and stores blood.  
Energetically, represents **renewal and cleansing**.  

##White Pulp##  
Contains immune cells and lymphoid tissue.  
Energetically, symbolizes **protection and awareness**.  

###Function###  
Filters old red blood cells, stores iron, and contributes to immune defense.  
Energetically, governs **vitality, trust, and emotional steadiness**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Heart and Solar Plexus Chakras.  
**Meridian:** Spleen Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Compassion and nourishment.  
**Balanced Energy:** Warmth and resilience.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Weakness or infection.  
Energetically, **worry or emotional depletion**.  

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###  
Provide warmth, comfort, and balanced food.  
**Colors:** Yellow and pink support vitality.  
**Crystals:** Rose quartz and citrine renew energy.  
**Elements:** Earth nurtures life.  
**Affirmation:** “I am nourished and full of strength.”
