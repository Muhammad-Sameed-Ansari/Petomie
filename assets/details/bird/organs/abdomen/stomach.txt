#Stomach#

##Location##  
Located between the **esophagus and small intestine**, forming two main sections: the proventriculus and gizzard.  

###Shape###  
A **dual-chambered organ** — one glandular and one muscular.  

###Parts###  

##Proventriculus##  
Glandular stomach that secretes digestive enzymes.  
Energetically, represents **initiation and transformation**.  

##Gizzard##  
Muscular stomach that grinds food with grit.  
Energetically, symbolizes **strength and processing**.  

###Function###  
Softens and grinds food for digestion.  
Regulates flow into the intestines.  
Energetically, governs **integration, grounding, and transformation**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Solar Plexus Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Stomach Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Strength, confidence, and focus.  
**Balanced Energy:** Stable digestion and empowerment.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Poor grinding, impaction, or indigestion.  
Energetically, **inability to process experiences**.  

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###  
Provide grit, warmth, and calm feeding.  
**Colors:** Yellow and golden tones strengthen energy.  
**Crystals:** Carnelian and citrine aid digestion.  
**Elements:** Earth and fire encourage transformation.  
**Affirmation:** “I transform nourishment into strength.”
