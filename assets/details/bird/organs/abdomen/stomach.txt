#Stomach#

##Location##
Situated between the esophagus and duodenum, on the left side of the abdominal cavity.

###Shape###
Two-part organ: the proventriculus (glandular stomach) and gizzard (muscular stomach).

##Function##
Proventriculus secretes digestive enzymes, while gizzard mechanically grinds food with grit.
