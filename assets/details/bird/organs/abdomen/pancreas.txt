#Pancreas#

##Location##  
Lies between the **loops of the duodenum**, attached to the small intestine and close to the liver.  

###Shape###  
A **lobulated, elongated organ** with pale yellowish tissue.  

###Parts###  

##Head##  
Connected to the duodenum.  
Energetically, represents **integration and flow**.  

##Body##  
Central section producing enzymes and hormones.  
Energetically, symbolizes **balance and nourishment**.  

##Tail##  
Extends toward the spleen.  
Energetically, reflects **completion and harmony**.  

###Function###  
Produces digestive enzymes and regulates glucose through insulin and glucagon.  
Energetically, governs **sweetness, balance, and acceptance**.
