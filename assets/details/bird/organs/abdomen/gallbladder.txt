#Gallbladder#

##Location##
Located under the right lobe of the liver.

###Shape###
Small, pear-shaped sac.

##Function##
Stores and concentrates bile produced by the liver, releasing it into the duodenum for fat digestion.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Solar Plexus Chakra.
Meridian Connection: Gallbladder Meridian.
Emotional Meaning: Decision-making and courage.
Imbalance: Indecision or resentment.
Balanced Energy: Clear purpose and flexibility.
Energetic Support: Self-trust and movement in nature.
