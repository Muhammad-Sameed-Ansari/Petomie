#Intestines (Small and Large)#

##Location##
Occupy the caudal portion of the abdominal cavity, extending from the gizzard to the cloaca.

###Shape###
Long coiled tubular structure divided into small intestine (duodenum, jejunum, ileum) and large intestine (colon, rectum).

##Function##
Absorbs nutrients, digests food, and eliminates waste.
