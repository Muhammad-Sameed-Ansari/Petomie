#Intestines#

##Location##  
Occupy the **lower abdominal cavity**, extending from the gizzard to the cloaca.  

###Shape###  
A **coiled tubular system** divided into small and large segments.  

###Parts###  

##Small Intestine##  
Includes duodenum, jejunum, and ileum for nutrient absorption.  
Energetically, represents **assimilation and vitality**.  

##Ceca##  
Paired blind sacs that aid fermentation and water absorption.  
Energetically, symbolize **adaptation and balance**.  

##Large Intestine##  
Short segment leading to the cloaca.  
Energetically, embodies **completion and release**.  

###Function###  
Digests and absorbs nutrients, ferments fiber, and manages waste elimination.  
Energetically, governs **balance, discernment, and acceptance**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Solar Plexus and Root Chakras.  
**Meridian:** Small and Large Intestine Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Understanding and letting go.  
**Balanced Energy:** Calm digestion and steady flow.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Loose droppings or malabsorption.  
Energetically, **confusion or resistance to change**.  

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###  
Provide clean food, rest, and emotional security.  
**Colors:** Yellow and brown promote stability.  
**Crystals:** Amber and smoky quartz support grounding.  
**Elements:** Earth and fire balance digestion.
