#Liver#

##Location##  
Located in the **front of the abdominal cavity**, below the heart and in front of the stomach.  

###Shape###  
A **large, two-lobed organ** with smooth reddish-brown tissue.  

###Parts###  

##Left and Right Lobes##  
Perform metabolism, detoxification, and bile production.  
Energetically, represent **balance and strength**.  

##Gallbladder Fossa##  
Depression housing the gallbladder in species that have one.  
Energetically, symbolizes **storage and patience**.  

##Hepatic Vessels##  
Carry blood and nutrients through the organ.  
Energetically, embody **flow and transformation**.  

###Function###  
Metabolizes nutrients, detoxifies the blood, and produces bile.  
Stores energy and supports immunity.  
Energetically, governs **direction, courage, and regeneration**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Solar Plexus Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Liver Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Vision, leadership, and motivation.  
**Balanced Energy:** Purpose and adaptability.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Toxin buildup or poor metabolism.  
Energetically, **anger, stagnation, or lack of direction**.
