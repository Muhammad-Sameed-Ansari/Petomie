#Liver#

##Location##
Occupies the cranial portion of the abdominal cavity, just behind the heart and lungs. 
It extends across the midline with two large lobes that partially cover other abdominal organs.

###Shape###
Large, dark reddish-brown organ with two main lobes (right and left) and a smaller median section.

##Function##
Filters blood, metabolizes fats and proteins, and stores glycogen.
Produces bile for digestion and detoxifies harmful substances.
