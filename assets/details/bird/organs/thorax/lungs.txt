#Lungs#

##Location##  
Located within the **dorsal part of the thoracic cavity**, firmly attached to the ribs and vertebrae, and connected to a system of air sacs.  

###Shape###  
Two **small, spongy, pinkish organs** that are relatively firm and immobile compared to mammalian lungs.  

###Parts###  

##Bronchi##  
Primary airways that connect the trachea to the lungs.  
Energetically, represent **flow and direction**.  

##Parabronchi##  
Tiny tubes that allow continuous air passage in both inhalation and exhalation.  
Energetically, symbolize **balance and renewal**.  

##Air Capillaries##  
Facilitate oxygen exchange.  
Energetically, embody **vitality and clarity**.  

###Function###  
Enable efficient oxygen exchange during both inhalation and exhalation, supplying air to the body and air sacs.  
Energetically, govern **life force, freedom, and renewal**.
