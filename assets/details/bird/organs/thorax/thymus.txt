#Thymus#

##Location##  
Situated along both sides of the **neck**, just under the skin and near the jugular vein. It is most active in young birds and reduces with age.  

###Shape###  
A series of **small, pale lobes** arranged in a chain-like formation.  

###Parts###  

##Lobules##  
Contain developing immune cells (T-lymphocytes).  
Energetically, represent **growth and resilience**.  

##Capsule##  
Outer layer protecting the organ.  
Energetically, symbolizes **protection and strength**.  

###Function###  
Develops immune cells to protect the bird from infections.  
Energetically, governs **trust, protection, and life vitality**.
