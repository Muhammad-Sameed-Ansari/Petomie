#Heart#

##Location##  
Located centrally in the **thoracic cavity**, between the lungs and surrounded by the pericardium.  

###Shape###  
A **cone-shaped muscular organ** with four chambers: two atria and two ventricles.  

###Parts###  

##Right Atrium and Ventricle##  
Receive deoxygenated blood and pump it to the lungs.  
Energetically, represent **cleansing and renewal**.  

##Left Atrium and Ventricle##  
Receive oxygenated blood and distribute it through the body.  
Energetically, symbolize **strength and generosity**.  

##Pericardium##  
Protective sac surrounding the heart.  
Energetically, embodies **safety and compassion**.  

###Function###  
Pumps blood throughout the body, sustaining oxygen and nutrient flow.  
Energetically, governs **love, rhythm, and balance**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Heart Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Heart and Pericardium Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Love, vitality, and courage.  
**Balanced Energy:** Strong rhythm and emotional harmony.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Arrhythmia or circulatory weakness.  
Energetically, **heartache or emotional exhaustion**.
