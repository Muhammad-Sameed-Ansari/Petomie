#Diaphragm (Muscle)#

##Location##
Unlike mammals, birds **do not have a true diaphragm** separating the chest from the abdomen.  
Instead, thin **membranous walls and air sacs** perform a similar role in dividing and supporting breathing function.

###Shape###
No distinct muscle layer; instead, **air sacs and intercostal muscles** expand and contract to move air through the lungs.

##Function##
- Acts as a **respiratory partition** through air sac expansion and body wall motion.  
- Assists in **airflow and pressure balance**.  
- Coordinates movement between breathing, flight, and sound.
