#Diaphragm#

##Location##  
Unlike mammals, birds **lack a true diaphragm**; instead, they use coordinated movement of ribs and sternum for respiration.  

###Shape###  
No separate muscular partition; instead, **air sacs and skeletal motion** regulate airflow.  

###Parts###  

##Ribs and Sternum##  
Move rhythmically to draw air through the lungs.  
Energetically, represent **expansion and adaptability**.  

##Air Sacs##  
Work like bellows to circulate air.  
Energetically, symbolize **flow and freedom**.  

###Function###  
Supports breathing by movement of the thoracic skeleton and air sac system.  
Energetically, governs **flexibility and continuous life flow**.
