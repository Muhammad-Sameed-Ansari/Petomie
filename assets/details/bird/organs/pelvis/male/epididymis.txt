#Epididymis#

##Location##
A narrow, coiled structure lying along the **upper edge of each testis**.
It connects the testis to the sperm duct.

###Shape###
A **thin curved band** of tubules.
In birds, smaller and less distinct than in mammals.

###Parts###
Head, body, and tail regions — though less clearly divided in birds.
Each part helps transport and mature sperm.

###Function###
Stores and matures sperm before they move into the ductus deferens.
Regulates temperature and fluid composition.
Energetically, it symbolizes refinement and readiness.

###Energetic Connection###
Linked to **growth and preparation** — transforming raw potential into aligned action.

###Common Imbalances###
Congestion, inflammation, or poor sperm development.
Energetically, linked to hesitation or unprocessed creative energy.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Apply gentle heat or frequency balancing around the lower back area.
Meditate on transformation and fluid progress from potential to expression.
