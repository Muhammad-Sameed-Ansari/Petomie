#Testes#

##Location##
Situated inside the body cavity near the **kidneys**, attached to the dorsal wall by a thin membrane called the mesorchium.

###Shape###
Paired **oval organs**, smooth and pale in color.
They enlarge during the breeding season and shrink afterward.

###Parts###
Seminiferous tubules, interstitial tissue, blood vessels, and connective layers.

###Function###
Produce sperm and testosterone, regulating reproduction and behavior.
Respond to seasonal changes in daylight and hormones.
Energetically, they symbolize vitality, creation, and inner drive.

###Energetic Connection###
Linked with the **root and sacral chakras**, representing life force and the creative spark.

###Common Imbalances###
Swelling, infection, or reduced fertility.
Energetically, linked to depletion, low vitality, or blocked creative impulse.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Rebalance through rest, sunlight, grounding, and creative expression.
Visualize warm golden light pulsing gently in the lower abdomen, restoring energy and renewal.
