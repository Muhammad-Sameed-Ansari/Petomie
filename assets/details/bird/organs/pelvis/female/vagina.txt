#Vagina#

##Location##  
The vagina is the final section of the oviduct, connecting the uterus (shell gland) to the cloaca.  
It lies near the vent and forms the passage for egg laying.

###Shape###  
A **short, muscular tube** with elastic walls that expand during oviposition.  
Contains folds that prevent egg return and aid smooth passage.

###Parts###  

##Muscular Wall##  
Contracts rhythmically to expel the egg.  
Energetically, represents **release and flow**.

##Mucosal Lining##  
Produces protective secretions.  
Energetically, symbolizes **ease and comfort**.

###Function###  
Passes the egg from the uterus to the cloaca during laying.  
Prevents backward contamination.  
Energetically, governs **release, trust, and transition**.
