#Uterus (Shell Gland)#

##Location##  
The uterus, or shell gland, lies between the isthmus and the vagina in the oviduct.  
It is where the eggshell forms and pigmentation develops.

###Shape###  
A **short, muscular chamber** with a highly vascular lining for calcium secretion.

###Parts###  

##Glandular Layer##  
Secretes calcium carbonate for shell formation.  
Energetically, symbolizes **strength and completion**.

##Muscle Layer##  
Contracts to move the egg into the vagina.  
Energetically, represents **action and readiness**.

###Function###  
Forms the eggshell and adds pigment and outer coating.  
Regulates water exchange and shell hardening.  
Energetically, governs **structure, manifestation, and strength**.

**Emotional Meaning:** Completion and confidence.  
**Balanced Energy:** Firm structure and calm focus.

Energetically, **instability or overpressure**.
