#Isthmus#

##Location##  
The isthmus is the **middle portion of the oviduct**, located between the magnum and the uterus (shell gland).  
It adds protective membranes around the developing egg.

###Shape###  
A **narrow, tubular section** with folded mucous lining and smooth muscle walls.

###Parts###  

##Mucosal Folds##  
Produce the inner and outer shell membranes.  
Energetically, symbolize **structure and protection**.

##Muscle Layer##  
Moves the egg toward the uterus.  
Energetically, represents **direction and stability**.

###Function###  
Forms the shell membranes that cushion and protect the yolk and albumen.  
Energetically, governs **stability and containment**.
