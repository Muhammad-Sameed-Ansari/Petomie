#Ovary#

##Location##  
Birds typically have only the **left ovary**, located near the kidney on the dorsal body wall.  
It connects to the oviduct via a short stalk.

###Shape###  
A **clustered organ** that resembles a bunch of grapes, containing follicles of varying sizes.

###Parts###  

##Follicles##  
Contain yolks at different development stages.  
Energetically, symbolize **creation and potential**.

##Stroma##  
Connective tissue supporting blood flow.  
Energetically, represents **nourishment and foundation**.

###Function###  
Produces and releases ova (yolks) and secretes reproductive hormones.  
Energetically, governs **creativity, vitality, and renewal**.
