#Magnum#

##Location##  
The magnum is the **largest segment of the oviduct**, located after the infundibulum and before the isthmus.  
It adds the albumen (egg white) around the yolk.

###Shape###  
A **thick-walled, coiled tube** lined with glandular tissue that secretes proteins for the egg white.

###Parts###  

##Glandular Cells##  
Secrete albumen and enzymes.  
Energetically, represent **nourishment and protection**.

##Ciliated Lining##  
Moves the forming egg smoothly forward.  
Energetically, symbolizes **flow and cooperation**.

###Function###  
Adds layers of albumen, cushions the yolk, and provides nutrients for embryo protection.  
Energetically, governs **support, nourishment, and balance**.
