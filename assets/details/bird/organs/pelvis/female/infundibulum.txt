#Infundibulum#

##Location##  
The infundibulum is the **funnel-shaped opening** at the start of the oviduct, closest to the ovary.  
It captures the released ovum (yolk) after ovulation.

###Shape###  
A **wide, funnel-like structure** with delicate, fringed edges (fimbriae) that guide the yolk into the oviduct.

###Parts###  

##Fimbriae##  
Fringed extensions that capture the yolk.  
Energetically, symbolize **receptivity and precision**.

##Inner Lining##  
Ciliated epithelium moves the yolk gently inward.  
Energetically, represents **guidance and flow**.

###Function###  
Collects the yolk and initiates fertilization if sperm are present.  
Provides protection and controlled movement.  
Energetically, governs **receptivity, intuition, and beginning of creation**.
