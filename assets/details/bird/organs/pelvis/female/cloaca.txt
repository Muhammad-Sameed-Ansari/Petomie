#Cloaca#

##Location##  
The cloaca is located at the **end of the digestive and reproductive tracts**, opening to the outside through the vent.  
It serves as the shared chamber for the intestinal, urinary, and reproductive systems.

###Shape###  
A **muscular, expandable cavity** divided into three chambers: coprodeum, urodeum, and proctodeum.  
Lined with mucous membrane for protection and secretion.

###Parts###  

##Coprodeum##  
Receives waste from the intestines.  
Energetically, represents **release and cleansing**.

##Urodeum##  
Receives urinary and reproductive products.  
Energetically, symbolizes **flow and connection**.

##Proctodeum##  
The terminal section that opens to the vent.  
Energetically, reflects **transition and renewal**.

###Function###  
Excretes waste and reproductive materials through the vent.  
Acts as a junction point for multiple body systems.  
Energetically, governs **integration, elimination, and renewal**.
