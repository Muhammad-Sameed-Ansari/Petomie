#Kidneys#

##Location##  
The kidneys of birds are located **deep within the pelvic cavity**, fitting snugly into depressions of the **synsacrum** (the fused lower spine).  
They lie along the dorsal body wall on either side of the vertebral column.  

###Shape###  
Three-lobed **elongated and flattened organs** that adapt closely to the surrounding bone.  

###Parts###  

##Cranial Lobe##  
Receives blood from the renal arteries.  
Energetically, represents **vitality and initiation**.  

##Middle Lobe##  
Processes and filters waste; connects with ureters.  
Energetically, symbolizes **flow and transformation**.  

##Caudal Lobe##  
Final section leading to excretion.  
Energetically, reflects **release and completion**.  

###Function###  
Filters nitrogenous waste (mainly uric acid), maintains water and electrolyte balance, and excretes through the ureters into the cloaca.  
Energetically, governs **cleansing, grounding, and energy renewal**.
