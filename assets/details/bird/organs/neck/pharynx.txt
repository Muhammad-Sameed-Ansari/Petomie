#Pharynx#

##Location##  
Located at the **back of the oral cavity**, forming a shared passage for air and food before dividing into the esophagus and trachea.  

###Shape###  
A **short, funnel-shaped muscular chamber** lined with mucosa.  

###Parts###  

##Oropharynx##  
Connects the mouth to the esophagus and trachea.  
Energetically, represents **coordination and choice**.  

##Infundibular Cleft##  
Opening that connects to the Eustachian tubes for ear pressure balance.  
Energetically, symbolizes **communication and balance**.  

###Function###  
Coordinates swallowing and breathing, directing food and air correctly.  
Energetically, governs **adaptation, trust, and clarity**.
