#Pharynx#

##Location##
Located at the back of the mouth, connecting the nasal passages and oral cavity to the esophagus and trachea.

###Shape###
Short muscular tube lined with mucous membrane.

##Function##
Acts as a common passage for both food and air. Directs food into the esophagus and air into the trachea during breathing.
