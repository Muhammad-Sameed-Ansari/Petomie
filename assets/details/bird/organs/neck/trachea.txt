#Trachea#

##Location##
Extends from the larynx down the neck into the thorax, where it divides into two bronchi leading to the lungs.

###Shape###
Long flexible tube made of cartilaginous rings. In birds, it is often longer and coiled, especially in species that sing or produce complex sounds.

##Function##
Carries air from the mouth and nose to the lungs. At its base lies the syrinx, the bird’s true sound-producing organ.

Meridian Connection: Lung Meridian.  
Emotional Meaning: Breath of life and vocal freedom.  
Imbalance: Anxiety or shallow breathing.  
Balanced Energy: Calm rhythm and flow.  
Energetic Support: Conscious breathing, singing, and open posture.
