#Trachea#

##Location##  
Extends from the **larynx** through the neck into the **thoracic cavity**, where it connects to the syrinx before dividing into the bronchi.  

###Shape###  
A **long, flexible tube** composed of complete cartilaginous rings.  

###Parts###  

##Cartilaginous Rings##  
Maintain open air passage.  
Energetically, represent **strength and rhythm**.  

##Tracheal Lining##  
Contains cilia and mucous glands to trap dust.  
Energetically, symbolizes **cleansing and clarity**.  

##Syrinx##  
Located at the lower end of the trachea, producing sound.  
Energetically, embodies **creative communication**.  

###Function###  
Conducts air to the lungs and air sacs and serves as a passage for sound through the syrinx.  
Energetically, governs **flow of life force, breath, and expression**.  

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Throat and Heart Chakras.  
**Meridian:** Lung Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Communication, flow, and vitality.  
**Balanced Energy:** Open breathing and joyful sound.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Respiratory infection or obstruction.  
Energetically, **blocked flow or fear of expression**.
