#Larynx#

##Location##  
Located at the **top of the trachea**, just behind the base of the tongue. Unlike mammals, birds have no vocal cords here.  

###Shape###  
A **cartilaginous structure** forming the entrance to the trachea.  

###Parts###  

##Glottis##  
The slit-like opening at the top of the trachea.  
Energetically, represents **expression and control of breath**.  

##Epiglottis (Absent)##  
Birds lack this structure; protection occurs through reflexes.  
Energetically, symbolizes **instinctive trust and natural rhythm**.  

###Function###  
Controls airflow into the trachea and prevents food entry during swallowing.  
True sound is produced in the syrinx, not the larynx.  
Energetically, governs **breath control and instinctual communication**.
