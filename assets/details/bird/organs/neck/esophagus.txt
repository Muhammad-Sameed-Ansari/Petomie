#Esophagus#

##Location##  
The esophagus extends from the **pharynx** to the **crop** and then to the **proventriculus**, running along the right side of the neck.  

###Shape###  
A **flexible muscular tube** lined with mucous membrane, capable of expansion to store food.  

###Parts###  

##Cervical Portion##  
Passes through the neck to the crop.  
Energetically, represents **reception and trust in nourishment**.  

##Crop##  
An expansion used for temporary food storage and softening.  
Energetically, symbolizes **patience and preparation**.  

##Thoracic Portion##  
Carries food to the proventriculus.  
Energetically, reflects **flow and transition**.  

###Function###  
Transports and stores food, moistens it before digestion, and supports feeding flexibility.  
Energetically, governs **acceptance, flow, and nourishment**.

**Emotional Meaning:** Trust, expression, and receptivity.  
**Balanced Energy:** Calm swallowing and open flow.

Energetically, **resistance to receiving or blocked flow**.
