#Esophagus (Including Crop)#

##Location##
Runs from the pharynx down the neck to the stomach. The crop, an expanded part of the esophagus, is located in the lower neck or upper chest area.

###Shape###
Muscular tube with a flexible wall. The crop is a pouch-like enlargement for temporary food storage.

##Function##
Transports food from the mouth to the stomach. The crop stores food and softens it before digestion. It allows birds to eat quickly and digest later when safe.
