#Testes#

##Location##
Inside the body cavity, right next to the kidneys. Unlike mammals, birds don’t have external testes — everything stays inside the body.

###Shape###
Smooth oval glands, pale or creamy in color. They grow larger during the breeding season and shrink again afterward.

##Function##
Produce sperm and the male hormone testosterone. Testosterone controls mating behavior, singing, and the seasonal breeding cycle.
