#Epididymis#

##Location##
Small structure on top of each testis, connecting to the sperm duct.

###Shape###
A fine, coiled tube that’s easy to miss without magnification.

##Function##
Stores and matures sperm before they travel through the sperm duct. Helps regulate sperm movement and readiness.
