#Gonads#

##Location##  
In male birds, the testes are located **inside the abdominal cavity**, positioned near the **cranial poles of the kidneys**, adjacent to the backbone.  
They do **not descend** and remain internal throughout life.

###Shape###  
Small, oval organs in non-breeding season that can **increase dramatically in size** during breeding season.  
Smooth and pale when inactive; larger, vascular, and bright during reproductive periods.

###Parts###  

##Seminiferous Tubules##  
Microscopic tubules where sperm is produced.  
Energetically, represent **vitality, seasonal renewal, and instinctual creation**.

##Interstitial (Leydig) Cells##  
Produce testosterone, influencing courtship, song, behaviour, and fertility.  
Energetically, symbolize **drive, expression, and masculine rhythm**.

##Tunica Albuginea##  
Outer connective capsule that provides protection and structure.  
Energetically, reflects **stability, grounding, and inner boundaries**.

###Function###  
Produce sperm and testosterone, regulating breeding behaviour, fertility, and seasonal patterns.  
Energetically, maintain **creative rhythm, instinct, and expressive vitality**.
