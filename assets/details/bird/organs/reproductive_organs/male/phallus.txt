#Phallus (Penis Equivalent)#

##Location##
At the vent (opening of the cloaca). Present in some species like ducks and geese, but not in most birds.

###Shape###
Small and spiral when present, hidden inside the cloaca until mating.

##Function##
Transfers sperm during mating. In birds without a phallus, sperm is passed by brief “cloacal contact.”
