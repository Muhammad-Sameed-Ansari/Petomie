#Vagina#

##Location##
The final part of the oviduct, leading to the cloaca.

###Shape###
Muscular tube that can contract to help lay the egg.

##Function##
Guides the egg from the uterus to the cloaca. Also stores sperm in special pockets called sperm storage tubules.
