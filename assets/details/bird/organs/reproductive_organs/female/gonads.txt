
#Gonads#

##Location##  
Female birds typically have **only one functional ovary**, the **left ovary**, located near the upper pole of the left kidney in the abdominal cavity.  
The right ovary regresses early in development.

###Shape###  
Cluster-like, resembling a bunch of grapes when developing follicles are present.  
Large follicles mature dramatically during breeding season.

###Parts###  

##Follicles##  
Egg cells developing in fluid-filled sacs.  
Energetically, represent **potential, renewal, and cyclical creation**.

##Stroma Tissue##  
Supportive tissue that nourishes follicles.  
Energetically, symbolizes **inner support, emotional strength, and nurturing stability**.

##Tunica Albuginea##  
Protective outer layer of the ovary.  
Energetically, reflects **resilience, safety, and inner boundaries**.

###Function###  
Produce eggs and release estrogen and progesterone to regulate breeding cycles.
