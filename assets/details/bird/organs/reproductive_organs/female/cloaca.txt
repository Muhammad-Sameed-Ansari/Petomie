#Cloaca#

##Location##
At the end of the digestive and reproductive tracts; shared exit for both.

###Shape###
Chamber-like space that opens externally through the vent.

##Function##
Eggs, waste, and reproductive fluids all pass through the cloaca. During mating, it allows sperm exchange through brief “cloacal contact.”

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Root Chakra (Muladhara) — grounding and safety.
Meridian Connection: Bladder and Kidney Meridians.
Emotional Meaning: Release and balance between holding and letting go.
Imbalance: Fear, control issues, or instability.
Balanced Energy: Grounded, safe, and calm.
Energetic Support: Grounding exercises and connection to nature.
