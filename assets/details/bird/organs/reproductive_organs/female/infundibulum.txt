#Infundibulum#

##Location##
The first part of the oviduct, right next to the ovary.

###Shape###
A funnel-shaped opening with thin walls.

##Function##
Catches the egg as it’s released from the ovary and is the place where fertilization happens if sperm are present.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Sacral Chakra — reception and beginning of creation.
Meridian Connection: Conception Vessel and Liver Meridian.
Emotional Meaning: Openness and acceptance of new beginnings.
Imbalance: Resistance to change or blocked creativity.
Balanced Energy: Trust in timing and flow.
Energetic Support: Deep breathing, gratitude, and gentle stretching.
