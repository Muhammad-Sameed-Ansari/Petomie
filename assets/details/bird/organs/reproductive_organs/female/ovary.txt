#Ovary#

##Location##
Usually only the left ovary develops in birds. It sits near the left kidney inside the body cavity.

###Shape###
Looks like a small bunch of grapes, made up of developing eggs (ova).

##Function##
Produces eggs and female hormones (estrogen and progesterone). These hormones control reproduction, nesting behavior, and the breeding cycle.
