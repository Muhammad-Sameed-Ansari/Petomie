#Brain#

##Location##  
The brain lies within the **cranial cavity**, protected by the skull and enclosed by meninges. It sits above the eyes and connects to the spinal cord at the base of the skull.  

###Shape###  
A **compact, smooth organ** with large optic lobes and a well-developed cerebellum.  

###Parts###  

##Cerebrum##  
Responsible for behavior, learning, and coordination of voluntary actions.  
Energetically, represents **awareness and adaptability**.  

##Cerebellum##  
Controls balance and flight coordination.  
Energetically, symbolizes **grace and rhythm**.  

##Optic Lobes##  
Highly developed for vision and motion tracking.  
Energetically, embody **clarity and focus**.  

###Function###  
Processes sensory input, maintains balance and flight coordination, and supports instinctual reactions.  
Energetically, governs **perception, precision, and harmony**.
