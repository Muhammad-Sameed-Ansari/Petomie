#Brain#

##Location##
Located inside the skull, protected by bone and connected directly to the spinal cord.

###Shape###
Compact and smooth with distinct lobes — the cerebrum (thinking and coordination), cerebellum (balance and movement), and brainstem (vital functions).

##Function##
Controls all bodily functions, movement, vision, hearing, and instincts like flying, feeding, and communication.
Also regulates breathing, heart rate, and hormonal balance through the hypothalamus and pituitary area.

Meridian Connection: Governing Vessel and Pericardium Meridians.
