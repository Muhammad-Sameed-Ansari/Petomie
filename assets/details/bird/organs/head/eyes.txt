#Eyes#

##Location##  
Situated on the **sides of the head**, providing a wide field of vision. Each eye connects to the optic lobes of the brain via the optic nerve.  

###Shape###  
Large, rounded eyes with a flat or tubular shape depending on the species.  

###Parts###  

##Cornea##  
Transparent front surface focusing light.  
Energetically, represents **clarity and openness**.  

##Lens##  
Changes shape to focus near and far objects.  
Energetically, symbolizes **adaptability and perception**.  

##Retina##  
Contains rods and cones for color and light sensitivity.  
Energetically, reflects **insight and awareness**.  

###Function###  
Provides keen vision, detects motion, and perceives ultraviolet light.  
Energetically, governs **clarity, perception, and intuitive sight**.
