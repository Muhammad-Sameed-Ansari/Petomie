#Ears#

##Location##
Hidden beneath feathers on each side of the head, just behind and below the eyes.

###Shape###
No external pinna like mammals. The ear consists of three parts — outer (opening), middle (with a single bone called columella), and inner ear (for balance and hearing).

##Function##
Detects sound direction, tone, and rhythm. The inner ear also controls balance and spatial orientation during flight and movement.
