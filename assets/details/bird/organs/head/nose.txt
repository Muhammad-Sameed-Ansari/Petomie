#Nose (Olfactory System)#

##Location##
At the upper part of the beak, with two small openings called nares (nostrils). Connected to the olfactory bulbs in the brain.

###Shape###
Small paired openings; size and sensitivity vary depending on the bird’s species and sense of smell needs.

##Function##
Used for smelling, breathing, and detecting environmental changes. Helps birds recognize their nests, mates, and food sources.
