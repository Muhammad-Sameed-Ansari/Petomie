#TCM Inspired Supplements#

###About Supplements###
Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) views birds as highly sensitive beings with strong Shen, Lung, and Heart associations. TCM-inspired supplements for birds are selected very conservatively and are often best used energetically.

Alcohol-free, additive-free formulations are essential. Tinctures are generally not suitable for birds.

##Important Bird Safety Note##
TCM-inspired supplements for birds should be used with extreme caution. Many herbs are best suited for energetic testing only. Physical use must always be supervised by an avian veterinarian or qualified practitioner.
