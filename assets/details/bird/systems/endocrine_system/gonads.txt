#Gonads#

##Location##
Gonads (testes or ovaries) are located near the bird’s kidneys within the abdominal cavity.

###Shape###
Testes: oval and smooth.  
Ovary: cluster-like structure with follicles.

###Parts###

##Testes/Ovary##
Primary reproductive glands.  
Energetically, symbolize **creation, expression, and life force**.

##Reproductive Ducts##
Carry gametes outward.  
Energetically, represent **flow and creative expression**.

###Function###
Produce hormones and gametes, regulate reproductive cycles.  
Energetically, govern **creativity, vitality, and expression of life energy**.
