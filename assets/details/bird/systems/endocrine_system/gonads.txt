#Gonads (Testes / Ovaries)#

##Location##
Located near the **cranial poles of the kidneys** in the abdominal cavity.  
Enlarged during breeding season and reduced otherwise.

###Shape###
Testes: paired, oval, and whitish in males.  
Ovary: single (left side) and lobulated in females.

##Parts##
###1. Testes###
Produce sperm and testosterone.  
**Function:** Control secondary sexual traits and behavior.

###2. Ovary###
Contains follicles at various stages of development.  
**Function:** Produces ova and estrogen.

###3. Oviduct (in females)###
Connected to ovary.  
**Function:** Transports eggs and secretes shell layers.

##Function##
- **Reproduction:** Produces gametes and sex hormones.  
- **Behavior:** Regulates courtship and breeding cycles.  
- **Seasonal Change:** Hormonal activity peaks during mating periods.
