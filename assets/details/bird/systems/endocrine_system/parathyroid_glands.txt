#Parathyroid Glands#

##Location##
Small paired glands located near or embedded in the **thyroid glands**.  
Sometimes found independently along the carotid arteries.

###Shape###
Tiny, flattened bodies composed of chief cells and connective tissue.

##Parts##
###1. Chief Cells###
Main secretory cells.  
**Function:** Produce parathyroid hormone (PTH).

###2. Blood Capillaries###
Dense vascular supply.  
**Function:** Rapid hormone distribution.

##Function##
- **Calcium Regulation:** Increases calcium release from bones.  
- **Phosphate Balance:** Promotes phosphate excretion via kidneys.  
- **Bone Health:** Maintains mineral levels essential for muscle and nerve function.
