#Thyroid Glands#

##Location##
Paired glands located on each side of the **trachea** near the thoracic inlet.

###Shape###
Small, oval, reddish-brown glands surrounded by connective tissue.  
Highly vascular and often asymmetric in size.

##Parts##
###1. Follicles###
Microscopic sacs filled with colloid.  
**Function:** Store thyroglobulin, the precursor of thyroid hormones.

###2. Capillary Network###
Dense blood supply surrounding follicles.  
**Function:** Facilitates hormone release.

##Function##
- **Metabolism:** Controls basal metabolic rate.  
- **Growth and Molt:** Regulates feather replacement and body growth.  
- **Thermoregulation:** Maintains body temperature.  
- **Development:** Affects nervous system maturation.
