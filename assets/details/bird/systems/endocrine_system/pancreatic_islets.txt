##Location##
Located within the **duodenal loop**, extending between the limbs of the duodenum.

###Shape###
Elongated, lobulated organ composed of both endocrine and exocrine tissue.  
Endocrine cells form small clusters known as islets of Langerhans.

##Parts##
###1. Alpha Cells###
Secrete glucagon.  
**Function:** Raise blood glucose by stimulating glycogen breakdown.

###2. Beta Cells###
Secrete insulin.  
**Function:** Lower blood glucose by promoting uptake into cells.

###3. Delta Cells###
Secrete somatostatin.  
**Function:** Inhibit other pancreatic secretions.

##Function##
- **Glucose Regulation:** Balances blood sugar levels.  
- **Energy Metabolism:** Supports sustained muscle activity.  
- **Coordination:** Integrates with liver and adrenal glands for metabolic control.
