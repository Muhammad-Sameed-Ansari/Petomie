#Pancreas (Endocrine)#

##Location##
The endocrine pancreas lies between the loops of the duodenum.

###Shape###
A soft, lobulated gland containing clusters of hormone-producing cells.

###Parts###

##Islets of Langerhans##
Secrete insulin, glucagon, and somatostatin.  
Energetically, symbolize **balance, harmony, and inner regulation**.

###Function###
Regulates blood sugar and metabolic harmony.  
Energetically, governs **balance, flow, and centeredness**.
