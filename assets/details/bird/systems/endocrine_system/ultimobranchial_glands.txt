#Ultimobranchial Glands#

##Location##
Located near the **thyroid glands** or embedded in the wall of large blood vessels in the neck.

###Shape###
Small, pale oval glands distinct from the thyroid.  
Composed of calcitonin-secreting cells arranged in clusters.

##Parts##
###1. Parafollicular (C) Cells###
Hormone-secreting cells.  
**Function:** Produce calcitonin to lower blood calcium levels.

###2. Connective Capsule###
Surrounds and protects the gland.  
**Function:** Maintains structural integrity.

##Function##
- **Calcium Homeostasis:** Reduces blood calcium concentration.  
- **Bone Regulation:** Counteracts parathyroid hormone activity.  
- **Specialization:** Unique gland in birds for fine-tuned calcium control.
