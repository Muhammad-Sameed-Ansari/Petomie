#Adrenal Glands#

##Location##
Paired glands located **anterior to the kidneys**, embedded in surrounding fat tissue.

###Shape###
Flattened, yellowish organs composed of two functional regionsâ€”cortex and medulla.  
Highly vascular and irregular in outline.

##Parts##
###1. Cortex###
Outer layer producing corticosteroids.  
**Function:** Regulates metabolism, stress response, and electrolyte balance.

###2. Medulla###
Inner region producing catecholamines (adrenaline, noradrenaline).  
**Function:** Controls fight-or-flight reactions.

##Function##
- **Stress Response:** Releases hormones for flight and alertness.  
- **Metabolism:** Regulates carbohydrate and protein metabolism.  
- **Osmoregulation:** Maintains salt and water balance.  
- **Energy Mobilization:** Supports endurance and thermoregulation during flight.
