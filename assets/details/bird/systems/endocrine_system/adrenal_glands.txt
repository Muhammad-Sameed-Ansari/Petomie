#Adrenal Glands#

##Location##
The adrenal glands sit near the cranial end of the bird’s kidneys, embedded within surrounding tissue.

###Shape###
Small, oval to elongated glands divided into cortical and medullary regions.

###Parts###

##Cortex##
Produces corticosteroids for metabolism and stress response.  
Energetically, symbolizes **endurance, stability, and grounded strength**.

##Medulla##
Releases catecholamines for rapid action.  
Energetically, represents **alertness, activation, and inner fire**.

###Function###
Regulates stress, metabolism, electrolyte balance, and energy release.  
Energetically, governs **resilience, motivation, and adaptive response**.
