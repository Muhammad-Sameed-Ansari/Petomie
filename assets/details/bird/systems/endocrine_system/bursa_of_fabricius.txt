#Bursa of Fabricius#

##Location##
Located **dorsal to the cloaca**, present only in young birds.  
Regresses with age and disappears in adults.

###Shape###
Pouch-like organ lined with lymphoid tissue.  
Connected to the cloaca by a narrow duct.

##Parts##
###1. Folds of Mucosa###
Contain numerous lymphoid follicles.  
**Function:** Site of B-lymphocyte development.

###2. Lymphoid Tissue###
Active during juvenile stage.  
**Function:** Supports immune and endocrine interactions.

##Function##
- **Immunity:** Produces B-cells for adaptive immune response.  
- **Hormonal Role:** Interacts with other endocrine organs during development.  
- **Growth Regulation:** Affects immune maturation and reproduction indirectly.
