#Air Sacs#

##Location##
Distributed throughout the thoracic and abdominal cavities, extending into the neck, wings, and sometimes the bones via pneumatic openings.

###Shape###
Thin-walled, balloon-like sacs acting as bellows that move air through the rigid lungs.

###Parts###

##Cervical Air Sacs##
Located in the neck.  
Energetically: symbolize **expression and breath awareness**.

##Clavicular Air Sac##
Surrounds the sternum and extends into wing bones.  
Energetically: represents **expansion and lightness**.

##Cranial Thoracic Air Sacs##
Located near the front of the thorax.  
Energetically: embody **forward movement and vitality**.

##Caudal Thoracic Air Sacs##
Located toward the back of the thorax.  
Energetically: symbolize **support and integration**.

##Abdominal Air Sacs##
Largest sacs aiding airflow and buoyancy.  
Energetically: represent **grounding and flow**.

###Function###
Drive continuous airflow through the lungs, regulate temperature, reduce weight, and support vocalization and flight.  
Energetically, govern **expansion, freedom, and rhythmic expression**.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakras: Heart & Solar Plexus  
Meridian: Lung  
Emotional Meaning: Freedom, expansion, lightness  
Balanced Energy: Ease, buoyancy, and emotional openness

###Common Imbalances###
Air sac infection, rupture, air leakage  
Energetically, restricted expansion or emotional pressure
