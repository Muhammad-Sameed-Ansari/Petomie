#Bronchi#

##Location##
The bronchi branch from the lower trachea and extend into the lungs and air sacs, forming the major conduits of airflow.

###Shape###
Two primary bronchi diverge and further divide into secondary and tertiary bronchi organized into a loop-like airflow pattern unique to birds.

###Parts###

##Primary Bronchi##
Carry air from the trachea into the lungs.  
Energetically: symbolize **direction and initiation**.

##Secondary Bronchi##
Distribute air through lung regions.  
Energetically: represent **organization and balance**.

##Parabronchi##
Tiny passageways where gas exchange occurs.  
Energetically: embody **refinement and transformation**.

###Function###
Transport air deeply into the lungs, maintain one-way airflow, and distribute oxygen efficiently.  
Energetically, govern **movement, transformation, and energetic renewal**.
