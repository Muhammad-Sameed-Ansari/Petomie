#Trachea#

##Location##
The trachea extends from the larynx down the neck toward the thoracic cavity, where it divides into the primary bronchi.

###Shape###
A long, flexible tube supported by complete or semi-complete cartilaginous rings, preventing collapse during airflow.

###Parts###

##Cartilaginous Rings##
Hold the airway open.  
Energetically: symbolize **support, structure, and resilience**.

##Ciliated Lining##
Moves debris upward toward the pharynx.  
Energetically: represents **cleansing and upward flow**.

##Lower Tracheal Region##
Leads toward the syrinx in many species.  
Energetically: embodies **transition and expression**.

###Function###
Conducts air from the upper respiratory system to the bronchi, filters debris, and maintains stable airflow.  
Energetically, governs **flow, clarity, and steady communication**.
