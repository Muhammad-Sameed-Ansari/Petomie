#Lungs#

##Location##
Located within the dorsal thoracic cavity, tightly attached to the ribs and vertebrae, forming a compact respiratory core.

###Shape###
Rigid, compact, pale organs with a sponge-like network of parabronchi and capillaries.

###Parts###

##Parabronchi##
Tiny channels where gas exchange happens.  
Energetically: symbolize **transformation and clarity**.

##Air Capillaries##
Fine branches exchanging oxygen and carbon dioxide.  
Energetically: represent **vitality and renewal**.

##Interconnected Airflow Network##
Creates continuous, one-way air movement.  
Energetically: embodies **flow and constancy**.

###Function###
Support unidirectional airflow, allow continuous gas exchange even during exhalation, and maintain high oxygen efficiency.  
Energetically, govern **clarity, vitality, and inner transformation**.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Heart  
Meridian: Lung  
Emotional Meaning: Openness, vitality, and emotional flow  
Balanced Energy: Strong breath, steady emotional regulation

###Common Imbalances###
Respiratory infection, inflammation, reduced exchange  
Energetically, emotional heaviness or constriction
