#Pharynx#

##Location##
The pharynx lies between the nasal cavities, oral cavity, and the openings of the esophagus and glottis.

###Shape###
A muscular, funnel-shaped region guiding airflow and swallowed food into the correct pathways.

###Parts###

##Choanal Opening##
Connects nasal cavity to the pharynx.  
Energetically: symbolizes **pathway and transition**.

##Esophageal Entrance##
Leads to the digestive tract.  
Energetically: represents **direction and choice**.

##Glottis Entrance##
Leads to the trachea.  
Energetically: embodies **breath and expression**.

###Function###
Directs air toward the trachea and food into the esophagus; essential for coordinated swallowing and breathing.  
Energetically, governs **direction, decision, and balanced flow**.
