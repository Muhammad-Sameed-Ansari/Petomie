#Nostrils (Nares)#

##Location##
Located on the upper beak, either at the base near the cere (in parrots and pigeons) or further along the beak in other species.

###Shape###
Two rounded or oval external openings leading into the nasal cavities.

###Parts###

##External Nares##
Openings that admit air.  
Energetically: symbolize **invitation and openness**.

##Cere (Species-Dependent)##
Soft tissue surrounding the nares in some birds.  
Energetically: represents **sensitivity and protection**.

###Function###
Allow air entry, enable smelling, and filter large particles.  
Energetically, govern **openness, awareness, and first contact with the world**.
