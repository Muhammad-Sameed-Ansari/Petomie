#Veins#

##Location##
Form a widespread network returning **deoxygenated blood** from the body to the **right atrium**.

###Shape###
Thin-walled and equipped with **valves** to prevent backflow.  
Often run parallel to arteries and merge into larger trunks.

##Parts##
###1. Jugular Veins###
Large paired veins in the neck.  
**Function:** Drain blood from the head and brain.

###2. Brachial Veins###
Located in the wings.  
**Function:** Return blood from wing muscles.

###3. Hepatic Portal Vein###
Connects digestive organs to the liver.  
**Function:** Filters and processes nutrient-rich blood.

###4. Renal Portal System###
Unique to birds and reptiles.  
**Function:** Channels blood from hindlimbs through the kidneys for filtration.

###5. Caudal Vena Cava###
Main vessel returning blood to the heart.  
**Function:** Collects systemic venous blood.

##Function##
- **Return Flow:** Bring deoxygenated blood to the heart.  
- **Filtration:** Aid waste removal via renal system.  
- **Regulation:** Maintain one-way circulation.  
- **Thermoregulation:** Balance temperature during activity.
