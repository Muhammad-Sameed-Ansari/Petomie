#Blood#

##Location##
Blood circulates throughout the entire avian body, flowing through arteries, veins, and capillaries.  
It reaches every organ, muscle, and feather, supporting the bird’s high metabolic demands.

###Shape###
Not a solid structure but a fluid tissue composed of plasma, red and white blood cells, and thrombocytes.

###Parts###

##Plasma##
The fluid component carrying nutrients, hormones, and waste products.  
Energetically, symbolizes flow, balance, and nourishment.

##Red Blood Cells##
Oval, nucleated cells that transport oxygen.  
Energetically, represent vitality, energy, and life force.

##White Blood Cells##
Cells responsible for immune defense.  
Energetically, embody protection, resilience, and inner strength.

##Thrombocytes##
Blood elements that support clotting.  
Energetically, symbolize repair, renewal, and restoration.

###Function###
Transports oxygen and nutrients, removes waste, regulates temperature, and supports immunity.  
Energetically, governs vitality, flow, and life force distribution.
