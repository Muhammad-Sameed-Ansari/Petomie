#Feather Follicles#

##Location##
Embedded throughout the skin in specific tracts (pterylae), with featherless regions in between (apteria).

###Shape###
Deep tubular invaginations in the skin where feathers originate.

###Parts###

##Follicular Collar##
Generates new feather cells.  
Energetically, symbolizes **growth and renewal**.

##Follicular Sheath##
Protects the developing feather.  
Energetically, represents **support and nurturing**.

###Function###
Supports feather growth, regeneration, and molting cycles.  
Energetically, governs **rebirth, renewal, and transition**.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Sacral Chakra  
Element: Water  
Emotional Meaning: Letting go of the old and embracing renewal  
Balanced Energy: Smooth transitions and strong regeneration

###Common Imbalances###
Folliculitis, weak regrowth, stress-related feather loss.  
Energetically, difficulty transitioning or regeneration.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Orange and white.  
Crystals: Moonstone, carnelian.  
Affirmation: “I renew and regenerate with ease and softness.”
