##Location##
Found mainly at the **base of the tail**, though some species have additional small glands on other parts of the body.

##Shape##
Small, tubular or lobed structures specialized for secretion and preening.

##Parts##

###1. Uropygial (Preen) Gland###
- Located at the tail base.  
- **Function:** Secretes oil for feather waterproofing and conditioning.  

###2. Salt Glands (in seabirds)###
- Found near the eyes or nasal openings.  
- **Function:** Excrete excess salt from the bloodstream.  

###3. Other Minor Glands###
- Rare or vestigial in most species.  
- **Function:** Assist in lubrication and protection.  

##Function##
- **Waterproofing:** Maintains feather condition and buoyancy.  
- **Protection:** Prevents microbial growth on feathers.  
- **Osmoregulation:** Maintains salt balance in marine species.
