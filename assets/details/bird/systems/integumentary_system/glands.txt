#Skin Glands#

##Location##
Distributed across the skin, though birds have very few glands compared to mammals.

###Shape###
Microscopic clusters of secretory cells.

###Parts###

##Uropygial Gland (Primary)##
Major gland producing preen oil.  
Energetically, symbolizes protection and renewal.

##Minor Cutaneous Glands##
Small glands near eyelids or cloaca in some species.  
Energetically, represent subtle support and lubrication.

###Function###
Lubricate feathers, provide waterproofing, and support antimicrobial defense.  
Energetically, govern **protection, cleansing, and maintenance**.
