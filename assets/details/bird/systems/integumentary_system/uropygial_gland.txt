#Uropygial Gland (Preen Gland)#

##Location##
Located at the base of the tail on the dorsal surface, just above the pygostyle.

###Shape###
A small, elongated gland with one or two openings (papillae).

###Parts###

##Gland Lobes##
Produce oily secretions.  
Energetically, symbolize **self-maintenance, protection, and resilience**.

##Papilla##
External opening birds rub with their beak.  
Energetically, represents **activation and purification**.

###Function###
Produces preen oil used for waterproofing feathers, maintaining flexibility, and supporting antimicrobial protection.  
Energetically, governs **self-care, cleansing, and energetic shielding**.

Balanced Energy: Strong protection and healthy self-care rituals

###Common Imbalances###

Energetically, weakened boundaries or poor self-maintenance.
