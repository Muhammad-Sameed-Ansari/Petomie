#Feathers#

##Location##
Feathers cover the bird’s body in specific tracts, with bare regions (apteria) between them.

###Shape###
Complex keratin structures ranging from contour to down to flight feathers.

###Parts###

##Rachis##
Central shaft.  
Energetically, symbolizes direction and alignment.

##Barbs & Barbules##
Interlocking feather branches.  
Energetically, represent connection and cohesion.

##Calamus##
Hollow base anchoring the feather.  
Energetically, reflects grounding and stability.

###Function###
Enable flight, insulation, waterproofing, communication, and display.
