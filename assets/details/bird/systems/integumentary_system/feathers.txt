##Location##
Cover the **entire body** except the beak, legs, and parts of the feet.  
Feathers arise from follicles in the skin.

##Shape##
Lightweight but strong **keratinized structures** with a central shaft and branching barbs.

##Parts##

###1. Contour Feathers###
- Outer feathers forming the visible body surface.  
- **Function:** Streamline the body and protect underlying feathers.  

###2. Down Feathers###
- Soft feathers beneath contour layers.  
- **Function:** Provide insulation by trapping air.  

###3. Flight Feathers###
- Large feathers on wings and tail.  
- **Function:** Enable flight and maneuverability.  

###4. Semiplumes, Filoplumes, Bristles###
- Specialized small feathers.  
- **Function:** Aid in sensory detection, insulation, and protection.  

##Function##
- **Flight:** Provide lift, thrust, and balance.  
- **Insulation:** Regulate temperature.  
- **Display:** Used for communication and mating displays.  
- **Protection:** Shield skin from sunlight and injury.
