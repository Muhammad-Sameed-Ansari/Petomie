#Claws / Talons#

##Location##
At the tips of the toes, forming the end of each digit.

###Shape###
Curved keratinized structures that vary from delicate to heavily hooked.

###Parts###

##Keratin Sheath##
Outer protective layer.  
Energetically, symbolizes **strength and precision**.

##Bone Core##
Internal digit bone supporting the claw.  
Energetically, represents **structure and purpose**.

###Function###
Used for perching, grooming, defense, hunting, and balance.  
Energetically, governs **precision, grounding, and decisive action**.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Root Chakra  
Element: Earth  
Emotional Meaning: Stability, grounded movement, assertiveness  
Balanced Energy: Confident steps and strong presence

###Common Imbalances###
Overgrowth, breaks, infections.  
Energetically, lack of grounding or over-aggression.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###

Crystals: Hematite, smoky quartz.  
Affirmation: “I move with grounded strength and precision.”
