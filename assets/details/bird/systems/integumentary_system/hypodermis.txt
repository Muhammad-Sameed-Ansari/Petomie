#Hypodermis#

##Location##
The hypodermis is the deepest layer of the skin, lying beneath the dermis and connecting it to muscles and underlying structures.

###Shape###
A soft layer of loose connective tissue that may include fat deposits and air sac expansions.

###Parts###

##Subcutaneous Fat##
Stores energy and provides insulation.  
Energetically, symbolizes reserves, comfort, and inner safety.

##Connective Tissue Layers##
Bind the skin to muscle.  
Energetically, represent grounding and structural connection.

###Function###
Insulates, cushions, stores energy, and anchors skin.  
Energetically, governs **stability, grounding, and inner security**.
