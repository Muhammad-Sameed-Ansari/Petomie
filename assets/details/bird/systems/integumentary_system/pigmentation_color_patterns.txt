##Location##
Found in **feathers, beak, skin, and eyes** across the body.

##Shape##
Color results from pigments and microscopic feather structures that refract light.

##Parts##

###1. Melanin###
- Dark pigments in feathers and skin.  
- **Function:** Strengthens feathers and provides UV protection.  

###2. Carotenoids###
- Yellow, red, or orange pigments from diet.  
- **Function:** Create bright colors for mating displays.  

###3. Structural Colors###
- Produced by light scattering through feather layers.  
- **Function:** Generate blues, greens, and iridescent hues.  

##Function##
- **Camouflage:** Blends birds into surroundings.  
- **Display:** Attracts mates and signals health.  
- **Protection:** Prevents feather degradation from UV light.
