#Dermis#

##Location##
The dermis lies beneath the epidermis and forms the supportive inner layer of the bird’s skin.

###Shape###
A thicker, fibrous layer containing blood vessels, nerves, connective tissue, and feather follicles.

###Parts###

##Papillary Layer##
Contains fine fibers and capillaries.  
Energetically, symbolizes gentle nourishment and sensitivity.

##Reticular Layer##
Dense collagen fibers providing strength.  
Energetically, represents structure, resilience, and emotional stability.

##Sensory & Vascular Network##
Houses nerves and vessels.  
Energetically, embodies communication and inner flow.

###Function###
Provides elasticity, support, and cushioning. Nourishes the epidermis and feathers and carries sensory information.  
Energetically, governs **inner support, resilience, and subtle awareness**.
