##Location##
Located around the **eyes, beak, head, and along feather tracts**.

##Shape##
Fine, hair-like feathers or modified contour feathers with nerve endings at their bases.

##Parts##

###1. Filoplumes###
- Thin, hair-like feathers near contour feathers.  
- **Function:** Monitor feather position and movement.  

###2. Bristles###
- Stiff, hair-like feathers around eyes and beak.  
- **Function:** Protect sensory organs and aid in prey capture.  

###3. Nerve Endings###
- Found at the feather base.  
- **Function:** Detect touch and vibration.  

##Function##
- **Sensation:** Provides tactile feedback and environmental awareness.  
- **Protection:** Guards eyes and facial areas.  
- **Coordination:** Assists in preening and flight feather control.
