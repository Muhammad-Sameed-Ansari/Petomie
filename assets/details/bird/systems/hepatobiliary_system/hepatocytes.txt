#Hepatocytes#

Hepatocytes are the primary functional cells of the liver.  
They produce bile, detoxify the blood, store nutrients, and regulate metabolism.  
Energetically, they represent cellular intelligence, adaptability, and self-healing capacity.

###Parts###

##Cell Body##  
The main body of the hepatocyte where metabolism occurs.  
Energetically, it reflects core vitality.

##Cell Membrane##  
The boundary that controls what enters and leaves the cell.  
Energetically, it symbolizes healthy energetic boundaries.

###Function###  
Carry out detoxification, bile production, and nutrient processing.  
Energetically, they support regeneration and energetic balance.

###Common Imbalances###  
Toxic overload: exhaustion or stagnation.  
Damage: loss of vitality or resilience.
