#Hepatic Artery#

The hepatic artery supplies oxygen-rich blood to the liver.  
It supports the high metabolic and detoxification demands of avian liver tissue.  
Energetically, it represents vitality, nourishment, and life force.

###Parts###

##Arterial Lumen##  
The inner passage carrying blood into the liver.  
Energetically, it reflects the flow of vital energy.

##Arterial Wall##  
Strong elastic tissue that maintains blood pressure.  
Energetically, it symbolizes strength and resilience.

###Function###  
Delivers oxygen and nutrients to liver cells.  
Energetically, it fuels cleansing and regeneration.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weak flow: low vitality or fatigue.  
Constriction: stress or tension.  
Overpressure: inflammatory stress.
