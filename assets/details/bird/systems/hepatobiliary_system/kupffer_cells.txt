#Kupffer Cells#

Kupffer cells are specialized immune cells located within the liver.  
They remove bacteria, toxins, and damaged cells from the bloodstream.  
Energetically, they represent protection, immune awareness, and energetic boundary defense.

###Parts###

##Cell Body##  
The main immune-active structure of the Kupffer cell.  
Energetically, it reflects alertness and defense.

##Cell Processes##  
Extensions that capture and digest unwanted material.  
Energetically, they symbolize active protection.

###Function###  
Filter pathogens and debris from blood flowing through the liver.  
Energetically, they support safety and energetic cleanliness.

###Common Imbalances###  
Overactivation: inflammation or immune stress.  
Weakness: reduced protection or vulnerability.
