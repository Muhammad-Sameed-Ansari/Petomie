#Portal Vein#

The portal vein carries nutrient-rich and toxin-containing blood from the digestive tract to the liver.  
It delivers substances that must be processed, stored, or detoxified.  
Energetically, it represents incoming information, assimilation, and integration.

###Parts###

##Portal Vein Lumen##  
The channel through which blood flows into the liver.  
Energetically, it reflects openness to receiving and processing.

##Vein Wall##  
Flexible tissue that adapts to changing blood volume.  
Energetically, it symbolizes adaptability and flow.

###Function###  
Transports blood from the intestines to the liver for filtering and metabolism.  
Energetically, it supports assimilation of experiences and resources.

###Common Imbalances###  
Congestion: overload or emotional burden.  
Weak flow: poor assimilation.  
Pressure: internal stress.
