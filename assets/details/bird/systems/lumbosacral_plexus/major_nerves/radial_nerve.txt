#Radial Nerve#

##Location##
Emerges from the brachial plexus and travels along the dorsal wing and forelimb, supplying extensor muscles.

###Shape###
A robust nerve that branches widely across the upper wing.

###Parts###

##Motor Branches##
Activate wing and wrist extensors.  
Energetically, symbolize **expansion, power, and outward expression**.

##Sensory Branches##
Provide sensation to the dorsal wing and limb.  
Energetically, represent **clarity, awareness, and outward perception**.

###Function###
Controls extension, wing lift, and stabilizing movements.  
Energetically, governs **empowerment, outward movement, and strength**.
