#Obturator Nerve#

##Location##
Emerges from the lumbosacral plexus and passes through the pelvic region to innervate the inner thigh muscles.

###Shape###
A medium-sized nerve that divides into anterior and posterior branches along the medial thigh.

###Parts###

##Motor Branches##
Innervate thigh adductor muscles.  
Energetically, symbolize **control, focus, and alignment**.

##Sensory Branches##
Provide sensation to the inner thigh.  
Energetically, represent **awareness and emotional balance**.

###Function###
Controls limb adduction, stabilizes the leg during landing and perching, and contributes to balance.  
Energetically, governs **centering, alignment, and internal balance**.
