#Femoral Nerve#

##Location##
The femoral nerve arises from the lumbosacral plexus and travels along the front of the thigh, supplying key muscles of the bird’s upper hind limb.

###Shape###
A strong, cord-like nerve that divides into several branches as it descends the thigh.

###Parts###

##Motor Branches##
Innervate major thigh extensors.  
Energetically, symbolize **strength, propulsion, and confident forward motion**.

##Cutaneous Branches##
Provide sensation to the medial thigh.  
Energetically, represent **awareness, sensitivity, and grounded presence**.

###Function###
Controls extension of the hip and knee, supports standing and takeoff, and supplies sensation to parts of the thigh.  
Energetically, governs **forward movement, stability, and confident action**.
