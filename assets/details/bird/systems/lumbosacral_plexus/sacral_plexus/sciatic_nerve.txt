#Sciatic Nerve#

##Location##
The sciatic nerve originates from the lumbosacral plexus and travels down the back of the thigh, becoming the largest nerve of the hind limb.

###Shape###
A thick, powerful nerve trunk that branches into the tibial and peroneal nerves.

###Parts###

##Proximal Trunk##
Carries major motor fibers.  
Energetically, symbolizes **power, drive, and forward movement**.

##Tibial Branch##
Innervates lower leg flexors and foot muscles.  
Energetically, represents **grounding, flow, and purposeful action**.

##Peroneal Branch##
Controls extensors of the foot and toes.  
Energetically, embodies **lift, agility, and directional change**.

###Function###
Controls hind limb strength, propulsion, leg flexion and extension, and provides sensation to most of the limb.  
Energetically, governs **movement power, motivation, and direction**.
