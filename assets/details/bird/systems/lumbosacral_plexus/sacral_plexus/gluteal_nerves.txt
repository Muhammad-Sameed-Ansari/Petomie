#Gluteal Nerves#

##Location##
The gluteal nerves arise from the lumbosacral plexus and run across the outer pelvis into the muscles of the hip and upper hind limb.

###Shape###
Two primary nerves — cranial and caudal gluteal — forming strong branches that fan into the large hip muscles.

###Parts###

##Cranial Gluteal Nerve##
Supplies the main hip abductors and extensors.  
Energetically, symbolizes **power, lift, and confident propulsion**.

##Caudal Gluteal Nerve##
Innervates deeper hip extensors and stabilizers.  
Energetically, represents **grounding, stability, and controlled action**.

###Function###
Controls hip extension, abduction, balance during landing, and strong takeoff movements.  
Energetically, governs **power, lift, stability, and confident motion**.
