#Lymphoid Tissue#

##Location##
Found throughout the bird’s body, especially in the digestive system, respiratory tract, skin, and around major blood vessels.

###Shape###
Clusters or patches of soft, spongy immune tissue distributed in strategic locations.

###Parts###

##Diffuse Lymphoid Tissue##
Scattered immune cells in mucosal surfaces.

Small, focused areas of immune activity.

##Gut‑Associated Lymphoid Tissue (GALT)##
Highly developed in birds.  
Energetically, embodies **nourishment, gut instinct, and emotional digestion**.
