#Lymph Hearts#

##Location##
Unique to birds, lymph hearts are found along the vertebral column and pelvic region, pumping lymph fluid toward major veins.

###Shape###
Small, paired, rhythmic muscular sacs resembling miniature pulsating nodes.

###Parts###

##Dorsal Lymph Hearts##
Located near the spine.  
Energetically, symbolize **upward movement, lift, and renewal**.

##Ventral Lymph Hearts##
Closer to the pelvis.  
Energetically, represent **grounding, rhythm, and deep vitality**.

###Function###
Actively pump lymph fluid, maintain flow, and prevent stagnation.  
Energetically, govern **momentum, rhythm, and emotional circulation**.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Root & Heart  
Meridian: Pericardium  
Emotional Meaning: Rhythm, emotional movement, resilience  
Balanced Energy: Strong, steady internal flow

###Common Imbalances###
Sluggish lymph movement, swelling, immune challenges  
Energetically, emotional stagnation or disrupted internal rhythm
