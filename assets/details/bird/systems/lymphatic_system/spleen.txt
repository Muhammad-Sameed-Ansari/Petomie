#Spleen#

##Location##
Located between the **proventriculus** and **pancreas**, on the right side of the body near the duodenal loop.

###Shape###
Small, round to oval organ (red to dark brown) with smooth surface.  
Lacks lobulation and is encased in a thin connective capsule.

##Parts##
###1. Capsule###
Connective covering with blood vessels.  
**Function:** Provides structural support.

###2. White Pulp###
Lymphoid tissue with immune cells.  
**Function:** Filters blood and initiates immune responses.

###3. Red Pulp###
Contains red blood cells and macrophages.  
**Function:** Removes aged erythrocytes and stores iron.

##Function##
- **Filtration:** Cleanses blood of debris and pathogens.  
- **Immune Response:** Produces lymphocytes and antibodies.  
- **Recycling:** Destroys old blood cells and recycles components.  
- **Storage:** Serves as a reservoir for blood and immune cells.
