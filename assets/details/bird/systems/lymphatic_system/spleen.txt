#Spleen#

##Location##
Situated near the proventriculus and liver, the spleen is a round, compact organ involved in immune filtration and blood maintenance.

###Shape###
Small, round to oval, reddish organ with a dense texture.

###Parts###

##White Pulp##
Immune filtration and antibody production.  
Energetically, symbolizes **purity, protection, and discernment**.

##Red Pulp##
Filters old blood cells and stores iron.  
Energetically, represents **renewal, life force, and vitality**.

###Function###
Filters blood, stores immune cells, removes old red blood cells, and maintains iron balance.  
Energetically, governs **renewal, vitality, and internal cleansing**.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Solar Plexus  
Meridian: Spleen  
Emotional Meaning: Strength, renewal, and emotional clarity  
Balanced Energy: Clean, energized internal flow

###Common Imbalances###
Enlargement, immune weakness  
Energetically, heaviness or difficulty letting go

Colors: Yellow & deep red  
Crystals: Bloodstone, citrine  
Element: Earth
