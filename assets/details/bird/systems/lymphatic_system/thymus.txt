#Thymus#

##Location##
The thymus lies along both sides of the bird’s neck, consisting of multiple lobes extending from the jaw to the upper thorax.

###Shape###
A soft, lobulated gland that decreases in size as the bird matures.

###Parts###

##Cortical Region##
Site of immature T‑cell development.  
Energetically, symbolizes **growth, learning, and potential**.

##Medullary Region##
Final maturation and selection of T‑cells.  
Energetically, represents **refinement, clarity, and inner strength**.

###Function###
Develops T‑cells essential for adaptive immunity and trains immune recognition.  
Energetically, governs **identity, boundaries, and inner fortitude**.
