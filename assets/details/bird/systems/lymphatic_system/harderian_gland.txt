#Harderian Gland#

##Location##  
Situated **behind and beneath the eye** within the orbit, closely associated with the nictitating membrane.

###Shape###  
A softly triangular or almond-shaped gland of dense, immune-rich tissue.

###Parts###

##Lymphoid Tissue##  
Immune cells embedded within the gland.  
Energetically, symbolize **inner sight, awareness, and subtle protection**.

##Secretory Lobules##  
Produce secretions for eye lubrication and defense.  
Energetically, represent **flow, comfort, and emotional soothing**.

##Nictitating Membrane Interface##  
Where the gland connects to the third eyelid.  
Energetically, reflects **clarity of vision and intuitive perception**.

###Function###  
Supports eye lubrication, produces immune-active secretions, and contributes to ocular immunity.  
Energetically, assists **clarity, insight, perception, and emotional ease**.
