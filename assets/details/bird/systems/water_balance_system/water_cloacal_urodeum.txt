#Cloacal Urodeum – Water Reabsorption#

##Location##
Middle chamber of the cloaca, situated between the coprodeum and proctodeum.

###Shape###
A smooth, soft-walled chamber with specialized mucosa for water and electrolyte uptake.

###Parts###

##Absorptive Lining##
Pulls water back into the bloodstream.  
Energetically: symbolizes **conservation and renewal**.

##Ureter Openings##
Deliver uric acid paste.  
Energetically: represent **transition and flow**.

###Function###
Reabsorbs remaining water before waste is expelled, playing a major role in hydration and moisture retention.  
Energetically, it governs **renewal, conservation, and healthy boundaries**.
