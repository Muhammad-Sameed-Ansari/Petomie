#Kidneys – Water Regulation#

##Location##
Deep within the dorsal body wall, embedded in the recesses of the synsacrum on either side of the spine.

###Shape###
Elongated, lobulated organs divided into cranial, middle, and caudal segments specialized for water conservation.

###Parts###

##Nephrons (Avian Type)##
Filter blood and conserve water by producing uric acid instead of liquid urine.  
Energetically: symbolize **purification and adaptive balance**.

##Tubular System##
Reabsorbs essential water and electrolytes.  
Energetically: represents **restoration and refinement**.

##Ureter Entry Points##
Channel concentrated uric acid out of the kidney.  
Energetically: embody **release and movement**.

###Function###
The kidneys minimize water loss by reabsorbing fluids, regulating salts, and excreting nitrogen as uric acid paste.  
Energetically, they govern **deep cleansing, stability, and inner fluid balance**.
