#Ureters – Water Conservation Role#

##Location##
Extend from each kidney to the urodeum of the cloaca, traveling along the dorsal cavity.

###Shape###
Narrow muscular tubes lined with smooth mucosa carrying thick uric acid paste.

###Parts###

##Upper Ureter##
Receives concentrated filtrate.  
Energetically: symbolizes **movement and transition**.

##Middle Ureter##
Conducts uric acid without losing moisture.  
Energetically: represents **preservation and flow**.

##Lower Ureter##
Delivers waste to the urodeum.  
Energetically: embodies **release and clarity**.

###Function###
Carry highly concentrated uric acid while preventing fluid loss, supporting exceptional water conservation.  
Energetically, they govern **flow, channeling, and emotional release**.
