#Accessory Nerve (XI)#

The **accessory nerve** assists with neck and upper body movement in birds.  
Energetically, it represents **strength, posture, and empowered expression**.

###Parts###

##Spinal Portion##
Supports neck muscles and posture.  
Energetically, symbolizes **support and confidence**.

###Function###
Controls muscles involved in head positioning and posture.  
Energetically, governs **strength, confidence, and alignment**.

###Common Imbalances###
Neck weakness or poor head control.  
Energetically, lack of support or diminished confidence.
