#Glossopharyngeal Nerve (IX)#

The **glossopharyngeal nerve** supports swallowing, taste, and part of vocalization.  
Energetically, it represents **clearing, communication, and internal balance**.

###Parts###

##Sensory Branch##
Carries taste and throat sensation.  
Energetically, symbolizes **awareness and inner truth**.

##Motor Branch##
Assists swallowing and throat movement.  
Energetically, represents **flow and expression**.

###Function###
Supports swallowing, taste, and digestive initiation.  
Energetically, governs **expression, truth, and internal harmony**.

###Common Imbalances###
Difficulty swallowing, reduced taste, or throat tension.  
Energetically, blocked communication or emotional restriction.
