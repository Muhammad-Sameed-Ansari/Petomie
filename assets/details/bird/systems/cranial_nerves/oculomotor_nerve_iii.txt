###Parts###

##Motor Fibers##

Energetically, symbolize **direction and purposeful movement**.

###Function###
Moves the eye, controls focus, and adjusts light intake.  
Energetically, supports **clarity, alignment, and inner focus**.

###Common Imbalances###
Difficulty tracking movement, drooping eyelid, or pupil dysfunction.  
Energetically, lack of direction or difficulty adjusting to change.
