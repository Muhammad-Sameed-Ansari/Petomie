#Vestibulocochlear Nerve (VIII)#

The **vestibulocochlear nerve** manages hearing and balance.  
Birds rely heavily on this nerve for flight coordination and environmental awareness.  
Energetically, it represents **harmony, balance, and attunement**.

###Parts###

##Cochlear Branch##
Carries sound information.  
Energetically, symbolizes **listening and resonance**.

##Vestibular Branch##
Monitors head position and movement.  
Energetically, represents **balance and internal harmony**.

###Function###
Supports hearing, equilibrium, and flight balance.  
Energetically, governs **inner harmony and attuned awareness**.

###Common Imbalances###
Dizziness, disorientation, or reduced hearing.  
Energetically, imbalance or difficulty attuning to surroundings.
