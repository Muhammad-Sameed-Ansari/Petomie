#Facial Nerve (VII)#

The **facial nerve** controls muscles of facial expression and carries taste sensations from parts of the tongue and beak.  
Energetically, it represents **emotion, expression, and communication**.

###Parts###

##Motor Branches##
Control facial muscles used in communication and grooming.  
Energetically, symbolize **expression and emotional flow**.

##Sensory Branches##
Carry taste and tactile information.  
Energetically, represent **connection and receptivity**.

###Function###
Supports expression, feeding behaviors, and communication.  
Energetically, governs **emotional flow and expressive balance**.

###Common Imbalances###
Weak expression, reduced grooming behavior, or taste changes.  
Energetically, blocked emotional expression.
