#Vagus Nerve (X)#

The **vagus nerve** is one of the most important nerves in birds, influencing the heart, lungs, and digestive tract.  
Energetically, it represents **calm, regulation, and whole-system harmony**.

###Parts###

##Cardiac Branches##
Influence heartbeat and rhythm.  
Energetically, symbolize **emotional regulation and steady flow**.

##Respiratory Branches##
Support lung and air sac movement.  
Energetically, represent **breath, flow, and expansion**.

##Digestive Branches##
Guide esophagus, crop, and stomach activity.  
Energetically, embody **processing, nourishment, and grounded calm**.

###Function###
Regulates major organ systems and maintains internal balance.  
Energetically, governs **calm, harmony, and emotional regulation**.

###Common Imbalances###
Stress, digestive issues, or irregular heartbeat.
