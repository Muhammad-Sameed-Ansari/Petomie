#Hypoglossal Nerve (XII)#

The **hypoglossal nerve** controls the tongue and is essential for feeding, grooming, and communication.  
Energetically, it represents **expression, nourishment, and articulation**.

###Parts###

##Motor Fibers##
Move the tongue with precision.  
Energetically, symbolize **articulation and expressive clarity**.

###Function###
Guides tongue movement for swallowing, feeding, and vocal actions.  
Energetically, governs **expression, nourishment, and purposeful communication**.

###Common Imbalances###
Tongue weakness, feeding difficulty, or grooming issues.  
Energetically, blocked expression or difficulty articulating needs.
