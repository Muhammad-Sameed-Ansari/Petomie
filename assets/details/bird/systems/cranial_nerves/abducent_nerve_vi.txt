#Abducent Nerve (VI)#

The **abducent nerve** controls the lateral rectus muscle, enabling outward eye movement.  
Birds rely on this for wide field-of-view tracking.  
Energetically, it symbolizes **expansion and outward awareness**.

###Parts###

##Lateral Rectus Muscle##
Moves the eye outward.  
Energetically, represents **opening perspective and widening awareness**.

###Function###
Controls outward gaze and stabilizes vision.  
Energetically, supports **expansion, openness, and visual freedom**.

###Common Imbalances###
Difficulty turning the eye outward or maintaining visual stability.  
Energetically, resistance to widening perspective.
