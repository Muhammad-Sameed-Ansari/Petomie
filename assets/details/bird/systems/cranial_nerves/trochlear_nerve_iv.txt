#Trochlear Nerve (IV)#

The **trochlear nerve** controls the superior oblique muscle, allowing precise downward and rotational eye movement.  
This is essential for depth perception during flight.  
Energetically, it represents **perspective and refined awareness**.

###Parts###

##Superior Oblique Muscle Connection##
Enables controlled rotation and downward gaze.  
Energetically, symbolizes **precision and fine-tuned perception**.

###Function###
Assists with coordinated eye movement and stabilization.  
Energetically, supports **refined perception and adaptability**.

###Common Imbalances###
Difficulty moving the eye downward or maintaining visual stability.  
Energetically, trouble adjusting perspective.
