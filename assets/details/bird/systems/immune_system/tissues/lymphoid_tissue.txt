#Lymphoid Tissue#

##Location##
Distributed throughout the respiratory tract, digestive tract, skin, cloaca, and mucosal surfaces.

###Shape###
Clusters, layers, or scattered aggregates of lymphocytes integrated into mucosal tissues.
