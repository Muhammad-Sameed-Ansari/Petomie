#Spleen#

##Location##
Located in the abdomen near the proventriculus and gizzard.

###Shape###
Small, round to oval organ with a dark reddish color.

###Parts###

##Red Pulp##
Filters blood and removes old cells.  
Energetically, symbolizes **cleansing and renewal**.

##White Pulp##
Produces immune cells and supports defense.  
Energetically, symbolizes **clarity and protection**.

###Function###
Filters blood, stores immune cells, and supports rapid immune responses.  
Energetically, governs **purification, clarity, and resilience**.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Solar Plexus Chakra  
Meridian: Spleen Meridian  
Emotional Meaning: Centering, stability, and inner strength  
Balanced Energy: Strong resilience and emotional clarity

###Common Imbalances###
Enlargement, infection, or stress-related dysfunction.  
Energetically, overwhelm or depletion.

Colors: Yellow and gold.  
Crystals: Citrine, tiger’s eye.  
Element: Earth.  
Affirmation: “I am strong, balanced, and renewed.”
