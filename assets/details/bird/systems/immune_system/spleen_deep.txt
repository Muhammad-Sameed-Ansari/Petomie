#Spleen#

##Location##
Situated near the proventriculus and gizzard in the abdomen.

##Shape##
Small, round to oval dark-red organ.

###Parts###

##White Pulp##
Produces immune cells and filters pathogens.  
Energetically: **clarity, purification, protection**.

##Red Pulp##
Filters blood and removes old cells.  
Energetically: **renewal and grounding**.

###Function###
Supports blood filtration, immune activation, and rapid defense.  
Energetically maintains **centeredness and resilience**.

###Common Imbalances###
Enlargement, infection, or stress-related dysfunction.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Yellow and gold  
Crystals: Citrine, amber  
Element: Earth  
Affirmation: “I remain centered and strong.”
