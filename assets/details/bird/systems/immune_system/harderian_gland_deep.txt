#Harderian Gland#

##Location##
Located behind and around the eye orbit.

##Shape##
Soft, lobed gland.

###Parts###

##Secretory Lobules##
Produce immune-supportive secretions.  
Energetically symbolize **clarity and protective insight**.

###Function###
Contributes to local immunity, especially eye protection and antibody production.  
Energetically supports **vision, intuition, and inner clarity**.

###Applies to##
All bird species, especially developed in chickens and ducks.

###Common Imbalances###
Eye irritation, infection, swelling.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Indigo  
Crystals: Lapis lazuli  
Element: Light  
Affirmation: “I see clearly and remain protected.”
