#Thymus#

##Location##
Paired elongated glands running along both sides of the neck near the trachea, most active in young birds.

###Shape###
Bead-like lobes arranged in a chain.

###Parts###

##Cortex##
Outer layer where immature T-cells begin development.  
Energetically symbolizes **adaptation, boundary formation, and early resilience**.

##Medulla##
Inner region where T-cells mature and specialize.  
Energetically represents **clarity, trust, and refined inner strength**.

##Thymic Lobules##
Segmented clusters supporting staged immune maturation.  
Energetically reflect **guidance, structure, and organized internal growth**.

###Function###
Trains and matures T-lymphocytes, establishes adaptive immunity, and protects chicks during early growth.  
Energetically supports **inner stability, courage, and balanced responsiveness**.
