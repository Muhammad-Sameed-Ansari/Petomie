#Liver (Immune Role)#

##Location##
Large organ located in the central abdomen.

##Shape##
Two large lobes, smooth and dark reddish-brown.

###Parts###

##Kupffer Cells##
Specialized immune cells that filter toxins and old blood cells.  
Energetically: **cleansing, transformation, release**.

###Function###
Supports detoxification, early immune function in chicks, and pathogen filtering.  
Energetically helps **release, renewal, and emotional clearing**.

###Common Imbalances###
Toxin buildup, infections, metabolic issues.
