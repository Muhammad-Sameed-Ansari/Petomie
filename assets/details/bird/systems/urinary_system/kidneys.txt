#Kidneys#

##Location##
The kidneys are located deep within the dorsal body wall on either side of the spine, nestled inside the bony recesses of the synsacrum.

###Shape###
Elongated, lobulated organs divided into cranial, middle, and caudal lobes, forming a compact, dark red structure.

###Parts###

##Nephrons##
Filtering units removing waste.  
Energetically: symbolize **purification and renewal**.

##Tubules##
Reabsorb water and regulate salt balance.  
Energetically: represent **balance and restoration**.

##Ureteral Connections##
Carry waste toward the urodeum.  
Energetically: embody **movement and release**.

###Function###
Filter blood, excrete uric acid, conserve water, and regulate electrolytes.  
Because birds produce uric acid instead of urea, their kidneys excel at water preservation.  
Energetically, they govern **cleansing, vitality, stability, and deep energetic filtration**.
