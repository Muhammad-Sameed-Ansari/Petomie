#Ureters#

##Location##
The ureters run from each kidney to the urodeum of the cloaca, traveling along the dorsal abdominal cavity.

###Shape###
Narrow, muscular tubes with smooth mucosal lining, allowing steady flow of uric acid paste.

###Parts###

##Upper Ureter##
Collects filtrate from kidney lobes.  
Energetically: symbolizes **gathering and initiation**.

##Middle Ureter##
Transports concentrated uric acid.  
Energetically: represents **movement and transformation**.

##Lower Ureter##
Delivers waste to the urodeum.  
Energetically: embodies **release and direction**.

###Function###
Carry uric acid from the kidneys to the cloaca without storing urine.  
Support water conservation and efficient excretion.  
Energetically, govern **flow, release, and clearing of stagnant energy**.
