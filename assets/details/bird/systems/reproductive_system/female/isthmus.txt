#Isthmus#

##Location##
The isthmus sits between the magnum and the shell gland (uterus), serving as the connective narrow region of the oviduct.

###Shape###
A slim, tubular section with firm walls that form key membranes around the developing egg.

###Parts###

##Inner Membrane Zone##
Forms the inner eggshell membrane.  
Energetically: symbolizes **structure and boundary**.

##Outer Membrane Zone##
Forms the outer eggshell membrane.  
Energetically: represents **protection and stability**.

###Function###
Creates the inner and outer shell membranes that support shell formation and protect the embryo.  
Energetically, governs **boundary-setting and foundational integrity**.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Solar Plexus  
Meridian: Spleen  
Emotional Meaning: Boundaries, stability, inner strength  
Balanced Energy: Strong structural integrity

###Common Imbalances###
Thin membranes, poor egg quality  
Energetically, weak boundaries or instability
