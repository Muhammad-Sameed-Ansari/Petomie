#Isthmus#

##Location##
Short region between the **magnum** and **shell gland (uterus)**.  
Lies midway along the oviduct.

###Shape###
Narrow, muscular tube about 7â€“10 cm long with fewer glands than the magnum.

##Parts##
###1. Glandular Epithelium###
Secretes keratin-based fibers.  
**Function:** Produces inner and outer shell membranes.

###2. Smooth Muscle Layer###
Performs rhythmic contractions.  
**Function:** Moves egg forward.

###3. Connective Framework###
Supports vessels and glands.  
**Function:** Maintains shape and function.

##Function##
- **Membrane Formation:** Adds two shell membranes around albumen.  
- **Shape Definition:** Begins giving egg its final form.  
- **Barrier Protection:** Prevents bacterial invasion.  
- **Transition Zone:** Prepares egg for shell deposition in uterus.
