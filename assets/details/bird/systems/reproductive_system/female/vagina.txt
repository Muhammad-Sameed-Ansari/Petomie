#Vagina#

##Location##
Terminal section of the **oviduct**, leading into the **cloaca**.  
Connects the uterus to the urodeum portion of the cloaca.

###Shape###
Short, muscular tube with a folded mucosal lining.  
Lacks glands but has strong muscles for egg expulsion.

##Parts##
###1. Circular and Longitudinal Muscles###
Provide peristaltic contractions.  
**Function:** Propel egg into cloaca.

###2. Mucosal Folds###
Highly folded inner lining.  
**Function:** Allows expansion during egg passage.

###3. Cloacal Opening###
Terminal exit point for eggs.  
**Function:** Releases egg to external environment.

##Function##
- **Egg Expulsion:** Contracts to push egg out.  
- **Sperm Passage:** Receives sperm during copulation.  
- **Protection:** Prevents contamination between digestive and reproductive tracts.  
- **Transition:** Serves as final conduit before cloaca.
