#Shell Gland (Uterus)#

##Location##
Situated after the isthmus within the oviduct.

###Shape###
A muscular, spacious chamber with strong secretory capacity.

###Parts###

##Calcium-Secreting Glands##
Produce the hard eggshell.  
Energetically: symbolize **strength and external protection**.

##Pigment Glands##
Add coloration and patterns.  
Energetically: represent **individuality and expression**.

###Function###
Deposits calcium carbonate to form the eggshell and adds pigmentation.  
Energetically, governs **protection, identity, and outer strength**.
