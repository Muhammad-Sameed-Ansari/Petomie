#Cloacal Region (Female)#

##Location##
Located at the terminal end of the reproductive, digestive, and urinary tracts, positioned just beneath the tail.  
It functions as the shared passage for egg-laying, waste elimination, and reproductive processes.

###Shape###
A muscular, expandable chamber divided into three internal compartments with smooth mucosal lining.

###Parts###

##Coprodeum##
Receives digestive waste.  
Energetically: symbolizes **release, clearing, and letting go**.

##Urodeum##
Receives reproductive and urinary outputs.  
Energetically: represents **transition, purification, and flow**.

##Proctodeum##
Final shared chamber before the vent.  
Energetically: embodies **integration and completion**.

###Function###
Coordinates egg passage, manages reproductive secretions, and regulates elimination.  
Energetically, governs **release, flow, and emotional clearing**.
