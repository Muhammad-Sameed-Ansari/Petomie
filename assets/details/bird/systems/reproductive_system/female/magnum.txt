#Magnum#

##Location##
Located after the infundibulum, forming the longest section of the oviduct.

###Shape###
A thick, coiled tube with secretory glands lining its walls.

###Parts###

##Albumen Glands##
Secrete egg white layers.  
Energetically: symbolize **nourishment and protection**.

##Ciliated Epithelium##
Moves the developing egg forward.  
Energetically: represents **flow and guidance**.

###Function###
Produces the albumen (egg white), supplying protection and nutrition.  
Energetically, governs **nourishment, support, and emotional buffering**.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Heart & Sacral  
Meridian: Spleen  
Emotional Meaning: Care, nurturing, and emotional softness  
Balanced Energy: Strong protection and gentle emotional cushioning

###Common Imbalances###
Watery albumen, reduced secretion  
Energetically, lack of support or emotional depletion

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Soft green  
Crystals: Rose quartz  
Element: Water  
Affirmation: “I nurture and support with gentle strength.”
