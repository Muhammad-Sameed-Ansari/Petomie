#Infundibulum#

##Location##
The infundibulum is the first segment of the oviduct, located near the ovary on the left side of the bird’s body.

###Shape###
A funnel-shaped, delicate, fringed structure designed to capture the ovulated yolk.

###Parts###

##Fimbriae##
Finger-like projections that guide the yolk.  
Energetically: symbolize **guidance and receptivity**.

##Upper Funnel##
Receives the yolk.  
Energetically: represents **invitation and beginning**.

###Function###
Captures the ovulated yolk and initiates fertilization if sperm is present.  
Energetically, governs **receptivity, beginnings, and openness**.
