#Oviduct#

##Location##
Extends from the ovary to the cloaca on the left side of the bird’s body, composed of multiple specialized regions.

###Shape###
A long, tubular structure made of muscular, glandular tissue and ciliated lining.

###Parts###

##Infundibulum##
Captures the yolk.  
Energetically: receptivity.

##Magnum##
Forms albumen.  
Energetically: nourishment.

##Isthmus##
Forms shell membranes.  
Energetically: boundaries.

##Shell Gland (Uterus)##
Forms the shell.  
Energetically: protection.

##Vagina##
Expels the egg.  
Energetically: release and strength.

###Function###
Transports the egg, forms albumen, membranes, and the shell, and supports the fertilization process.  
Energetically, governs **creation, transformation, and cyclical flow**.
