#Ovary#

##Location##
The female bird typically has a single functional ovary on the left side of the body, connected to the infundibulum.

###Shape###
A cluster of follicles resembling a bunch of grapes, containing yolks at various stages of development.

###Parts###

##Developing Follicles##
Contain yolks at multiple stages.  
Energetically: symbolize **growth, potential, and creation**.

##Mature Follicle##
Ready for ovulation.  
Energetically: represents **expression and readiness**.

##Stroma##
Supportive ovarian tissue.  
Energetically: embodies **foundation and inner vitality**.

###Function###
Produces yolks, releases them during ovulation, and regulates reproductive hormones.  
Energetically, governs **creation, fertility, and inner spark**.
