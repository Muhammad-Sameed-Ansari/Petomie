#Cloaca (Male)#

##Location##
Located at the terminal end of the digestive, urinary, and reproductive tracts, beneath the tail.  
It serves as the shared exit chamber for semen, urine, and feces.

###Shape###
A muscular, tri-chambered structure capable of expanding and contracting during reproduction and elimination.

###Parts###

##Coprodeum##
Receives digestive waste.  
Energetically: symbolizes **release and clearing**.

##Urodeum##
Receives urine and semen.  
Energetically: represents **transition and flow**.

##Proctodeum##
Final chamber before the vent.  
Energetically: embodies **integration and completion**.

###Function###
Coordinates elimination and reproductive output, including semen transfer in species without a phallus.  
Energetically, governs **release, grounding, and energetic clearing**.

Balanced Energy: Smooth, stable flow
