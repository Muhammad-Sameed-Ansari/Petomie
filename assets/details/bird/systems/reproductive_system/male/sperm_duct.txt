#Sperm Duct (Vas Deferens)#

##Location##
Extends from the epididymis to the cloaca, running along the kidneys on each side.

###Shape###
A long, thin, white tubular structure that becomes thicker near its terminal end.

###Parts###

##Proximal Section##
Transports newly matured sperm.  
Energetically: symbolizes **movement and transition**.

##Middle Section##
Conveys sperm with rhythmic contractions.  
Energetically: represents **flow and alignment**.

##Terminal Swelling##
Connects to the seminal glomus.  
Energetically: embodies **release readiness**.

###Function###
Moves sperm from the epididymis to the cloaca and prepares it for transfer.  
Energetically, governs **flow, progression, and purposeful movement**.
