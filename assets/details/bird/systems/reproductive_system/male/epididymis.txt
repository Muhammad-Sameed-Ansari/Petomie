#Epididymis#

##Location##
Lies adjacent to each testis, forming a small elongated mass along its surface.

###Shape###
A narrow, folded, tubular structure that appears as a compact ridge.

###Parts###

##Head Region##
Receives sperm from the testis.  
Energetically: symbolizes **beginning and refinement**.

##Body Region##
Supports sperm maturation.  
Energetically: represents **growth and alignment**.

##Tail Region##
Stores mature sperm.  
Energetically: embodies **readiness and potential**.

###Function###
Matures and stores sperm before transport into the sperm duct.  
Energetically, governs **development, refinement, and preparation**.
