#Seminal Glomus#

##Location##
Located near the vas deferens (sperm duct), forming the primary sperm storage area in male birds.

###Shape###
An enlarged, coiled, gland-like swelling along the duct.

###Parts###

##Storage Chamber##
Holds concentrated sperm.  
Energetically: symbolizes **conservation, potential, and readiness**.

##Secretory Lining##
Maintains sperm viability.  
Energetically: represents **support and preservation**.

###Function###
Stores and concentrates sperm prior to ejaculation.  
Energetically, governs **potential, timing, and internal strength**.
