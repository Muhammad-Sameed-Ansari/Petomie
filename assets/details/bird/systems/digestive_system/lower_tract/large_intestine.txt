#Large Intestine (Colon and Rectum)#

##Location##
Short section following the **ceca**, extending to the **cloaca**.

###Shape###
Straight, tubular structure with smooth walls and limited villi.  
Relatively short compared to mammals.

##Parts##
###1. Colon###
Anterior portion connected to ceca.  
**Function:** Absorbs water and electrolytes.

###2. Rectum###
Terminal section leading to cloaca.  
**Function:** Stores and compacts waste temporarily.

##Function##
- **Water Recovery:** Absorbs remaining moisture.  
- **Electrolyte Balance:** Maintains internal stability.  
- **Waste Transport:** Moves feces to cloaca for expulsion.
