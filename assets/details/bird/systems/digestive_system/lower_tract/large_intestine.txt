#Large Intestine#

##Location##
The large intestine lies between the ileum and the cloaca.

###Shape###
A relatively short, wide tube.

###Parts###

##Colon##
The primary segment absorbing water.  
Energetically, symbolizes **release and grounding**.

###Function###
Absorbs water and electrolytes and forms waste.  
Energetically, governs **letting go, grounding, and emotional release**.
