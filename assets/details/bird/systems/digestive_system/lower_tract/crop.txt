#Crop#

##Location##
The crop is located at the base of the neck, just before the esophagus enters the thoracic cavity.

###Shape###
A thin-walled muscular pouch that expands to store food.

###Parts###

##Crop Sac##
The main storage chamber for food.  
Energetically, symbolizes **patience, preparation, and trust**.

###Function###
Stores food, softens it, and allows gradual passage to the stomach.  
Energetically, governs **timing, nourishment, and allowing things to unfold**.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Solar Plexus  
Meridian: Spleen and Stomach Meridians  
Emotional Meaning: Patience, trust in timing  
Balanced Energy: Feeling adequately nourished and supported

###Common Imbalances###
Impaction, sour crop, or slowed emptying.  
Energetically, impatience or difficulty trusting the process.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Yellow and warm gold.  
Crystals: Citrine and yellow calcite.  
Element: Fire supports transformation.  
Affirmation: “I trust the timing of nourishment and life.”
