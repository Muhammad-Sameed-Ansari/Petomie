#Ceca#

##Location##
The ceca are two pouch-like structures located at the junction of the small intestine and large intestine.  
They vary in size across species and play a larger role in herbivorous and omnivorous birds.

###Shape###
Elongated, tubular sacs that range from small bumps to long, prominent structures.

###Parts###

##Right Cecum##
Often slightly longer, involved in fermentation and water absorption.  
Energetically, symbolizes **processing, release, and integration**.

##Left Cecum##
Works with the right cecum to break down plant fibers.  
Energetically, represents **balance, duality, and paired harmony**.

###Function###
Aids fermentation, absorbs water, and processes fibrous foods.  
Energetically, governs **letting go, processing the old, and integrating nourishment**.
