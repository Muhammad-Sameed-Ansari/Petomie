#Proventriculus (Glandular Stomach)#

##Location##
Lies between the **esophagus/crop** and the **gizzard (ventriculus)**, in the midline of the thoracic cavity.

###Shape###
Tubular or spindle-shaped organ with **thick glandular walls**.  
Contains numerous glands secreting hydrochloric acid and digestive enzymes.

##Parts##
###1. Mucosal Glands###
Secrete pepsin and hydrochloric acid.  
**Function:** Begin chemical digestion of proteins.

###2. Lumen###
Central cavity where food mixes with digestive fluids.  
**Function:** Prepares material for mechanical breakdown in the gizzard.

###3. Junction to Gizzard###
Narrow passage connecting to muscular stomach.  
**Function:** Controls food flow and pressure.

##Function##
- **Secretion:** Produces enzymes and acid for digestion.  
- **Digestion:** Begins breakdown of proteins and fats.  
- **Regulation:** Coordinates with gizzard for food passage.  
- **Lubrication:** Keeps food moist for grinding.
