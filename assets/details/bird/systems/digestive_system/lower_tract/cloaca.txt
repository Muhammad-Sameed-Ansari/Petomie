#Cloaca#

##Location##
Posterior chamber of the digestive tract, opening to the **vent (anus)**.  
Also receives outputs from the urinary and reproductive systems.

###Shape###
A three-part cavity with muscular walls and mucous lining.  
Opens externally through the vent.

##Parts##
###1. Coprodeum###
Upper section receiving fecal matter from the intestine.  
**Function:** Stores digestive waste.

###2. Urodeum###
Middle compartment where urinary and reproductive tracts enter.  
**Function:** Receives urine and gametes.

###3. Proctodeum###
Distal chamber before the vent.  
**Function:** Mixes waste and controls expulsion.

##Function##
- **Excretion:** Common outlet for digestive, urinary, and reproductive wastes.  
- **Storage:** Temporarily holds material before elimination.  
- **Coordination:** Regulates timing of waste release.  
- **Hygiene:** Maintains closure to prevent contamination.
