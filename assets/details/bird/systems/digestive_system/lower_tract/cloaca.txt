#Cloaca#

##Location##
The cloaca lies at the end of the digestive tract, serving as a shared opening for the digestive, urinary, and reproductive systems.

###Shape###
A muscular chamber divided into three functional regions.

###Parts###

##Coprodeum##
Receives fecal matter from the intestine.  
Energetically, symbolizes **completion and release**.

##Urodeum##
Connects urinary and reproductive outputs.  
Energetically, represents **transition and purification**.

##Proctodeum##
The final chamber leading to the vent.  
Energetically, embodies **cleansing and outward expression**.

###Function###
Eliminates waste, manages reproductive output, and supports water balance.  
Energetically, governs **release, cleansing, and completion of cycles**.
