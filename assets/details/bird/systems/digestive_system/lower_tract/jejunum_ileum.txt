#Jejunum & Ileum#

##Location##
These sections form the majority of the small intestine, extending between the duodenum and ceca.

###Shape###
Long, coiled tubes adapted for maximum absorption.

###Parts###

##Jejunum##
Mid-intestine absorbing nutrients.  
Energetically, symbolizes **integration and nourishment**.

##Ileum##
End portion connecting to the ceca.  
Energetically, represents **completion and assimilation**.

###Function###
Absorbs nutrients, water, and minerals.  
Energetically, governs **receiving, integrating, and sustaining energy**.
