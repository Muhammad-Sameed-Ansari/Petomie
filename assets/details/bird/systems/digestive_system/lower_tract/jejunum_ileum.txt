#Jejunum and Ileum#

##Location##
Extends from the **duodenum** to the **ceca**.  
Suspended by the mesentery in the middle abdomen.

###Shape###
Long, coiled sections of small intestine with numerous villi and microvilli.  
Folded mucosa increases surface area for nutrient absorption.

##Parts##
###1. Jejunum###
Proximal portion with dense vascularization.  
**Function:** Absorbs carbohydrates, proteins, and fats.

###2. Ileum###
Distal portion leading to the ceca.  
**Function:** Absorbs remaining nutrients and bile salts.

##Function##
- **Absorption:** Main site of nutrient uptake.  
- **Transport:** Moves digested material to large intestine.  
- **Support:** Provides surface area for efficient digestion.  
- **Integration:** Links upper and lower digestive systems.
