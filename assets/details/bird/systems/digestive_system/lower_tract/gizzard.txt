#Gizzard#

##Location##
The gizzard lies behind the proventriculus as the muscular second stomach of the bird.

###Shape###
A round, muscular organ with thick walls for grinding.

###Parts###

##Muscular Walls##
Powerful layers that contract to grind food.  
Energetically, symbolize **strength, processing, and determination**.

##Grit Chamber##
Contains stones used to break down tough foods.  
Energetically, represents **support, structure, and resilience**.

###Function###
Grinds food mechanically, breaking down seeds and fibers.  
Energetically, governs **inner strength, transformation, and resilience**.
