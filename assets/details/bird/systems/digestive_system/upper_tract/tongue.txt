#Tongue#

##Location##
The tongue is located on the floor of the oral cavity, attached to the hyoid apparatus.

###Shape###
Varies by species—from long and slender to short and broad.

###Parts###

##Tip##
Used for grasping and manipulating food.  
Energetically, symbolizes **precision and expression**.

##Body##
Main muscular portion.  
Energetically, represents **communication and nourishment**.

##Root##
Anchored to the hyoid bones.  
Energetically, embodies **stability and grounded expression**.

###Function###
Guides food, aids swallowing, assists in grooming, and contributes to vocalization.  
Energetically, governs **expressive clarity, nourishment, and articulation**.
