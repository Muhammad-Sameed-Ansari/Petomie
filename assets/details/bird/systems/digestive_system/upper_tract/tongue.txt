#Tongue#

##Location##
Lies in the **floor of the oral cavity**, attached to the hyoid apparatus.  
Extends toward the beak tip.

###Shape###
Highly variable; may be **short and triangular** (chickens) or **long and slender** (hummingbirds).  
Usually covered with **keratinized papillae** and may include cartilaginous rods for stiffness.

##Parts##
###1. Apex###
Tip of the tongue.  
**Function:** Collects and manipulates food.

###2. Body###
Central muscular portion.  
**Function:** Aids swallowing and movement.

###3. Root###
Posterior section near pharynx.  
**Function:** Pushes food toward esophagus.

##Function##
- **Feeding:** Moves and positions food for swallowing.  
- **Taste:** Contains taste buds in limited species.  
- **Manipulation:** Assists preening or drinking.  
- **Species Adaptation:** Shape varies with diet and feeding style.
