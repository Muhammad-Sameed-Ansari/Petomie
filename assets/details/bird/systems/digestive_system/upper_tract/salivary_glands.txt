#Salivary Glands#

##Location##
Scattered throughout the oral cavity, especially near the tongue and palate.

###Shape###
Clusters of small glandular tissues.

###Parts###

##Lingual Glands##
Located near the tongue.  
Energetically, symbolize **moisture, ease, and flow**.

##Palatine Glands##
Near the roof of the mouth.  
Energetically, represent **lubrication, protection, and gentle support**.

###Function###
Produce saliva to moisten food and assist swallowing.  
Energetically, govern **ease, fluidity, and gentle nourishment**.
