#Pharynx#

##Location##
The pharynx lies behind the oral cavity, connecting the mouth to the esophagus and airway.

###Shape###
A muscular, funnel-shaped chamber.

###Parts###

##Oropharynx##
Food passage area.  
Energetically, symbolizes **transition and flow**.

##Nasopharynx##
Region connected to nasal cavities.  
Energetically, represents **breath, clarity, and openness**.

###Function###
Directs food to the esophagus and air to the respiratory tract.  
Energetically, governs **choice, direction, and coordinated flow**.
