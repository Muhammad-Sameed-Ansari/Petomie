#Pharynx#

##Location##
Located behind the **oral cavity** and in front of the **esophagus**.  
Forms the junction between the mouth, nasal cavity, and upper digestive tract.

###Shape###
Short, muscular passage lined with mucus-secreting cells.  
Lacks a distinct separation from the oral cavity.

##Parts##
###1. Nasopharyngeal Region###
Upper area behind the choana.  
**Function:** Channels air from nasal cavity.

###2. Oropharyngeal Region###
Middle section continuous with the oral cavity.  
**Function:** Guides food toward esophagus.

###3. Laryngopharyngeal Region###
Lower area near glottis opening.  
**Function:** Directs food to digestive tract and air to trachea.

##Function##
- **Swallowing:** Pushes food into the esophagus.  
- **Respiration:** Coordinates airflow to trachea.  
- **Vocalization:** Supports sound resonance.  
- **Protection:** Prevents food entry into airways.
