#Beak#

##Location##
The beak forms the external opening of the bird’s digestive tract, located at the front of the face.  
It is composed of bone covered by keratin and varies greatly among species based on feeding behavior.

###Shape###
A hard, lightweight, tapered structure that may be curved, straight, hooked, or specialized.

###Parts###

##Upper Beak (Maxilla)##
The fixed upper portion.  
Energetically, symbolizes **guidance, perception, and direction**.

##Lower Beak (Mandible)##
The movable lower portion used for grasping and feeding.  
Energetically, represents **action, expression, and purposeful motion**.

##Rhamphotheca##
Keratin covering enclosing both beaks.  
Energetically, embodies **strength, adaptability, and protection**.

###Function###
Used for feeding, grooming, manipulating objects, nest building, and communication.  
Energetically, governs **expression, nourishment, and interaction with the world**.
