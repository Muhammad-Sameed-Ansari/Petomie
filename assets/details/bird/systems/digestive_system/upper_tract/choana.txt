#Choana#

##Location##
The choana is an internal slit-like opening on the roof of the bird’s oral cavity.  
It connects the nasal cavity to the throat.

###Shape###
A long, narrow cleft located along the midline of the palate.

###Parts###

##Palatal Slit##
Main central opening.  
Energetically, symbolizes **connection, flow, and internal passage**.

###Function###
Allows air movement between the nasal cavity and respiratory system.  
Energetically, governs **clarity, breath flow, and openness**.
