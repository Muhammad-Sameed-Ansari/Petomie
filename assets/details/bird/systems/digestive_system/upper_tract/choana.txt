#Choana#

##Location##
Located in the **midline of the palate** within the roof of the oral cavity.  
Connects the nasal cavity to the oral cavity.

###Shape###
A **slit-like median opening** surrounded by raised ridges of the palate.  
May vary in size and position among species.

##Parts##
###1. Anterior Edge###
Near the front palate.  
**Function:** Connects nasal passage to mouth.

###2. Posterior Edge###
Leads toward the pharynx.  
**Function:** Guides airflow to trachea.

##Function##
- **Respiration:** Allows breathing when mouth is closed.  
- **Filtering:** Aids in expelling dust and debris.  
- **Coordination:** Links nasal and oral airflow for vocalization and feeding.
