#Oral Cavity#

##Location##
The oral cavity lies at the entrance of the digestive system, extending from the beak to the pharynx.

###Shape###
A spacious chamber lined with mucosa, adapted to the bird’s diet and feeding style.

###Parts###

##Palate##
The roof of the oral cavity.  
Energetically, symbolizes **support and inner structure**.

##Floor of Mouth##
Area beneath the tongue.  
Energetically, represents **receptivity and grounding**.

##Mucosal Lining##
Soft tissues that aid lubrication.  
Energetically, embody **sensitivity and flow**.

###Function###
Initiates feeding, manipulates food, and guides it to the esophagus.  
Energetically, governs **intake, receptivity, and early nourishment**.
