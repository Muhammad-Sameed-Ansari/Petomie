#Oral Cavity#

##Location##
The internal chamber between the **beak and pharynx**, forming the upper section of the digestive tract.

###Shape###
Triangular space bounded by the **beak, tongue, and palate**.  
Lined with mucous membrane and containing several small glands.

##Parts##
###1. Roof of Mouth###
Formed by the hard palate with the **choana** in the center.  
**Function:** Separates oral and nasal passages.

###2. Floor of Mouth###
Contains tongue and openings of salivary glands.  
**Function:** Supports food manipulation and swallowing.

###3. Buccal Cavity###
Lateral walls of mouth.  
**Function:** Directs food toward the pharynx.

##Function##
- **Food Intake:** Receives and positions food.  
- **Lubrication:** Moistens material with saliva.  
- **Breathing Passage:** Allows airflow via choana.  
- **Swallowing Preparation:** Directs food toward esophagus.
