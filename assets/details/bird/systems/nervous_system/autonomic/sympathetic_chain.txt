#Sympathetic Chain#

##Location##
Runs along both sides of the vertebral column from the neck to the pelvis, forming a chain of interconnected ganglia.

###Shape###
Bead-like clusters of ganglia connected by nerve fibers, appearing as a long segmented cord.

###Parts###

##Cervical Ganglia##
Regulate head, neck, and eye responses.  
Energetically, symbolize **awareness and alertness**.

##Thoracic Ganglia##
Influence heart and lung activity.  
Energetically, represent **courage, breath, and internal strength**.

##Lumbar & Sacral Ganglia##
Control hindlimb vessels and cloacal function.  
Energetically, embody **grounding and survival energy**.

###Function###
Prepares the bird for action by increasing heart rate, redirecting blood flow, and activating alertness.  
Energetically, governs **protection, readiness, and strength**.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Solar Plexus & Root  
Meridian: Gallbladder  
Emotional Meaning: Strength, drive, and assertiveness  
Balanced Energy: Confident reactions and strong boundaries

###Common Imbalances###
Overactivation, tension, rapid heartbeat  
Energetically, stress, fear, or hypervigilance
