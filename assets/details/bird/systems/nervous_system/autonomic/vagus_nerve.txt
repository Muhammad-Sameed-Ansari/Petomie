#Vagus Nerve#

##Location##
Originates in the brainstem and travels down the neck into the heart, lungs, digestive tract, and internal organs.

###Shape###
A long, branching nerve with widespread parasympathetic influence.

###Parts###

##Cervical Branches##
Supply throat and esophageal function.  
Energetically, symbolize **expression and release**.

##Cardiac Branches##
Slow the heart and regulate rhythm.  
Energetically, represent **calmness and emotional rhythm**.

##Gastrointestinal Branches##
Control digestive motility and secretion.  
Energetically, embody **nourishment, intuition, and emotional digestion**.

###Function###
Promotes rest, digestion, calmness, and internal repair.  
Energetically, governs **soothing, peace, and emotional grounding**.
