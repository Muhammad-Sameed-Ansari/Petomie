#Peripheral Ganglia#

##Location##
Distributed along major nerves and close to visceral organs, forming clusters of nerve cell bodies outside the brain and spinal cord.

###Shape###
Small, rounded clusters appearing like beads along nerves.

###Parts###

##Sensory Ganglia##
Relay sensory information.  
Energetically: awareness, interpretation.

##Autonomic Ganglia##
Sympathetic and parasympathetic relay points.  
Energetically: balance and regulation.

##Visceral Ganglia##
Located near digestive and reproductive organs.  
Energetically: intuition and emotional integration.

###Function###
Relay signals, regulate autonomic responses, support sensory feedback, and coordinate visceral activity.  
Energetically, govern responsiveness, adaptation, and internal communication.
