#Sensory Receptors#

##Location##
Found throughout the skin, feathers, beak, eyes, ears, joints, and internal organs—allowing birds to sense their environment with extreme precision.

###Shape###
Microscopic structures tuned to specific stimuli such as pressure, vibration, temperature, chemicals, and light.

##Chemoreceptors##

##Photoreceptors##
Rods and cones in the retina.  
Energetically: insight, clarity, perception.

##Proprioceptors##
Sense body position.  
Energetically: stability, coordination, presence.
