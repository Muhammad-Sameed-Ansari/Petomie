#Spinal Nerves#

##Location##
Emerge segmentally from the spinal cord along the entire vertebral column, branching outward into the wings, trunk, and hind limbs.

###Shape###
Paired nerves forming symmetrical rows, each splitting into dorsal and ventral branches.

###Parts###

##Dorsal Roots##
Carry sensory information inward.  
Energetically: awareness and perception.

##Ventral Roots##
Carry motor signals outward.  
Energetically: action and expression.

##Plexus Branches##
Form cervical, brachial, and lumbosacral plexuses.  
Energetically: integration and coordination.

###Function###
Transmit sensory and motor signals, coordinate limb movement, and support reflex actions.  
Energetically, govern flow, communication, and structural balance.
