#Brain Meninges#

##Location##
Three protective layers surrounding the bird's brain and spinal cord within the skull and vertebral canal.

###Shape###
Thin, layered membranes forming smooth, flexible coverings.

###Parts###

##Dura Mater##
Outer tough layer.  
Energetically, symbolizes protection and boundaries.

##Arachnoid Layer##
Middle web-like membrane.  
Energetically, represents connection and integration.

##Pia Mater##
Inner delicate layer hugging the brain.  
Energetically, embodies intimacy, sensitivity, and deep awareness.

###Function###
Protect the brain, cushion against injury, circulate cerebrospinal fluid, and maintain internal stability.  
Energetically, govern containment, safety, and emotional buffering.
