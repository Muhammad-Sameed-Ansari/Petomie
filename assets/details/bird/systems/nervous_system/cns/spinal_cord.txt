#Spinal Cord#

##Location##
Runs through the vertebral column from the base of the brain to the tail, encased in protective bone.

###Shape###
A long, slender nerve column with segmental spinal nerves branching outward.

###Parts###

##Cervical Region##
Controls neck and wing sensation.  
Energetically, symbolizes communication and freedom.

##Thoracic Region##
Connects with wing and trunk muscles.  
Energetically, represents strength and expansion.

##Lumbar Region##
Supplies hindlimbs and lower body.  
Energetically, embodies grounding and stability.

###Function###
Transmits nerve signals between brain and body, regulates reflexes, and coordinates movement.  
Energetically, governs alignment, energy flow, and structural integrity.
