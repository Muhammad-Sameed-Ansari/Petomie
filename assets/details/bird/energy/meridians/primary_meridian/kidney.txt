#Kidney Meridian#

The kidney meridian anchors the bird’s essence and vitality.  
It supports bones, hearing, and endurance.  
Energetically, it represents **willpower, courage, and regeneration**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins near the inner claws, ascends along the legs, abdomen, and spine, connecting to the kidneys and bladder.  
Its upward flow nourishes energy reserves.

###Function###  
Physically, it governs bone strength, fluid balance, and reproductive health.  
It stabilizes temperature and supports long-term stamina.  
Energetically, it strengthens **resilience, courage, and calm focus**, grounding the bird’s spirit for sustainable vitality.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Fatigue or cold extremities.  
- Weak flight endurance.  
- Emotional signs: fear or insecurity.  
- Energetic blockage: depletion or loss of drive.
