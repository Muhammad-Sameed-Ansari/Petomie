#Lung Meridian#

The lung meridian governs breath, vitality, and purity of energy in birds.  
It links the rhythm of flight and respiration with emotional clarity.  
Energetically, it represents **inspiration, release, and renewal**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins near the chest, flows through the wings and down the inside of the legs, ending near the inner claws.  
Its descending flow balances air exchange and rhythm between flight and rest.

###Function###  
Physically, it regulates breathing, skin moisture, and oxygenation through the air sacs.  
It supports feather health and immune strength.  
Energetically, it fosters **freedom, trust, and expression**, helping the bird embody serenity and flow.  
It aligns the breath of life with emotional balance.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Shallow breathing or wheezing.  
- Feather dullness or weak immunity.  
- Emotional signs: grief, fatigue, or fear of confinement.  
- Energetic blockage: difficulty letting go or receiving peace.
