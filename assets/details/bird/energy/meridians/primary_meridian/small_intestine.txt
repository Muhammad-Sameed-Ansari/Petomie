#Small Intestine Meridian#

The small intestine meridian separates nourishment from waste.  
It governs clarity in both digestion and emotion.  
Energetically, it represents **discernment, clarity, and trust**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins near the outer claws, flows up the outer wings and neck, and ends near the ear openings.  
Its ascending flow refines physical and sensory awareness.

###Function###  
Physically, it assists digestion, absorption, and temperature balance.  
It supports clear vision and awareness.  
Energetically, it enhances **focus, understanding, and calm response**, helping the bird act with precision and grace.  
It separates truth from confusion.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Indigestion or imbalance in crop function.  
- Wing or neck stiffness.  
- Emotional signs: confusion or overreaction.  
- Energetic blockage: unclear direction or sensory overload.
