#Pericardium Meridian#

The pericardium meridian protects the heart and moderates emotional flow.  
It maintains balance between excitement and peace.  
Energetically, it represents **compassion, protection, and calm connection**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins in the chest, runs through the wings, and ends at the middle claws.  
Its gentle flow balances activity with emotional stability.

###Function###  
Physically, it regulates circulation, recovery, and cardiac resilience.  
It helps the bird calm after exertion or stress.  
Energetically, it sustains **empathy and gentle awareness**, allowing open connection without overwhelm.  
It softens emotional boundaries while maintaining strength.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Heart strain or overheating.  
- Overexcitement or irritability.  
- Emotional signs: clinginess or withdrawal.  
- Energetic blockage: emotional tension or guardedness.
