#Stomach Meridian#

The stomach meridian governs digestion, grounding, and nourishment.  
It transforms food into vital energy for flight and song.  
Energetically, it represents **stability, satisfaction, and calm focus**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins near the beak, flows along the neck, chest, and abdomen, and descends to the outer claws.  
Its downward flow stabilizes digestion and emotional presence.

###Function###  
Physically, it regulates appetite, digestion, and absorption of nutrients.  
It supports muscle tone and strength for flight.

- Weak flight muscles or fatigue.  
- Emotional signs: nervousness or unease.  
- Energetic blockage: worry or tension in the abdomen.
