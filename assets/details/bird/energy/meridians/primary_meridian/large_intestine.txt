#Large Intestine Meridian#

The large intestine meridian governs elimination and clarity.  
It works with the lungs to maintain internal purity and lightness.  
Energetically, it represents **release, renewal, and emotional clarity**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins near the outer claws, travels along the wings, shoulders, and ends near the beak.  
Its ascending path clears physical and emotional waste.

###Function###  
Physically, it regulates hydration, digestion, and elimination of toxins.  
It supports skin and feather detoxification.  
Energetically, it promotes **lightness and renewal**, encouraging emotional cleansing and openness to change.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Constipation, loose droppings, or skin irritation.  
- Dull feathers or blocked energy in the wings.  
- Emotional signs: irritability or stagnation.  
- Energetic blockage: difficulty releasing tension or fear.
