#Bladder Meridian#

The bladder meridian governs energy flow along the spine and wings.  
It connects the nervous system with posture and stability.  
Energetically, it represents **release, flow, and renewal**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins near the inner eye, runs along the head, spine, wings, and down to the outer claws.  
Its dual flow on both sides balances coordination and energy rhythm.

###Function###  
Physically, it supports muscle tone, elimination, and balance.  
It connects the spinal column and tail with flight control.  
Energetically, it releases **tension and stored fear**, restoring rhythm and ease.  
It harmonizes flight energy with peaceful grounding.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Stiffness, tremors, or poor balance.  
- Nervous agitation or fatigue.  
- Emotional signs: fear or defensiveness.  
- Energetic blockage: tension along spine or wings.
