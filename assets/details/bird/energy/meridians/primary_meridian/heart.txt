#Heart Meridian#

The heart meridian governs circulation, warmth, and emotional expression.  
It harmonizes joy, rhythm, and song.  
Energetically, it represents **love, vitality, and peace**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins in the chest, flows down the wings and inner limbs, and ends at the inner claws.  
Its balanced flow connects feeling with movement.

###Function###  
Physically, it regulates heartbeat, blood flow, and warmth.  
It influences alertness and emotional harmony.  
Energetically, it radiates **joy, empathy, and calm**, guiding peaceful interaction and balanced social connection.  
It harmonizes rest and enthusiasm.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Rapid heartbeat or restlessness.  
- Weakness or fatigue.  
- Emotional signs: agitation, anxiety, or sadness.  
- Energetic blockage: overexcitement or guarded heart.
