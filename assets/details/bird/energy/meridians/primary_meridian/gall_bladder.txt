#Gallbladder Meridian#

The gallbladder meridian governs coordination, timing, and decision-making.  
It refines balance during flight and perching.  
Energetically, it represents **courage, clarity, and movement**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins near the outer eye, flows along the neck, wings, sides, hips, and down the outer legs to the claws.  
Its winding flow coordinates stability and motion.

###Function###  
Physically, it supports joint flexibility, digestion, and nerve timing.  
It ensures smooth transitions between flight and landing.  
Energetically, it encourages **decisive yet graceful action**, allowing confident adaptation to change.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Stiff joints or uneven flight.  
- Poor coordination or hesitation.  
- Emotional signs: frustration or indecision.  
- Energetic blockage: imbalance between rest and motion.
