#Triple Heater Meridian#

The triple heater meridian regulates body temperature and energy coordination.  
It links upper, middle, and lower body zones for harmony.  
Energetically, it represents **adaptability, cooperation, and balance**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins at the outer claws, moves up the wings, shoulders, and neck, and ends near the ear openings.  
Its linking flow connects respiratory, digestive, and reproductive systems.

###Function###  
Physically, it regulates fluid balance, temperature, and metabolism.  
It coordinates breathing with digestion and circulation.  
Energetically, it promotes **flexibility and rhythm**, helping the bird adjust easily between activity and rest.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Heat or cold imbalance.  
- Fatigue or poor coordination.  
- Emotional signs: restlessness or tension.  
- Energetic blockage: disconnection between systems.
