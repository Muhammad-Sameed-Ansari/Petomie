#Du Mai (Governing Vessel)#

The Du Mai, or Governing Vessel, is the central Yang channel that runs along the birdâ€™s back.  
It governs the spine, wings, and coordination of all Yang energy.  
Energetically, it represents **leadership, freedom, and strength**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins near the tail base, travels along the midline of the back, over the head, and ends at the upper beak.  
Its smooth upward flow supports posture, flight balance, and alert awareness.

###Function###  
Physically, it regulates the spine, muscles, and nervous system involved in flight control and balance.  
It integrates the wings and tail into coordinated motion.  
Energetically, it channels **confidence and inner command**, giving the bird grace in motion and stability in air.  
It harmonizes freedom of flight with inner focus.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Stiff wings or difficulty perching.  
- Loss of balance or poor coordination.  
- Emotional signs: nervousness, disorientation, or insecurity.  
- Energetic blockage: loss of focus or grounded stability.
