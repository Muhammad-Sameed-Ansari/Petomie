#Yang Wei Mai (Yang Linking Vessel)#

The Yang Wei Mai connects all Yang meridians, coordinating outer energy and stamina.  
It maintains alertness, balance, and environmental response.  
Energetically, it represents **resilience, coordination, and adaptability**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins at the outer claws, travels up the wings, sides, and neck, linking with the head and back.  
Its connecting flow harmonizes outer movement and awareness.

###Function###  
Physically, it regulates stamina, immune defense, and muscle performance.  
It maintains temperature and balance during long flights.  
Energetically, it sustains **rhythm, strength, and adaptability**, allowing the bird to adjust smoothly to air currents and stress.  
It harmonizes external and internal flow.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Fatigue or reduced endurance.  
- Muscle soreness or poor coordination.  
- Emotional signs: agitation, tension, or sensitivity.  
- Energetic blockage: imbalance between alertness and rest.
