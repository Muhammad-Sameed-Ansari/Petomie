#Yin Wei Mai (Yin Linking Vessel)#

The Yin Wei Mai connects all Yin meridians, supporting emotional calm and inner rhythm.  
It harmonizes heart, lungs, and digestion, promoting emotional depth and peace.  
Energetically, it represents **compassion, serenity, and inner unity**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins in the abdomen, travels up the chest and throat, and connects with the Ren Mai.  
Its vertical flow harmonizes breath, emotion, and rhythm.

###Function###  
Physically, it supports circulation, hormone balance, and emotional calm.  
It integrates internal systems for steady energy.  
Energetically, it cultivates **empathy, stability, and deep awareness**, helping the bird remain peaceful and balanced in social or environmental changes.  
It harmonizes flight instincts with emotional tranquility.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Fatigue or shallow breathing.  
- Emotional imbalance or stress.  
- Emotional signs: sadness, withdrawal, or over-sensitivity.  
- Energetic blockage: emotional overload or loss of serenity.
