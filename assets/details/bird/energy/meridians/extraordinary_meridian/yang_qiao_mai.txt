#Yang Qiao Mai (Yang Motility Vessel)#

The Yang Qiao Mai governs motion, coordination, and outward awareness.  
It supports quick reactions and strength in flight.  
Energetically, it represents **alertness, agility, and confidence**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins near the outer claws, ascends along the legs, over the flanks, shoulders, neck, and ends near the eyes.  
Its ascending, dynamic flow sustains alertness and action.

###Function###  
Physically, it regulates muscle tone, joint flexibility, and wing coordination.  
It maintains balance during landing and turning.  
Energetically, it enhances **clarity, focus, and responsive energy**, keeping the bird calm yet ready to move.  
It maintains balance between vigilance and relaxation.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Muscle stiffness, unsteady flight, or twitching.  
- Over-alertness or poor rest.  
- Emotional signs: nervousness or hyperactivity.  
- Energetic blockage: excess tension or loss of calm focus.
