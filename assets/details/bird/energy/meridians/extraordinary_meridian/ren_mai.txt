#Ren Mai (Conception Vessel)#

The Ren Mai, or Conception Vessel, flows along the underside of the bird’s body, nurturing Yin energy.  
It supports respiration, digestion, and reproduction.  
Energetically, it represents **nurturing, calmness, and receptivity**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins near the cloaca, ascends along the keel bone and chest, and ends beneath the beak.  
Its rising flow unites breathing and digestion, grounding the bird’s inner rhythm.

###Function###  
Physically, it harmonizes the heart, lungs, and abdominal air sacs.  
It balances hydration, hormone flow, and reproduction.  
Energetically, it promotes **emotional comfort, stillness, and self-acceptance**, allowing rest after flight.  
It softens alertness with warmth and trust.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Breathing or digestive issues.  
- Weakness or poor posture at rest.  
- Emotional signs: anxiety, neediness, or stress.  
- Energetic blockage: difficulty relaxing or receiving comfort.
