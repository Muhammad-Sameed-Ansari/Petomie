#Yin Qiao Mai (Yin Motility Vessel)#

The Yin Qiao Mai balances motion with stillness and governs the ability to rest peacefully.  
It helps transition between flight, perching, and sleep.  
Energetically, it represents **grace, harmony, and inner quiet**.

###Shape and Pathway###  
Begins at the inner claws, travels up the inside of the legs and abdomen, and ends near the eyes.  
Its soft upward flow aligns relaxation and balanced awareness.

###Function###  
Physically, it regulates joint movement and rest posture.  
It aids muscle relaxation and recovery after flight.  
Energetically, it restores **peace, reflection, and emotional balance**, helping the bird shift from activity to rest smoothly.  
It harmonizes stillness and vigilance.

###Common Imbalances###  
- Muscle tension or stiffness in legs or wings.  
- Restlessness or poor sleep.  
- Emotional signs: irritability or fatigue.  
- Energetic blockage: inability to calm or transition between states.
