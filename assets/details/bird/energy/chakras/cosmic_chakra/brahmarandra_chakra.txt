#Brahmarandra Chakra#

###Location###
Located at the highest point of the skull, slightly behind the crown chakra.

###Color###
White / Violet / Pale Gold

###Element###
Light / Ether

###Meaning & Function###
This chakra represents spiritual opening and divine connection, enhancing intuition, purity, and elevated awareness in birds.

##Connection##
Crown and intuitive centers
Nervous system
Divine energetic pathways

###Signs of Balance###
Serene presence
Intuitive flock awareness
Calm energy
High perceptive clarity

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Sensitivity to subtle energies
Withdrawn behavior
Startle reactions

##Physical##
Head tension
Neck or poll discomfort
Sleep disruption

###Balancing Activities###
Soft vocal reassurance
Energy work above head (species gentle)
Quiet bonding presence
Serene perch environment
