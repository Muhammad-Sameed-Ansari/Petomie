#Earth Star Chakra#

###Location###
Located 5–15 cm below the bird’s feet, anchoring into the earth or perch surface energetically.

###Color###
Black / Deep Brown / Earth Tones

###Element###
Earth

###Meaning & Function###
The Earth Star Chakra grounds the bird’s energy, supporting safety, flock stability, and calm emotional regulation.

##Connection##
Feet, claws, and lower limb chakras
Environmental grounding
Instinctual awareness

###Signs of Balance###
Calm perching
Stable flock interaction
Relaxed body posture
Confidence in new environments

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Skittish or startled easily
Restlessness or panic reactions
Difficulty settling on perch

##Physical##
Foot tension
Lower-limb fatigue
Energetic instability

###Balancing Activities###
Natural perches
Grounding nearby objects
Calm, stable environment
Earth-toned crystals near aviary (not on bird)
