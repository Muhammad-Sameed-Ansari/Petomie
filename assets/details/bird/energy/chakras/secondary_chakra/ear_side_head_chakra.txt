#Ear-Side Head Chakra#

###Location###
Located behind and slightly below each eye, near the ear opening on both sides of the head.

###Color###
Lavender / Pale Blue

###Element###
Air / Ether

###Meaning & Function###
This chakra governs sensory processing, awareness, alertness, intuition, and energetic orientation.
It helps birds interpret sound, vibration, flock communication, and subtle energy shifts.

##Connection##
Ear openings, skull sides
Upper nervous system
Auditory and intuitive pathways

###Signs of Balance###
Calm alertness
Smooth head movements
Responsive but not reactive
Balanced sensory processing

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Hypervigilance
Sudden fear reactions
Over-sensitivity to noise
Difficulty interpreting flock cues

##Physical##
Head shaking
Ear sensitivity
Tension behind eyes

###Balancing Activities###
Soft vocal reassurance
Quiet environment
Energetic clearing along head sides
Lavender or blue crystals nearby
