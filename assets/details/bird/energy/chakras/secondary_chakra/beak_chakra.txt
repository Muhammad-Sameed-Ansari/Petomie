#Beak Chakra#

##Location##
Tip of the beak, extending slightly inward.

##Colour##
Yellow or turquoise (linked to expression and clarity).

##Function##
Supports communication, vocalization, environmental interaction, and receiving/giving energy.

##Imbalances##
##Physical Signs##
Pecking issues, beak overgrowth or cracking, reluctance to vocalize, or excessive screaming.

##Behavioral Signs##
Aggression, over-preening, biting, or avoidance of interaction.

##Energetic Signs##
Disconnection from flock/companions, difficulty adapting to new environments, or appearing “shut down.”

##Balancing##
Gentle beak massage, sound therapy, color visualization, enrichment toys, and energy work.
