#Wing Chakra#

###Location###
Located at the base of each wing where the humerus meets the body, extending into the shoulder, covert feathers, and flight muscles.

###Color###
Sky Blue / Turquoise / White

###Element###
Air / Ether

###Meaning & Function###
The Wing Chakras govern freedom, expression, movement, and emotional expansion.
They regulate energetic flow through the wings, influence flight confidence, and support emotional release through movement.

##Connection##
Wing joints, shoulder girdle
Flight feathers and covert layers
Freedom, expression, emotional liberation

###Signs of Balance###
Graceful flight
Confidence in takeoff and landing
Relaxed wing posture
Curiosity and social engagement

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Fear of flying or reluctance to move
Wing-clamping behavior
Emotional withdrawal or stress

##Physical##
Wing stiffness
Shoulder tension
Difficulty extending wings fully

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle stretching (species-dependent)
Safe flight opportunities
Calm bonding near wings
Air-element crystals near aviary
