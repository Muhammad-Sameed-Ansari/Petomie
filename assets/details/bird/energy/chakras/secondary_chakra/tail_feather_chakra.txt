#Tail Feather Chakra#

###Location###
Located at the base of the tail (uropygial gland area), extending into the tail feathers and their energetic field.

###Color###
Red / Gold / Deep Brown

###Element###
Earth / Fire

###Meaning & Function###
The Tail Feather Chakra governs direction, balance, flight steering, grounding, and emotional release.
It stabilizes the entire energy system during flight and regulates emotional expression.

##Connection##
Tail feathers, preen gland
Spinal base and grounding channels
Balance, direction, emotional flow

###Signs of Balance###
Smooth flight steering
Relaxed tail posture
Calm preening behavior
Stable body orientation

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Erratic flight patterns
Tail fanning from stress
Difficulty balancing on perches
Agitated preening

##Physical##
Tail feather weakness
Uropygial gland tension
Lower spine stiffness

###Balancing Activities###
Clean, stable perches
Safe preening environment
Energetic grounding near tail
Warm, earthy colors in aviary
