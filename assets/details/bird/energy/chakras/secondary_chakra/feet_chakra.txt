#Feet Chakra#

###Location###
Located in the sole of each foot, extending into toes, claws, and tendon pathways.

###Color###
Earth Brown / Deep Red / Black

###Element###
Earth

###Meaning & Function###
Feet Chakras support stability, grounding, balance, security, and flock orientation.
They regulate how birds connect to perches, land, and other surfaces.

##Connection##
Feet, claws, tendons
Balance centers and grounding channels
Environmental awareness and security

###Signs of Balance###
Calm perching
Stable balance on different surfaces
Relaxed grip strength
Confidence exploring new textures

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Difficulty settling on perches
Restlessness or foot shuffling
Fearfulness or anxiety in new areas

##Physical##
Foot tension
Claw overuse or soreness
Toe stiffness or hesitation to grasp

###Balancing Activities###
Natural wooden perches
Varied textures
Grounding energy work
Earth crystals near aviary
