#Heart Chakra (Anahata)#

###Location###
Located in the center of the chest, near the sternum and extending through the wings.

###Color###
Green

###Element###
Air

###Meaning & Function###
The Heart Chakra governs emotional bonding, affection, flock harmony, and gentle communication.
It supports trust with humans and other birds, emotional warmth, and a sense of belonging.
This chakra enhances compassion, soft vocalization, and balanced interaction.

##Connection##
Heart and lungs
Respiratory and circulatory systems
Chest, sternum, and wing bases
Emotional bonding and flock connection

###Signs of Balance###
Gentle, affectionate behavior
Calm vocalization and social engagement
Relaxed breathing and strong vitality
Comfort within flock or with trusted humans

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Difficulty bonding or accepting affection
Nervousness or emotional withdrawal
Over-attachment or excessive calling
Irritability around chest handling

##Physical##
Breathing irregularities
Heart or circulatory stress
Chest or wing-base tension
Low energy or lethargy

###Balancing Activities###
Soft vocal reassurance
Gentle presence and bonding time
Calm, loving flock environment
Energy work near the chest (species-sensitive)
Green crystals near the aviary (never on bird)
