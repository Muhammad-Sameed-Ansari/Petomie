#Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)#

###Location###
Located in the upper abdomen, between the sternum and the navel region.

###Color###
Yellow

###Element###
Fire

###Meaning & Function###
The Solar Plexus Chakra governs confidence, personal expression, and emotional resilience in birds.
It supports assertiveness, healthy vocalization, curiosity, and comfort within flock dynamics.
This chakra fuels motivation, awareness, and balanced independence.

##Connection##
Stomach, liver, pancreas
Upper digestive and metabolic systems
Core muscles and breathing rhythm
Confidence, expression, and emotional stability

###Signs of Balance###
Confident vocalization and communication
Calm curiosity and interactive behavior
Healthy appetite and digestion
Stable posture and balanced energy

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Timidity, fearfulness, or hiding
Overdominant or territorial behavior
Excessive vocalization or sudden withdrawal
Overreactivity to change or stress

##Physical##
Digestive discomfort or poor appetite
Liver or pancreatic stress
Chest or abdominal tension
Fatigue or overstimulation

###Balancing Activities###
Provide safe exploration and enrichment
Gentle interaction that supports confidence
Soft touch or energy work near upper abdomen (species dependent)
Stable routines and calm environments
Yellow crystals placed near the aviary (never on the bird)
