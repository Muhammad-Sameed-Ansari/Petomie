#Thyroid Glands#

Birds have paired thyroid glands located in the neck near the trachea.  
They regulate metabolism, growth, feather molting, and temperature control.  
Energetically, they represent transformation, vitality, and adaptive flow.

###Parts###

##Follicular Cells##  
Produce thyroid hormones.  
Energetically, they symbolize transformation and renewal.

##Colloid Chambers##  
Store hormone precursors.  
Energetically, they reflect patience and inner preparation.

##Blood Supply##  
Delivers iodine and nutrients.  
Energetically, it represents movement and nourishment.

###Function###  
The thyroid glands regulate energy use, molting cycles, and overall vitality.  
Energetically, they support growth, change, and life-force balance.

###Common Imbalances###  
Hypothyroid signs: lethargy or slow molting.  
Hyperthyroid signs: restlessness or rapid feather loss.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect instability or strained transformation.
