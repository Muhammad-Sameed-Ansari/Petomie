#Pituitary Gland#

The bird’s pituitary gland, or hypophysis, sits at the base of the brain.  
It regulates growth, metabolism, reproduction, and stress responses through hormone release.  
Energetically, it represents alignment, direction, and higher guidance.

###Parts###

##Anterior Lobe##  
Produces hormones for growth, reproduction, and metabolism.  
Energetically, it symbolizes expansion and purposeful action.

##Posterior Lobe##  
Releases hormones that balance fluids and bonding responses.  
Energetically, it reflects emotional connection and inner flow.

##Pituitary Stalk##  
Connects the gland to the brain’s hypothalamus.  
Energetically, it represents communication between intuition and action.

###Function###  
The pituitary coordinates hormonal balance throughout the body.  
Energetically, it supports alignment with purpose and clarity.

###Common Imbalances###  
Hormone fluctuation: stress or energetic misalignment.  
Weak output: fatigue or overwhelm.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect loss of direction or inner clarity.
