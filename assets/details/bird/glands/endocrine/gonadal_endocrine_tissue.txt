#Gonadal Endocrine Tissue#

In birds, endocrine tissue in the testes or ovary regulates reproduction, courtship behavior, and seasonal cycles.  
Energetically, it represents creation, vitality, and alignment with natural rhythms.

###Parts###

##Hormone-Producing Cells##  
Produce estrogen, testosterone, and related hormones.  
Energetically, they symbolize creativity and expression.

##Follicular or Testicular Structure##  
Supports hormone release and reproductive function.  
Energetically, it reflects growth, potential, and renewal.

##Vascular Connections##  
Carry hormones throughout the body.  
Energetically, they represent movement and activation.

###Function###  
Gonadal endocrine tissue controls fertility, behavior, and seasonal changes.  
Energetically, it supports creation, inspiration, and natural cycles.

###Common Imbalances###  
Hormone fluctuation: seasonal stress or imbalance.  
Reduced output: fatigue or environmental disruption.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect blocked creativity or disharmony with cycles.
