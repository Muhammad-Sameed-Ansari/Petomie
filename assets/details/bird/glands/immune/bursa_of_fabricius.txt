#Bursa of Fabricius#

The bursa of Fabricius sits just above the cloaca.  
It is a unique bird organ essential for developing B cells and building strong immune function.  
Energetically, it represents growth, purification, and the awakening of inner strength.

###Parts###

##Follicular Folds##  
House developing B cells.  
Energetically, they symbolize layers of learning and rising resilience.

##Central Lumen##  
The hollow center where immune maturation occurs.  
Energetically, it reflects clarity and inner expansion.

##Mucosal Lining##  
Protects the organ and supports immune activation.  
Energetically, it represents gentle protection and inner nourishment.

###Function###  
The bursa develops B cells that help birds fight infections.  
Energetically, it strengthens personal power and clarity in boundaries.

###Common Imbalances###  
Atrophy: weakened immunity or stress.  
Inflammation: overactivation.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect insecurity or energetic depletion.
