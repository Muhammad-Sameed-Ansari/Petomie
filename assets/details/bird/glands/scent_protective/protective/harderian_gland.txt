#Harderian Gland#

The bird’s Harderian gland is a large gland located behind the eye, closely associated with the nictitating membrane.  
It produces lubrication, immune‑protective secretions, and plays a role in preening oil distribution.  
Energetically, it represents **intuition, clarity, and inner vision**.

###Parts###

##Lymphoid Tissue##  
Immune‑active tissue providing pathogen defense.  
Energetically symbolizes **inner protection and awareness**.

##Glandular Lobules##  
Produce oily and watery secretions.  
Energetically reflect **fluidity, insight, and cleansing**.

##Nictitating Membrane Interface##  
Allows lubrication of the third eyelid.  
Energetically represents **movement, flexibility, and subtle perception**.

###Function###  
Lubricates the eye, supports immunity, and assists in maintaining feather hygiene through oil integration.  
Energetically strengthens **inner vision, clarity, and spiritual perception**.
