#Meibomian Glands#

The bird’s meibomian glands line the eyelid margins and produce oils essential for tear stability.  
They protect the eye by reducing tear evaporation and maintaining smooth blinking.  
Energetically, they represent **clarity, perception, and protection**.

###Parts###

##Lipid‑Secreting Cells##  
Create the oily tear layer.  
Energetically symbolize **emotional smoothness and clarity**.

##Eyelid Margin Ducts##  
Carry oils outward.  
Energetically represent **direction and openness**.

##Tarsal Bands##  
Structural supports in the eyelids.  
Energetically reflect **strength and grounding**.

###Function###  
Prevent tear evaporation, protect the cornea, and support comfortable movement of the eyelids.  
Energetically maintain **clarity and ease in perception**.
