#Third Eyelid Gland#

The bird’s third eyelid gland lies at the base of the nictitating membrane.  
It produces immune‑rich tears that protect the eye and assist in rapid blinking.  
Energetically, it symbolizes **instinct, intuition, and unseen protection**.

###Parts###

##Lymphoid Zones##  
Provide immune defense.  
Energetically symbolize **inner strength and protective awareness**.

##Secretory Lobules##  
Create the aqueous tear layer.  
Energetically reflect **fluidity and adaptability**.

##Nictitating Attachment##  
Anchors the gland to the membrane.  
Energetically represents **guidance and quiet direction**.

###Function###  
Supports lubrication, immunity, and debris removal during blinking.  
Energetically enhances **intuitive protection and clarity**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling or dysfunction — energetically tied to **overalertness or strain**.
