#Lacrimal Gland#

The bird’s lacrimal gland produces the watery portion of the tear film.  
It keeps the cornea moist, clean, and protected.  
Energetically, it represents **release, emotional flow, and cleansing**.

###Parts###

##Acinar Cells##  
Produce fluid tears.  
Energetically symbolize **renewal and purification**.

##Duct System##  
Carries tears to the eye surface.  
Energetically reflects **communication and energetic flow**.

##Orbital Support Tissue##  
Holds the gland in place.  
Energetically represents **structured emotional support**.

###Function###  
Maintains hydration, removes debris, and prevents infection.  
Energetically supports **clarity, release, and emotional movement**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Dryness or excess tearing — energetically linked to **blocked or overflowing emotion**.
