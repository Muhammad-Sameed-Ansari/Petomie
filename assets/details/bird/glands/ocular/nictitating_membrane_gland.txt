#Nictitating Membrane Gland#

##Location##
At the base of the third eyelid (nictitating membrane), beneath the eye surface.

###Shape###
Small, soft gland embedded in tissue near the inner corner of the eye.

##Function##
Secretes lubricating fluid that spreads across the eye when the third eyelid moves. Helps remove dust, protect the cornea, and keep the eye hydrated during flight and activity.
