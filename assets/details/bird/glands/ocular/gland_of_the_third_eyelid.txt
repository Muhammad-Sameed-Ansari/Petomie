#Gland of the Third Eyelid#

##Location##
Attached to the nictitating membrane, deep in the eye socket.

###Shape###
Elongated and flattened gland that runs along the membrane tissue.

##Function##
Produces secretions that keep the third eyelid flexible and lubricated, protecting the eye during blinking and cleaning movements.
