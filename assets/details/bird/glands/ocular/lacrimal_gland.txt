#Lacrimal Gland#

##Location##
Situated in the upper outer part of the orbit above the eyeball.

###Shape###
Small, oval gland with ducts that open into the conjunctival sac.

##Function##
Produces tears that keep the surface of the eye moist and free of dust. The tears contain enzymes that help prevent bacterial infections and support clear vision.
