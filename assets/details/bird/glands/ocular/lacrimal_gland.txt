#Lacrimal Gland#

The bird’s lacrimal gland lies near the upper outer corner of the eye.  
It produces watery components of the tear film and helps keep the eye clean and protected.  
Energetically, it represents emotional flow, cleansing, and clear vision.

###Parts###

##Glandular Tissue##  
Produces watery secretions for eye lubrication.  
Energetically, it symbolizes refreshment and emotional release.

##Duct System##  
Carries tears across the eye’s surface.  
Energetically, it reflects movement, flow, and gentle transition.

##Immune Cells##  
Provide antimicrobial protection.  
Energetically, they embody inner safeguarding and clarity.

###Function###  
The lacrimal gland maintains moisture, washes away dust, and supports eye comfort.  
Energetically, it brings clarity, release, and renewed perspective.

###Common Imbalances###  
Dryness: emotional stagnation or environmental stress.  
Overtearing: irritation or energetic overwhelm.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect difficulty processing emotions.
