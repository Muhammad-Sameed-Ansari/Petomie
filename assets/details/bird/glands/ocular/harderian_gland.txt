#Harderian Gland#

##Location##
Located behind the eye within the orbit, near the nictitating membrane (the third eyelid).

###Shape###
Small, lobed gland enclosed in connective tissue.

##Function##
Produces oily and watery secretions that lubricate the nictitating membrane and keep the cornea clean and moist.  
Its secretions may also contain immune factors that help protect the eye from infections.
