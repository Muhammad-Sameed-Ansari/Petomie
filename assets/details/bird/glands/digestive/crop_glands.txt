#Crop Glands#

In birds with a crop, specialized glands soften food and produce crop milk in certain species.  
They nourish young birds and aid in early digestion.  
Energetically, they represent nurturing, comfort, and nourishment.

###Parts###

##Epithelial Secretory Cells##  
Produce crop secretions or crop milk.  
Energetically symbolize unconditional support.

##Crop Wall Folds##  
Hold and mix food.  
Energetically reflect holding space and care.

##Blood Supply##  
Supports nutrition delivery.  
Energetically represents generosity and flow.

###Function###  
Soften food, store food, and nourish chicks.  
Energetically provide comfort and nurturing presence.

###Common Imbalances###  
Crop stasis or sour crop.  
Energetically linked to stagnation or emotional overload.
