#Esophageal Mucous Glands#

These glands line portions of the upper esophagus.  
They produce mucus that protects the esophageal lining and helps food glide smoothly toward the stomach.  
Energetically, they represent flow, release, and movement through transitions.

###Parts###

##Mucous Cells##  
Produce thick protective lubrication.  
Energetically, they symbolize ease and gentle support.

##Ducts##  
Deliver secretion to the esophageal surface.  
Energetically, they reflect direction and movement.

##Submucosal Layer##  
Anchors and protects the glands.  
Energetically, it represents grounding and internal safety.

###Function###  
These glands protect the esophagus, ease swallowing, and prevent irritation from dry food.  
Energetically, they support transition, emotional flow, and adaptability.

###Common Imbalances###  
Dryness: tension or dehydration.  
Inflammation: stress or harsh feeding texture.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect difficulty moving forward or processing experiences.
