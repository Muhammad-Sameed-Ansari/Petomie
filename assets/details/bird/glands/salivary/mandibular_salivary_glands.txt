#Mandibular Salivary Glands#

The mandibular salivary glands lie near the lower jaw.  
They produce mucus and small amounts of watery secretion that help with swallowing and food passage.  
Energetically, they represent nourishment, stability, and supportive communication.

###Parts###

##Glandular Lobes##  
Produce mucous secretions.  
Energetically, they symbolize nurturing and inner comfort.

##Excretory Ducts##  
Deliver secretions into the mouth.  
Energetically, they reflect guidance and flow.

##Connective Tissue##  
Supports and protects the gland.  
Energetically, it represents structure and grounding.

###Function###  
These glands moisten food and help protect the mouth and throat during feeding.  
Energetically, they enhance gentle expression and grounded interaction.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling: irritation or infection.  
Low secretion: dryness or stress.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect withheld expression or emotional tension.
