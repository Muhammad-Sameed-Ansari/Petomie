#Posterior Lingual Glands#

##Location##
At the back of the tongue near the pharynx.

###Shape###
Small lobular glands.

##Function##
Help lubricate food before swallowing, preventing dryness and irritation.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Throat Chakra — flow and comfort.  
Meridian Connection: Spleen Meridian.  
Emotional Meaning: Comfort in expression and internal balance.  
Imbalance: Difficulty “swallowing” emotions.  
Balanced Energy: Inner peace and acceptance.
