#Cricoidal Salivary Glands#

The cricoidal salivary glands lie near the back of the mouth, close to the laryngeal area.  
They produce mucus that protects the throat and assists with swallowing.  
Energetically, they represent voice, truth, and protected expression.

###Parts###

##Glandular Tissue##  
Creates lubricating secretions.  
Energetically, it symbolizes support and expressive ease.

##Ducts##  
Carry mucus to the throat.  
Energetically, they reflect flow and clear passage.

##Protective Surface##  
Shields delicate throat structures.  
Energetically, it represents boundaries and safety.

###Function###  
These glands ease swallowing, protect the airway, and maintain throat comfort.  
Energetically, they support truthful expression and vocal clarity.

###Common Imbalances###  
Dryness: strain or dehydration.  
Swelling: irritation or stress.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect blocked communication.
