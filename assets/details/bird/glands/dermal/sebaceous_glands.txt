#Sebaceous Glands#

##Location##
Tiny glands found at the base of some feathers, especially around the vent, beak, and feet.

###Shape###
Microscopic spherical clusters attached to feather follicles.

##Function##
Secrete small amounts of oil that condition the skin and feathers.  
Work in combination with the uropygial gland to keep the plumage smooth and healthy.
