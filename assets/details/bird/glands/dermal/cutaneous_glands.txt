#Cutaneous Glands#

##Location##
Scattered within the skin layers throughout the body, though birds have very few compared to mammals.

###Shape###
Simple microscopic glandular structures.

##Function##
Assist in producing minor secretions that maintain skin moisture and flexibility, especially in areas exposed to friction (like the legs and feet).
