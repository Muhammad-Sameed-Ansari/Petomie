#Uropygial Gland (Preen or Oil Gland)#

##Location##
Situated at the base of the tail, above the rump, on the dorsal side of the bird.

###Shape###
Small, paired or bi-lobed gland with a nipple-like opening (papilla).

##Function##
Produces oily secretions that birds spread over their feathers while preening.  
This oil waterproofs feathers, maintains flexibility, and has antibacterial properties that protect the skin and plumage.
