#Cere Gland#

##Location##
Located at the base of the upper beak, forming the fleshy, often colored area called the cere (prominent in parrots, pigeons, and raptors).

###Shape###
Soft, smooth tissue embedded with small secretory glands.

##Function##
Produces secretions that help protect and clean the nostrils. May also play a role in maintaining beak moisture and sensitivity.
