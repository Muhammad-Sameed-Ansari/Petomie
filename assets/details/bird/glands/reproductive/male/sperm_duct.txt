#Sperm Duct#

The sperm duct, also called the vas deferens, carries sperm from the testes toward the cloaca.  
It is essential for reproductive transport and timing.  
Energetically, it represents direction, movement, and purposeful flow.

###Parts###

##Tubular Channel##  
Moves sperm through rhythmic motion.  
Energetically, it symbolizes guided energy and clear intention.

##Smooth Muscle Layer##  
Contracts to push sperm forward.  
Energetically, it reflects momentum and strength.

##Lining Cells##  
Protect and nourish passing sperm.  
Energetically, they represent support and gentle assistance.

###Function###  
The sperm duct transports sperm efficiently toward release.  
Energetically, it supports purposeful action and aligned movement.

###Common Imbalances###  
Blockage: stagnation or stress.  
Weak contraction: fatigue or imbalance.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect hesitation or disrupted flow.
