#Seminal Glomus#

The seminal glomus sits near the end of the sperm duct.  
It stores and concentrates sperm before release.  
Energetically, it represents preparation, focus, and readiness.

###Parts###

##Storage Chamber##  
Holds sperm prior to mating.  
Energetically, it symbolizes containment and refinement.

##Muscular Wall##  
Contracts during release.  
Energetically, it reflects activation and power.

##Vascular Layer##  
Provides nourishment and oxygen.  
Energetically, it represents movement and vitality.

###Function###  
The seminal glomus prepares sperm for effective fertilization.  
Energetically, it supports readiness, clarity, and deliberate action.

###Common Imbalances###  
Overfilling: tension or stress.  
Weak contraction: lowered fertility.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect lack of readiness or withheld expression.
