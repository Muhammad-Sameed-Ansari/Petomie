#Ovary#

Female birds have a single functional ovary on the left side of the body.  
It produces eggs, yolks, and reproductive hormones.  
Energetically, it represents creation, intuition, and nurturing potential.

###Parts###

##Follicles##  
Develop yolks and eggs.  
Energetically, they symbolize possibility, growth, and blossoming.

##Stroma##  
Supports and nourishes developing follicles.  
Energetically, it reflects grounding and support.

##Vascular Network##  
Delivers nutrients and hormones.  
Energetically, it represents flow and nourishment.

###Function###  
The ovary develops eggs and produces hormones for reproduction.  
Energetically, it supports creativity, intuition, and feminine vitality.

###Common Imbalances###  
Cyst formation: stagnation or hormonal imbalance.  
Low output: stress or exhaustion.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect depleted creativity or emotional strain.
