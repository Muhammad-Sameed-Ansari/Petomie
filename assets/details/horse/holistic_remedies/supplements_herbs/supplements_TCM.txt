#Supplements – TCM Inspired#

All substances listed here are plant-based and commonly referenced in TCM or TCM-influenced herbal practice. Supplements may be used energetically for testing or physically under professional guidance.

##Important Horse Safety Note##
TCM-inspired supplements should be selected according to the individual horse’s constitution and current state of balance. Some herbs are warming or cooling in nature and may not suit every horse. Always consult a qualified veterinarian or equine health professional before physical administration.
