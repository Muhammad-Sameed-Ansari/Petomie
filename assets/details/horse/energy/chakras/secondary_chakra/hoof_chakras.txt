#Hoof Chakras (4)#

##Location##
Center of each hoof (all four feet)

##Color##
Deep Brown or Black

##Function##
Provide grounding and energetic connection to the earth
Support balance, stability, and clear energetic flow through the legs
Influence how confidently and gracefully the horse moves

##Imbalances##
Tripping, stumbling, or dragging feet
Resistance to leaving familiar ground or herd (separation anxiety)
Unsettled or restless energy, especially when footing changes

##Balancing##
Turnout on varied, natural terrain
Grounding exercises such as liberty work or mindful leading
Hoof care (trimming/shoeing) to ensure comfort and balance
