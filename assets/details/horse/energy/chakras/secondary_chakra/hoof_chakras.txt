#Hoof Chakras (4)#

###Location###
Located in the center of each hoof, extending through the digital cushion, sole, and frog into the energetic field of the limb.

###Color###
Deep Red / Earth Brown

###Element###
Earth

###Meaning & Function###
The hoof chakras serve as grounding portals, allowing the horse to connect deeply with the earth and discharge or absorb energy as needed.
They regulate stability, balance, energetic release, and the horse’s connection to movement and safety.

##Connection##
Hooves, digital cushion, sole and frog
Hind and forelimb meridians
Grounding, stability, and movement
Fight–flight regulation through contact with earth

###Signs of Balance###
Strong, confident movement
Sure-footedness and balance
Calm energy during handling and groundwork
Healthy hoof function and circulation

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Uncertainty in movement
Reluctance to step forward
Nervousness in new footing or environments

##Physical##
Hoof soreness or imbalance
Circulation issues in lower limb
Tension in fetlock or pastern
Uneven weight-bearing

###Balancing Activities###
Groundwork on varied natural terrain
Hoof care and gentle lower-limb massage
Slow, mindful movement sessions
Allowing turnout to restore natural grounding
Earth-based crystals placed in the environment
