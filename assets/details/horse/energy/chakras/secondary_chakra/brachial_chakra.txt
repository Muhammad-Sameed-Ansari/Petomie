#Brachial Chakra#

###Location###
Located in front of the shoulder, just above the chest where the neck meets the body.
Extends into the shoulder joint, scapula, and surrounding energetic field.

###Color###
Turquoise / Aquamarine

###Element###
Ether / Air

###Meaning & Function###
The Brachial Chakra is the primary connection point between horses and humans.
It governs bonding, communication, emotional partnership, and energetic exchange.
Through this chakra, horses interpret intention, build trust, and open to deeper connection.
It also influences shoulder freedom, willingness, and emotional attunement during training.
