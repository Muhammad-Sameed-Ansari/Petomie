#Whorl Chakra#

###Location###
Located at the center of the forehead whorl (hair swirl).

###Color###
Indigo / White

###Element###
Light

###Meaning & Function###
This chakra acts as an intuitive gateway, blending Third Eye and Crown energy.
It governs insight, psychic awareness, emotional attunement, and deep intuitive communication between horse and handler.

##Connection##
Forehead whorl region
Third Eye chakra
Emotional and intuitive centers

###Signs of Balance###
Strong intuitive bond with handler
Calm, wise expression
Emotional awareness and confidence

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Disconnection or lack of focus
Misinterpretation of cues
Over-sensitivity to subtle energies

##Physical##
Forehead tension
Poll sensitivity
Eye fatigue

###Balancing Activities###
Forehead stroke or circular massage
Calm, intuitive handling
Quiet environments for clarity
Light-based energetic work
