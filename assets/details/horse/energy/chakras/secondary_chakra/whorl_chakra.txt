#Whorl Chakra#

##Location##
At the hair whorl (spiral pattern) on the forehead, chest, or neck — varies between horses

##Color##
White or Gold

##Function##
Represents the horse’s individual energetic blueprint — like a spiritual fingerprint
Governs personality expression, individuality, and alignment with soul purpose
Supports confidence and self-awareness within the herd or human partnership

##Imbalances##
Confused, withdrawn, or inconsistent behavior
Difficulty finding place in herd hierarchy
Nervousness, lack of trust in handler or surroundings

##Balancing##
Gentle circular massage around the whorl area
Slow, consistent training sessions that build confidence
Energy work focused on bringing harmony and self-assurance
