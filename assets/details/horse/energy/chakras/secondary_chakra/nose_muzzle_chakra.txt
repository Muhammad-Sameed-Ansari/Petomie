#Nose/Muzzle Chakra#

##Location##
Tip of the muzzle, including lips and nostrils

##Color##
Silver or Pale Blue

##Function##
Governs curiosity, exploration, and environmental interaction
Horses use their muzzle to greet, investigate, and connect
Strongly tied to emotional bonding and trust

##Imbalances##
Overly mouthy (nipping, chewing, grabbing lead ropes)
Avoidance of touch around muzzle, flinching
Dullness or lack of curiosity about surroundings

##Balancing##
Allow horse to sniff and explore new objects at their own pace
Gentle muzzle massage and soft touch work
Positive reinforcement and clicker training for respectful interaction
