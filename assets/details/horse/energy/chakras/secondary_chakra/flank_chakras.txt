#Flank Chakras#

###Location###
Located on each side of the ribcage between barrel and hindquarters.

###Color###
Orange / Gold

###Element###
Fire / Water

###Meaning & Function###
These chakras support emotional processing, movement fluidity, sensitivity, and body awareness.
They help horses regulate emotional flow and respond to touch with softness instead of tension.

##Connection##
Lateral abdomen, ribs, and obliques
Emotional centers (solar plexus & sacral chakras)
Movement, bending, and sensitivity

###Signs of Balance###
Softness to touch
Relaxed lateral flexion
Comfort with grooming
Fluid movement

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Sensitivity or defensiveness
Difficulty bending or flexing
Emotional reactivity to lateral pressure

##Physical##
Tension in obliques
Rib-cage tightness
Side-specific stiffness

###Balancing Activities###
Soft grooming and flank brushing
Suppling exercises and lateral work
Slow stretching and belly lifts
Energetic clearing along ribs
