#Spleen Chakra#

##Location##
Left side of the body, just behind the girth area, near the physical spleen

##Color##
Golden Yellow

##Function##
Acts as an energy filter, helping the horse process and release emotional and environmental stress
Supports immune function and detoxification
Prevents the horse from “absorbing” too much negative energy from humans, herd, or surroundings

##Imbalances##
Overly empathetic or “soaking up” human stress
Frequent digestive upset, colic, or lowered immunity
Easily overwhelmed or emotionally reactive in busy environments

##Balancing##
Energy clearing techniques such as Reiki, Body Code, or TTouch
Calming environment and consistent routine
Gentle work focused on emotional resilience and self-confidence
