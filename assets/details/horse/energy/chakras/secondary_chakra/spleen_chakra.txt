#Spleen Chakra#

###Location###
Located on the left side behind the ribs, over the spleen region.

###Color###
Yellow / Gold

###Element###
Earth / Fire

###Meaning & Function###
The spleen chakra governs immunity, vitality, blood energy, and emotional nourishment.
It supports resilience, physical strength, and the processing of emotional stress.

##Connection##
Spleen, lymphatic system, immune regulation
Blood vitality and emotional processing
Energy distribution

###Signs of Balance###
Strong vitality and stamina
Healthy immune response
Emotional resilience
Calm and grounded energy

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Lethargy or low motivation
Emotional fragility
Stress-related behaviors

##Physical##
Low immunity
Lymphatic congestion
Fatigue or poor recovery

###Balancing Activities###
Warm compresses on rib area
Gentle massage and lymph support
Low-stress environment
Yellow or gold crystals near space
