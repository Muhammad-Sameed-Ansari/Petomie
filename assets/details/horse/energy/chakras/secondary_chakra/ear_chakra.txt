#Ear Chakra#

###Location###
Located within and around each ear, extending through the inner ear and surrounding energy field.

###Color###
Light Blue / Lavender

###Element###
Air / Ether

###Meaning & Function###
The ear chakras govern listening, perceptive awareness, intuition, and energetic sensitivity.
They help the horse interpret environment, sound, intention, and emotional tone.

##Connection##
Inner and outer ear
Balance and vestibular system
Intuition and environmental awareness

###Signs of Balance###
Attentive but calm ear movement
Clear communication through ears
Healthy responsiveness to cues

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Overreactivity to sound
Difficulty focusing
Hyper-vigilance or confusion

##Physical##
Ear sensitivity
Vestibular imbalance
Tension at poll

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle ear stroking
Poll release exercises
Quiet, predictable environments
Soft vocal reassurance
