#Ear Chakras (2)#

##Location##
Just behind and slightly below the ears

##Color##
Lavender or Light Violet

##Function##
Enhance energetic and auditory perception
Help horse tune in to subtle frequencies and emotional “vibes”
Linked to communication and alertness

##Imbalances##
Overreaction to noises, spooking
Flattened ears, head tossing, resistance to bridling
Constantly swiveling ears showing hypervigilance

##Balancing##
Massage at the base of the ears or along the poll
Use of calming sounds or music to desensitize
Energy work to soothe the nervous system
