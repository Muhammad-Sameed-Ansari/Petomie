#Tail Chakra#

##Location##
At the tip of the tail (dock area extending into tail hair)

##Color##
Deep Red or Brown

##Function##
Acts as an energy release point, helping to discharge excess or stagnant energy
Governs balance, coordination, and emotional expression
Reflects the horse’s mood — tail carriage shows relaxation, irritation, or fear

##Imbalances##
Constant tail swishing (frustration, irritation)
Clamped or tucked tail (fear, insecurity)
Unexplained tension or soreness in hindquarters

##Balancing##
Gentle stroking or energy sweeping down the tail
Allowing movement and play to naturally release energy
Acupressure or massage around the tail dock
