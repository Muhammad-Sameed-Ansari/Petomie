#Heart Chakra (Anahata)#

###Location###
Located in the center of the chest, extending widely through the ribcage and upper thoracic region.

###Color###
Green

###Element###
Air

###Meaning & Function###
The Heart Chakra governs emotional openness, bonding, compassion, and herd connection.
It supports trust between horse and handler, emotional balance, and social harmony.
This chakra encourages calmness, empathy, and mutual understanding.

##Connection##
Heart and lungs
Circulatory and respiratory systems
Upper thoracic spine and ribcage
Emotional bonding and herd connection

###Signs of Balance###
Calm, relaxed interaction with herd and humans
Trusting behavior and soft expression
Healthy breathing and steady rhythm
Willing emotional connection and openness

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Difficulty bonding or trusting
Emotional shutdown or guardedness
Over-attachment or separation anxiety
Irritability or tension around the chest area

##Physical##
Breathing tension or shallow respiration
Circulatory concerns
Chest or upper rib tightness
Fatigue or reduced stamina

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle grooming across chest and shoulders
Heart-centered groundwork and bonding
Quiet companionship and reassurance
Stable herd environment
Green crystals placed safely in the environment
