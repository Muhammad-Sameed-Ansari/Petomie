#Crown Chakra (Sahasrara)#

###Location###
Located at the top of the head, between the ears, extending upward through the poll region.

###Color###
Violet / White

###Element###
Spirit / Universal Energy

###Meaning & Function###
The Crown Chakra governs spiritual connection, higher awareness, and calm intuitive understanding.
It allows horses to feel energetically supported, aware, and emotionally at peace.
This chakra enhances trust, serenity, and connection to the herd and handler on an energetic level.

##Connection##
Top of head, poll, upper cervical region
Energetic connection to higher guidance
Intuitive awareness and emotional balance
Deep inner calm and clarity

###Signs of Balance###
Soft, peaceful expression
Calmness in new or stressful situations
Strong intuitive connection to handler
Relaxed poll and upper neck

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Unexplained anxiety or disconnection
Heightened sensitivity to subtle energies
Difficulty focusing or grounding
Over-reactivity without clear cause

##Physical##
Poll tension or head sensitivity
Neurological imbalance
Sleep disruption
Restlessness or mental fatigue

###Balancing Activities###
Soft poll release and gentle head touch
Quiet bonding and meditative presence
Calm, peaceful environments
Supportive energetic work
Violet crystals placed safely in environment
