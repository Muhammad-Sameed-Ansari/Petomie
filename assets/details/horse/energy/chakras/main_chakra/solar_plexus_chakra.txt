#Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)#

###Location###
Located in the upper abdomen, between the ribs and the navel.

###Color###
Yellow

###Element###
Fire

###Meaning & Function###
The Solar Plexus Chakra governs confidence, leadership, inner strength, and emotional resilience in the horse.

###Signs of Balance###
Willing participation in training
Calm curiosity and emotional steadiness
Good digestion and healthy appetite
Balanced independence and trust in the handler

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Dominant or controlling behavior
Insecurity, fearfulness, or refusal to move forward
Overreactivity or stress responses
Difficulty trusting leadership

##Physical##
Ulcers, stomach tension, or digestive irritation
Liver or pancreatic imbalance
Tightness in diaphragm or girth area
Fatigue or restlessness

###Balancing Activities###
Confidence-building groundwork
Calm exposure to new environments
Gentle massage along ribs and belly
Consistent leadership and clear boundaries
Yellow crystals placed safely in the environment (not on the horse)
