#Sacral Chakra (Svadhisthana)#

###Location###
Located in the lower abdomen, between the navel and pelvis.

###Color###
Orange

###Element###
Water

###Meaning & Function###
Supports emotional flow, social bonding, creativity, and fluid movement.
Governs herd interaction, affection, and trust.
Encourages softness, flexibility, and sensitivity.

##Connection##
Reproductive organs and kidneys
Lower abdomen, pelvis, and hips
Emotional expression and connection
Fluidity of movement and rhythm

###Signs of Balance###
Relaxed, rhythmic movement
Comfort in social interactions
Willing expression of emotion and connection
Confidence in training and new environments

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Emotional suppression or unpredictability
Difficulty bonding with herd or handler
Over-reactivity or overstimulation
Avoidance of touch around abdomen or hips

##Physical##
Reproductive issues or hormonal imbalance
Tension in pelvis or lumbar region
Urinary discomfort or kidney strain
Stiff, restricted movement

###Balancing Activities###
Soft groundwork and flowing movement exercises
Gentle grooming, especially belly and flank areas
Calm herd environment and emotional stability
Energetic support around the lower abdomen
Orange crystals placed safely in the environment (not on horse)
