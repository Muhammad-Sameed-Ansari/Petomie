#Third Eye Chakra (Ajna)#

###Location###
Located between the eyes on the forehead, extending into the brow and poll region.

###Color###
Indigo

###Element###
Light

###Meaning & Function###
The Third Eye Chakra governs intuition, awareness, sensory perception, and emotional insight.
It helps horses understand energetic intention, read emotional states, and respond with clarity.
This chakra enhances calm evaluation, confidence, and mental presence.

##Connection##
Forehead, brow, poll area
Energetic intuitive centers
Awareness, perception, and understanding
Emotional insight and focus

###Signs of Balance###
Calm, thoughtful responses
Strong intuitive awareness with humans and herd
Clear decision-making and confidence
Relaxed poll and expressive eyes

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Spookiness or misinterpretation of stimuli
Difficulty focusing or understanding cues
Emotional confusion or unpredictability
Disconnection or withdrawal

##Physical##
Poll tension or forehead sensitivity
Eye strain or tension
Sleep irregularities
Overactive startle reflex

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle poll release and forehead touch
Calm, predictable training environments
Soft, intuitive communication
Indigo crystals placed safely in environment
