#Soul Star Chakra#

###Location###
Located 20–30 cm above the horse’s head, above the crown chakra.

###Color###
White / Gold / Opalescent Light

###Element###
Spirit / Light

###Meaning & Function###
The Soul Star Chakra connects the horse to higher consciousness, spiritual awareness, past-life memory, soul purpose, and divine guidance.
It supports emotional clarity, deep wisdom, intuitive understanding, and connection to the spiritual aspects of the herd and caretaker.

##Connection##
Above-head energy field
Crown and whorl chakras
Soul-level awareness and intuitive pathway

###Signs of Balance###
Peaceful, elevated presence
Deep intuitive sensitivity
Calm understanding and emotional maturity
Strong harmonious bond with handler

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Appearing disconnected or “far away”
Difficulty interpreting emotional cues
Energetic overstimulation

##Physical##
Head tension
Sleep disruption
Crown-area sensitivity

###Balancing Activities###
Calm environments and gentle presence
Light-based energy work above head
Soft vocal reassurance
Gold or white crystals placed in environment
