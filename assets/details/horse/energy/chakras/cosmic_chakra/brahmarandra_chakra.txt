#Brahmarandra Chakra#

###Location###
A sacred point at the very top of the head, slightly behind the crown chakra.
Acts as a spiritual “fountain” where divine energy enters the equine field.

###Color###
White / Violet / Pale Gold

###Element###
Light / Ether

###Meaning & Function###
The Brahmarandra Chakra represents the sacred opening to divine consciousness.
It governs spiritual awakening, transcendence, intuition, and higher energetic sensitivity.
For horses, it enhances wisdom, emotional purity, and the ability to sense subtle energies in the environment.

##Connection##
Crown and whorl chakras
Upper nervous system
Divine intuitive channels

###Signs of Balance###
Deep serenity and softness
Heightened intuitive awareness
Emotional purity and presence
Strong spiritual connection with herd or handler

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Energetic overwhelm
Sensitivity to subtle changes
Withdrawn or overly reactive behavior

##Physical##
Poll tension
Head discomfort
Disrupted sleep or rest

###Balancing Activities###
Gentle poll release and soft touch
Quiet spiritual bonding moments
Light visualization work above head
Supportive, serene environment
