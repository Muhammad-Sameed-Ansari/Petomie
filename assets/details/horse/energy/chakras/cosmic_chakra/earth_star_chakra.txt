#Earth Star Chakra#

###Location###
Located 20–30 cm below the horse’s hooves, anchoring into the earth.
Forms the deepest grounding point of the horse’s energetic field.

###Color###
Black / Dark Brown / Deep Earth Tones

###Element###
Earth

###Meaning & Function###
The Earth Star Chakra stabilizes the horse’s entire energy system.
It governs deep grounding, survival instinct, connection to the land, herd stability, and energetic cleansing through the ground.
It helps the horse feel safe, rooted, present, and emotionally regulated.

##Connection##
Hooves and lower limb chakras
Grounding pathways and earth-energy flow
Instinct, safety, environmental connection

###Signs of Balance###
Calm and secure energy
Grounded movement and confidence
Healthy herd connection
Ability to settle quickly after stress

###Signs of Imbalance###
##Emotional / Behavioral##
Skittishness, flightiness, difficulty grounding
Hypervigilance or anxiety
Inability to relax in new environments

##Physical##
Lower-limb tension
Hoof sensitivity
Fatigue from energy instability

###Balancing Activities###
Turnout on natural ground
Barefoot walking, varied terrain
Groundwork promoting body awareness
Energetic grounding at the hooves
Earth-toned crystals placed in stable area
