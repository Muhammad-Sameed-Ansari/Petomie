#Conception Vessel (Ren Mai)#

##Location##
Begins at the ventral midline just below the anus (REN-1), ascends along the midline between the hind limbs, over the abdomen and chest, and ends at the lower lip (REN-24).
Internally, it connects with the reproductive organs, bladder, and stomach.

##Shape##
A single midline pathway running along the ventral surface of the body.
It serves as the Yin counterpart to the Governing Vessel, nourishing all Yin meridians and internal organs.
