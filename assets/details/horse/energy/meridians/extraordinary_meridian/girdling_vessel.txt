#Girdling Vessel (Dai Mai)#

##Location##
Encircles the body at the level of the lumbar region like a belt, crossing the flanks and abdomen near the navel.
Links with the Gall Bladder meridian at the hip and connects to the Conception and Penetrating Vessels.

##Shape##
A horizontal meridian that wraps around the waist and connects upper and lower body energy.
It is the only channel that travels laterally around the torso rather than longitudinally.

##Function##
Balances the flow between upper and lower body
Stabilizes the spine, hips, and abdomen
Coordinates movement and posture
Supports digestion and reproductive health
Aids emotional stability and grounded confidence

##Signs of Imbalance##
Tightness or weakness in the lower back, flanks, or abdomen
Sagging topline or poor coordination between front and hindquarters
Digestive stagnation or sluggish elimination
Emotional imbalance between control and freedom
Feeling “split,” uncentered, or lacking support in movement
