#Penetrating Vessel (Chong Mai)#

##Location##
Originates from the region near the perineum and ascends through the abdomen close to the ventral midline.
Branches connect internally with the stomach and heart regions, while others travel laterally along the thorax and abdomen.
It distributes energy through the chest and lower body, linking with the Conception and Kidney meridians.

##Shape##
A deep internal channel often called the “Sea of Blood.”
It branches through the abdomen and chest, uniting Yin meridians and connecting lower and upper body energetically.

##Function##
Regulates reproductive energy, blood, and hormonal balance
Supports the uterus, heart, and digestive organs
Coordinates Qi movement between torso and limbs
Harmonizes emotional surges, cycles, and vitality
Acts as a reservoir of energy for the body

##Signs of Imbalance##
Abdominal tension or swelling
Irregular heat cycles or reproductive issues
Fatigue, weakness in the core, or faint pulse
Emotional instability or overwhelm
Circulatory issues or imbalance between upper and lower body energy
