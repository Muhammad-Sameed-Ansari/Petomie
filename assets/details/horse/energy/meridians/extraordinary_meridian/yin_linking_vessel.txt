#Yin Wei Mai(Yin Linking Vessel)#

##Location##
Begins near the inner side of the hock and ascends the medial aspect of the body, passing through the thigh, abdomen, and chest.
Connects internally with the Heart, Pericardium, and Conception Vessel, and ends at the throat region.

##Shape##
A deep, medial channel linking the major Yin meridians of the lower and upper body.
It unites emotional and physical aspects of internal regulation.

##Function##
Integrates emotional and physical harmony among Yin organs
Supports inner awareness, calmness, and endurance
Balances the connection between chest, abdomen, and throat
Regulates blood, hormones, and emotional transitions
Assists in adapting to change with resilience

##Signs of Imbalance##
Emotional overwhelm or apathy
Chest tightness, shallow breathing, or digestive tension
Fatigue or poor circulation
Reproductive irregularities or hormonal imbalance
Feeling disconnected, withdrawn, or lacking purpose
Fluctuating moods without external cause
