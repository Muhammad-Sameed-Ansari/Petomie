#Small Intestine Meridian#

##Location##
Begins at the lateral corner of the front hoof (SI-1) on the caudal edge of the coronet.
Ascends the caudolateral aspect of the forelimb, passing behind the carpus and up the triceps region toward the shoulder.
Curves around the scapula, travels along the lateral neck to the base of the ear, then crosses the cheek, and ends at the outer canthus of the eye (SI-19).
