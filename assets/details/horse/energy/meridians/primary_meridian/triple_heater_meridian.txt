#Triple Heater Meridian#

##Location##
Begins at the lateral corner of the front hoof (TH-1).
Travels up the caudolateral aspect of the forelimb, passing behind the carpus and over the extensor surface of the upper arm.
Curves around the shoulder and ascends the lateral neck to the base of the ear.
Continues around the ear and ends at the outer canthus of the eye (TH-23).

##Shape##

Harmonizes communication between organs and body systems
Influences the ears and balance mechanisms
Assists adaptation to environmental or emotional stress
