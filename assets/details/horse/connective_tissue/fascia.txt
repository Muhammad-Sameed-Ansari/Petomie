#Fascia#

Fascia is a continuous connective tissue network that surrounds muscles, organs, and bones.  
It maintains structural integrity and transmits mechanical and energetic signals throughout the body.  
Energetically, it represents **connection, communication, and unity**.

###Parts###

##Superficial Fascia##  
Lies beneath the skin, storing fat and allowing movement.  
Energetically, it reflects comfort and adaptability.

##Deep Fascia##  
Encases muscles and supports posture.  
Energetically, it represents strength and coherence.

##Visceral Fascia##  
Surrounds and supports organs.  
Energetically, it symbolizes protection and integration of life forces.

###Function###  
Fascia connects and supports every structure, transmitting both physical tension and energetic flow.  
Energetically, it sustains **wholeness and harmony**, linking the physical and subtle bodies.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tension or pain: restriction or emotional rigidity.  
Adhesions: past trauma or suppressed emotion.  
Weakness: fatigue or lack of integration.  
Energetic blockage: disconnection from unity.
