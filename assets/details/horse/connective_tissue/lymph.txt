#Lymph#

Lymph is a clear fluid circulating through lymphatic vessels, carrying immune cells and waste.  
It cleanses tissues and maintains fluid balance.  
Energetically, it represents **purification, renewal, and emotional release**.

###Parts###

##Lymph Fluid##  
Contains lymphocytes and waste from tissues.  
Energetically, it symbolizes cleansing and movement.

##Lymph Vessels##  
Channels that carry lymph through the body.  
Energetically, they represent flow and direction of healing.

##Lymph Nodes##  
Filter lymph and store immune cells.  
Energetically, they symbolize protection and discernment.

###Function###  
Lymph removes toxins, supports immunity, and maintains balance between fluids.  
Energetically, it promotes **emotional flow and clarity**, helping the body release stagnation.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling: stagnation or emotional heaviness.  
Infection: overload of toxins or stress.  
Fatigue: sluggish lymph flow or low vitality.  
Energetic blockage: holding onto past emotions.
