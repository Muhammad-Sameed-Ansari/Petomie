#Proximal Tarsals#

##Location##  
Located in the **upper part of the hock joint**, connecting the **tibia** to the **distal tarsal bones**.  

###Shape###  
A **compact cluster of bones** that form the hinge of the hock.  

###Parts###  

##Talus (Tibial Tarsal Bone)##  
Forms the main articulation with the tibia.  
Energetically, represents **movement and flow**.  

##Calcaneus (Fibular Tarsal Bone)##  
Projects backward to form the point of the hock.  
Energetically, symbolizes **power and leverage**.  

###Function###  
Enable flexion and extension of the hock, bearing significant weight and generating propulsion.  
Energetically, govern **drive, action, and stability in motion**.
