#Metacarpals#

##Location##  
Located in the **forelimb**, between the **carpus (knee)** and the **phalanges (digits)**.  
The third metacarpal is the main weight-bearing bone, known as the **cannon bone**.  

###Shape###  
A **long, cylindrical bone** flanked by two smaller splint bones (second and fourth metacarpals).  

###Parts###  

##Third Metacarpal (Cannon Bone)##  
Primary support bone for the forelimb.  
Energetically, represents **strength and stability**.  

##Second and Fourth Metacarpals (Splint Bones)##  
Thin, elongated bones providing lateral support.  
Energetically, symbolize **balance and adaptability**.  

###Function###  
Provide structure and support for the forelimb, transmitting forces from the carpus to the fetlock joint.  
Energetically, govern **foundation, support, and movement alignment**.
