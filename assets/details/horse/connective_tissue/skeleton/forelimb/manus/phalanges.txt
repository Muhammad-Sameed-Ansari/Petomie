#Phalanges#

##Location##  
Located at the **end of each limb**, forming the bones of the **digit (foot)** between the fetlock and the hoof.  

###Shape###  
A **series of three bones** forming the pastern and coffin region, articulating vertically within the hoof capsule.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal Phalanx (Long Pastern)##  
Connects the fetlock to the middle phalanx.  
Energetically, represents **connection and flow**.  

##Middle Phalanx (Short Pastern)##  
Forms the joint above the coffin bone.  
Energetically, symbolizes **flexibility and transition**.  

##Distal Phalanx (Coffin Bone)##  
Encased within the hoof, anchoring the laminae.  
Energetically, reflects **grounding and stability**.  

###Function###  
Support the hoof, absorb shock, and allow movement precision.  
Energetically, govern **connection to the earth and alignment with purpose**.
