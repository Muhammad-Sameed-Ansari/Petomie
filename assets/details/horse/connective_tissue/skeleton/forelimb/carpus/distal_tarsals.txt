#Distal Tarsals#

##Location##  
Located in the **hock (tarsal) joint**, between the **central tarsal bone** and the **metatarsal bones** of the hind limb.  

###Shape###  
Small, **irregularly shaped bones** forming the lower row of the tarsus.  

###Parts###  

##First and Second Tarsal Bones##  
Often fused into one in horses.  
Energetically, represent **efficiency and unity**.  

##Third Tarsal Bone##  
Connects with the third metatarsal (cannon bone).  
Energetically, symbolizes **stability and movement**.  

##Fourth Tarsal Bone##  
Bridges the distal and proximal rows.  
Energetically, embodies **connection and flexibility**.  

###Function###  
Distribute weight through the hock and assist in propulsion.  
Energetically, govern **adaptation, coordination, and endurance**.

**Chakra:** Root Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Gallbladder Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Grounding and movement stability.  
**Balanced Energy:** Strong yet flexible support.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Arthritis, bone spavin, or joint stiffness.  
Energetically, **resistance to change or overburdening**.
