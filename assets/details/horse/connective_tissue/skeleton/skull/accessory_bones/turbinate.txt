#Turbinate#

##Location##
The nasal conchae are located inside the horse’s nasal cavity, attached to the inner walls of the nasal passages.
They extend from the front of the nose deep toward the throat.

###Shape###
Thin, curled, scroll-like bones that create layered passages.
Their spiral form increases surface area for air filtration.

##Dorsal Concha##
Upper nasal scroll that directs airflow.
Energetically, represents clarity and perception.

##Ventral Concha##
Lower scroll that warms and filters inhaled air.
Energetically, symbolizes nourishment and emotional intake.

##Ethmoidal Conchae##
Deep, fine structures near the brain.
Energetically, reflect intuition and sensory awareness.

###Function###
Filters, warms, and moistens incoming air.
Enhances smell and protects the lungs from particles.
