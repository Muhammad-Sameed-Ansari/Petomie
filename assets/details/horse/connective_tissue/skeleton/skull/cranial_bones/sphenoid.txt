#Sphenoid Bone#

##Location##
The sphenoid bone lies at the base of the skull, forming part of the cranial floor and connecting with nearly all other cranial bones.

###Shape###
A butterfly-shaped bone with wings extending outward to support the skull’s base.

###Parts###

##Body##
Central portion housing the sphenoidal sinuses.  
Energetically, represents balance and integration.

##Greater and Lesser Wings##
Extend outward to support the cranial base.  
Energetically, symbolize expansion and creativity.

##Pterygoid Processes##
Project downward for muscle attachment.  
Energetically, reflect willpower and grounded action.

Energetically, governs stability, integration, and creative balance.
