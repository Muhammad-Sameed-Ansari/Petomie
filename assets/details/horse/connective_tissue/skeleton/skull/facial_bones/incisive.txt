#Incisive Bone#

##Location##
The incisive bones lie at the front of the upper jaw, forming the rostral part of the face and holding the upper incisor teeth.  
They connect to the nasal and maxillary bones.

###Shape###
Small, paired bones that are roughly rectangular with tooth sockets at their lower edge.

###Parts###

##Alveolar Border##
Houses the sockets for the incisor teeth.  
Energetically, symbolizes grounding and nourishment.

##Palatine Process##
Forms the front part of the hard palate.  
Energetically, represents communication and connection.

###Function###
Supports the incisor teeth and helps form the front of the mouth and palate.  
Energetically, governs nourishment, communication, and the intake of life experience.
