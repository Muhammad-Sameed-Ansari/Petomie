#Molar 1#

##Location##
Located in the lower right quadrant of the horse’s mouth, anchored within the mandible.  
This tooth supports balance and coordination in chewing and jaw alignment, reflecting how the horse grounds nourishment and experience into the body.

###Shape###
A strong, elongated tooth with a broad surface and deep roots designed for grinding or cutting depending on position.

###Function###
Assists in chewing, grinding, and maintaining jaw stability.  
Energetically, it symbolizes grounding, integration, and stability in daily life and emotional flow.
