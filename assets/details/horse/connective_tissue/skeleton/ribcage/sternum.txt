#Sternum#

##Location##
The sternum, or breastbone, forms the floor of the thoracic cavity.
It lies along the midline of the chest and connects to the true ribs via costal cartilage, providing the central support of the ribcage.

###Shape###
A long, flat bone composed of several fused segments called sternebrae, ending in the xiphoid cartilage toward the abdomen.

###Function###
Supports and stabilizes the ribcage, protects the heart and lungs, and provides attachment for chest and neck muscles.
Energetically, it represents centeredness, balance, and inner strength.
