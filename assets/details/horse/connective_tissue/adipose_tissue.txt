#Adipose Tissue#

Adipose tissue is a specialized connective tissue composed mainly of fat cells.  
It cushions organs, insulates the body, and stores energy for future use.  
Energetically, it represents **comfort, protection, and nourishment**, providing both physical and emotional reserves.

###Parts###

##White Adipose Tissue##  
Stores energy in the form of fat and provides insulation.  
Energetically, it symbolizes safety and stability.

##Brown Adipose Tissue##  
Generates heat and supports thermoregulation.  
Energetically, it reflects vitality and the power of transformation.

##Connective Framework##  
Fibers and blood vessels support the fat cells.  
Energetically, this framework represents structure and gentle containment.

###Function###  
Adipose tissue regulates energy balance, cushions organs, and maintains warmth.  
Energetically, it sustains **inner comfort and grounding**, supporting calmness and resilience.

###Common Imbalances###  
Excess fat accumulation: reduced movement or emotional protection.  
Fat depletion: stress, overexertion, or loss of security.  
Poor circulation: stagnation or emotional heaviness.  
Energetic blockage: resistance to self-nourishment.
