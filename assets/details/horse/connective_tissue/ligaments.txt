#Ligaments#

Ligaments are tough bands of connective tissue connecting bones at joints.  
They provide stability, limit movement, and prevent injury.  
Energetically, they represent **trust, stability, and integrity**, holding things together with strength.

###Parts###

##Collagen Fibers##  
Dense fibers giving tensile strength.  
Energetically, they reflect resilience and courage.

##Elastic Fibers##  
Provide limited flexibility for motion control.  
Energetically, they symbolize adaptability and balanced tension.

##Joint Attachments##  
Points where ligaments connect bones.  
Energetically, they represent grounded connection and loyalty.

###Function###  
Ligaments maintain joint stability and prevent excessive movement.  
Energetically, they sustain **balance and security**, anchoring trust within motion.

###Common Imbalances###  
Sprains or tears: overstretching or emotional strain.  
Stiffness: rigidity or fear of change.  
Weakness: lack of grounding or insecurity.  
Energetic blockage: feeling unsupported.
