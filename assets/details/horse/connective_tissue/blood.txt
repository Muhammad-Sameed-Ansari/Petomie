#Blood#

Blood is a vital connective tissue composed of plasma and cellular elements.  
It circulates through the body, carrying oxygen, nutrients, hormones, and waste products.  
Energetically, it represents **life force, vitality, and emotional flow**, connecting every part of the being.

###Parts###

##Plasma##  
The liquid component that transports nutrients, hormones, and waste.  
Energetically, it embodies the flow of nourishment and emotional movement.

##Red Blood Cells##  
Carry oxygen and carbon dioxide throughout the body.  
Energetically, they symbolize vitality and the breath of life.

##White Blood Cells##  
Defend the body against infection.  
Energetically, they reflect protection and immune strength.

###Function###  
Blood sustains all tissues by delivering nutrients and removing waste.  
Energetically, it maintains **connection, rhythm, and vitality**, circulating energy and emotion throughout the body.

###Common Imbalances###  
Anemia: depletion of life energy or emotional exhaustion.  
Clotting: stagnation or holding onto old patterns.  
Inflammation: energetic conflict or internal resistance.  
Energetic weakness: fatigue or loss of enthusiasm.
