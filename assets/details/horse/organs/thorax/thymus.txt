#Thymus#

##Location##  
Located in the **upper chest**, in front of the heart and behind the sternum.  
Larger and more active in foals, it gradually reduces in size as the horse matures.

###Shape###  
A **lobed, soft gland** made of lymphatic tissue, pale pink in color during youth.

###Parts###  

##Lobules##  
Contain developing immune cells (T-cells).  
Energetically, represent **growth and learning**.

##Capsule and Septa##  
Provide structure and divide the gland.  
Energetically, symbolize **boundaries and focus**.

###Function###  
Produces and trains immune cells, vital for immunity in young horses.  
Energetically, governs **trust, development, and adaptability**.

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** High Heart Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Lung and Heart Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Love, courage, and immune strength.  
**Balanced Energy:** Emotional resilience and vitality.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weak immunity or fatigue.  
Energetically, **low confidence or over-sensitivity**.

**Colors:** Soft green and white restore vitality.  
**Crystals:** Aventurine and clear quartz strengthen balance.
