#Heart#

##Location##  
The heart lies within the **thoracic cavity**, between the lungs, slightly to the left of the midline, protected by the ribs.

###Shape###  
A **cone-shaped muscular organ** enclosed by the pericardium.  
Divided into four chambers — two atria and two ventricles.

###Parts###  

##Atria##  
Receive blood returning to the heart.  
Energetically, represent **acceptance and receptivity**.

##Ventricles##  
Pump blood to the lungs and body.  
Energetically, symbolize **action and giving**.

##Valves##  
Ensure one-way blood flow.  
Energetically, reflect **balance and timing**.

###Function###  
Pumps oxygenated and deoxygenated blood throughout the body.  
Maintains circulation, temperature, and nutrient distribution.  
Energetically, governs **love, vitality, and emotional flow**.

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Heart Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Heart and Pericardium Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Love, trust, and connection.  
**Balanced Energy:** Compassion, stability, and vitality.

###Common Imbalances###  
Arrhythmia, weakness, or tension.  
Energetically, **emotional overstrain or guardedness**.
