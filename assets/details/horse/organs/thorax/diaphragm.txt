#Diaphragm#

##Location##  
The diaphragm is a **muscular partition** separating the **thoracic cavity** (containing heart and lungs) from the **abdominal cavity** (containing digestive organs).  
It attaches to the ribs, sternum, and lumbar vertebrae.

###Shape###  
A **dome-shaped sheet of muscle and tendon** that flattens during inspiration and rises during expiration.  
It forms the main muscle of respiration.

###Parts###  

##Central Tendon##  
Fibrous middle portion connecting to the muscle fibers.  
Energetically, symbolizes **unity and center of breath**.

##Muscular Portions##  
Costal, sternal, and lumbar regions that contract rhythmically.  
Energetically, represent **strength and flow between upper and lower energy**.

###Function###  
Controls breathing by expanding and contracting the thoracic cavity.  
Supports circulation and lymph flow.  
Energetically, governs **balance between grounding and expansion**.
