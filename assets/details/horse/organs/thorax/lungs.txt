#Lungs#

##Location##  
The lungs occupy most of the **thoracic cavity**, on both sides of the heart, extending from the diaphragm to the shoulders.

###Shape###  
Large, spongy, lobed organs divided into right and left sections.  
Their elastic structure allows expansion during inhalation.

###Parts###  

##Bronchi and Bronchioles##  
Branching airways distributing air through the lungs.  
Energetically, represent **communication and flow**.

##Alveoli##  
Tiny sacs where gas exchange occurs.  
Energetically, symbolize **receptivity and renewal**.

##Pleura##  
Membrane covering and protecting the lungs.  
Energetically, reflects **ease and movement**.

###Function###  
Facilitate oxygen and carbon dioxide exchange.  
Support temperature regulation and acid–base balance.  
Energetically, govern **inspiration, release, and life-force flow**.

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Heart and Throat Chakras.  
**Meridian:** Lung and Large Intestine Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Grief, openness, and acceptance.  
**Balanced Energy:** Calm breath and emotional clarity.

###Common Imbalances###  
Coughing, congestion, or shallow breathing.  
Energetically, **sadness or inability to release**.
