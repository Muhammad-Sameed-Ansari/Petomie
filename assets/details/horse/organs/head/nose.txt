#Nose#

##Location##  
The nose forms the front of the face, leading into the **nasal cavity** and **sinuses**.  
It connects directly with the **respiratory tract** and **olfactory system**.

###Shape###  
A **long, flexible structure** with wide nostrils that flare to increase airflow.  
The inner cavity is lined with mucosa and cilia that filter, warm, and humidify air.

###Parts###  

##Nostrils##  
External openings that expand for efficient breathing and scent detection.  
Energetically, they symbolize **receptivity and instinctive awareness**.

##Nasal Cavity##  
Divides into chambers lined with turbinates that filter air and trap dust.  
Energetically, represents **flow and purification**.

##Olfactory Region##  
Contains sensory neurons that detect scents and communicate them to the brain.  
Energetically, linked to **intuition and memory**.

###Function###  
Filters air, detects smell, and supports respiration and communication.  
The horse’s keen sense of smell aids recognition, emotion, and safety.  
Energetically, it connects **breath, life-force, and grounded awareness**.
