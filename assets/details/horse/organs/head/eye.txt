#Eye#

##Location##  
The eyes are positioned laterally on the head, offering nearly 350° of vision.  
Each eye connects through the **optic nerve** to the brain’s visual centers.

###Shape###  
A **spherical organ** protected by eyelids and lashes.  
Its front section includes the **cornea**, **iris**, and **lens**, while the back holds the **retina** and **optic nerve**.

###Parts###  

##Cornea and Lens##  
Focus incoming light to create sharp images.  
Energetically, they relate to **clarity and focus in perception**.

##Iris and Pupil##  
Regulate light entry and adapt to brightness.  
Energetically, symbolize **adaptability and openness to insight**.

##Retina and Optic Nerve##  
Convert light to signals transmitted to the brain.  
Energetically, they represent **vision and connection to inner awareness**.

###Function###  
The eyes detect motion, light, and color, providing spatial orientation and emotional communication.  
They guide behavior through awareness of body position and movement.  
Energetically, they embody **perception, focus, and understanding**.
