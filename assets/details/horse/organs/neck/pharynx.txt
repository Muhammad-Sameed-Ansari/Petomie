#Pharynx#

##Location##  
The pharynx is a muscular chamber located behind the **nasal cavity**, **mouth**, and **larynx**, connecting them to the **esophagus** and **trachea**.

###Shape###  
A **funnel-shaped passage** composed of overlapping muscle layers lined with mucosa.  
It serves as a shared pathway for both air and food.

###Parts###  

##Nasopharynx##  
Upper section behind the nasal cavity.  
Energetically, symbolizes **breath and inspiration**.

##Oropharynx##  
Middle section behind the mouth.  
Energetically, relates to **taste, expression, and acceptance**.

##Laryngopharynx##  
Lower section connecting to the esophagus and larynx.  
Energetically, represents **decision and transition between expression and digestion**.

###Function###  
Directs air to the trachea and food to the esophagus.  
Coordinates breathing, swallowing, and vocalization.  
Energetically, integrates **communication, nourishment, and flow between systems**.
