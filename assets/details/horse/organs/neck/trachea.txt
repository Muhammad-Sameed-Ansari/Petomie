#Trachea#

##Location##  
The trachea, or **windpipe**, extends from the larynx through the neck and into the thoracic cavity, dividing into the two bronchi at the lungs.

###Shape###  
A **flexible tube** reinforced by C-shaped cartilage rings and lined with ciliated mucous membrane that traps dust and pathogens.

###Parts###  

##Cartilaginous Rings##  
Maintain open airflow and prevent collapse.  
Energetically, symbolize **stability and protection**.

##Mucous Membrane##  
Filters and moistens inhaled air.  
Energetically, relates to **purification and resilience**.

##Bifurcation##  
The division where the trachea splits into bronchi.  
Energetically, reflects **choice and direction**.

###Function###  
Carries air between the larynx and lungs while filtering and conditioning it.  
Supports cough reflex to clear airways.  
Energetically, governs **flow of life-force and clarity of breath**.

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Throat and Heart Chakras.  
**Meridian:** Lung Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Linked to expression, breath, and release.  
**Balanced Energy:** Calm and open respiration.

###Common Imbalances###  
Coughing, airway inflammation, or constriction.  
Energetically, **restriction or tension in breath and voice**.
