#Esophagus#

##Location##  
The esophagus is a muscular tube extending from the **pharynx** to the **stomach**.  
It runs along the left side of the neck, passes through the chest cavity, and enters the stomach through the diaphragm.

###Shape###  
A **long, flexible tube** with smooth muscle layers that contract rhythmically to move food.  
Its lining is moist and folded to allow stretching during swallowing.

###Parts###  

##Cervical Portion##  
Located in the neck region, behind the trachea.  
Energetically, linked to **communication and trust in flow**.

##Thoracic Portion##  
Runs through the chest between the lungs and heart.  
Energetically, represents **balance between heart and expression**.

##Abdominal Portion##  
The short section entering the stomach.  
Energetically, symbolizes **transition and readiness to receive**.

###Function###  
Carries food and water from the mouth to the stomach via peristaltic contractions.  
Prevents reflux with its sphincter muscles.  
Energetically, governs **ease in receiving nourishment and communication flow**.
