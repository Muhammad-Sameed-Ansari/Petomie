#Larynx#

##Location##  
The larynx, or **voice box**, lies in the throat region between the pharynx and trachea.  
It is suspended by the hyoid bone and connected to the respiratory and digestive systems.

###Shape###  
A **cartilaginous structure** forming a protective framework for the vocal cords and airway entrance.  
Composed of flexible plates allowing controlled movement during breathing and swallowing.

###Parts###  

##Epiglottis##  
A leaf-shaped cartilage that closes during swallowing.  
Energetically, represents **protection and control of expression**.

##Vocal Folds##  
Vibrate to produce sound and regulate airflow.  
Energetically, linked to **authentic voice and resonance**.

##Cartilages and Muscles##  
Provide support and adjust tone.  
Energetically, symbolize **adaptability and clarity**.

###Function###  
Regulates airflow, prevents food from entering the airway, and contributes to vocalization.  
Energetically, aligns physical voice with inner truth and expression.
