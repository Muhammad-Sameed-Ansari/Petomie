#Anus#

##Location##  
The anus is located at the end of the **digestive tract**, following the rectum.  
It lies beneath the tail and opens externally at the perineal region.

###Shape###  
A **circular muscular opening** surrounded by smooth and skeletal muscle layers.  
It remains closed by default and relaxes during defecation.

###Parts###  

##Internal Anal Sphincter##  
Smooth muscle that provides involuntary control.  
Energetically, represents **inner strength and protection**.

##External Anal Sphincter##  
Skeletal muscle under voluntary control.  
Energetically, symbolizes **release and conscious letting go**.

###Function###  
Controls the expulsion of feces, maintaining continence and regulating elimination.  
Acts as the final gate in the digestive process.  
Energetically, relates to **release of what no longer serves** and the ability to finish cycles.
