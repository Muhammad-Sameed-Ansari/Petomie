#Anus#

##Location##
Located beneath the tail, forming the final opening of the digestive tract.

###Shape###
A circular muscular sphincter that controls release of feces.

##Function##
Regulates elimination and protects against infection by sealing the rectal passage.

###Energetic Connection###
- **Chakra:** Root Chakra (Muladhara)
- **Meridian Connection:** Large Intestine and Bladder Meridians
- **Emotional Meaning:** Symbolizes healthy boundaries and ability to release tension.
- **Balanced Energy:** Ease and flow in release, grounded and calm energy.
- **Energetic Support:** Relaxation and gentle grooming around the tail support comfort and trust.
