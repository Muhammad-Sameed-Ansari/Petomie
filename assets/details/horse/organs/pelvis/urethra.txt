#Urethra#

##Location##
Runs from the urinary bladder through the pelvis, opening externally to release urine.

###Shape###
A narrow, flexible tube lined with smooth muscle for transport of urine.

##Function##
Carries urine from the bladder to the outside of the body, regulating fluid excretion.
