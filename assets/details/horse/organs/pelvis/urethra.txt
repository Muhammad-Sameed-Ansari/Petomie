#Urethra#

##Location##  
The urethra runs from the **urinary bladder** to the external opening, varying slightly in length between sexes.  
In stallions, it passes through the penis; in mares, it opens just ahead of the vaginal opening.

###Shape###  
A **narrow muscular tube** lined with mucous membrane that expands slightly during urination.

###Parts###  

##Proximal Urethra##  
Connects to the bladder and controls urine release.  
Energetically, relates to **balance between holding and releasing**.

##Distal Urethra##  
Leads to the external opening.  
Energetically, symbolizes **direction and clarity of flow**.

###Function###  
Transports urine from the bladder to the outside of the body.  
Supports elimination and regulates urinary flow.  
Energetically, governs **expression of trust and release**.
