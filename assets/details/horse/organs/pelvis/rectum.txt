#Rectum#

##Location##  
The rectum is the final section of the **large intestine**, connecting the colon to the anus.  
It lies within the pelvic cavity, curving slightly along the sacrum.

###Shape###  
A **short, muscular tube** with expandable walls to store fecal matter before elimination.

###Parts###  

##Upper Rectum##  
Stores waste and absorbs remaining water.  
Energetically, represents **containment and readiness**.

##Lower Rectum##  
Initiates defecation reflex and signals the need for release.  
Energetically, symbolizes **timing and surrender**.

###Function###  
Stores and compacts feces, coordinates elimination with the anus, and protects from bacterial contamination.  
Energetically, governs **completion and letting go with control**.
