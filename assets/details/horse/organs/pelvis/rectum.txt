#Rectum#

##Location##
Located in the caudal pelvis, continuing from the large intestine and ending at the anus.

###Shape###
A straight, muscular tube lined with mucosa that stores feces before elimination.

##Function##
Stores and expels waste through rhythmic muscle contractions and aids in detoxification.
