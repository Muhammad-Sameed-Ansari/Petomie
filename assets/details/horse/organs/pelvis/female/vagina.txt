##Location##
Extends from the cervix to the vulva, lying between the urethra and rectum within the pelvic cavity.

A muscular, flexible tube with a moist lining that allows expansion during mating or birth.

Acts as the copulatory organ, birth canal, and passage for reproductive secretions.
