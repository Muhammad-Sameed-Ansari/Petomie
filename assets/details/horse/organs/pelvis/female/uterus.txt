#Uterus#

##Location##
Located within the pelvic cavity between the bladder and rectum, composed of two long uterine horns and a short uterine body leading to the cervix.

###Shape###
A Y-shaped, muscular organ capable of expanding significantly during pregnancy.

##Function##
Provides nourishment and protection for developing embryos and contracts to expel the foal during birth.
