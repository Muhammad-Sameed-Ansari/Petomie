#Uterine Tubes (Oviducts)#

##Location##  
Extend from each ovary to the **uterine horns**, suspended in the broad ligament.  
They lie close to the dorsal abdominal wall.

###Shape###  
Narrow, coiled tubes lined with cilia that guide the ovum toward the uterus.

###Parts###  

##Infundibulum##  
Funnel-shaped opening near the ovary that captures the released egg.  
Energetically, represents **receptivity and connection**.

##Ampulla##  
Middle region where fertilization typically occurs.  
Energetically, symbolizes **union and transformation**.

##Isthmus##  
Narrow passage leading to the uterus.  
Energetically, reflects **guidance and gentle transition**.

###Function###  
Transport ova from ovary to uterus, provide a site for fertilization, and nourish early embryos.  
Energetically, govern **connection, transition, and cooperation**.
