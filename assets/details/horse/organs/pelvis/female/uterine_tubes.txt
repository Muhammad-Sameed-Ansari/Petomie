#Uterine Tubes (Oviducts)#

##Location##
Extend from each ovary to the uterine horns, lying within the dorsal abdomen and suspended by the mesosalpinx.

###Shape###
Two delicate, coiled tubes that guide the egg from the ovary to the uterus.

##Function##
Serve as the site of fertilization and transport of the fertilized egg toward the uterus.
