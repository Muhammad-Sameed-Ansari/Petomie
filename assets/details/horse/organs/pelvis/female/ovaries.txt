#Ovaries#

##Location##
The ovaries are located in the dorsal abdomen, near the kidneys, one on each side of the body.

###Shape###
Two small, oval organs with a firm texture, enclosed by connective tissue and linked to the uterus by ligaments.

##Function##
Produce eggs (ova) and secrete hormones such as estrogen and progesterone that regulate the estrous cycle and fertility.
