#Sperm Duct (Vas Deferens)#

##Location##
Extends from the tail of the epididymis, travels through the spermatic cord, and joins the urethra near the bladder.

###Shape###
A firm, muscular tube surrounded by connective tissue and blood vessels.

##Function##
Transports mature sperm from the epididymis to the urethra during ejaculation, completing the pathway for reproduction.
