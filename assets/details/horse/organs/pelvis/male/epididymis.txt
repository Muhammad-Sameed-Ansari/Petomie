#Epididymis#

##Location##
Attached along the upper and side surface of each testis within the scrotum.

###Shape###
A narrow, coiled tube forming a long curved structure that connects the testis to the vas deferens.

##Function##
Stores and matures sperm, providing an environment where they gain mobility and readiness for fertilization.
