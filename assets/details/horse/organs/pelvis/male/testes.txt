#Testes#

##Location##  
Located within the **scrotum**, outside the abdominal cavity, suspended by the **spermatic cords**.  
This external position allows for temperature regulation necessary for sperm production.

###Shape###  
Oval, smooth organs enclosed in a fibrous capsule, with a spongy internal texture composed of seminiferous tubules.

###Parts###  

##Seminiferous Tubules##  
Site of sperm production.  
Energetically, represent **creativity and vitality**.

##Interstitial Cells (Leydig Cells)##  
Produce testosterone for reproductive and physical strength.  
Energetically, symbolize **drive and masculine balance**.

##Tunica Albuginea##  
Protective outer layer maintaining form and support.  
Energetically, reflects **structure and containment**.

###Function###  
Produce sperm and hormones regulating reproductive function and secondary traits.  
Energetically, maintain **strength, motivation, and creative potential**.
