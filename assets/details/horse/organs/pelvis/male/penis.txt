#Penis#

##Location##  
The penis is positioned within the **prepuce (sheath)** on the lower abdomen between the hind limbs.  
It extends externally during urination or erection and retracts afterward into the preputial cavity for protection.

###Shape###  
A **long, cylindrical, muscular organ** composed of erectile tissue that fills with blood during erection.  
It consists of the **root**, **body**, and **glans penis**, enclosed by the prepuce.

###Parts###  

##Root##  
Anchors the penis to the pelvis via the ischiocavernosus muscles.  
Energetically, represents **foundation and stability**.

##Body (Shaft)##  
Contains erectile tissue — **corpora cavernosa** and **corpus spongiosum** — that engorge with blood during erection.  
Energetically, symbolizes **strength and focused energy**.

##Glans Penis##  
Sensitive, expanded tip involved in mating and stimulation.  
Energetically, reflects **connection, sensitivity, and expression**.

###Function###  
Used for urination and reproduction.  
Erectile tissues fill with blood, enabling penetration and semen transfer.  
Energetically, governs **expression of life force, confidence, and vitality**.
