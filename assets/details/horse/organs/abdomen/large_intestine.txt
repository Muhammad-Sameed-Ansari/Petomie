#Large Intestine#

##Location##
The large intestine occupies most of the **caudal and ventral abdomen**.  
It connects the **small intestine** to the **rectum** and consists of the **cecum**, **large colon**, and **small colon**.

###Shape###
A **wide, looping structure** forming a large portion of the abdominal cavity.  
It contains billions of microorganisms that help break down fibrous plant material.

##Function##
- **Ferments fiber** to produce energy-rich fatty acids.  
- Absorbs **water and electrolytes**.  
- Forms and stores **feces**.  
- Maintains healthy intestinal flora for overall well-being.
