#Large Intestine#

##Location##  
Occupies the hindgut region, filling much of the right and lower abdomen.  
Connects the small intestine to the rectum and anus.

###Shape###  
A broad, looping structure composed of the cecum, large colon, small colon, and rectum.  
Capable of holding up to 30 liters of digested material.

###Parts###  

##Cecum##  
Primary fermentation chamber where microbes break down fiber.  
Energetically, symbolizes **transformation through collaboration**.

##Large and Small Colon##  
Absorb nutrients, water, and minerals, forming solid waste.  
Energetically, linked to **assimilation and clarity**.

##Rectum##  
Final storage and elimination path.  
Energetically, represents **completion and release**.

###Function###  
Ferments fiber, reabsorbs water, and removes waste.  
Supports digestive rhythm and microbiome balance.  
Energetically, governs the body’s and mind’s ability to **let go of what is no longer needed**.
