#Pancreas#

##Location##
The pancreas is located **behind the stomach**, nestled in the loop of the **duodenum** (first part of the small intestine).

###Shape###
A **flat, elongated organ** with a pinkish-grey color.  
It consists of two main parts:  
- **Exocrine portion:** produces digestive enzymes.  
- **Endocrine portion:** produces hormones like **insulin** and **glucagon**.

##Function##
- Secretes **enzymes** to digest proteins, fats, and carbohydrates.  
- Produces **insulin** to regulate blood sugar levels.  
- Helps maintain energy balance and metabolism.  
- Works closely with the **liver and small intestine** for nutrient absorption.
