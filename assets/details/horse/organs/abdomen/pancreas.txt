#Pancreas#

##Location##  
Lies between the stomach and duodenum, close to the liver and small intestine.  
Embedded within surrounding fat and connective tissue.

###Shape###  
A soft, elongated gland composed of lobes, pale in color, with ducts leading to the duodenum.

###Parts###  

##Exocrine Portion##  
Releases digestive enzymes.  
Energetically, linked to **assistance and flow of nourishment**.

##Endocrine Portion##  
Contains islets that produce insulin and glucagon to regulate blood sugar.  
Energetically, represents **balance between giving and receiving**.

###Function###  
Regulates digestion and energy metabolism.  
Maintains harmony between nutrient absorption and utilization.  
Energetically, reflects emotional balance and sense of worth.
