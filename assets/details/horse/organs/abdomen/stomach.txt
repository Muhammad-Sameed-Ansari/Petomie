#Stomach#

##Location##  
Positioned in the upper left abdomen beneath the diaphragm, connecting the esophagus to the small intestine.

###Shape###  
A muscular, curved sac divided into the cardia, fundus, body, and pylorus.

###Parts###  

##Cardia##  
Entry point for ingested food.  
Energetically, represents **reception and readiness**.

##Fundus and Body##  
Mix food with acid and enzymes.  
Energetically, symbolize **processing and acceptance**.

##Pylorus##  
Controls release to the small intestine.  
Energetically, reflects **timing and patience**.

###Function###  
Begins digestion by breaking down food and regulating emptying.  
Protects from pathogens and manages acidity.  
Energetically, processes experiences and emotions.

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Solar Plexus Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Stomach Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Linked to assimilation of experience.  
**Balanced Energy:** Calm focus and strength.

###Common Imbalances###  
Bloating or ulcers.  
Energetically, **inability to process stress or emotion**.

**Colors:** Golden yellow supports calm digestion.
