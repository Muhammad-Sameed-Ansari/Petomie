#Kidneys#

##Location##  
The kidneys are located on either side of the spine in the upper abdominal cavity, just beneath the last ribs.  
The right kidney lies slightly forward and close to the liver, while the left kidney is positioned nearer to the spleen.

###Shape###  
The right kidney is heart-shaped, and the left is bean-shaped.  
Each is smooth, firm, and surrounded by a capsule of connective tissue and protective fat.

###Parts###  

##Cortex##  
The outer layer that contains nephrons, the functional units of filtration.  
Energetically, it represents **clarity and cleansing**, releasing emotional and physical waste.

##Medulla##  
The inner region that channels filtered fluid toward the renal pelvis.  
Energetically, symbolizes **depth and retention of what is essential**.

##Renal Pelvis and Ureters##  
Collect and funnel urine toward the bladder.  
Energetically, linked to **flow and release**.

###Function###  
Filters blood, removes toxins, and maintains water, electrolyte, and acid–base balance.  
Also regulates blood pressure and supports red blood cell formation.  
Energetically, governs vitality, courage, and willpower.

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Root Chakra.  
**Meridian:** Kidney Meridian.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Relates to fear, trust, and endurance.  
**Balanced Energy:** Steady vitality and emotional grounding.

###Common Imbalances###  
Fatigue, fluid retention, or lower back sensitivity.  
Energetically, **fear, insecurity, or lack of life-force flow**.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###  
Support with warmth, hydration, and gentle movement.  
**Colors:** Deep blue and soft red strengthen grounding.  
**Crystals:** Black tourmaline and garnet reinforce courage and stability.  
**Elements:** Water and stillness renew inner strength.  
**Affirmation:** “I am safe, vital, and supported by life.”
