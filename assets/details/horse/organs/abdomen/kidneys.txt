#Kidneys#

##Location##
The kidneys are located on the **dorsal wall of the abdomen**, just below the spine.  
The **right kidney** lies slightly more forward than the **left**.

###Shape###
The **left kidney** is bean-shaped, and the **right kidney** is heart-shaped.  
Both are dark red and smooth in texture.

##Function##
- **Filter blood** to remove waste products and toxins.  
- Maintain **fluid, electrolyte, and pH balance**.  
- Regulate **blood pressure** and **red blood cell production**.  
- Produce urine, which passes to the bladder through the ureters.
