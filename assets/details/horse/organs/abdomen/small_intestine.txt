#Small Intestine#

##Location##
The small intestine lies in the **central and lower abdomen**, extending from the **stomach** to the **large intestine**.  
It is suspended by the **mesentery**, which contains blood vessels and lymph nodes.

###Shape###
A **long, coiled tube** about 20 meters in length.  
It has three parts: **duodenum, jejunum, and ileum**.

##Function##
- Completes **digestion** of food using enzymes from the pancreas and liver.  
- **Absorbs nutrients**, vitamins, and minerals into the bloodstream.  
- Moves digested material into the large intestine.  
- Maintains **fluid balance** and supports immune function.
