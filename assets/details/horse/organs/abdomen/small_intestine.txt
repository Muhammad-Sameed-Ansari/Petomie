#Small Intestine#

##Location##  
Occupies the central and lower abdomen, connecting the stomach to the large intestine.  
Suspended by the mesentery containing blood vessels and lymph nodes.

###Shape###  
A long, coiled tube divided into duodenum, jejunum, and ileum.

###Parts###  

##Duodenum##  
Receives bile and enzymes for digestion.  
Energetically, relates to **preparation and openness**.

##Jejunum##  
Main absorption site for nutrients.  
Energetically, symbolizes **integration and nourishment**.

##Ileum##  
Transfers residual content to the large intestine.  
Energetically, reflects **completion and clarity**.

###Function###  
Breaks down food and absorbs nutrients and fluids.  
Supports microbiome balance and energy distribution.  
Energetically, governs discernment — knowing what to keep and what to release.
