#Liver#

##Location##  
Situated in the upper right abdomen, beneath the diaphragm and in front of the stomach.  
Extends across the midline to the left side, in close contact with the right kidney.

###Shape###  
A large, reddish-brown, lobed organ with smooth surfaces and rich blood supply.

###Parts###  

##Lobes##  
Anatomical divisions that manage filtration and metabolism.  
Energetically, represent **organization and flow of life energy**.

##Hepatic Cells##  
Perform detoxification, storage, and synthesis of vital proteins.  
Energetically, symbolize **renewal and balance**.

##Bile Ducts##  
Transport bile to the small intestine for fat digestion.  
Energetically, relate to **clarity and direction**.

###Function###  
Detoxifies blood, stores nutrients, regulates hormones, and produces bile.  
Maintains glucose and lipid balance.  
Energetically, governs vitality, creativity, and emotional flexibility.
