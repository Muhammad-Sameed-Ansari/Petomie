#Liver#

##Location##
The liver is located in the **right upper abdomen**, just behind the diaphragm and below the ribs.  
It extends slightly toward the left but is mostly on the right side.

###Shape###
A **large, reddish-brown organ** divided into several lobes.  
It has a smooth surface and soft texture.  
Horses do **not have a gallbladder**; bile flows continuously from the liver to the small intestine.

##Function##
- **Processes nutrients** from the digestive system.  
- Produces **bile** to help digest fats.  
- **Detoxifies** harmful substances in the blood.  
- Stores **glycogen**, **vitamins**, and **minerals**.  
- Plays a key role in **metabolism and hormone regulation**.
