#Urinary Bladder#

##Location##
The urinary bladder is located in the **caudal abdomen**, just in front of the pelvis.  
It connects to the **urethra**, which carries urine out of the body.

###Shape###
A **flexible, hollow sac** made of smooth muscle that expands as it fills with urine and contracts during urination.

##Function##
- **Stores urine** produced by the kidneys.  
- Releases urine through the urethra when full.  
- Maintains **fluid and electrolyte balance**.  
- Works with kidneys to excrete waste and toxins.
