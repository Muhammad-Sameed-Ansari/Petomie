#Spleen#

##Location##
The spleen is found on the **left side of the abdomen**, near the **stomach** and below the **ribs**.  
It lies against the left abdominal wall and is attached to the stomach by connective tissue.

###Shape###
A **flat, curved, dark-red organ** resembling a crescent moon.  
It can expand and contract depending on blood volume and activity level.

##Function##
- **Stores and filters blood**, removing old or damaged red blood cells.  
- Produces and releases **white blood cells** for immune defense.  
- Acts as a **blood reservoir** during exercise.  
- Helps regulate body fluids and immune function.

###Energetic Connection###
- **Chakra:** Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura).  
- **Meridian Connection:** Spleen Meridian.  
- **Emotional Meaning:** Symbolizes **nurturing, stability, and trust in life**.  
  - Imbalance may cause worry, fatigue, or emotional depletion.  
- **Balanced Energy:** Emotional steadiness and inner strength.  
- **Energetic Support:** Gentle movement, regular feeding, and grounding time in nature.
