#Spleen#

##Location##  
Located on the left side of the abdomen near the stomach and diaphragm.  
It lies under the last ribs and is attached by connective tissue.

###Shape###  
A flat, oval organ that is dark red and spongy in texture.

###Parts###  

##Red Pulp##  
Filters blood and removes old red cells.  
Energetically, represents **renewal and resilience**.

##White Pulp##  
Stores immune cells and supports defense.  
Energetically, symbolizes **awareness and protection**.

###Function###  
Cleanses blood, stores platelets, and supports immune strength.  
Maintains iron balance and vitality.  
Energetically, linked to stability, nourishment, and grounded focus.
