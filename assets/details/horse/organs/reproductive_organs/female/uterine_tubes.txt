#Uterine Tubes (Oviducts / Fallopian Tubes)#

##Location##
Extend from the **ovaries** to the **uterine horns**, lying within the **mesosalpinx**.  
Each tube connects the ovulation fossa of an ovary to the respective uterine horn.

###Shape###
Delicate, **coiled tubes** lined with cilia.  
The ends form funnel-shaped openings called **infundibula** that capture the released eggs.

##Function##
- Transport **eggs** from the ovary to the uterus.  
- Serve as the **site of fertilization**.  
- Provide a **nourishing environment** for early embryos.  
- Support smooth communication between ovaries and uterus.
