#Ovaries#

##Location##
The ovaries are located in the **abdominal cavity**, just below the **lumbar vertebrae**, near the **kidneys**.  
They are attached by the **mesovarium** and connected to the **uterine tubes**.

###Shape###
Large, **bean-shaped organs** that change slightly in size and texture depending on the mare’s reproductive cycle.  
Each ovary has a unique **ovulation fossa**, the only site where ovulation occurs.

##Function##
- Produce **eggs (ova)** for reproduction.  
- Secrete **hormones** such as estrogen, progesterone, and inhibin.  
- Regulate the **estrous cycle** and fertility.  
- Maintain hormonal and energetic balance.
