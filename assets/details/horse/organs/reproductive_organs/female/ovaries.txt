#Ovaries#

##Location##  
The ovaries are located in the abdominal cavity near the **kidneys**, suspended by the **broad ligament** on each side.  
They are positioned slightly asymmetrically, with the left ovary usually further back than the right.

###Shape###  
Oval, flattened structures that vary in size throughout the reproductive cycle.  
Their surface changes due to developing and regressing follicles.

###Parts###  

##Cortex##  
The outer functional zone where follicles develop.  
Energetically, represents **creation and renewal**.

##Medulla##  
Contains blood vessels and connective tissue that nourish the cortex.  
Energetically, symbolizes **stability and nourishment**.

##Follicles and Corpus Luteum##  
Sites of ovum development and hormone production.  
Energetically, reflect **cycles of growth and release**.

###Function###  
Produce ova (eggs) and secrete reproductive hormones such as estrogen and progesterone.  
Regulate heat cycles and fertility.  
Energetically, govern **creative life energy and hormonal rhythm**.
