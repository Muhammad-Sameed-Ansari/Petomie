#Cervix#

##Location##  
The cervix is positioned between the **uterus** and the **vagina**, located deep within the pelvic cavity.  
It acts as a gateway controlling passage between the inner reproductive system and the external environment.

###Shape###  
A **short, firm, muscular canal** approximately 6–8 cm long, composed of thick circular muscle layers and fibrous connective tissue.  
Its internal lining features longitudinal folds that form a seal during pregnancy and relax during heat or foaling.

###Parts###  

##External Os##  
The outer opening into the vagina that changes shape with hormonal cycles.  
Energetically, it represents **transition and controlled openness**.

##Cervical Canal##  
The central passage lined with mucous-secreting cells, creating protective barriers.  
Energetically, symbolizes **flow, adaptability, and safe communication**.

##Internal Os##  
The inner opening connecting to the uterus.  
Energetically, reflects **connection between inner and outer balance**.

###Function###  
Serves as a barrier protecting the uterus from infection while allowing controlled sperm entry.  
Seals the uterus during pregnancy and dilates during parturition.  
Energetically, governs **boundaries, transitions, and readiness to open when safe**.
