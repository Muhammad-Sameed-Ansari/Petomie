#Cervix#

##Location##
Located between the **uterus** and **vagina**, forming the **lower end of the uterus**.  
It acts as a barrier and passage connecting the two organs.

###Shape###
A **short, firm, muscular tube** with longitudinal folds.  
The cervix changes texture and openness throughout the estrous cycle.

##Function##
- Controls **entry of sperm** and **exit of uterine fluids**.  
- Produces **mucus** that varies in consistency to aid or block passage.  
- Opens during **parturition** to allow delivery.  
- Serves as a **protective gateway** against infection.
