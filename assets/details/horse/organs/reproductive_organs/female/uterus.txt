#Uterus#

##Location##  
Located in the pelvic and lower abdominal cavity, between the bladder and rectum.  
It consists of two **uterine horns**, a **body**, and a **cervix**.

###Shape###  
A **Y-shaped muscular organ**, capable of expansion during pregnancy and contraction during labor.

###Parts###  

##Uterine Horns##  
Extend toward each ovary and serve as the site of early embryonic development.  
Energetically, symbolize **growth and nurturing**.

##Uterine Body##  
Central cavity connecting horns to the cervix.  
Energetically, represents **union and grounding**.

##Endometrium and Myometrium##  
Lining and muscular layers that nourish and protect the fetus.  
Energetically, relate to **protection and creation**.

###Function###  
Provides an environment for conception, fetal growth, and birth.  
Regulates hormonal communication and prepares for implantation.  
Energetically, embodies **nurturing, patience, and creative stability**.

###Energetic Connection###  
**Chakra:** Sacral and Heart Chakras.  
**Meridian:** Spleen and Conception Vessel.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Care, fertility, and balance.  
**Balanced Energy:** Openness to creation and emotional grounding.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation, infection, or tension.  
Energetically, **suppressed nurturing or unresolved emotion**.
