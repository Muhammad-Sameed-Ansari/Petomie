#Uterus#

##Location##
Located in the **pelvic cavity**, between the **rectum** and the **urinary bladder**.  
It consists of a **body**, **two long uterine horns**, and a **cervix** leading into the vagina.

###Shape###
A **T- or Y-shaped muscular organ** with long, flexible horns.  
The walls are thick and elastic to allow stretching during pregnancy.

##Function##
- Provides the **site for implantation and fetal development**.  
- Nourishes and protects developing foals.  
- Contracts during **parturition** to deliver the foal.  
- Plays a role in hormonal feedback and reproductive cycles.
