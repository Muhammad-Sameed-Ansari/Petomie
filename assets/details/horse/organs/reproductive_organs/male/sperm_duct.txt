#Sperm Duct (Vas Deferens)#

##Location##  
Extends from the tail of the **epididymis** to the **urethra**, running through the spermatic cord into the pelvic cavity.

###Shape###  
A **narrow, muscular tube** lined with smooth muscle to propel sperm forward through rhythmic contractions.

###Parts###  

##Ampulla##  
Expanded portion near the urethra that stores sperm.  
Energetically, represents **focus and readiness**.

##Duct Wall##  
Contains muscle layers aiding transport.  
Energetically, reflects **direction and strength**.

##Terminal Opening##  
Where the duct joins the urethra.  
Energetically, symbolizes **connection and release**.

###Function###  
Transports mature sperm from the epididymis to the urethra during ejaculation.  
Coordinates with accessory glands to deliver semen.  
Energetically, governs **directed flow, focus, and purpose**.
