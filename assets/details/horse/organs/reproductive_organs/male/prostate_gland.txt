#Prostate Gland#  

##Location##  
Located in the **pelvic cavity**, near the **neck of the urinary bladder**, associated with the **proximal urethra**.  
In the stallion, the prostate sits dorsally and laterally around the urethra and contributes to the accessory sex gland system.

###Shape###  
A **flattened, lobulated gland**.  
In the horse it is typically described as having **two lobes** connected by a thin tissue bridge (appearance can vary by individual).
