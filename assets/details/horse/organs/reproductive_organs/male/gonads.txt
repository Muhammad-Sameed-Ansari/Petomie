#Gonads#

##Location##  
The testes in the stallion are located within the **scrotum**, suspended by the **spermatic cords** outside the abdominal cavity.  
They descend through the inguinal canal during late fetal development or shortly after birth.

###Shape###  
Large, oval, smooth organs enclosed by a strong fibrous capsule.  
Internally filled with tightly packed tubules responsible for sperm production.

###Parts###  

##Seminiferous Tubules##  
Microscopic coiled tubes where sperm cells are produced.  
Energetically, represent **creative vitality and masculine expression**.

##Interstitial (Leydig) Cells##  
Produce testosterone, influencing fertility, behaviour, muscle tone, and stallion presence.  
Energetically, symbolize **drive, strength, and assertive life-force**.

##Tunica Albuginea##  
Firm, fibrous outer protective layer.  
Energetically, reflects **structure, grounding, and strong boundaries**.

###Function###
