#Accessory Glands#

##Location##  
Situated in the **pelvic cavity** near the base of the bladder and urethra.  
Include the **seminal vesicles**, **prostate gland**, and **bulbourethral glands**.

###Shape###  
Each gland is distinct in form and function — lobulated or rounded — and all are richly supplied with blood vessels.

###Parts###  

##Seminal Vesicles##  
Secrete nutrient-rich fluid that nourishes sperm.  
Energetically, represent **abundance and vitality**.

##Prostate Gland##  
Produces fluid that supports sperm motility and protects against acidity.  
Energetically, symbolizes **strength and flow**.

##Bulbourethral Glands##  
Release lubricating secretion before ejaculation.  
Energetically, reflect **ease and preparation**.

###Function###  
Produce and secrete fluids that combine with sperm to form semen.  
These secretions provide nourishment, protection, and mobility.  
Energetically, maintain **balance between power and gentleness**.

**Emotional Meaning:** Expression, fertility, and harmony.  
**Balanced Energy:** Controlled flow and cooperative energy.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation, blockage, or irregular secretion.  
Energetically, **excessive pressure or emotional restraint**.
