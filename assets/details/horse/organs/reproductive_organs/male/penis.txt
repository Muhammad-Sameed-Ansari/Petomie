##Location##
Located on the **ventral midline** of the body, enclosed in a **sheath (prepuce)** that protects it when not erect.  
It extends forward between the hind legs during urination or mating.

###Shape###
A **muscular, cylindrical organ** composed of erectile tissues — the **corpus cavernosum** and **corpus spongiosum**.  
Horses have a **retractable penis** with a large glans covered by the prepuce.

##Function##
- Enables **urination** and **sperm transfer** during mating.  
- Facilitates **ejaculation** through vascular engorgement and muscular contraction.  
- Plays a role in **reproductive communication and behavior**.
