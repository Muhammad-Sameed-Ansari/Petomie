#Epididymis#

##Location##
Lies along the **top and back of each testis**, connecting the testis to the **vas deferens**.  
It is a crucial link between sperm production and transport.

###Shape###
A **coiled tubular structure** composed of three parts:  
- **Head (Caput):** Receives sperm from the testis.  
- **Body (Corpus):** Area where sperm mature.  
- **Tail (Cauda):** Stores mature sperm until ejaculation.

##Function##
- Stores and **matures sperm cells**.  
- Transports sperm to the vas deferens.  
- Secretes fluids that nourish and protect sperm.  
- Regulates sperm temperature and environment.
