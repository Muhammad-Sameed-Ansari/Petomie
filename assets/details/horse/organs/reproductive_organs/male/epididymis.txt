#Epididymis#

##Location##  
The epididymis lies along the upper and posterior surface of each **testis**, within the scrotum.  
It connects the testis to the sperm duct (vas deferens).

###Shape###  
A **coiled tubular structure** divided into three regions: head, body, and tail.  
Its soft texture supports delicate storage and maturation processes.

###Parts###  

##Head (Caput)##  
Receives sperm from the testis.  
Energetically, represents **beginning and preparation**.

##Body (Corpus)##  
Area where sperm mature and gain motility.  
Energetically, reflects **growth and refinement**.

##Tail (Cauda)##  
Stores mature sperm until ejaculation.  
Energetically, symbolizes **readiness and potential**.

###Function###  
Stores, matures, and transports sperm from the testis to the sperm duct.  
Maintains optimal conditions for sperm health.  
Energetically, governs **patience, development, and readiness for creation**.
