#Scrotum#

##Location##  
The scrotum is a **pouch of skin and connective tissue** located beneath the tail and between the hind legs.  
It encloses and protects the **testes**, **epididymides**, and **spermatic cords**.

###Shape###  
A **double-chambered sac** divided by a central septum.  
The outer skin is thin and elastic, with sweat glands for temperature regulation.

###Parts###  

##Skin and Dartos Muscle##  
Regulate surface tension and temperature by contracting or relaxing.  
Energetically, represent **adaptation and protection**.

##Spermatic Cord Region##  
Contains blood vessels, lymphatics, and nerves.  
Energetically, symbolizes **connection and flow**.

##Tunica Vaginalis##  
A serous membrane that cushions the testes.  
Energetically, reflects **balance and comfort**.

###Function###  
Protects and maintains an optimal temperature for sperm production.  
Allows free movement and temperature adjustment of the testes.  
Energetically, governs **protection, stability, and adaptability**.
