#Masseter#

##Location##
Located on the lateral surface of the mandible, extending from the zygomatic arch to the angle of the jaw. In the horse, it forms the prominent cheek muscle visible below the facial crest.

##Shape##
Thick, rectangular, and powerful muscle composed of superficial, middle, and deep layers separated by fibrous partitions. Fibers run obliquely and vertically to allow multidirectional chewing motion.

##Function##
Elevates the mandible to close the mouth during chewing
Provides the main force for grinding and crushing fibrous feed
Assists with lateral (side-to-side) movements of the jaw
Stabilizes the temporomandibular joint during mastication
