#Superficial & Deep#

##Location##
Situated on the ventral chest between the forelimbs, connecting the sternum to the humerus and scapula. In the horse, these muscles form part of the thoracic sling that supports the trunk between the forelimbs.

##Shape##
Composed of two main layers:
- **Superficial pectoral muscles (descending and transverse):** Flat and fan-shaped, covering the front and sides of the chest.
- **Deep pectoral muscle (ascending):** Thick and powerful, extending from the sternum to the humerus beneath the superficial layer.
