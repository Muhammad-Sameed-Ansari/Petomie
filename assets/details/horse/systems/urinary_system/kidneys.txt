#Kidneys#

The kidneys are two bean-shaped organs located on either side of the spine.  
They filter blood, remove waste, and regulate fluid, electrolyte, and pH balance.  
Energetically, they represent **strength, balance, and courage**, storing the essence of life and vitality.

###Parts###

##Cortex##  
Outer layer where filtration begins.  
Energetically, it symbolizes clarity and purification.

##Medulla##  
Inner region containing pyramids that concentrate urine.  
Energetically, it reflects focus and transformation.

##Renal Pelvis##  
Collects urine before it passes to the ureters.  
Energetically, it represents transition and readiness for release.

###Function###  
The kidneys purify blood, control water balance, and maintain electrolyte harmony.  
They also produce hormones that regulate blood pressure and red blood cell production.  
Energetically, they sustain **inner strength and emotional equilibrium**, helping the horse adapt to stress and change.

###Common Imbalances###  
Urine changes: dehydration or imbalance.  
Swelling or sensitivity: toxin overload or stagnation.  
Fatigue: poor filtration or low vitality.  
Energetic blockage: fear, insecurity, or loss of grounding.
