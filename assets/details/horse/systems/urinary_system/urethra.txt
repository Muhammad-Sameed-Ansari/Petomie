#Urethra#

The urethra is the tube that carries urine from the bladder to the outside of the body.  
It serves as the final pathway of the urinary system.  
Energetically, it represents **completion, release, and freedom**, guiding the final act of letting go.

###Parts###

##Internal Opening##  
Connects the bladder neck to the urethra.  
Energetically, it symbolizes initiation of release and trust in flow.

##Muscular Wall##  
Smooth muscle controls the passage of urine.  
Energetically, it reflects strength and conscious control.

##External Opening##  
The exit where urine leaves the body.  
Energetically, it represents cleansing, completion, and renewal.

###Function###  
The urethra expels urine from the bladder and maintains flow control.  
Energetically, it completes **the cycle of purification**, finalizing the process of physical and emotional release.

###Common Imbalances###  
Straining: blockage or fear of release.  
Irritation: infection or energetic sensitivity.  
Dribbling: weakness or lack of control.  
Energetic blockage: hesitation or fear of final expression.
