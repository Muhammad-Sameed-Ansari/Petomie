#Urinary Bladder#

The urinary bladder is a muscular sac that stores urine before it is expelled.  
It expands as it fills and contracts during urination.  
Energetically, it represents **release, relief, and emotional cleansing**, symbolizing the ability to let go with ease.

###Parts###

##Apex##  
Top of the bladder that expands during filling.  
Energetically, it reflects openness and acceptance.

##Body##  
Main storage area for urine.  
Energetically, it represents containment and patience.

##Neck##  
Lower portion that leads to the urethra.  
Energetically, it symbolizes readiness and decision to release.

###Function###  
The urinary bladder temporarily stores urine and coordinates controlled release through muscle contractions.  
Energetically, it governs **letting go and renewal**, helping clear physical and emotional waste for new balance.

###Common Imbalances###  
Retention: tension or fear of release.  
Frequent urination: anxiety or energetic overload.  
Infection: irritation or stored frustration.  
Energetic blockage: difficulty releasing emotional residue.
