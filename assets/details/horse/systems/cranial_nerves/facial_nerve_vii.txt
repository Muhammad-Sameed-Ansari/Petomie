#Facial Nerve (VII)#

The horse’s facial nerve governs facial expression, emotion, and sensory perception around the mouth and ears.  
It influences communication, empathy, and social bonding.  
Energetically, it represents emotional flow and authentic expression.

###Location###
Arises from the medulla and travels through the temporal bone to the face, branching widely.

###Parts###
Auriculopalpebral branch – controls ear and eyelid movement.  
Buccal branches – move lips, nostrils, and cheeks.  
Cervical branch – controls muscles of the lower face and jawline.

###Function###
Controls expression, ear motion, and some taste functions.  
Energetically, it encourages openness, emotional communication, and genuine connection.
