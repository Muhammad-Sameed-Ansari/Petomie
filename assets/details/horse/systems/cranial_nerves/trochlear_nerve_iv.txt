#Trochlear Nerve (IV)#

The horse’s trochlear nerve assists in fine-tuning eye motion, enabling smooth coordination and awareness.  
It brings subtle balance and flexibility to vision.  
Energetically, it represents adaptability and seeing situations from different perspectives.

###Location###
Arises from the dorsal aspect of the midbrain and travels to the eye, innervating the superior oblique muscle.

###Parts###
Single branch – supplies the superior oblique muscle, allowing eye rotation and downward movement.

###Function###
Adjusts eye rotation and helps coordinate both eyes in harmony.  
Energetically, it encourages perspective, flexibility, and understanding from multiple viewpoints.
