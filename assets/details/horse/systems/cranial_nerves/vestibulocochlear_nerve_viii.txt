#Vestibulocochlear Nerve (VIII)#

The horse’s vestibulocochlear nerve carries signals of hearing and balance from the inner ear to the brain.  
It connects physical and energetic equilibrium, maintaining orientation and harmony.  
Energetically, it represents awareness, sensitivity, and balance within stillness.

###Location###
Originates from the inner ear and travels to the medulla, combining vestibular (balance) and cochlear (hearing) components.

###Parts###
Vestibular branch – detects motion and body position.  
Cochlear branch – transmits sound vibrations to the brain.

###Function###
Maintains hearing, coordination, and posture.  
Energetically, it aligns the inner and outer world, helping the horse remain calm, aware, and centered.
