#Vagus Nerve (X)#

The horse’s vagus nerve is a major pathway for parasympathetic regulation of internal organs.  
It connects the brain to the heart, lungs, and digestive system, promoting calm and balance.  
Energetically, it represents peace, trust, and alignment with the natural flow of life.

###Location###
Originates from the medulla and travels through the neck, thorax, and abdomen, branching extensively to major organs.

###Parts###
Cardiac branches – regulate heartbeat.  
Pulmonary branches – control breathing rhythm.  
Abdominal branches – support digestion and gut communication.

###Function###
Controls heart rate, breathing, and digestion.  
Energetically, it fosters inner calm, connection, and harmony with life’s rhythm.
