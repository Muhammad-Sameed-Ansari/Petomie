#Abducent Nerve (VI)#

The horse’s abducent nerve supports outward eye movement, ensuring balance and wide peripheral vision.  
It contributes to spatial awareness and the freedom of sight.  
Energetically, it symbolizes exploration and openness to new perspectives.

###Location###
Originates from the medulla and extends to the eye, controlling the lateral rectus muscle.

###Parts###
Single branch – supplies the lateral rectus muscle to move the eye outward.

###Function###
Moves the eye laterally to expand vision and awareness.  
Energetically, it supports freedom, curiosity, and open observation of the environment.
