#Lymph#
Part of the Lymphatic System: A clear fluid circulating through lymph vessels that collects waste and immune cells.  
In the horse, lymph supports detoxification, immunity, and recovery after exertion.

###Parts###

##Composition##  
Contains water, proteins, immune cells, and lipids.  
Energetically, symbolizes renewal and clearing of old energy.

##Circulation##  
Flows from tissues through lymph vessels and nodes back into blood.  
Energetically, reflects patience and release.

###Function###  
Removes waste, supports immune defense, and maintains fluid balance.  
Energetically, allows emotional and physical purification.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling: stagnation or emotional holding.  
Infection: imbalance or overload.  
Energetic blockage: suppressed flow or resistance to change.
