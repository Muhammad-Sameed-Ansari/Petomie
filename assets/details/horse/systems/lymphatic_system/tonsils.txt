#Tonsils#
Part of the Lymphatic System: Lymphoid tissues at the throat that help protect against infection.  
In horses, tonsils are located near the base of the tongue and soft palate.  
Energetically, they represent communication, truth, and protective awareness.

###Parts###

##Palatine and Lingual Tonsils##  
Located around the throat entrance.  
Energetically, symbolize expression and honesty.

###Function###  
Trap pathogens from food and air, triggering immune response.  
Energetically, purify communication and expression of truth.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: suppressed emotion or conflict.  
Swelling: overload or unspoken truth.  
Energetic stagnation: fear of expressing needs.
