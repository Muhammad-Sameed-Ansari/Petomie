#Right Lymphatic Duct#
Part of the Lymphatic System: A vessel draining lymph from the right side of the head, neck, and thorax.  
In horses, it balances the smaller regions of drainage with the main thoracic duct.  
Energetically, it represents direction, symmetry, and trust in natural flow.

###Parts###

##Origin##  
Collects lymph from right upper regions.  
Energetically, symbolizes harmony and balance.

##Termination##  
Empties into the right jugular vein.  
Energetically, reflects connection and balance in duality.

###Function###  
Maintains local lymph drainage and supports immune clarity.  
Energetically, sustains focused release and flow.

###Common Imbalances###  
Blockage: imbalance or rigidity.  
Weak flow: stagnation or emotional fatigue.  
Energetic disharmony: resistance to balance.
