#Spleen#
Part of the Lymphatic System: A large organ filtering blood, storing red cells, and regulating immune balance.  
In horses, it also serves as a blood reservoir during exertion.  
Energetically, it represents strength, renewal, and balance of energy.

###Parts###

##Red Pulp##  
Filters blood and recycles red cells.  
Energetically, symbolizes release and renewal.

##White Pulp##  
Contains immune cells for defense.  
Energetically, represents awareness and discernment.

###Function###  
Filters blood, stores immune cells, and regulates energy reserves.  
Energetically, sustains clarity, vitality, and balanced giving.

###Common Imbalances###  
Enlargement: overwork or emotional burden.  
Weakness: depletion or low vitality.  
Energetic stagnation: trapped emotion or fatigue.
