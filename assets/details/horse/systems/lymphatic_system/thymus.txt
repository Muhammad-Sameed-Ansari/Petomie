#Thymus#
Part of the Lymphatic System: A gland behind the sternum producing T-cells for immunity.  
In horses, it is most active in young foals and recedes with maturity.  
Energetically, it represents trust, resilience, and connection with inner strength.

###Parts###

##Lobes##  
Located near the upper thorax.  
Energetically, symbolize courage and emotional balance.

##Tissue##  
Rich in lymphocytes for immune education.  
Energetically, reflects learning and adaptation.

###Function###  
Trains immune cells and maintains resistance to infection.  
Energetically, supports emotional growth and courage.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weakness: insecurity or lack of faith.  
Overactivity: anxiety or hyper-alertness.  
Energetic imbalance: loss of trust or confidence.
