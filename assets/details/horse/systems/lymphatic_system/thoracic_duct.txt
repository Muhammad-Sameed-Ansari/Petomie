#Thoracic Duct#
Part of the Lymphatic System: The main channel returning lymph to the bloodstream.  
In horses, it carries lymph from the lower body into the venous system.  
Energetically, it represents release, renewal, and integration.

###Parts###

##Origin##  
Begins at the cisterna chyli near the abdomen.  
Energetically, symbolizes grounded flow and transformation.

##Pathway##  
Runs through the thorax to empty near the jugular vein.  
Energetically, reflects the journey from cleansing to renewal.

###Function###  
Returns lymph to circulation, balancing fluids and immunity.  
Energetically, unites purification with restoration.

###Common Imbalances###  
Blockage: restriction or suppressed release.  
Inflammation: inner agitation or stress.  
Energetic stagnation: unresolved emotions or resistance.
