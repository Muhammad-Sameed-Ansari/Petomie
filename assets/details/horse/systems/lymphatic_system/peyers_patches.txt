#Peyer's Patches#
Part of the Lymphatic System: Immune structures in the small intestine monitoring microbes.  
In horses, they help balance gut flora and immunity.  
Energetically, they represent intuition, integration, and harmony within nourishment.

###Parts###

##Clusters##  
Located along the ileum of the small intestine.  
Energetically, symbolize inner awareness and digestion of life experience.

###Function###  
Monitor intestinal health and defend against pathogens.  
Energetically, balance nourishment and protection.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: internal stress or imbalance.  
Weakness: poor digestion or depletion.  
Energetic stagnation: distrust in inner guidance.
