#Gonads (Testes and Ovaries)#

##Parts##
###Testes (Male)###
Located in the scrotum; produce **sperm** and **testosterone** for reproduction and muscle development.  

###Ovaries (Female)###
Located near the kidneys; release **eggs** and produce **estrogen** and **progesterone**, regulating cycles and pregnancy.  

###Reproductive Ducts###
Transport sperm or eggs and help deliver hormones to reproductive organs.  

##Function##
Control **fertility, reproductive behavior, and hormone balance**.  
Testosterone enhances muscle tone and drive; estrogen and progesterone regulate cycles and foal development.  
Together, they influence energy, growth, and temperament.
