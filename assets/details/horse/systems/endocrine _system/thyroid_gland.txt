#Thyroid Gland#

##Parts##
###Right and Left Lobes###
Located on both sides of the trachea below the larynx, connected by a narrow isthmus.  

###Thyroid Follicles###
Store and release the hormones **T3 (triiodothyronine)** and **T4 (thyroxine)**, which control metabolism and growth.  

###Parafollicular Cells###
Produce **calcitonin**, which lowers calcium levels in the blood when they rise too high.  

##Function##
Regulates **energy use, metabolism, heart rate, and temperature**.  
Supports proper muscle tone, coat condition, and digestive health.  
Thyroid balance ensures vitality, stamina, and normal growth in young horses.
