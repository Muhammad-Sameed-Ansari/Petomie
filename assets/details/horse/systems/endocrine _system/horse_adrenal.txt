#Adrenal Glands (Cortex and Medulla)#

##Parts##
###Adrenal Cortex###
Outer region producing steroid hormones:  
- **Cortisol:** Manages stress and metabolism.  
- **Aldosterone:** Balances salt and water for proper blood pressure.  
- **Sex Steroids:** Contribute to muscle strength and mood stability.  

###Adrenal Medulla###
Inner region producing **adrenaline (epinephrine)** and **noradrenaline (norepinephrine)** for the body’s emergency “fight or flight” response.  

##Function##
Regulates **stress adaptation, metabolism, hydration, and alertness**.  
Adrenal hormones prepare the horse for physical effort, danger, or excitement.  
Chronic stress may overwork this gland, reducing immunity and energy reserves.
