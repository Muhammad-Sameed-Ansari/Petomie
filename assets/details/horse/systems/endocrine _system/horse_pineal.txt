#Pineal Gland#

##Parts##
###Pinealocytes###
Specialized cells that secrete **melatonin**, primarily at night in response to darkness.  

###Nerve Pathways###
Receive signals from the eyes, detecting day length and light levels.  

###Blood Capillaries###
Distribute melatonin through the bloodstream to influence other glands.  

##Function##
Produces **melatonin**, a hormone that regulates **sleep cycles**, **seasonal breeding**, and **circadian rhythm**.  
It helps synchronize reproductive activity with daylight hours and promotes rest, recovery, and mood balance.
