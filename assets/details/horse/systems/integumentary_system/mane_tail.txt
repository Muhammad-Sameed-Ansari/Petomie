#Mane and Tail#

The mane and tail of the horse are extensions of the skin and coat, providing protection, communication, and energetic balance.  
They express vitality and serve as natural energy conductors.  
Energetically, they symbolize strength, beauty, and flow of life force.

###Parts###

##Mane##  
Thick hair along the neck protecting it from insects and weather.  
Energetically, it represents pride, self-expression, and energetic shielding.

##Tail##  
Extends from the dock, aiding in communication and fly protection.  
Energetically, it reflects grounding, release, and connection to the root chakra.

##Hair Roots##  
Anchor and nourish the mane and tail through strong follicles.  
Energetically, they symbolize rooted strength and emotional stability.

###Function###  
The mane and tail protect, express emotion, and maintain energy flow.  
They help regulate temperature and convey communication signals.  
Energetically, they balance upper and lower body energy, blending intuition with grounding.

###Common Imbalances###  
Brittle or Thinning Hair: stress or nutritional deficiency.  
Tangled or Matted Areas: stagnant energy or emotional knots.  
Excessive Shedding: release of old patterns or hormonal shift.  
Tail Swishing: irritation, stress, or emotional agitation.
