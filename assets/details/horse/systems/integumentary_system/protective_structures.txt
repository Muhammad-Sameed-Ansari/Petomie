##Location##
Found on the limbs, muzzle, and areas of high contact or movement.

##Shape##
Composed of **keratinized, hardened skin structures** specialized for support and protection.

##Parts##

###1. Hooves###
- Hard keratinized capsules covering the distal digits.  
- **Function:** Protect the feet and absorb shock during movement.  

###2. Chestnuts and Ergots###
- Small, calloused keratinized growths on legs.  
- **Function:** Vestigial protective remnants from evolutionary ancestors.  

###3. Muzzle Skin###
- Thickened, sensitive area on the nose and lips.  
- **Function:** Aids in tactile exploration and feeding.  

##Function##
- **Support:** Provides stability and cushioning during locomotion.  
- **Protection:** Prevents injury from rough terrain.  
- **Sensation:** Enhances environmental awareness.
