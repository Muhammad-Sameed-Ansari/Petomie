##Location##
Distributed throughout the **dermis**, often associated with hair follicles or located in specialized skin regions.

##Shape##
Microscopic tubular glands specialized for secretion and cooling.

##Parts##

###1. Sebaceous Glands###
- Attached to hair follicles.  
- **Function:** Secrete sebum to lubricate the hair and skin.  

###2. Sweat Glands###
- Present across most of the skin, especially the neck, shoulders, and flanks.  
- **Function:** Control temperature through sweat evaporation.  

###3. Mammary Glands###
- Located between the hind limbs in mares.  
- **Function:** Produce milk for foals.  

##Function##
- **Thermoregulation:** Maintains body temperature during exertion.  
- **Lubrication:** Keeps skin and coat supple.  
- **Excretion:** Eliminates small amounts of waste through sweat.
