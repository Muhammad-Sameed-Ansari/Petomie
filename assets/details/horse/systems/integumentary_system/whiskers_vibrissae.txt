##Location##
Located around the **muzzle, eyes, and chin**.

##Shape##
Long, thick hairs anchored deeply into the skin with a nerve-rich blood sinus at the base.

##Parts##

###1. Follicle-Sinus Complex###
- Connective tissue capsule with sensory nerves and blood vessels.  
- **Function:** Detects vibration, distance, and texture.  

###2. Whisker Shaft###
- Long keratinized structure projecting outward.  
- **Function:** Acts as a tactile probe for spatial awareness.  

##Function##
- **Navigation:** Helps horses sense objects and surfaces near the muzzle.  
- **Sensation:** Detects vibration and environmental feedback.  
- **Communication:** Provides information about surroundings when grazing or interacting.
