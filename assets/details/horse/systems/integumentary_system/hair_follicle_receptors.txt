#Hair Follicle Receptors#

Hair follicle receptors are sensory nerve endings around hair roots that detect motion and pressure.  
They provide awareness of touch and environmental changes.  
Energetically, they represent sensitivity, perception, and intuitive communication.

###Parts###

##Nerve Endings##  
Surround each follicle and detect even light movements.  
Energetically, they symbolize alertness and subtle awareness.

##Hair Bulb##  
Base of the follicle where the hair grows and connects to blood vessels and nerves.  
Energetically, it represents creation and connection between inner life and outer response.

##Sebaceous Connection##  
Oil glands linked to follicles for protection and lubrication.  
Energetically, this reflects cooperation and balanced interaction.

###Function###  
Follicle receptors help the horse sense insects, air movement, and touch.  
Energetically, they serve as extensions of awareness and communication with the environment.

###Common Imbalances###  
Hypersensitivity: irritation or emotional overstimulation.  
Dull Sensation: reduced nerve response or blocked energy.  
Hair Loss at Follicles: inflammation, toxins, or disconnection from sensory field.
