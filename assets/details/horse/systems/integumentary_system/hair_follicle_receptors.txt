##Location##
Surround the **base of each hair follicle** within the dermis.

##Shape##
Network of fine sensory nerves encircling the follicle root.

##Parts##

###1. Merkel Cells###
- Specialized cells that sense touch and pressure.  
- **Function:** Detect gentle movement and tactile contact.  

###2. Sensory Nerve Endings###
- Fine nerve fibers near the follicle.  
- **Function:** Respond to motion and vibration.  

##Function##
- **Sensation:** Detects movement and external stimuli.  
- **Communication:** Helps horses respond to environmental changes.
