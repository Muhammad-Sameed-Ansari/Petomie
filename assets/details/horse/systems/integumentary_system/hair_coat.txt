##Location##
Covers the **entire body** except for the muzzle, lips, and hooves.

##Shape##
A **uniform coat of short or long hairs** depending on season, supported by skin follicles.

##Parts##

###1. Guard Hairs###
- Long, coarse hairs forming the outer layer.  
- **Function:** Protect against rain, dust, and insects.  

###2. Undercoat###
- Softer hair layer that thickens in winter.  
- **Function:** Provides insulation during colder months.  

###3. Mane and Tail###
- Long, thick hairs on the neck and tail base.  
- **Function:** Protects against insects and sun exposure.  

##Function##
- **Temperature regulation:** Controls heat retention and dissipation.  
- **Protection:** Shields skin from UV and environmental damage.  
- **Communication:** Indicates health and social cues through shine and condition.
