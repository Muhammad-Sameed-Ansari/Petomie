#Bile Ducts#
Part of the Hepatobiliary System: Channels that carry bile from the liver to the small intestine.  
Energetically, they represent purification, release, and allowing life energy to flow freely.

###Parts###

##Intrahepatic Ducts##  
Small ducts that gather bile from hepatocytes.  
Energetically, represent inner clarity and order.

##Hepatic Ducts##  
Carry bile outward from liver lobes.  
Energetically, symbolize communication and alignment.

##Common Bile Duct##  
Delivers bile into the duodenum.  
Energetically, reflects release, transition, and renewal.

###Function###  
Transport bile for digestion and detoxification.  
Ensure smooth fat metabolism and elimination of waste.

###Common Imbalances###  
Obstruction: frustration or digestive tension.  
Inflammation: emotional pressure or overcontrol.  
Energetic stagnation: fear of release or flow.
