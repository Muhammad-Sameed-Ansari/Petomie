###Parts###

##Lobes##  
The lungs are divided into lobes that expand and contract during breathing.  
Energetically, they represent expansion and the ability to receive.

##Alveoli##  
Tiny air sacs where gas exchange occurs with the blood.  
Energetically, they symbolize transformation and renewal with every breath.

##Pleura##  
A thin membrane surrounding each lung, reducing friction during movement.  
Energetically, it reflects protection and harmony in motion.

##Pulmonary Vessels##  
Carry oxygen-poor blood to the lungs and oxygen-rich blood back to the heart.  
Energetically, they embody flow, nourishment, and life circulation.
