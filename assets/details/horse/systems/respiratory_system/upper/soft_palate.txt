###Function###  
The soft palate directs airflow into the nasal passages and prevents food from entering the airway.  
In horses, its long structure contributes to obligate nasal breathing.  
Energetically, it supports balance, smooth transitions, and ease of direction.

###Common Imbalances###  
Dorsal displacement: airway noise or exercise intolerance.  
Inflammation: irritation or blockage.  
Stiffness: difficulty coordinating breath and swallow.
