#Pituitary Gland#

The horse’s pituitary gland is a small endocrine organ at the brain’s base, known as the master gland.  
It controls hormone production in other glands.  
Energetically, it symbolizes harmony, leadership, and connection between higher and lower systems.

###Parts###

##Anterior Lobe##  
Secretes growth hormone, ACTH, and reproductive hormones.  
Energetically, it represents manifestation and creation.

##Posterior Lobe##  
Releases antidiuretic hormone and oxytocin.  
Energetically, it symbolizes emotional bonding and flow.

###Function###  
The pituitary gland regulates growth, reproduction, metabolism, and water balance.  
It works closely with the hypothalamus to maintain stability.  
Energetically, it acts as the conductor of the hormonal orchestra, ensuring unity across systems.

###Common Imbalances###  
Hormonal Disorders: communication failure in the axis.  
Growth Issues: imbalance in hormone release.  
Water Retention: misregulation of ADH.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect disharmony or difficulty coordinating different aspects of life.
