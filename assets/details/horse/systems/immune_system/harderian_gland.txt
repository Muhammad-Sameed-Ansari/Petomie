#Harderian Gland#

##Location##
Located behind the horse’s eye within the orbit, closely associated with the nictitating membrane (third eyelid).  
It contributes to eye lubrication and immune defense in the surrounding tissues.

###Shape###
A soft, elongated, lobulated gland nestled deep in the orbital space.

###Parts###

##Glandular Lobes##
Produce immune‑active secretions containing enzymes and antibodies.  
Energetically represent protection, clarity, and gentle cleansing.

##Ducts##
Transport glandular secretions to the surface of the eye and third eyelid.  
Energetically symbolize flow, renewal, and purification.

###Function###
The Harderian gland supports ocular immunity by producing protective secretions rich in antimicrobial components.  
It lubricates the eye, enhances third‑eyelid movement, and helps maintain a stable tear film.  
Energetically it protects vision, intuition, and inner clarity.

###Common Imbalances###
Dry eye, irritation, third‑eyelid swelling, infection, reduced tear production.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Soft silver, pale blue  
Crystals: Moonstone, celestite  
Element: Water  
Affirmation: “My vision is protected, nourished, and clear.”
