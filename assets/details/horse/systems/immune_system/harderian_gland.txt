#Harderian Gland#

##Location##
Located behind the eye, associated with the third eyelid (nictitating membrane).

###Shape###
Soft, multilobed gland.

###Parts###

##Secretory Lobules##
Produce immune-rich and lubricating secretions for the eye.  
Energetically symbolize **clarity, intuition, and subtle protection**.

###Function###
Supports eye immunity, tear film stability, and surface lubrication.  
Energetically enhances **insight, perception, and emotional clarity**.

###Applies to##
All horses.

###Common Imbalances###
Eye irritation, tearing, swelling of the third eyelid.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Indigo, soft blue  
Crystals: Lapis lazuli, celestite  
Element: Light  
Affirmation: “I see clearly and remain protected.”
