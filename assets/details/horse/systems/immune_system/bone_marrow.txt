#Bone Marrow#

##Location##
Found inside large bones such as the femur, humerus,
sternum, ribs, pelvis, and vertebrae.

###Shape###
Soft, spongy, semi-gelatinous tissue with a rich blood
supply filling the medullary cavities of bones.

###Parts###

##Red Marrow##
Active in producing red blood cells, white blood cells,
and platelets. Energetically represents life force, grounding, and
renewal.

##Yellow Marrow##
Fat‑rich tissue that can revert to red marrow during
high demand. Energetically symbolizes stored strength, reserve power,
and protection.

###Function###
Bone marrow produces all immune cells (lymphocytes,
monocytes, neutrophils, eosinophils, basophils). It also supports oxygen
transport, clotting, detoxification, and tissue repair. Energetically
maintains foundation, vitality, and deep-rooted stability.

###Common Imbalances###
Anemia, low immunity, chronic infections, toxin
overload, nutrient deficiencies.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Deep red, earthy brown
Crystals: Garnet, hematite, bloodstone Element: Earth Affirmation: “My
body renews itself with strength and harmony.”
