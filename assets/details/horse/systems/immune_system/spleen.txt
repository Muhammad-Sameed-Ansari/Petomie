#Spleen#

##Location##
Located on the left side of the abdomen, attached to the
stomach by the gastrosplenic ligament. It sits beneath the ribs and
expands or contracts depending on blood storage needs.

###Shape###
A long, crescent-shaped, flat organ with a smooth, dark red
surface.

###Parts###

##Red Pulp##
Filters blood, removes old or damaged red blood cells, and
stores large volumes of blood. Energetically represents purification,
release, and life force flow.

##White Pulp##
Contains lymphoid tissue that produces and activates
lymphocytes. Energetically symbolizes awareness, protection, and
energetic alertness.

###Function###
The spleen filters blood, stores red blood cells,
produces lymphocytes, and initiates immune responses. It also maintains
blood quality and helps regulate circulation. Energetically maintains
clarity, protection, and harmonious flow.

###Common Imbalances###
Low immunity, fatigue, anemia, poor circulation,
sluggish digestion, stress-related weakness.
