#Spleen#

##Location##
Located on the left side of the abdomen near the stomach.

###Shape##
Crescent-shaped, firm, dark red organ.

###Parts###

##White Pulp##
Produces immune cells and detects blood pathogens.  
Energetically: **clarity, insight, purification**.

##Red Pulp##
Stores red blood cells and filters blood.  
Energetically: **grounding, renewal, and inner strength**.

###Function###
Filters blood, stores large red blood cell reserves, supports immunity, and regulates energy output during exercise.  
Energetically maintains **power, centeredness, and resilience**.

###Applies to##
All horses (high oxygen storage capacity).

###Common Imbalances###
Infections, anemia, poor performance, enlarged spleen.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Gold, yellow  
Crystals: Citrine, amber  
Element: Earth  
Affirmation: “I am centered, strong, and full of inner power.”
