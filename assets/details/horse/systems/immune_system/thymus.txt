#Thymus#

##Location##
Located in the upper chest (cranial mediastinum), sitting
in front of the heart and behind the sternum. Most active in young
horses and gradually shrinks with age.

###Shape###
A soft, lobulated, pale-pink gland with two main lobes
extending along the trachea.

###Parts###

##Cortex##
Outer region where immature T‑cells multiply. Energetically
represents growth, awakening, and new potential.

##Medulla##
Inner region where T‑cells mature and become specialized.
Energetically symbolizes refinement, wisdom, and inner strength.

###Function###
The thymus trains and matures T‑lymphocytes, the immune
system’s key defense cells. It teaches the body to distinguish between
self and non‑self, preventing autoimmunity. Energetically supports
clarity, protection, and balanced boundaries.

###Common Imbalances###
Weakened immunity, slow healing, chronic
infections, stress‑related immune suppression.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Soft pink, white‑gold
Crystals: Rose quartz, selenite
Element: Air
Affirmation: “I am protected, clear, and balanced from within.”
