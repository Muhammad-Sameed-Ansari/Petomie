#Thymus#

##Location##
Located in the upper chest in front of the heart; most active in young horses.

###Shape###
Soft multi-lobed gland.

###Parts###

##Cortex##
Outer layer for early T-cell development.  
Energetically: **growth, protection, and boundary creation**.

##Medulla##
Inner region for T-cell maturation.  
Energetically: **clarity, courage, inner resilience**.

##Thymic Lobules##
Organized compartments for immune maturation.  
Energetically: **order, guidance, and structured development**.

###Function###
Creates and trains T-cells, forming early immunity and long-term immune memory.  
Energetically supports **confidence, grounded energy, and emotional balance**.

###Applies to##
All horses (especially foals).

###Common Imbalances###
Weak immunity in young horses, frequent infections, thymic atrophy with age.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Turquoise, light green  
Crystals: Amazonite, aquamarine  
Element: Air  
Affirmation: “My inner strength grows as I grow.”
