#MALT (Mucosa-Associated Lymphoid Tissue)#

##Location##
Found throughout the mucosal surfaces of the mouth, nose, airway, and intestines.

###Shape###
Diffuse lymphoid tissue within mucosa.

###Parts###

###Function###
Protects mucosal surfaces, triggers early immune responses, and supports respiratory and digestive immunity.

All horses.
