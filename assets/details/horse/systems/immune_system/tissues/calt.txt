#CALT (Conjunctiva-Associated Lymphoid Tissue)#

##Location##
Located beneath the eyelids within the conjunctival tissue.

###Shape###
Small, soft lymphoid clusters.

###Parts###

##Lymphoid Follicles##
Detect airborne and environmental pathogens entering the eye.  
Energetically symbolize **clarity, awareness, and intuitive sight**.

###Function###
Protects the eye surface, supports tear film immunity, and prevents infection.  
Energetically supports **clear perception, gentle awareness, and emotional openness**.

###Applies to##
All horses.

###Common Imbalances###
Conjunctivitis, irritation from dust or wind, allergic reactions.

Crystals: Sodalite, celestite  
Element: Light
