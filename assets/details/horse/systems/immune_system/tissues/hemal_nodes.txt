#Hemal Nodes#

##Location##
Scattered along major blood vessels in the abdomen and pelvis.

###Shape###
Tiny, dark-red spleen-like nodules.

###Parts###

##Blood-Filled Sinuses##
Filter pathogens directly from blood.  
Energetically symbolize **deep cleansing and resilience**.

###Function###
Act like miniature spleens, filtering blood and supporting immune responses.  
Energetically support **renewal, stability, and inner strength**.

###Applies to##
Horses (present but small and less developed).

###Common Imbalances###
Swelling, inflammation, or infection.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Burgundy, dark red  
Crystals: Garnet  
Element: Earth  
Affirmation: “I am grounded, cleansed, and supported from within.”
