#SALT (Skin-Associated Lymphoid Tissue)#

##Location##
Distributed throughout the skin, especially in dermal layers.

###Shape###
Diffuse immune cells and small clusters beneath the skin’s surface.

###Parts###

##Dendritic Cells##
Sense pathogens on the skin.  
Energetically: **awareness and boundary strength**.

##Langerhans Cells##
Protect upper skin layers.  
Energetically: **sensitivity and self-protection**.

###Function###
Provides immune protection at the skin barrier and prevents external pathogens from entering.  
Energetically maintains **self-identity, boundaries, and emotional safety**.

###Applies to##
All horses.

###Common Imbalances###
Dermatitis, sweet itch, fungal infections, allergies.
