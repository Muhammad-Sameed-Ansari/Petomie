#Motor Nerves#
Motor nerves in the horse transmit signals from the brain and spinal cord to the muscles.  
They enable movement, posture, and expression through action.  
Energetically, they represent strength, motivation, and freedom of expression.

###Parts###

##Upper and Lower Motor Neurons##  
Carry commands from brain to muscles.  
Energetically, symbolize action aligned with will.

###Function###  
Control muscle contraction and body coordination.  
Energetically, connect inner intent with outer movement.

###Common Imbalances###  
Weakness: fear or hesitation.  
Spasms: over-effort or stress.  
Energetic stagnation: trapped energy or repressed drive.
