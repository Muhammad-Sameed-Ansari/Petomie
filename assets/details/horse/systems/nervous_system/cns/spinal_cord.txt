#Spinal Cord#
The horse’s spinal cord transmits signals between the brain and the body.  
It runs from the skull through the spine, branching to every limb.  
Energetically, it represents communication, strength, and alignment — the core channel of life force.

###Parts###

##Segments##  
Cervical, thoracic, lumbar, sacral, and caudal regions.  
Energetically, symbolize grounded energy and flow.

###Function###  
Relays motor and sensory impulses, supporting movement and reflexes.  
Energetically, channels energy between instinct and action.

###Common Imbalances###  
Back pain: resistance or misalignment.  
Weak reflexes: blocked flow or loss of trust.  
Energetic stagnation: disrupted connection between intention and motion.
