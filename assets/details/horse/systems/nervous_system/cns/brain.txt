#Brain#
The horse’s brain forms the control center of the entire nervous system.  
It interprets sensory information, controls movement, and guides emotion and instinct.  
Energetically, it represents awareness, freedom, and unity between mind and body — the inner rider guiding all motion.

###Parts###

##Cerebrum##  
Processes behavior, memory, and voluntary motion.  
Energetically, symbolizes awareness and clarity.

##Diencephalon##  
Regulates hormones, temperature, and emotional response.  
Energetically, reflects harmony and connection between body and intuition.

###Function###  
Coordinates sensory information, controls behavior, and maintains balance between thought and instinct.  
Energetically, guides clear perception and emotional calm.

###Common Imbalances###  
Stress: overstimulation or confusion.  
Fear: disconnection or loss of trust.  
Energetic stagnation: scattered awareness or suppressed expression.
