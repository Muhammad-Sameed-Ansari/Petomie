#Sympathetic System#
The sympathetic system prepares the horse for action and alertness.  
It activates energy in response to stimulation or stress.  
Energetically, it represents courage, readiness, and instinctive protection.

###Parts###

##Sympathetic Chain##  
Runs along the spine controlling organs and blood flow.  
Energetically, symbolizes vigilance and strength.

###Function###  
Increases heart rate, expands lungs, and redirects blood to muscles.  
Energetically, empowers movement and survival instincts.

###Common Imbalances###  
Overdrive: nervousness or reactivity.  
Fatigue: burnout from constant alertness.  
Energetic imbalance: overcontrol or fear of release.
