#Pharynx#

##Location##
A short muscular passage behind the mouth and nasal cavity, connecting to the esophagus and larynx.

##Shape##
A funnel-shaped chamber shared by the digestive and respiratory systems.

##Parts##
- **Nasopharynx:** Upper portion behind nasal cavity.
- **Oropharynx:** Central portion behind the mouth.
- **Laryngopharynx:** Lower portion leading to esophagus.

##Function##
- Directs food from the mouth to the esophagus.
- Closes off airways during swallowing.
- Prevents aspiration of feed into the lungs.
- Coordinates breathing and swallowing reflexes.
