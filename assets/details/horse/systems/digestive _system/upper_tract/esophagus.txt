#Esophagus#

##Location##
A long, muscular tube running from the pharynx down the left side of the neck to the stomach.

##Shape##
A smooth, elastic tube with circular and longitudinal muscle layers. It passes through the diaphragm into the stomach.

##Parts##
- **Cervical Section:** Runs along the neck.
- **Thoracic Section:** Travels through the chest.
- **Cardiac Sphincter:** Strong valve preventing regurgitation.

##Function##
- Moves food to the stomach via peristaltic waves.
- Prevents vomiting due to the strong cardiac sphincter.
- Connects upper and lower digestive tracts.
- Ensures one-way food movement.
