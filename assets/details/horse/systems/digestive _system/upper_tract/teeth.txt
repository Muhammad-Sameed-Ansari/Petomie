#Teeth#

##Location##
Set within the upper and lower jaws (maxilla and mandible), lining the sides of the mouth.

##Shape##
Large, flat-crowned teeth with rough surfaces, perfectly adapted for grinding fibrous plant material.

##Parts##
- **Incisors:** Cut and gather grass.
- **Canines (in males):** Small and pointed; limited use in eating.
- **Premolars and Molars:** Grind and crush feed.
- **Enamel & Dentine:** Layers that protect and strengthen the teeth.

##Function##
- Grind roughage and grains into small particles.
- Prepare feed for easy swallowing and digestion.
- Encourage saliva production through chewing.
- Maintain dental wear through constant grazing.
