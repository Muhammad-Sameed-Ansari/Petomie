#Tongue#

##Location##
Occupies the floor of the mouth, anchored to the hyoid bone.

##Shape##
Long, muscular, and strong with a slightly rough surface. Covered in papillae that help grip and move food.

##Parts##
- **Tip:** Helps gather and position feed.
- **Body:** Moves food during chewing.
- **Root:** Anchors tongue within the mouth.

##Function##
- Moves feed between the teeth for grinding.
- Helps form the food bolus for swallowing.
- Mixes food with saliva.
- Aids taste perception and moisture balance.
