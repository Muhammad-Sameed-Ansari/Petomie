#Stomach#

##Location##
Located on the left side of the upper abdomen, just behind the diaphragm.

##Shape##
A small, single-chambered organ shaped like a J. It has thick muscular walls and is divided into glandular and non-glandular regions.

##Parts##
- **Cardia:** Entry point from esophagus.
- **Fundus:** Expands to store feed.
- **Body:** Main area for mixing food and gastric juices.
- **Pylorus:** Exit leading to small intestine.
- **Margo Plicatus:** Internal line separating glandular and non-glandular areas.

##Function##
- Mixes feed with digestive enzymes and acid.
- Begins protein digestion.
- Kills bacteria and microorganisms.
- Stores feed temporarily before it moves to the small intestine.
- Prepares fibrous material for enzymatic breakdown.
