#Pancreas#

##Location##
Situated near the duodenum, between the stomach and the large intestine.

##Shape##
A soft, lobulated gland with both endocrine and exocrine functions.

##Parts##
- **Exocrine Portion:** Produces digestive enzymes.  
- **Endocrine Portion:** Contains the islets of Langerhans, producing insulin and glucagon.

##Function##
- Secretes enzymes for digestion of fats, proteins, and carbohydrates.  
- Releases hormones to regulate blood sugar levels.  
- Aids nutrient absorption and energy balance.
