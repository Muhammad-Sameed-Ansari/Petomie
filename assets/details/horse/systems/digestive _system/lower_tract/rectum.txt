#Rectum#

##Location##
Located at the terminal part of the large intestine, connecting the colon to the anus within the pelvic cavity.

##Shape##
A straight, muscular tube lined with mucus-producing glands to ease waste passage.

##Parts##
- **Upper Section:** Stores feces temporarily.  
- **Lower Section:** Leads to the anal canal.  

##Function##
- Stores formed feces prior to elimination.  
- Stimulates defecation reflexes.  
- Controls timing of waste expulsion through sphincter coordination.
