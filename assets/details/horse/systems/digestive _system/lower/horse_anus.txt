#Anus#

##Location##
At the end of the digestive tract, just below the tail, marking the external opening of the intestinal system.

##Shape##
A circular muscular opening controlled by two sphincters.  

##Parts##
- **Internal Anal Sphincter:** Involuntary smooth muscle control.  
- **External Anal Sphincter:** Voluntary skeletal muscle control.  

##Function##
- Regulates release of feces.  
- Maintains continence.  
- Protects the body from bacterial contamination.
