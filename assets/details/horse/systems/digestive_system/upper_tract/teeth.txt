#Teeth#

The horse’s teeth are designed for continuous grazing, grinding fibrous food throughout life.  
They play a vital role in digestion and communication.  
Energetically, they symbolize strength, clarity, and decision-making.

###Parts###

##Incisors##  
Front teeth for cutting grass.  
Energetically, they represent taking in and beginning new experiences.

##Canines##  
Smaller pointed teeth, more prominent in males.  
Energetically, they symbolize strength and protection.

##Premolars and Molars##  
Back teeth for grinding food.  
Energetically, they reflect patience and thoroughness.

###Function###  
The teeth grind, crush, and break down food, aiding digestion.  
They also play a role in communication through facial expression and bit contact.  
Energetically, they represent clarity, power, and the ability to “chew through” life’s challenges.

###Common Imbalances###  
Uneven Wear: imbalance in use or stress.  
Sharp Points: discomfort or internal friction.  
Loss or Decay: weakness or neglect.  
Energetically, dental issues may show difficulty processing or expressing personal power.
