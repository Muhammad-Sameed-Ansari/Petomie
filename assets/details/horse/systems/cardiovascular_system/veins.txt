#Veins#

The **horse’s veins** form an extensive network that returns deoxygenated blood to the heart.  
They have thinner walls and depend on valves and muscle contractions to assist blood flow against gravity.  
Energetically, veins symbolize **balance, integration, and emotional return**, representing the inward movement of experience and reflection.

###Parts###

##Vena Cava##
Divided into cranial and caudal sections returning blood to the right atrium.  
Energetically, represents **return to the heart’s center and emotional integration**.

##Pulmonary Veins##
Bring oxygenated blood from the lungs to the left atrium.  
Energetically, embody **renewal and peace**, connecting air and heart energy.

##Jugular Veins##
Prominent veins along the neck, returning blood from the head.  
Energetically, reflect **release of thoughts and expression of truth**.

##Femoral and Saphenous Veins##
Return blood from the limbs and lower body.  
Energetically, symbolize **grounding and stability through circulation**.

###Function###
Veins return blood to the heart under low pressure and serve as reservoirs that regulate volume.  
They ensure steady flow and prevent pooling.  

Energetically, veins represent **rest, recovery, and harmony between action and stillness**.

###Common Imbalances###
Venous congestion: Swelling or sluggish flow.  
Varicosities: Weak vessel tone.  
Energetic stagnation: Holding onto emotions or fatigue.  
Energetic deficiency: Difficulty restoring balance.
