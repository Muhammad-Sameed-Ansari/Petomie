#Arteries#

The **horse’s arteries** form a vast, high-pressure network that distributes oxygen-rich blood from the heart to every part of the body.  
These strong, elastic vessels are essential for maintaining the horse’s stamina and performance.  
Energetically, arteries represent **vitality, purpose, and the outward expression of power**, driving the energy of life through movement.

###Parts###

##Aorta##
The main artery emerging from the left ventricle.  
Distributes blood to the entire body through branching arteries.  
Energetically, symbolizes **direction and leadership**, guiding energy with purpose.

##Pulmonary Arteries##
Carry deoxygenated blood from the right ventricle to the lungs.  
Energetically, represent **renewal and transformation**, connecting breath and spirit.

##Carotid Arteries##
Supply the head and brain with oxygenated blood.  
Energetically, embody **clarity, focus, and higher awareness**.

##Coronary Arteries##
Feed the heart muscle itself.  
Energetically, reflect **self-nourishment and emotional balance**.

##Femoral, Iliac, and Brachial Arteries##
Deliver blood to the limbs, supporting strength and motion.  
Energetically, they represent **movement, endurance, and forward drive**.

###Function###
Arteries transport oxygenated blood under high pressure from the heart to all tissues, sustaining muscular strength and vitality.  
They also help regulate blood pressure and maintain rhythmic circulation.  

Energetically, they symbolize **drive, creation, and the force of life** moving outward with purpose.

###Common Imbalances###
Constriction: Reduced performance or cold extremities.  
Inflammation: Arteritis, stiffness, or restlessness.  
Blockage: Fatigue or poor oxygenation.  
Energetic blockage: Resistance to progress or control.  
Energetic depletion: Lack of inspiration or inner drive.
