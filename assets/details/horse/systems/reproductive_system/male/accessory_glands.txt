#Accessory Glands#

The accessory glands support reproduction by adding fluids and nutrients to semen.  
They ensure proper sperm function and transport.  
Energetically, they represent **support, cooperation, and nourishment**, providing essential balance in creative expression.

###Parts###

##Vesicular Glands##  
Produce nutrient-rich fluid that supports sperm vitality.  
Energetically, they symbolize generosity and teamwork.

##Prostate Gland##  
Adds protective enzymes and helps maintain pH balance.  
Energetically, it represents stability and harmony in action.

##Bulbourethral Glands##  
Secrete lubrication for smooth passage of semen.  
Energetically, they reflect ease, readiness, and gentle flow.

###Function###  
The accessory glands produce seminal fluids that nourish, protect, and transport sperm.  
Energetically, they sustain **balanced giving and harmonious support**, ensuring creative energy flows with ease.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: infection or emotional tension.  
Blockage: stagnation or poor circulation.  
Reduced secretion: dehydration or low energy.  
Energetic blockage: imbalance in giving and receiving.
