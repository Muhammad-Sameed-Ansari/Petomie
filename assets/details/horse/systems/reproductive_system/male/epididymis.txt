#Epididymis#

The epididymis is a long coiled tube where sperm mature and are stored before ejaculation.  
It ensures sperm mobility and readiness for fertilization.  
Energetically, it represents **patience, refinement, and preparation**, guiding creation toward purpose.

###Parts###

##Head##  
Receives immature sperm from the testes.  
Energetically, it symbolizes learning and early development.

##Body##  
Sperm mature and gain mobility.  
Energetically, it reflects refinement and transformation.

##Tail##  
Stores mature sperm until ejaculation.  
Energetically, it embodies readiness and disciplined waiting.

###Function###  
The epididymis matures and stores sperm, preparing them for reproduction.  
Energetically, it supports **focus and intention**, aligning potential with action.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling: inflammation or stagnation.  
Pain: infection or emotional tension.  
Reduced sperm quality: imbalance or fatigue.  
Energetic blockage: impatience or blocked expression.
