#Prepuce#

##Location##
Covers and protects the **penis** when it is not erect.  
Situated along the **ventral abdomen**, between the hind limbs.

###Shape###
A **double fold of skin** that forms an outer and inner sheath.  
The outer surface is covered with hair, while the inner lining is smooth and moist.

##Parts##

###1. Outer Fold###
The external layer continuous with the abdominal skin.

###2. Inner Fold###
The inner mucosal layer that encloses the penis and keeps it moist.

###3. Preputial Orifice###
The **opening** at the distal end of the prepuce that allows extension of the penis during erection.

###Function###
Protects the penis from **injury and contamination**.  
Maintains **cleanliness and moisture** of the penile surface.  
Retracts smoothly to permit **extension during mating**.  
Prevents debris and infection from entering the preputial cavity.
