#Testes#

The testes are paired organs that produce sperm and testosterone.  
They are housed in the scrotum and are vital for fertility and hormonal balance.  
Energetically, they represent **vitality, creativity, and grounded power**, generating both life and strength.

###Parts###

##Seminiferous Tubules##  
Sites of sperm production.  
Energetically, they symbolize creation and renewal.

##Interstitial Cells##  
Produce testosterone for masculine traits.  
Energetically, they reflect confidence and willpower.

##Tunica Albuginea##  
Protective capsule surrounding each testis.  
Energetically, it represents containment and security of power.

###Function###  
The testes produce sperm and hormones that regulate reproduction and vitality.  
Energetically, they sustain **creative force and stability**, supporting strength with balanced confidence.

###Common Imbalances###  
Swelling or tenderness: inflammation or stagnation.  
Hormonal imbalance: stress or energetic depletion.  
Reduced fertility: temperature imbalance or blocked energy.  
Energetic blockage: lack of confidence or creative frustration.
