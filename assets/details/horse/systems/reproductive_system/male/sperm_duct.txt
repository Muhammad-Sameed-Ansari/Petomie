#Vas Deferens (Sperm Duct)#

###Location###
Connects the epididymis to the urethra inside the body, running through the pelvic area.

###Shape###
A narrow, muscular tube that pushes sperm forward during ejaculation.

###Parts###
- **Beginning:** Joins the epididymis.
- **Middle Section:** Passes through the body wall.
- **End (Ampulla):** Joins the urethra near the bladder.

###Function###
- Carries sperm from the epididymis to the urethra.
- Stores sperm for a short time.
- Pushes sperm forward with strong muscular contractions.
