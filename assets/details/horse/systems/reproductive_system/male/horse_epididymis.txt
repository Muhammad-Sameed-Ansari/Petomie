#Epididymis#

###Location###
Lies along the upper and back edge of each testis.

###Shape###
A long, coiled tube that is divided into three parts where sperm mature and are stored.

###Parts###
- **Head:** Collects sperm from the testis.
- **Body:** Where sperm mature and become able to move.
- **Tail:** Stores sperm until the horse mates.

###Function###
- Helps sperm mature and gain movement ability.
- Stores sperm before they are released.
- Moves sperm into the next tube (vas deferens) when needed.
