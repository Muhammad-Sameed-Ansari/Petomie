#Accessory Glands#

###Location###
Found near the bladder and the beginning of the urethra inside the pelvis.

###Shape###
A group of small glands that make the fluid part of semen, which helps protect and feed sperm.

###Parts###
- **Seminal Vesicles:** Produce a sugary fluid that feeds sperm.
- **Prostate Gland:** Adds liquid that keeps sperm healthy and mobile.
- **Bulbourethral Glands:** Make slippery fluid that cleans and lubricates the urethra.

###Function###
- Create seminal fluid that mixes with sperm to form semen.
- Feed and protect sperm.
- Make the passage smooth and safe for sperm to travel.
