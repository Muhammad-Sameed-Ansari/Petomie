#Penis#

##Location##
Extends along the **ventral midline** between the hind limbs.  
When not erect, it lies within the **prepuce**, a protective sheath of skin.

###Shape###
A **musculocavernous organ** composed of erectile tissue that enlarges greatly during erection.  
The glans penis is **mushroom-shaped** and prominent when engorged.

##Parts##

###1. Root###
Anchors the penis to the pelvis through the **crura** and **ischiocavernosus muscles**.

###2. Body###
Contains erectile tissues (**corpora cavernosa** and **corpus spongiosum**) that fill with blood during erection.

###3. Glans Penis###
The expanded distal portion that enlarges during mating to ensure secure coupling.

###4. Urethra###
Passes through the penis, carrying both **urine and semen**.
