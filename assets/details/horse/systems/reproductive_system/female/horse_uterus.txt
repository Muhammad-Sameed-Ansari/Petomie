#Uterus#

###Location###
Situated partly within the pelvic cavity and extending forward into the abdominal cavity.
It lies between the bladder and rectum and is suspended by the broad ligament.

###Shape###
A Y-shaped, muscular organ consisting of two uterine horns and a short uterine body.
The uterine wall is thick, vascular, and elastic.

###Parts###
- **Uterine Horns:** Long branches extending toward the ovaries.
- **Uterine Body:** Short central portion connecting to the cervix.
- **Endometrium:** Inner mucosal layer with glands for embryo nourishment.
- **Myometrium:** Thick muscular layer responsible for contractions.
- **Perimetrium:** Outer protective connective tissue covering.

###Function###
- Serves as the site for embryo implantation and fetal development.
- Nourishes and protects the developing foal.
- Contracts to expel the foal and placenta during parturition.
- Responds to hormonal signals during the estrous cycle.
