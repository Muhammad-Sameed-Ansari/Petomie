#Ovaries#

The ovaries are paired organs that produce eggs and hormones like estrogen and progesterone.  
They regulate the reproductive cycle and influence mood and vitality.  
Energetically, they represent **creation, rhythm, and feminine balance**, holding the spark of potential life.

###Parts###

##Follicles##  
Structures that develop and release eggs.  
Energetically, they symbolize growth, renewal, and readiness for creation.

##Corpus Luteum##  
Temporary gland that produces progesterone after ovulation.  
Energetically, it reflects stability, grounding, and faith in timing.

##Ovarian Stroma##  
Supportive tissue that nourishes developing cells.  
Energetically, it represents foundation and strength behind creativity.

###Function###  
The ovaries produce reproductive cells and control hormonal balance.  
Energetically, they embody **cyclic flow and renewal**, guiding emotional and physical harmony.

###Common Imbalances###  
Irregular cycles: stress or hormonal imbalance.  
Cysts: stagnation or unexpressed emotions.  
Pain or tension: energetic congestion.  
Energetic blockage: fear of creativity or self-expression.
