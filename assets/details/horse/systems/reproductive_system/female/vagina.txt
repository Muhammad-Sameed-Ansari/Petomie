#Vagina#

The vagina is the muscular canal that connects the cervix to the external genitalia.  
It serves as the passage for breeding and foaling, maintaining protection and flexibility.  
Energetically, it represents **reception, trust, and transformation**, bridging the inner and outer worlds.

###Parts###

##Vaginal Wall##  
Elastic layers of muscle and connective tissue.  
Energetically, they symbolize strength and openness.

##Vaginal Mucosa##  
Moist lining protecting against infection.  
Energetically, it reflects nurturing and emotional sensitivity.

##Vaginal Fornix##  
Upper region near the cervix that collects fluids.  
Energetically, it represents transition and flow between realms.

###Function###  
The vagina allows reproduction, passage of the foal, and elimination.  
Energetically, it sustains **receptivity and renewal**, allowing safe expression and transformation.

###Common Imbalances###  
Dryness: hormonal imbalance or lack of emotional nourishment.  
Infection: stagnation or unprocessed emotion.  
Pain or tightness: trauma or resistance.  
Energetic blockage: fear of intimacy or vulnerability.
