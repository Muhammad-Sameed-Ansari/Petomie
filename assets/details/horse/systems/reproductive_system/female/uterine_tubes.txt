#Uterine Tubes#

The uterine tubes, or oviducts, connect the ovaries to the uterus.  
They are the site of fertilization and early embryo transport.  
Energetically, they represent **transition, flow, and communication**, guiding new beginnings toward manifestation.

###Parts###

##Infundibulum##  
Funnel-shaped opening that captures the egg.  
Energetically, it symbolizes receptivity and readiness.

##Ampulla##  
Site of fertilization where sperm meets egg.  
Energetically, it reflects union and creation.

##Isthmus##  
Narrow section leading to the uterus.  
Energetically, it represents focus and guidance toward purpose.

###Function###  
The uterine tubes transport eggs and embryos between ovary and uterus.  
Energetically, they support **flow, creation, and direction**, bridging inspiration and realization.

###Common Imbalances###  
Blockage: physical or energetic stagnation.  
Inflammation: irritation or emotional tension.  
Weak motility: hormonal or energetic depletion.  
Energetic blockage: fear of new beginnings.
