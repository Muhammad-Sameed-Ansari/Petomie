#Placenta#

The placenta forms during pregnancy, connecting mare and foal.  
It transfers nutrients, oxygen, and hormones while removing waste.  
Energetically, it represents **connection, nourishment, and sacred exchange**, symbolizing unity between two beings.

###Parts###

##Chorion##  
Outer membrane attaching to the uterine wall.  
Energetically, it represents grounding and attachment.

##Allantois##  
Carries waste away from the foal.  
Energetically, it symbolizes release and purification.

##Amnion##  
Encases the foal, cushioning it within fluid.  
Energetically, it reflects safety, care, and divine protection.

###Function###  
The placenta supports life by exchanging gases, nutrients, and waste.  
Energetically, it maintains **balance and connection**, nurturing both independence and unity.

###Common Imbalances###  
Retained placenta: stagnation or resistance to letting go.  
Infection: imbalance or toxic buildup.  
Poor development: lack of nourishment or energy flow.  
Energetic blockage: difficulty releasing attachment.
