#Cervix#

###Location###
Connects the uterus and vagina, positioned within the pelvic cavity.
It serves as the gateway between the internal uterus and the external environment.

###Shape###
A thick, muscular, cylindrical passage about 5â€“7 cm long, with firm folds that can open or close depending on hormonal state.

###Parts###
- **Cervical Canal:** The lumen passing through the cervix.
- **External Os:** Opening into the vagina.
- **Internal Os:** Opening into the uterus.
- **Mucosal Folds:** Create a seal during pregnancy.

###Function###
- Acts as a barrier protecting the uterus from infection.
- Opens during estrus to allow sperm entry.
- Closes tightly during pregnancy to maintain uterine integrity.
- Dilates during foaling for the passage of the foal.
