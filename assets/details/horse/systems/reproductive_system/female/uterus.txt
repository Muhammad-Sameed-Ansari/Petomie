#Uterus#

The uterus is the hollow muscular organ where the foal develops.  
It provides protection, nourishment, and hormonal support throughout pregnancy.  
Energetically, it represents **creation, nurturing, and divine feminine strength**, the sacred space of manifestation.

###Parts###

##Uterine Horns##  
Extend from the body of the uterus toward the ovaries.  
Energetically, they represent balance and expansion in creation.

##Uterine Body##  
Central chamber that supports fetal growth.  
Energetically, it symbolizes wholeness and unity.

##Endometrium##  
Inner lining that nourishes and supports the embryo.  
Energetically, it reflects receptivity and compassion.

###Function###  
The uterus supports conception, gestation, and parturition.  
Energetically, it holds **the creative potential of life**, grounding inspiration into form.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: infection or unprocessed emotion.  
Poor tone: weakness or hormonal imbalance.  
Retained material: stagnation or attachment.  
Energetic blockage: creative suppression or fear of creation.
