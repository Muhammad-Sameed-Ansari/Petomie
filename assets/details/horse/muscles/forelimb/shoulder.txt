###Parts###

##Trapezius##  
Raises and rotates the shoulder blade.  
Energetically, it symbolizes elevation, freedom, and readiness.

##Deltoid##  
Moves the forelimb outward and forward.  
Energetically, it reflects courage and expression in action.

##Supraspinatus##  
Stabilizes the shoulder joint and aids extension.  
Energetically, it represents steady confidence and balanced strength.

##Infraspinatus##  
Prevents shoulder overextension and provides flexibility.  
Energetically, it embodies adaptability and harmony in movement.

###Function###  
The shoulder muscles stabilize and move the scapula and forelimb, controlling stride and impact.  
Energetically, they anchor trust and strength, supporting smooth and confident forward motion.

###Common Imbalances###  
Tightness: restricted reach or stiffness.  
Weakness: shortened stride or instability.  
Pain on lift: overuse or strain.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect hesitation, burden, or loss of confidence in movement.
