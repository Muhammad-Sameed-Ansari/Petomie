#Rectus Thoracis#

##Location##
Situated along the **ventral thoracic wall**, running from the **first rib** to about the **fourth rib**.  
Lies next to the sternum and continues with the rectus abdominis.

###Shape###
A narrow, strap-like muscle with longitudinal fibers.  
Runs parallel to the ventral midline.

##Function##
Assists **inspiration** by pulling the ribs forward to expand the thoracic cavity.  
Supports ventral chest stability during breathing.
