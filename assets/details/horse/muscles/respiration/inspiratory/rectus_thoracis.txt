#Rectus Thoracis#

The rectus thoracis muscle lies along the front ribs, connecting them to the sternum.  
It assists with inhalation by lifting the ribs forward and upward.  
Energetically, it represents expansion, vitality, and the forward movement of breath.

###Parts###

##Rib Attachments##  
Pull the ribs forward during inhalation.  
Energetically, they symbolize progress and awakening energy.

##Sternal Connection##

Energetically, it reflects heart-led expansion and life rhythm.
