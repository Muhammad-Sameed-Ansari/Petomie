#Serratus Dorsalis Cranialis#

##Location##
Located on the **dorsolateral thoracic wall**, beneath the rhomboideus and trapezius.  
Extends from the **thoracolumbar fascia** to the **cranial ribs**.

###Shape###
A thin, fan-shaped muscle with **fibers running caudoventrally**.  
Covers the upper portion of the first five ribs near their vertebral attachments.

##Function##
Draws the cranial ribs upward and forward to enlarge thoracic space.  
Supports **inspiration** during active breathing and helps maintain thoracic flexibility.
