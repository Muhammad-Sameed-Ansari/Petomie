#External Intercostal Muscles#

The external intercostal muscles lie between the ribs, lifting them during inhalation.  
They expand the chest cavity, allowing fresh air to fill the lungs.  
Energetically, they represent openness, inspiration, and the lightness of new beginnings.

###Parts###

##Outer Rib Fibers##  
Lift and expand the ribs during inhalation.  
Energetically, they symbolize openness and willingness to receive.

##Intercostal Spaces##  
Maintain structure and rhythm of rib motion.  
Energetically, they reflect harmony and balance within breath.

###Function###  
The external intercostals assist inhalation, coordinating with the diaphragm to draw air in smoothly.  
Energetically, they embody the breath of inspiration, the welcoming of life energy with ease and grace.

###Common Imbalances###  
Stiff ribs: limited expansion.  
Tension: shallow or uneven breath.  
Soreness: overuse or imbalance.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect resistance to inspiration or guarded openness.
