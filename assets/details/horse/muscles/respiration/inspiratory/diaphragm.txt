#Diaphragm#

##Location##
Forms the muscular partition between the **thoracic and abdominal cavities**, extending from the **sternum, ribs, and lumbar vertebrae** to the **central tendon**.  
Separates the lungs and heart from abdominal organs.

###Shape###
A **broad, dome-shaped muscle** with a tendinous center and muscular periphery.  
The convex surface faces the thoracic cavity; the concave surface faces the abdomen.

##Function##
Primary muscle of **inspiration**.  
Contracts and flattens to increase thoracic volume and draw air into the lungs.  
Also aids abdominal compression for defecation, urination, and foaling.
