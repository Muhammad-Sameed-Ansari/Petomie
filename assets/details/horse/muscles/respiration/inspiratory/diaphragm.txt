#Diaphragm#

The diaphragm is the main muscle of respiration in the horse.  
It forms a dome-shaped partition between the chest and abdominal cavities, expanding and contracting with each breath.  
Energetically, it represents flow, renewal, and the sacred rhythm of life itself.

###Parts###

##Muscular Dome##  
Contracts to draw air into the lungs.  
Energetically, it symbolizes expansion and receiving new energy.

##Central Tendon##  
Anchors the diaphragm and connects upper and lower body.  
Energetically, it reflects unity and balance between body and spirit.

##Crura Attachments##  
Secure the diaphragm to the spine, aiding deep breathing.  
Energetically, they embody stability and grounding within breath.

###Common Imbalances###

Energetically, imbalance may reflect resistance to change or emotional holding.
