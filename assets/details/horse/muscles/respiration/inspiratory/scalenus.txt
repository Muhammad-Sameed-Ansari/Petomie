#Scalenus#

##Location##
Situated on the **lateral neck**, connecting the **cervical vertebrae** to the **first few ribs**.  
Lies deep to the brachiocephalicus and omotransversarius.

###Shape###
Long, strap-like muscle divided into dorsal, middle, and ventral parts.  
Fibers run obliquely downward and backward.

##Function##
Assists **inspiration** by elevating the first ribs.  
Stabilizes the neck and ribcage during breathing and aids slight flexion of the cervical spine.
