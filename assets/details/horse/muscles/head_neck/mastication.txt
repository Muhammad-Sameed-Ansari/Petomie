##Pterygoid Muscles##

###Function###

###Common Imbalances###  
Tightness: stress, dental issues, or tension.  
Uneven chewing: imbalance in jaw alignment.  
Tenderness: overuse or bruxism.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect unprocessed emotions or difficulty integrating experience.
