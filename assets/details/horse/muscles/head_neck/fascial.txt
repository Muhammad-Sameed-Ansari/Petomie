###Parts###

##Orbicularis Oris##  
Surrounds the mouth, controlling lip movement.  
Energetically, it symbolizes communication and giving voice to emotion.

##Levator Nasolabialis##  
Lifts the upper lip and dilates the nostrils.  
Energetically, it reflects curiosity, openness, and readiness to receive.

##Zygomaticus##  
Pulls the corners of the mouth back, creating expressions of tension or relaxation.  
Energetically, it represents expression and social connection.

##Orbicularis Oculi##  
Closes the eyelids, protecting the eyes and showing emotional states.  
Energetically, it embodies awareness and emotional sensitivity.

###Function###  
The facial expression muscles enable social interaction, emotional display, and fine control of the lips and nostrils.  
Energetically, they translate inner feeling into visible expression, allowing connection between horses and humans.

###Common Imbalances###
