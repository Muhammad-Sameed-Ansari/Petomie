###Parts###

##Brachiocephalicus##  
Moves the head and neck forward and sideways.  
Energetically, it symbolizes communication and expression of intention.

##Splenius##  
Lifts and extends the neck, supporting posture.  
Energetically, it represents pride and confident bearing.

##Sternocephalicus##  
Flexes the neck and helps in lowering the head.  
Energetically, it reflects humility, grounding, and calm submission.

##Rhomboideus and Serratus Ventralis##  
Support the neck and suspend the trunk between the forelimbs.  
Energetically, they embody trust and balance.

###Function###  
The neck muscles maintain posture, allow head movement, and connect the forelimbs with the skull.  
Energetically, they bridge thought and action, balancing communication with strength and calm focus.

###Common Imbalances###  
Stiffness: tension, training imbalance, or emotional stress.  
Uneven bend: asymmetry in musculature.  
Tight crest: lack of relaxation or resistance.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect poor communication between mind and body or emotional rigidity.
