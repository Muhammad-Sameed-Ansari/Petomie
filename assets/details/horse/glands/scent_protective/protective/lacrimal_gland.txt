#Lacrimal Gland#

The horse’s lacrimal gland is the primary tear-producing gland, located in the upper outer corner of the orbit.  
It produces the watery portion of tears, essential for eye moisture, cleansing, and protection.  
Energetically, the lacrimal gland represents **release, emotional cleansing, and openness**.

###Parts###

##Acinar Units##  
Clusters of cells producing fluid tears.  
Energetically, they symbolize **emotional purification and renewal**.

##Ductal Network##  
Carries tears to the eye’s surface.  
Energetically, it represents **flow, communication, and fluid expression**.

##Orbital Capsule##  
Connective tissue supporting and surrounding the gland.  
Energetically, it reflects **containment and gentle emotional structure**.

###Function###  
The lacrimal gland clears debris, prevents dryness, and protects the cornea from infection through tear production.  
Energetically, it supports **emotional release, clarity, and adaptability**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Reduced tearing: dryness, irritation — energetically tied to **suppressed emotion**.  
Excess tearing: irritation or stress — energetically linked to **overflowing feelings or heightened sensitivity**.
