#Third Eyelid Gland#

The horse’s third eyelid gland, located at the base of the nictitating membrane, produces a significant portion of the tear film.  
It provides lubrication, immune protection, and cleansing to the eye.  
Energetically, this gland represents **instinct, subtle awareness, and hidden protection**.

###Parts###

##Lymphoid Tissue##  
Contains immune cells that defend against pathogens.  
Energetically, it symbolizes **inner strength and intuitive defense**.

##Glandular Lobules##  
Produce watery secretions for eye lubrication.  
Energetically, they represent **fluidity, adaptability, and gentle support**.

##Nictitating Membrane Base##  
Anchors the gland to the third eyelid.  
Energetically, it reflects **stability and unseen guidance**.

###Function###  
This gland contributes to tear production, supports immune defense, and helps cleanse the eye’s surface.  
Energetically, it enhances **intuition, subtle perception, and protective instincts**.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: swelling or redness — energetically linked to **over-alertness or internal conflict**.  
Reduced secretion: dryness — tied to **blocked intuition or emotional fatigue**.
