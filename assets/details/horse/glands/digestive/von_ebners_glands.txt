#Von Ebner’s Glands#

The horse’s Von Ebner’s glands are small glands near the circumvallate papillae on the tongue.  
They secrete watery fluid that cleans and refreshes the taste buds.  
Energetically, they represent clarity, enjoyment, and refined awareness.

###Parts###

##Glandular Acini##  
Produce fluid that rinses the taste buds.  
Energetically, they symbolize sensitivity and flow.

##Ducts##  
Carry secretions into the grooves around papillae.  
Energetically, they reflect clarity and openness.

##Papillae Association##  
Supports taste perception.  
Energetically, it represents refined appreciation and insight.

###Function###  
Von Ebner’s glands maintain taste function and mouth freshness.  
Energetically, they enhance perception and joy in experience.

###Common Imbalances###  
Dryness: loss of enjoyment or dull perception.  
Inflammation: irritation or emotional overstimulation.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect blocked appreciation or confusion.
