#Brunner’s Glands#

The horse’s Brunner’s glands are found in the duodenum, the first part of the small intestine.  
They secrete an alkaline mucus that neutralizes stomach acid and protects the lining.  
Energetically, they represent calm transition, protection, and balance.

###Parts###

##Submucosal Glands##  
Produce mucus and bicarbonate to reduce acidity.  
Energetically, they symbolize comfort and neutrality.

##Ducts##  
Carry secretions to the intestinal surface.  
Energetically, they reflect flow and harmony.

##Lobules##  
Clusters maintaining steady secretion.  
Energetically, they represent peace and equilibrium.

###Function###  
Brunner’s glands neutralize stomach acid and prepare the intestine for digestion.  
Energetically, they bring calmness, patience, and inner peace.

###Common Imbalances###  
Low secretion: irritation or poor protection.  
Inflammation: stress or imbalance.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect agitation during change.
