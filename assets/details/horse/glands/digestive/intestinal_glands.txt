#Intestinal Glands#

The horse’s intestinal glands, or crypts of Lieberkühn, line the small and large intestines.  
They produce mucus and enzymes to support digestion and absorption.  
Energetically, they represent flow, renewal, and adaptability.

###Parts###

##Crypts of Lieberkühn##  
Microscopic glands that secrete digestive fluids and renew the intestinal lining.  
Energetically, they symbolize continuous renewal and vitality.

##Goblet Cells##  
Produce mucus for lubrication and protection.  
Energetically, they represent comfort and emotional ease.

##Enteroendocrine Cells##  
Release hormones that regulate digestion.  
Energetically, they reflect balance and harmony in inner communication.

###Function###  
Intestinal glands aid nutrient absorption and maintain digestive balance.  
Energetically, they promote flow, integration, and harmony between giving and receiving.

###Common Imbalances###  
Inflammation: irritation or sensitivity.  
Diarrhea: over-release or emotional overflow.  
Constipation: tension or resistance.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect holding on or fear of change.
