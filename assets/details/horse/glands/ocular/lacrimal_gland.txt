#Lacrimal Glands#

##Location##
Located above and to the outer side of each eye, under the upper eyelid near the edge of the eye socket.

###Shape###
Two soft, oval glands with small lobes, light in color.

##Function##
Produce tears that clean and moisten the eyes, protect from dust and infection, and nourish the cornea for clear vision.
