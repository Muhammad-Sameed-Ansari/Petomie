#Conjunctival Glands#

The horse’s conjunctival glands are small mucus-producing glands within the eye’s conjunctiva.  
They maintain moisture across the eye surface and inner eyelids.  
Energetically, they represent clarity, emotional balance, and gentle flow.

###Parts###

##Palpebral Glands##  
Located in the inner eyelid lining.  
Energetically, they symbolize protection and awareness.

##Bulbar Glands##  
Cover the white part of the eye (sclera).  
Energetically, they represent openness and perception.

##Mucous Ducts##  
Deliver lubrication to the eye surface.  
Energetically, they reflect smooth communication and flow.

###Function###  
Conjunctival glands maintain moisture and clarity on the eye’s surface.  
Energetically, they support emotional comfort and balanced vision.

###Common Imbalances###  
Dryness: strain or emotional depletion.  
Infection: irritation or stress.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect blurred clarity or blocked emotional release.
