#Conjunctival Glands#

##Location##
Located in the inner lining of the eyelids and over the white of the eye, inside the pink conjunctiva membrane.

###Shape###
Tiny clusters of mucous glands spread throughout the eyelid lining.

##Function##
Produce mucus that mixes with tears to keep the eye surface smooth, remove dust, and support comfort.
