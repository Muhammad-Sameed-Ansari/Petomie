#Gland of the Third Eyelid#

##Location##
Found at the base of the third eyelid in the inner corner of the eye, near the nose, beneath a protective fold of tissue.

###Shape###
A small, rounded gland surrounded by connective tissue.

##Function##
Produces tears and protective fluids that keep the eye moist and clean, working with the lacrimal gland to prevent dryness or irritation.
