#Tarsal (Meibomian) Glands#

##Location##
Located along the edges of the upper and lower eyelids, opening at the eyelash margins.

###Shape###
Tiny tube-like glands arranged in straight rows along the eyelid edges.

##Function##
Produce an oily substance that prevents tears from evaporating and keeps the eyelids flexible and smooth.
