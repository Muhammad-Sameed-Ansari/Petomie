#Urethral Glands (Littré’s Glands)#

The horse’s urethral glands are microscopic glands lining the urethra.  
They secrete mucus that protects and lubricates the urinary passage.  
Energetically, they represent purification, flow, and release.

###Parts###

##Mucous Cells##  
Produce protective secretions.  
Energetically, they symbolize gentle cleansing and harmony.

##Ducts##  
Carry mucus to the urethra.  
Energetically, they represent direction and emotional release.

##Tissue Support##  
Surrounding muscle and connective tissue providing structure.  
Energetically, it reflects flexibility and resilience.

###Function###  
These glands keep the urethra moist and protected during urination and reproduction.  
Energetically, they help cleanse and maintain balance in expression and release.

###Common Imbalances###  
Irritation: tension or blocked flow.  
Inflammation: infection or imbalance.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect resistance to release or emotional stagnation.
