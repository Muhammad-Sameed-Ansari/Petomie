#Mandibular Glands#

##Location##
Situated between the lower jaws (mandibles) beneath the tongue region, near the throat latch.

###Shape###
Two elongated, smooth glands located close to the midline beneath the tongue.

##Function##
Produce mixed saliva containing both watery and mucous components that help lubricate food and aid swallowing.
