#Parotid Glands#

##Location##
Located just below the base of each ear, extending along the angle of the jaw toward the throat latch area. Their ducts open into the mouth near the upper molars.

###Shape###
Large, V-shaped glands with a pale yellow color and lobulated texture.

##Function##
Produce watery saliva that lubricates and moistens food, aiding digestion and chewing. Also assist in cooling during heavy work by increasing salivation.
