#Sublingual Glands#

##Location##
Located along the floor of the mouth beneath the tongue, extending forward from the mandibular glands.

###Shape###
Two elongated, narrow glands with numerous small ducts opening under the tongue.

##Function##
Produce mucus-rich saliva that lubricates the mouth and helps smooth chewing and swallowing.
