#Lymph Nodes#

The horse’s lymph nodes are small, bean-shaped structures that filter lymph fluid.  
They are central to immune defense and the body’s cleansing system.  
Energetically, they represent discernment, purification, and flow.

###Parts###

##Mandibular Nodes##  
Under the jaw, filtering head and neck regions.  
Energetically, they symbolize communication and clarity.

##Prescapular and Inguinal Nodes##  
At shoulders and groin, filtering limbs and body extremities.  
Energetically, they represent protection and strength.

##Deep Thoracic and Abdominal Nodes##  
Filter internal organs and vital systems.  
Energetically, they reflect inner stability and awareness.

###Function###  
Lymph nodes remove waste, destroy pathogens, and maintain immunity.  
Energetically, they promote inner cleansing and self-awareness.

###Common Imbalances###  
Enlargement: immune activation or stress.  
Tenderness: detoxification in progress.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect emotional overload or unprocessed energy.
