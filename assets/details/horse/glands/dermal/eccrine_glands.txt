#Eccrine Glands#

##Location##
Present mainly in the frog of the hoof and around the lips. These glands are rare and specialized in horses.

###Shape###
Tiny coiled glands embedded within thick protective tissue.

##Function##
Secrete thin watery fluid that keeps the hoof frog moist and supports comfort, traction, and resilience under pressure.
