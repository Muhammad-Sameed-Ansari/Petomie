#Sebaceous Glands#

##Location##
Found throughout the skin, especially near the mane, tail, face, and back. They attach to hair follicles and open into the same pore.

###Shape###
Tiny sac-like glands filled with oily cells that rupture to release sebum.

##Function##
Produce sebum, an oily substance that keeps the skin and coat soft, shiny, and waterproof while protecting against bacteria and dryness.
