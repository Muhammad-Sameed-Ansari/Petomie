#Sweat (Apocrine) Glands#

##Location##
Found throughout the skin but densest in the neck, shoulders, and flanks, opening into hair follicles.

###Shape###
Coiled tubular glands located deep within the dermis, surrounded by capillaries.

##Function##
Produce sweat to cool the body, regulate temperature, and excrete waste through the skin.
