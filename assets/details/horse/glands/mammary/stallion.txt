#Mammary Glands (Stallion)#

The stallion has small, undeveloped mammary glands located between the forelimbs.  
They are non-functional but anatomically present.  
Energetically, they represent balance between masculine protection and gentle care.

##Glandular Base##  
Minimal tissue beneath the skin.  
Energetically, it symbolizes latent nurturing energy.

##Nipples##  
Two small, non-functional teats.  
Energetically, they represent emotional sensitivity and balance.

##Surrounding Skin##  
Smooth, hair-covered area with protective tissue.  
Energetically, it reflects subtle connection and gentleness.

###Function###  
Stallion mammary glands are vestigial and serve no milk-producing role.  
Energetically, they hold the blueprint for compassion and balance between giving and strength.

Infection: imbalance or stress.  
Energetically, imbalance may reflect repressed tenderness or imbalance in masculine-feminine harmony.
