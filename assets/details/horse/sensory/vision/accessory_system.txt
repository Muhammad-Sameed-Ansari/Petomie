#Accessory Structures#

The horse’s accessory structures support the eyes by protecting, stabilizing, nourishing, and positioning them for wide-field vision and environmental awareness.
They are essential for the horse’s prey-animal vision, motion detection, and safety.
Energetically, they represent protection, vigilance, sensitivity, and trust in the surroundings.

###Parts###

##Eyelids##
Protect the eye from injury, debris, and excessive light while spreading tears evenly.
Energetically, symbolize boundaries, alertness, and emotional protection.

##Eyelashes##
Prevent dust, insects, and foreign particles from entering the eye.
Energetically, reflect awareness and subtle defense.

##Conjunctiva##
A thin mucous membrane lining the eyelids and covering the sclera.
Provides lubrication and immune defense.
Energetically, represents adaptability and gentle protection.

##Nictitating Membrane (Third Eyelid)##
Provides additional protection and tear distribution, especially during movement or threat.
Energetically, symbolizes instinctive protection and survival awareness.

##Lacrimal Glands##
Produce tears to lubricate, cleanse, and nourish the eye.
Energetically, represent emotional release, cleansing, and regulation.

##Nasolacrimal Duct (Tear Ducts)##
Drain excess tears from the eye into the nasal cavity.
Energetically, reflect emotional flow and release.

##Cornea##
The transparent outer layer allowing light to enter the eye and aiding focus.
Energetically, embodies clarity and perception of the environment.

##Sclera##
The tough outer layer providing structure and protection.
Energetically, symbolizes stability, grounding, and resilience.

##Iris##
Regulates pupil size and light entry.
Energetically, represents adaptability and balance.

##Lens##
Focuses light onto the retina.
Energetically, reflects focus, direction, and perspective.

##Ciliary Body##
Adjusts lens shape and produces aqueous humor.
Energetically, symbolizes flexibility and adjustment.

##Extraocular Muscles##
Control eye movement and alignment.
Energetically, represent direction, coordination, and attention.

###Function###
Protect the eye, maintain moisture, regulate light entry, and support wide-field vision and movement detection.
Energetically, these structures foster vigilance, emotional safety, and trust in the environment.

###Common Imbalances###
Eye irritation, excessive tearing, corneal injury, eyelid disorders, or sensitivity to light.
Energetically, imbalance may reflect fear, hypervigilance, or feeling unsafe.
