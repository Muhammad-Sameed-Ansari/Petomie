#Humerus#

##Location##  
The humerus is located in the **upper forelimb**, connecting the **shoulder blade (scapula)** to the **radius and ulna** at the elbow joint.  
It lies diagonally between the chest and the forearm, forming part of the main limb support structure.

###Shape###  
A **strong, slightly curved bone** with prominent muscular attachments and broad joint surfaces.

###Parts###

##Proximal End##  
Forms the **shoulder joint** with the scapula via the glenoid cavity.  
Energetically, represents **connection between strength and freedom**.

##Shaft##  
Thick central portion providing leverage for muscles of the chest and forelimb.  
Energetically, symbolizes **stability and focus**.

##Distal End##  
Forms the **elbow joint** with the radius and ulna.  
Energetically, reflects **movement, direction, and coordination**.

###Function###  
Supports movement of the shoulder and elbow, transmits force from the body to the forelimb, and provides muscle attachment for propulsion and lift.  
Energetically, governs **strength in action and balanced effort**.
