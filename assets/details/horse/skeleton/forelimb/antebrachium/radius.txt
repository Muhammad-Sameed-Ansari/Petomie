#Radius#

##Location##  
Located in the **forearm**, extending from the **elbow to the knee (carpus)**.  
It lies in close connection with the ulna, forming the primary weight-bearing bone of the forelimb.  

###Shape###  
A **strong, cylindrical bone** slightly flattened for muscular attachment.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal End##  
Forms the elbow joint with the humerus.  
Energetically, represents **foundation and initiation**.  

##Shaft##  
Long and supportive structure between joints.  
Energetically, symbolizes **direction and focus**.  

##Distal End##  
Forms the upper part of the carpal joint.  
Energetically, reflects **movement and transition**.  

###Function###  
Supports body weight and facilitates forward motion.  
Energetically, governs **strength, precision, and flow of movement**.
