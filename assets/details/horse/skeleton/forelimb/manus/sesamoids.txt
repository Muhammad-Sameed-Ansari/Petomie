#Sesamoids#

##Location##  
Located at the **fetlock joint** (proximal sesamoids) and behind the **coffin joint** (navicular bone, or distal sesamoid).  

###Shape###  
Small, **rounded bones** embedded within tendons, acting as pulleys for movement.  

###Parts###  

##Proximal Sesamoids##  
Pair of bones behind the fetlock joint.  
Energetically, represent **support and balance under pressure**.  

##Distal Sesamoid (Navicular Bone)##  
Located behind the coffin joint within the hoof.  
Energetically, symbolizes **stability and smooth motion**.  

###Function###  
Reduce friction on tendons, aid joint flexion, and distribute force evenly through the limb.  
Energetically, govern **grace, support, and equilibrium**.

**Chakra:** Root and Sacral Chakras.  
**Meridian:** Bladder and Kidney Meridians.  
**Emotional Meaning:** Adaptability and steady flow under stress.  
**Balanced Energy:** Ease and fluid motion.  

###Common Imbalances###  
Navicular syndrome or tendon strain.  
Energetically, **overstrain, tension, or imbalance in support**.
