Forms part of the thoracic spine and connects to the ribs, supporting the chest cavity.

###Function###  
Supports the ribcage and protects thoracic organs.  
Allows flexibility while maintaining structural strength.  
Energetically, governs **balance, protection, and flow of life force**.
