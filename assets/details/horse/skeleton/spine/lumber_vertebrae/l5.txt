#L5#

##Location##  
Located in the **lower back** of the horse, between the thoracic and sacral regions.  
The L5 vertebra supports strength, flexibility, and energy transfer from the body to the hindquarters.

###Shape###  
A **large, broad vertebra** with strong transverse processes for muscle and ligament attachment.  
Designed for stability and propulsion.

##Processes##

Energetically, symbolize **strength and grounded flow**.

###Function###  
Supports the back, stabilizes movement, and transmits power from the hindquarters forward.  
Energetically, governs **stability and movement from the core** and stability of will.
