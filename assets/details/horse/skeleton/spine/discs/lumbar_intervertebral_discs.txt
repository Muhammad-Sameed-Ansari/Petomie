#Lumbar Intervertebral Discs#

##Location##
Between each lumbar vertebra from L1–L6, forming the powerful and flexible loin region responsible for propulsion and engagement.

###Shape###
Broad, thick fibrocartilage pads designed for maximum shock absorption, stability, and load-bearing during hind-end power transfer.

###Parts###

##Annulus Fibrosus##
Robust outer fibrocartilage ring providing containment, stability, and strength.
Energetically represents grounded structure, boundaries, and dependable support.

##Nucleus Pulposus##
Soft, gel-like inner core absorbing force and distributing pressure evenly.
Energetically reflects adaptability, fluid motion, and emotional flexibility.
