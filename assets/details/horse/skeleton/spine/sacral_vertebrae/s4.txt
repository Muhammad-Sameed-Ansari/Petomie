#S4#

##Location##  
The fourth sacral vertebra lies toward the lower end of the sacrum, linking the sacral and caudal regions.  

###Shape###  
Fused structure with decreasing width toward the tail.  

###Parts###  

##Body##  
Supports the transition from the pelvis to the tail.  
Energetically, represents **release and transition**.  

##Lateral Crests##  
Attachment points for ligaments and muscles.  
Energetically, reflect **connection and resilience**.  

###Function###  
Stabilizes tail base and transmits motion from pelvis to caudal vertebrae.  
Energetically, governs **release of tension and emotional letting go**.
