#S5#

##Location##  
The fifth sacral vertebra forms the lowest part of the sacrum, articulating with the first caudal vertebra.  
It marks the end of the fused sacral structure and beginning of the tail.  

###Shape###  
Smaller and tapering, blending into the caudal vertebrae.  

###Parts###  

##Body##  
Serves as a bridge to the tail’s flexible vertebrae.  
Energetically, represents **closure and renewal**.  

##Sacral Canal##  
Continues the spinal canal to the coccygeal region.  
Energetically, symbolizes **continuity and transition**.  

###Function###  
Transfers energy into tail motion and completes the spinal fusion.  
Energetically, governs **release, flow, and transformation**.
