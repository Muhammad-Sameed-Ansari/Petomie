#S1#

##Location##  
The first sacral vertebra lies directly behind the lumbar spine, forming the upper part of the sacrum and articulating with the last lumbar vertebra.  
It connects the spine to the pelvis and serves as the main link between the hindquarters and the upper body.

###Shape###  
A broad vertebra with strong wings extending laterally to join the ilium of the pelvis.  

###Parts###  

##Body##  
Wide and weight-bearing, supporting the transition from spine to pelvis.  
Energetically, represents **foundation and strength**.  

##Ala (Wing)##  
Connects to the ilium, transmitting force from hindquarters to spine.  
Energetically, symbolizes **stability and grounded power**.  

###Function###  
Transfers energy from the hind limbs to the spine, initiating propulsion and carrying weight evenly.  
Energetically, governs **root stability and physical confidence**.
