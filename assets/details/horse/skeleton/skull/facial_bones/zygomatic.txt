#Zygomatic Bone#

##Location##
Also known as the cheekbone, located on the lateral side of the face.  
Forms part of the orbit and the zygomatic arch.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Central portion that contributes to the orbit.  
   Provides surface for facial muscle attachment.

2. **Temporal Process** – Extends caudally to meet the zygomatic process of the temporal bone.  
   Together, they form the zygomatic arch.

3. **Orbital Margin** – The upper border forming part of the eye socket.  
   Protects the eye and supports surrounding soft tissues.

##Function##
 Forms the lateral wall of the orbit and the zygomatic arch.  
 Provides attachment for masseter muscles used in chewing.  
 Contributes to facial contour and strength.  
 Supports eye and jaw movement coordination.
