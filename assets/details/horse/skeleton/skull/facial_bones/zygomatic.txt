#Zygomatic Bone#

##Location##
The zygomatic bone, or cheekbone, lies on the side of the face and forms the prominence of the cheek and part of the eye socket.

###Shape###
Strong, curved bone connecting the maxilla to the temporal bone.

###Parts###

##Temporal Process##
Extends backward to meet the temporal bone.  
Energetically, represents communication and external expression.

##Orbital Margin##
Forms part of the eye socket.  
Energetically, symbolizes vision and awareness.

###Function###
Supports the face, protects the eye, and defines facial contour.  
Energetically, governs expression, social interaction, and perspective.
