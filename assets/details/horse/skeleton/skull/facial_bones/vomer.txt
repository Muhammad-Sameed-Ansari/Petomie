#Vomer#

##Location##
A thin, flat bone situated in the midline of the skull within the nasal cavity.  
It forms part of the nasal septum, dividing the left and right nasal passages.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Long, narrow plate forming the lower portion of the nasal septum.  
   Extends from the presphenoid bone to the incisive region.  
   Lies between the two halves of the nasal cavity.

2. **Wings (Alae)** – Small lateral expansions that articulate with the sphenoid and palatine bones.  
   Contribute to the stability and alignment of the nasal cavity.

##Function##
 Divides the nasal cavity into right and left chambers.  
 Supports the cartilage of the nasal septum.  
 Helps guide airflow through the nasal passages.  
 Contributes to the overall structure and symmetry of the face.
