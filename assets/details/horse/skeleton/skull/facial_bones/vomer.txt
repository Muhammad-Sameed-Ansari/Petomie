#Vomer#

##Location##
The vomer lies in the midline of the skull, forming the lower part of the nasal septum between the nostrils.

###Shape###
Thin, plow-shaped bone that runs along the nasal floor, dividing the nasal passages.

###Parts###

##Posterior Edge##
Joins the sphenoid and palatine bones.  
Energetically, symbolizes inner structure and direction.

##Anterior Edge##
Connects to the cartilage of the nasal septum.  
Energetically, represents communication and flow.

###Function###
Divides the nasal cavity and directs airflow.  
Energetically, governs balance between inner and outer awareness.
