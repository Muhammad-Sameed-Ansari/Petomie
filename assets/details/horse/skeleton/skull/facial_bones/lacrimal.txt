#Lacrimal Bone#

##Location##
A small bone located on the medial wall of the orbit (eye socket).  
Situated near the junction of the maxilla and frontal bones.

###Parts###
1. **Orbital Surface** – Forms part of the inner wall of the orbit.  
   Smooth surface that supports the eye and ocular structures.

2. **Lacrimal Fossa** – Small depression that houses the lacrimal sac.  
   Connected to the nasolacrimal duct, which drains tears into the nasal cavity.

##Function##
 Supports the eye and forms part of the orbital cavity.  
 Provides passage for the tear drainage system.  
 Contributes to facial symmetry and orbital protection.
