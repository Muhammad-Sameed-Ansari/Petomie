#Lacrimal Bone#

##Location##
The lacrimal bone lies at the inner corner of the orbit, forming part of the eye socket near the tear duct.  
It connects the frontal and maxillary bones.

###Shape###
Small, thin bone with grooves for the nasolacrimal canal.

###Parts###

##Lacrimal Fossa##
Houses the lacrimal sac and tear ducts.  
Energetically, represents emotional flow and release.

##Orbital Surface##
Forms part of the inner wall of the eye socket.  
Energetically, symbolizes insight and gentle perception.

###Function###
Supports tear flow from the eyes into the nasal cavity.  
Energetically, governs emotional cleansing and soft awareness.
