#Nasal Bone#

##Location##
Located along the midline of the face, forming the bridge of the nose.  
Extends from the frontal bone to the nasal cartilages.

###Parts###
1. **Body** – Elongated plate that meets its pair at the midline.  
   Covers the nasal cavity and provides protection.  
   Articulates with the frontal, maxilla, and incisive bones.

2. **Caudal End** – Joins the frontal bone and helps shape the nasal profile.  
   Forms part of the nasal opening and supports soft tissues of the muzzle.

##Function##
 Forms the upper surface of the nasal cavity.  
 Protects nasal structures and provides attachment for facial muscles.  
 Contributes to airflow direction and facial structure.
