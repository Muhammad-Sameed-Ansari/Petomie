#Nasal Bone#

##Location##
The nasal bones form the bridge of the nose, lying between the frontal and maxillary bones.  
They cover and protect the nasal cavity.

###Shape###
Paired, elongated bones that join at the midline and taper toward the nostrils.

###Parts###

##Dorsal Surface##
Forms the upper ridge of the nose.  
Energetically, represents direction and sensitivity.

##Ventral Border##
Connects to the nasal cartilage.  
Energetically, symbolizes breath and intuition.

###Function###
Protects the nasal cavity and forms part of the air passage.  
Energetically, governs intuition, breath, and awareness of environment.
