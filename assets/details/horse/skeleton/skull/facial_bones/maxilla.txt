#Maxilla#

##Location##
The maxilla forms the upper jaw and part of the face, holding the upper cheek teeth and contributing to the eye socket and nasal cavity.

###Shape###
Large, irregular bone forming the lateral facial structure with alveolar ridges for teeth.

###Parts###

##Alveolar Process##
Holds the upper molars and premolars.  
Energetically, symbolizes stability and nourishment.

##Infraorbital Foramen##
Opening for the infraorbital nerve and blood vessels.  
Energetically, represents awareness and sensitivity.

##Palatine Process##
Forms part of the roof of the mouth.  
Energetically, connects communication and nourishment.

###Function###
Supports teeth, forms the upper jaw, and contributes to breathing and chewing.  
Energetically, governs balance, nourishment, and sensory awareness.
