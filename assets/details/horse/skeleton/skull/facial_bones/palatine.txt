#Palatine Bone#

##Location##
The palatine bones form the back part of the hard palate and contribute to the floor of the nasal cavity.

###Shape###
Paired, L-shaped bones that connect the maxilla to the sphenoid and vomer.

###Parts###

##Horizontal Plate##
Forms the posterior part of the hard palate.  
Energetically, represents support and integrity.

##Perpendicular Plate##
Forms part of the nasal wall.  
Energetically, symbolizes structure and balance.

###Function###
Supports the roof of the mouth and separates oral and nasal cavities.  
Energetically, governs boundaries and communication balance.
