#Parietal Bone#

##Location##
The parietal bones form the upper sides and roof of the skull, meeting along the midline at the sagittal crest.

###Shape###
Broad, flat bones that provide strong protection for the brain.

###Parts###

##Sagittal Crest##
The midline ridge for muscle attachment.  
Energetically, represents focus and centered strength.

##Lateral Plates##
Form the sides of the cranial vault.  
Energetically, symbolize structure and harmony.

###Function###
Protects the brain and anchors muscles for head movement.  
Energetically, governs stability, focus, and inner balance.
