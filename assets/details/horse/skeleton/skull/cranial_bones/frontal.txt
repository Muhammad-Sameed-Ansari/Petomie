#Frontal Bone#

##Location##
The frontal bone forms the forehead and upper part of the horse’s skull, extending over the eyes and part of the nasal cavity.

###Shape###
A broad, slightly curved bone that forms part of the cranial roof and contains the frontal sinuses.
