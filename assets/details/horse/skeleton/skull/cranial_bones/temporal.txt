#Temporal Bone#

##Location##
The temporal bones lie on each side of the skull, forming the lower sides and base of the cranial structure.  
They house the ear canal and support the jaw joint.

###Shape###
Complex, irregular bones containing the auditory and balance organs.

###Parts###

##Squamous Part##
Flat upper section forming part of the skull wall.  
Energetically, represents clarity and openness.

##Tympanic Part##
Surrounds the ear canal.  
Energetically, symbolizes communication and listening.

##Petrous Part##
Dense portion housing the inner ear structures.  
Energetically, reflects deep awareness and stability.

##Zygomatic Process##
Forms part of the cheek arch.  
Energetically, connects expression and strength.

###Function###
Protects hearing organs and forms part of the jaw joint.  
Energetically, governs awareness, listening, and balance between thought and action.
