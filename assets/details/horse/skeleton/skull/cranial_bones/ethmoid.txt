#Ethmoid#

##Location##
The ethmoid bone lies between the eyes at the roof of the nasal cavity, forming part of both the cranial floor and nasal passages.  
It separates the nasal cavity from the brain and supports the olfactory structures.

###Shape###
A light, spongy, and perforated bone that allows passage of olfactory nerves through small openings.

###Parts###

##Cribriform Plate##
A perforated plate through which the olfactory nerves pass.
