#Rib 1#

##Location##
Rib 1 belongs to the group known as true ribs, which include ribs 1 to 8.
These ribs attach directly to the sternum via costal cartilage, forming the strong and protective front section of the ribcage.
They provide a stable yet flexible framework around the chest cavity.

###Shape###
Curved, elongated bones that arch from the thoracic vertebrae toward the sternum, with rounded dorsal ends and flatter ventral ends.

###Function###
Protect the lungs and heart, support the chest wall, and assist in breathing by expanding and contracting with each breath.
Energetically, they represent stability, protection, and strength through openness.

###Energetic Connection###
Chakra: Heart and Solar Plexus Chakras.
Meridian: Lung and Stomach Meridians.
Emotional Meaning: Security, courage, and calm strength.
Balanced Energy: Open-hearted yet centered and secure.

###Common Imbalances###
Joint stiffness, restricted breathing, or rib inflammation.
Energetically, overprotection, emotional tension, or shallow breathing.

###Energetic Healing Suggestions###
Colors: Green and yellow promote balance and vitality.
Crystals: Aventurine and citrine strengthen openness and peace.
Elements: Air and earth harmonize movement and grounding.
Affirmation: “I am open, secure, and breathe freely with strength.”
