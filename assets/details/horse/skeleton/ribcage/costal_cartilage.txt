#Costal Cartilage#

##Location##
The costal cartilage forms the flexible connection between the ribs and the sternum.
In horses, it extends from the ventral ends of the true and false ribs, allowing movement and elasticity of the ribcage.

###Shape###
Smooth, translucent cartilage that curves inward toward the sternum, gradually blending with the bone of each rib.

###Function###
Provides flexibility and resilience to the thoracic cage, enabling expansion during breathing while protecting internal organs.
Energetically, it represents adaptability, emotional flexibility, and balance between structure and flow.
