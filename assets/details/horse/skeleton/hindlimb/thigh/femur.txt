#Femur#

##Location##
The femur is the large bone of the upper hind limb, connecting the pelvis to the tibia at the stifle joint.
It lies between the hip and the knee, forming the main framework of the thigh.

###Shape###
A strong, slightly curved bone with a broad upper head and a solid, cylindrical shaft.

###Parts###

##Head##
Articulates with the pelvis at the acetabulum to form the hip joint.
Energetically, represents strength, grounding, and purpose.

##Shaft##
The long middle portion that provides leverage and support.
Energetically, symbolizes stability and forward drive.

##Distal End##
Forms the stifle joint with the tibia and patella.
Energetically, reflects flexibility, coordination, and confidence.

###Function###
Supports the body’s weight, allows powerful propulsion, and serves as an anchor for major muscles of the hind limb.
Energetically, governs strength in movement, determination, and stability.
