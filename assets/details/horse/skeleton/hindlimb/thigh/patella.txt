#Patella#

##Location##
The patella, or kneecap, is located at the front of the stifle joint between the femur and the tibia.
It sits within the tendon of the quadriceps muscle, protecting and supporting the joint.

###Shape###
A small, triangular, flat bone that glides over the femur during movement.

###Parts###

##Base##
Broad upper surface attached to the quadriceps tendon.
Energetically, represents support and alignment.

##Apex##
Pointed lower end connected to the patellar ligament.
Energetically, symbolizes focus and precision.

##Articular Surface##
Smooth underside that contacts the femur.
Energetically, reflects balance and fluidity in motion.

###Function###
Acts as a lever for the quadriceps muscles, protects the stifle joint, and improves movement efficiency.
Energetically, governs coordination, clarity, and flow in physical and emotional movement.
