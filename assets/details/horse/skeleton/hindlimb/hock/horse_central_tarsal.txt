#Central Tarsal Bone#

##Location##
Lies between the proximal and distal rows of the tarsal bones.
Articulates proximally with the talus and distally with the third tarsal bone.

##Shape##
Cuboidal bone located centrally in the tarsus.
Its smooth articular surfaces facilitate gliding between adjacent bones.

##Function##
Links the proximal and distal rows of the tarsus to provide flexibility and stability.
Distributes weight from the talus to the distal tarsal bones.
