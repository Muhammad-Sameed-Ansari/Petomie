#Pelvis#

##Location##  
The pelvis forms the **hindmost part of the trunk**, connecting the **spine** to the **hind limbs**.  
It lies between the lumbar vertebrae and the tail, forming the **pelvic cavity** that supports the abdominal organs and anchors the hindquarters.

###Shape###  
A **large, basin-like bony structure** composed of fused bones — the ilium, ischium, and pubis — creating strength and stability for propulsion.

###Parts###

##Ilium##  
The broad, wing-shaped upper portion connecting to the spine.  
Energetically, represents **freedom, expansion, and grounding**.

##Ischium##  
Forms the lower, rear part that supports sitting and muscle attachment.  
Energetically, symbolizes **support and endurance**.

##Pubis##  
Forms the lower front of the pelvis and joins both halves at the pelvic symphysis.  
Energetically, reflects **balance and unity**.

##Sacrum Connection##  
Fuses with the spine to form the **sacroiliac joint**, transmitting body weight to the hind limbs.  
Energetically, embodies **stability and core alignment**.

###Function###  
Supports the weight of the body, provides attachment for powerful hindlimb muscles, and protects pelvic and reproductive organs.  
Energetically, governs **grounding, power, and creative flow**.
