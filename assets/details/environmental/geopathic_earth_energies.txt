#Geopathic & Earth Energies#

###What It Is###
Geopathic and earth energies refer to natural and man-made energetic disturbances within the earth that can affect living beings. These energies arise from underground water veins, fault lines, mineral concentrations, disturbed land, and subterranean structures, as well as human-made interference such as underground cables and pipelines. Animals are highly sensitive to these subtle energies and may react strongly when resting, sleeping, or feeding in affected areas.

###How It May Form###
Geopathic stress may form through underground water streams, geological fault lines, disturbed soil, excavation, buried metal, underground electrical cables, pipelines, land with a history of trauma, or energetically imbalanced locations. Stress often develops when animals spend prolonged time resting or sleeping in geopathically stressed zones, especially when their recovery and rest areas are affected.

###Possible Effects###
Geopathic stress may contribute to chronic fatigue, restlessness, sleep disturbance, unexplained anxiety, immune weakness, joint or muscle discomfort, behavioural changes, poor recovery, or recurring health issues that do not resolve despite appropriate care. Animals may repeatedly avoid or be drawn to specific areas without obvious physical reasons.

###Energetic & Nervous System Impact###
Geopathic stress can interfere with grounding, energetic stability, and nervous system regulation. It may weaken the energy field, disrupt natural rhythms, and place the body in a constant state of low-grade stress. Long-term exposure can reduce resilience and impair the ability to rest, heal, and self-regulate.
